### Palace of Hearing TĪNGGŌNG
![[SI-19 听宫.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Small Intestine, Sanjiao and Gall Bladder channels

#### Location

With the mouth open, this point is located in the depression between the middle of the tragus and the condyloid process of the mandible.

#### Location Notes

In order to locate this point, ask the patient to open the mouth so that the condyloid process of the mandible slides forwards to reveal the depression.

#### Needling

i. Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun; ii. Needle with the mouth open; following insertion of the needle the patient may close their mouth.

#### Actions

- Benefits the ears
- Calms the spirit

#### Indications

- Deafness, tinnitus, purulent discharge from the ear.
- Mania, epilepsy, loss of voice, fullness of the Heart and abdomen, toothache.

#### Commentary

There are three points anterior to the tragus of the ear, [Ěrmén SJ-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-21) superiorly, Tīnggōng SI-19 in the middle, and [Tīnghuì GB-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-2) inferiorly. All are frequently employed for the treatment of a wide variety of ear disorders, including tinnitus, deafness, pain, itching and discharge. Due to the close proximity of these points and the similar indications for each, it is difficult to distinguish between them clinically. It is worth noting however that Tīnggōng SI-19 has a secondary action of calming the spirit, whilst [Tīnghuì GB-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-2) is the most effective to treat painful disorders of the surrounding area including the jaw and teeth. If it is necessary to needle points around the ear regularly, then these three points should be alternated.

In current practice, where points are numbered, it is easy to remember the relative locations of [Ěrmén SJ-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-21) (Gate of the Ear), Tīnggōng SI-19 (Palace of Hearing) and [Tīnghuì GB-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-2) (Meeting of Hearing), the highest point number being the uppermost point. In China, which has no tradition of numbering the points, one way of recalling the order of their location was by the image of entering through the 'gate' of the 'palace' to convene a 'meeting'.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Much used for all ear disorders including deafness, tinnitus and purulent discharge.

#### Combinations

- Deafness due to qi obstruction: Tīnggōng SI-19, [Tīnghuì GB-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-2) and [Yìfēng SJ-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-17); then needle [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) and [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) (Great Compendium).
- Deafness and tinnitus: Tīnggōng SI-19, [Tiānróng SI-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-17), [Tīnghuì GB-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-2) and [Zhōngzhǔ SJ-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-3) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Deafness: Tīnggōng SI-19, [Zhōngzhǔ SJ-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-3), [Wàiguān SJ-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-5), [Ěrhéliáo SJ-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-22), [Shāngyáng LI-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-1), [Tīnghuì GB-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-2), [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) and [Zhōngchōng PC-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-9) (Precious Mirror).
- Impaired hearing and deafness: Tīnggōng SI-19, [Ěrmén SJ-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-21), [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20), [Xiáxī GB-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-43) and [Tīnghuì GB-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-2) (Great Compendium).
- Sadness below the Heart: Tīnggōng SI-19 and [Píshū BL-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-20) (One Hundred Symptoms).

### **[SI19 听宫](/point/SI19.md)** (TĪNGGŌNG)

* **英文名称：** Palace of Hearing
* **中文名称：** 听宫
* **国际代码：** SI-19
* **穴位属性：** **[SI19 听宫](/point/SI19.md)** 是小肠经、三焦经和胆经的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

张口时，此穴位于耳屏中部与下颌骨髁状突之间的凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 为了定位此穴，请患者张口，使下颌骨髁状突向前滑动，以显露出凹陷。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

i. 垂直刺0.5至1寸； ii. 针刺时请患者张口；进针后患者可闭口。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利耳部
* 宁心安神

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

耳聋，耳鸣，耳部流脓。
癫狂，癫痫，失音，心腹胀满，牙痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

耳屏前方有三个穴位：上方是**[SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)**，中间是**[SI19 听宫](/point/SI19.md)**，下方是**[GB2 听会](/point/GB2.md)**。所有这些穴位都常用于治疗各种耳部疾病，包括耳鸣、耳聋、疼痛、瘙痒和流脓。由于这些穴位距离很近，且主治相似，临床上很难区分。然而值得注意的是，**[SI19 听宫](/point/SI19.md)** 具有宁心安神的次要作用，而**[GB2 听会](/point/GB2.md)** 在治疗包括颌部和牙齿在内的周围区域疼痛方面最为有效。如果需要定期针刺耳周穴位，这三个穴位应交替使用。

在目前的实践中，当穴位有编号时，很容易记住**[SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)** (Gate of the Ear)、**[SI19 听宫](/point/SI19.md)** (Palace of Hearing) 和**[GB2 听会](/point/GB2.md)** (Meeting of Hearing) 的相对位置，穴位编号最高的点在最上方。在中国，没有穴位编号的传统，记住它们位置顺序的一种方法是：如同通过“门”进入“宫殿”召开“会议”的意象。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 广泛用于治疗所有耳部疾病，包括耳聋、耳鸣和耳部流脓。
* 辅助宁心安神，治疗癫狂、癫痫等。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 气滞耳聋：**[SI19 听宫](/point/SI19.md)**，**[GB2 听会](/point/GB2.md)** 和**[SJ17 翳风](/point/SJ17.md)**；然后针刺**[ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md)** 和**[LI4 合谷](/point/LI4.md)**（《针灸大成》）。
* 耳聋耳鸣：**[SI19 听宫](/point/SI19.md)**，**[SI17 天容](/point/SI17.md)**，**[GB2 听会](/point/GB2.md)** 和**[SJ3 中渚](/point/SJ3.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 耳聋：**[SI19 听宫](/point/SI19.md)**，**[SJ3 中渚](/point/SJ3.md)**，**[SJ5 外关](/point/SJ5.md)**，**[SJ22 耳和髎](/point/SJ22.md)**，**[LI1 商阳](/point/LI1.md)**，**[GB2 听会](/point/GB2.md)**，**[LI4 合谷](/point/LI4.md)** 和**[PC9 中冲](/point/PC9.md)**（《备急千金要方》）。
* 听力障碍和耳聋：**[SI19 听宫](/point/SI19.md)**，**[SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)**，**[GB20 风池](/point/GB20.md)**，**[GB43 侠溪](/point/GB43.md)** 和**[GB2 听会](/point/GB2.md)**（《针灸大成》）。
* 心下悲伤：**[SI19 听宫](/point/SI19.md)** 和**[BL20 脾俞](/point/BL20.md)**（《百症赋》）。
