### Restraining Bone SHÙGǓ
![[BL-65 束骨.png|250]]
- Shu-Stream and Wood point of the Bladder channel

#### Location

On the lateral side of the foot, in the depression posterior and inferior to the head of the fifth metatarsal bone.

#### Location Notes

Run a finger distally along the lateral side of the foot from [Jīnggǔ BL-64](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-64) until it falls into the depression.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Clears the head and eyes
- Clears heat and dissipates swelling
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Headache, occipital headache, stiff neck, deafness, visual dizziness, redness and pain of the eyes, redness and erosion of the inner canthus, yellow eyes.
- Chills and fever, aversion to wind and cold, heat in the body, malaria, mania-depression, carbuncular swellings on the back, clove sores on the back, haemorrhoids, diarrhoea.
- Pain of the lumbar region and back, thigh pain.

#### Commentary

The Classic of Difficulties[74](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references) states "in cases of deficiency reinforce the mother, in cases of excess reduce the child". Shùgǔ BL-65 is the wood point of the Bladder channel, and according to the 'mother-child' theory is able to reduce excess in the Bladder channel (wood is the 'child' of Bladder water), especially fullness and heat in the head region. The Bladder channel ascends from the inner canthus over the vertex, connects with points of the Governing vessel and Gall Bladder channel on the head, and descends through the occipital region and neck. Shùgǔ BL-65, consequently, is indicated for redness and pain of the eyes and inner canthus, headache (especially in the occipital region) and stiff neck.

The heat clearing action of Shùgǔ BL-65 extends to resolving fever, especially due to injury by wind, and to draining heat from the anal region in the treatment of haemorrhoids. Like [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40), Shùgǔ BL-65 is also indicated for carbuncular swellings on the back and for clove sores (small, hard, clove-shaped purulent lesions).

#### Summary of clinical application

- Clears fullness and heat from the head: redness and pain of the eyes and inner canthus, headache (especially occipital), stiff neck.
- Clears heat: fever, haemorrhoids, carbuncular swellings on the back, clove sores.

#### Combinations

- Stiff neck with great aversion to wind: Shùgǔ BL-65 and [Tiānzhù BL-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-10) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Redness and erosion of the inner canthus: Shùgǔ BL-65 and [Jīnggǔ BL-64](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-64) (Supplementing Life).
- Mania, incessant talking without rest: Shùgǔ BL-65, [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60) and [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Lumbar pain as if broken: Shùgǔ BL-65, [Fēiyáng BL-58](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-58) and [Chéngjīn BL-56](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-56) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Hip pain: Shùgǔ BL-65, [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30), [Jiāoxìn KID-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-8), [Sānyīnjiāo SP-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-6) and [Yīngǔ KID-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-10) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[BL65 束骨](/point/BL65.md)** (Shùgǔ)

* **英文名称：** Restraining Bone
* **中文名称：** 束骨
* **国际代码：** BL-65
* **穴位属性：** **[BL65 束骨](/point/BL65.md)** 是膀胱经的输穴和木穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于足外侧，第五跖骨头后下方凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 沿足外侧从**[BL64 京骨](/point/BL64.md)** 向远端滑动手指，直到其落入凹陷中。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.3至0.5寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 清头明目
* 清热消肿
* 通经活络，止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

头痛，枕部头痛，颈项强直，耳聋，视物眩晕，眼睛红肿疼痛，内眼角红赤糜烂，眼睛发黄。
恶寒发热，恶风畏寒，身热，疟疾，癫狂，背部痈疽，背部疔疮，痔疮，腹泻。
腰痛，背痛，大腿疼痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

《难经》记载：“虚则补其母，实则泻其子”。**[BL65 束骨](/point/BL65.md)** 是膀胱经的木穴，根据“母子”理论，能够泻膀胱经的实证（木是膀胱水之“子”），特别是头部胀满和热邪。膀胱经从内眼角上行，越过头顶，与头部督脉和胆经交会，然后向下循行于枕部和颈部。因此，**[BL65 束骨](/point/BL65.md)** 主治眼睛和内眼角红肿疼痛，头痛（特别是枕部）和颈项强直。

**[BL65 束骨](/point/BL65.md)** 的清热作用延伸至解热，特别是因风邪所致者，以及清泻肛门区域的热邪以治疗痔疮。像**[BL40 委中](/point/BL40.md)** 一样，**[BL65 束骨](/point/BL65.md)** 也主治背部痈疽和疔疮（小而硬、丁香状的化脓性病变）。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* **清泻头部胀满和热邪**：眼睛和内眼角红肿疼痛，头痛（特别是枕部），颈项强直。
* **清热**：发热，痔疮，背部痈疽，疔疮。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 颈项强直伴剧烈怕风：**[BL65 束骨](/point/BL65.md)** 和 **[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)**（《百症赋》）。
* 内眼角红赤糜烂：**[BL65 束骨](/point/BL65.md)** 和 **[BL64 京骨](/point/BL64.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 癫狂，言语不止：**[BL65 束骨](/point/BL65.md)**，**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 和 **[DU16 风府](/point/DU16.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 腰痛如断：**[BL65 束骨](/point/BL65.md)**，**[BL58 飞扬](/point/BL58.md)** 和 **[BL56 承筋](/point/BL56.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 髋部疼痛：**[BL65 束骨](/point/BL65.md)**，**[GB30 环跳](/point/GB30.md)**，**[KID8 交信](/point/KID8.md)**，**[SP6 三阴交](/point/SP6.md)** 和 **[KID10 阴谷](/point/KID10.md)**（《千金方》）。
