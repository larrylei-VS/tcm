### Support the Sinews CHÉNGJĪN
![[BL-56 承筋.png|250]]
#### Location

On the lower leg, 5 cun below [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) and midway between [Héyáng BL-55](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-55) and [Chéngshān BL-57](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-57), in the centre of the belly of the gastrocnemius muscle.

#### Location Notes

First locate [Chéngshān BL-57](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-57) then [Héyáng BL-55](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-55). Cheng-Chéngjīn BL-56 is located midway between these two points.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.  
**Note:**: according to several classical texts, this point is contraindicated to needling.

#### Actions

- Relaxes the sinews, activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Benefits the foot and heel

#### Indications

- Contraction and pain of the lumbar spine, painful obstruction of the lower leg, shivering with inability to stand for long, heat sensation in the soles with inability to stand for long, pain and contraction of the foot and heel, swelling of the axilla.
- Haemorrhoids, constipation, incontinence of both urine and faeces.
- Nosebleed, rhinitis, heat in the head, dizziness and headache, sudden turmoil disorder with cramps, clonic spasm, urticaria.

#### Commentary

As its name 'Support the Sinews' implies, Chéngjīn BL-56 is indicated for pain and contraction of the muscles and sinews, especially in the lumbar region and calf.

It is one of the attributes of the channels that as they travel distally towards the hand or foot, their points have an increasingly pronounced action on the whole length of the channel. Although not a major point, Chéngjīn BL-56 is characterised by its ability to treat disorders of the whole Bladder channel in the head (dizziness, headache, heat in the head, nosebleed, rhinitis), the lumbar region, the lower leg and calf, and the foot and heel.

Heel pain is often a difficult disorder to treat, and requires not only local points on the heel itself, but also proximal points. Chéngjīn BL-56 and [Chéngshān BL-57](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-57) are the two principal proximal points that affect this region of the foot.

Finally, like several distal points of the Bladder channel, Chéngjīn BL-56 treats both swelling of the axilla and haemorrhoids. This may be explained by the pathways of the Bladder sinew channel to the axilla and the Bladder divergent channel which winds around the anus.

#### Summary of clinical application

- For pain and contraction of the muscles and sinews, especially in the lumbar region and calf.
- For disorders along the whole course of the Bladder channel, e.g. dizziness, headache, heat in the head, nosebleed, rhinitis, haemorrhoids, pain of the lumbar region, lower leg, calf, foot and heel.

#### Combinations

- Haemorrhoids, swelling of the axilla: Chéngjīn BL-56, [Chéngfú BL-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-36), [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) and [Yánggǔ SI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-5) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Difficulty in defecation: Chéngjīn BL-56, [Chéngshān BL-57](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-57), [Dàzhōng KID-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-4), [Zhōngliáo BL-33](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-33), [Guānyuán REN-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-4), [Tàichōng LIV-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-3), [Tàixī KID-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-3) and [Zhōngwǎn REN-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-12) (Supplementing Life).
- Pain of the lumbar region and aversion to cold: Chéngjīn BL-56, [Cìliáo BL-32](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-32) and [Bāohuāng BL-53](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-53) (Supplementing Life).
- Lumbar pain as if broken: Chéngjīn BL-56, [Shùgǔ BL-65](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-65) and [Fēiyáng BL-58](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-58) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Contraction of the legs: Chéngjīn BL-56, [Chéngshān BL-57](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-57), [Jīnggǔ BL-64](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-64) and [Shāngqiū SP-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-5) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Numbness of the lower leg: Chéngjīn BL-56, [Xīyángguān GB-33](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-33) and [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Heel pain: Chéngjīn BL-56, [Chéngshān BL-57](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-57), [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60) and Ahshi points.


### **[BL56 承筋](/point/BL56.md)** (Chéngjīn)

* **英文名称：** Support the Sinews
* **中文名称：** 承筋
* **国际代码：** BL-56

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于小腿部，**[BL40 委中](/point/BL40.md)** 下方5寸，**[BL55 合阳](/point/BL55.md)** 和**[BL57 承山](/point/BL57.md)** 之间中点，腓肠肌肌腹中央。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 首先定位**[BL57 承山](/point/BL57.md)**，然后是**[BL55 合阳](/point/BL55.md)**。**[BL56 承筋](/point/BL56.md)** 位于这两个穴位之间中点。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺1至1.5寸。

* **注意 (Note):** 根据几部经典文献，此穴禁针。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 舒筋活络，止痛
* 宣利足部和足跟

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

腰椎挛缩疼痛，小腿痹痛，战栗不能久站，足底热感不能久站，足部和足跟疼痛挛缩，腋窝肿胀。
痔疮，便秘，大小便失禁。
鼻出血，鼻炎，头热，眩晕和头痛，霍乱吐泻伴痉挛，痉挛，荨麻疹。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

正如其名称“承筋”所示，**[BL56 承筋](/point/BL56.md)** 主治肌肉和筋脉的疼痛和挛缩，尤其是在腰部和小腿。

经脉的一个特点是，它们向手或足远端循行时，其穴位对整条经脉的作用越来越显著。**[BL56 承筋](/point/BL56.md)** 虽然不是一个主要穴位，但其特点是能够治疗膀胱经在头部（眩晕，头痛，头热，鼻出血，鼻炎）、腰部、小腿和足部的全程疾病。

足跟痛常是难以治疗的疾病，不仅需要足跟本身的局部穴位，还需要近端穴位。**[BL56 承筋](/point/BL56.md)** 和**[BL57 承山](/point/BL57.md)** 是影响足部此区域的两个主要近端穴位。

最后，像膀胱经的几个远端穴位一样，**[BL56 承筋](/point/BL56.md)** 既治疗腋窝肿胀又治疗痔疮。这可以解释为膀胱经筋通向腋窝，以及膀胱经别绕行肛门。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗肌肉和筋脉的疼痛和挛缩，尤其是在腰部和小腿。
* 治疗膀胱经全程的疾病，例如眩晕、头痛、头热、鼻出血、鼻炎、痔疮、腰部、小腿、足部和足跟疼痛。
