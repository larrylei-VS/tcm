### SHÉNDÀO
![[DU-11 神道.png|250]]
#### Location

On the midline of the upper back, in the depression below the spinous process of the fifth thoracic vertebra.

#### Location Notes

This point is located five intervertebral spaces below C7. To locate C7 see [Surface Anatomy](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-location-needling/surface-anatomy "Surface Anatomy").

#### Needling

Oblique superior insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Note:** according to several classical texts, this point is contraindicated to needling.  
**Caution:** the spinal canal lies between 1.25 and 1.75 cun deep to the skin surface, varying according to body build.

#### Actions

- Tonifies the Heart and Lung and calms the spirit
- Clears heat and pacifies wind

#### Indications

- Sadness and anxiety, poor memory, fright palpitations, disorientation, timidity with shortness of breath, lack of qi, taxation consumption.
- Childhood wind epilepsy, clonic spasm, childhood fright wind, lockjaw.
- Fever due to injury by cold with headache that comes and goes, heat in the body, cough and hot dyspnoea, malaria, dizziness, heat in the Liver.
- Pain and cold sensation of the upper back.

#### Commentary

Shéndào DU-11 (Spirit Pathway) is located on the upper back in the region of the Lung, at the level of [Xīnshū BL-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-15) the back-shu point of the Heart. The Lung dominates qi and the Heart stores the spirit, and together they are activated by the gathering qi. Shéndào DU-11 is indicated for deficiency of the gathering qi and malnourishment of the Heart and spirit giving rise to such indications as lack of qi, timidity with shortness of breath, palpitations, disorientation, poor memory and sadness and anxiety.

In common with many points of this channel, Shéndào DU-11 is also able to pacify internally generated wind which ascends along the Governing vessel and gives rise to such disorders as epilepsy, clonic spasm, childhood fright wind and lockjaw.

Finally Shéndào DU-11 is indicated for a variety of heat symptoms including fever, heat in the body, hot dyspnoea and, according to the Essential Questions[32](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-governing-vessel/references), heat in the Liver. This last indication clearly fell out of favour and was not mentioned in subsequent texts.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Tonifies the gathering (zong) qi: lack of qi, timidity with shortness of breath, palpitations, disorientation, poor memory, sadness and anxiety.
- Treats interior wind: epilepsy, clonic spasm, lockjaw, childhood fright wind (a disorder of infants and children characterised by convulsive spasm of the limbs and loss of consciousness; it is generally subdivided into acute and chronic fright wind).

#### Combinations

- Poor memory: Shéndào DU-11, [Yōumén KID-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-21), [Lièquē LU-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-7) and [Gāohuāngshū BL-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-43) (Supplementing Life).
- Sadness, anxiety and disorientation: Shéndào DU-11, [Xīnshū BL-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-15) and [Tiānjǐng SJ-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-10) (Supplementing Life).
- Fright palpitations: Shéndào DU-11, [Yèmén SJ-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-2), [Tiānjǐng SJ-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-10) and [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) (Supplementing Life).
- Frequent attacks of wind epilepsy: Shéndào DU-11 and [Xīnshū BL-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-15) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Heat in the body with headache that comes and goes: Shéndào DU-11 and [Guānyuán REN-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-4) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).


### 1. [DU11 神道](point/DU11.md)

#### 1.1. Spirit Pathway 神道

#### 1.2. 定位

位于上背部中线，第五胸椎棘突下方的凹陷中。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

此穴位于第七颈椎（C7）下方五个椎间隙。定位C7见表面解剖学。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

向上斜刺，深0.5至1寸。  
**注意：**  
1. 多部经典文献认为此穴禁针；  
2. 脊管位于皮肤表面下1.25至1.75寸，深度因体型而异。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 补心肺，定神  
- 清热平风  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 悲忧焦虑、记忆差、惊悸、神志迷乱、怯弱气短、气虚、劳损。  
- 小儿风痫、痉挛、小儿惊风、牙关紧闭。  
- 寒伤发热伴头痛时来时去、身热、咳嗽热性呼吸困难、疟疾、眩晕、肝热。  
- 上背痛冷感。  

#### 1.7. 论述

[DU11 神道](point/DU11.md)（神道）位于上背肺区，与心俞[BL15 心俞](point/BL15.md)同高。肺主气，心藏神，二者由宗气激活。[DU11 神道](point/DU11.md)适用于宗气虚及心神失养引起的症状，如气虚、怯弱气短、悸动、神志迷乱、记忆差及悲忧焦虑。  

与督脉多穴相同，[DU11 神道](point/DU11.md)能平沿督脉上扰的内生风，适用于癫痫、痉挛、小儿惊风（婴幼儿四肢抽搐及意识丧失疾患，分为急慢性）及牙关紧闭。  

[DU11 神道](point/DU11.md)亦用于多种热证，包括发热、身热、热性呼吸困难，及《素问》所述肝热，此后一指征未获后世认同，后续文献不再提及。  

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 补宗气：气虚、怯弱气短、悸动、神志迷乱、记忆差、悲忧焦虑。  
- 治疗内风：癫痫、痉挛、牙关紧闭、小儿惊风（婴幼儿四肢抽搐及意识丧失疾患，分为急慢性）。  

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 记忆差：[DU11 神道](point/DU11.md) + [KID21 幽门](point/KID21.md) + [LU7 列缺](point/LU7.md) + [BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)（《补命》）。  
- 悲忧焦虑及神志迷乱：[DU11 神道](point/DU11.md) + [BL15 心俞](point/BL15.md) + [SJ10 天井](point/SJ10.md)（《补命》）。  
- 惊悸：[DU11 神道](point/DU11.md) + [SJ2 液门](point/SJ2.md) + [SJ10 天井](point/SJ10.md) + [DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)（《补命》）。  
- 风痫频发：[DU11 神道](point/DU11.md) + [BL15 心俞](point/BL15.md)（《百症赋》）。  
- 身热伴头痛时来时去：[DU11 神道](point/DU11.md) + [REN4 关元](point/REN4.md)（《千金方》）。
