### LÍNGTÁI
![[DU-10 灵台.png|250]]
#### Location

On the midline of the back, in the depression below the spinous process of the sixth thoracic vertebra.

#### Location Notes

This point is located six intervertebral spaces below C7. To locate C7 see [Surface Anatomy](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-location-needling/surface-anatomy "Surface Anatomy").

#### Needling

Oblique superior insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Note:** according to several classical texts, this point is contraindicated to needling.  
**Caution:** the spinal canal lies between 1.25 and 1.75 cun deep to the skin surface, varying according to body build.

#### Actions

- Alleviates cough and wheezing
- Clears heat and detoxifies poison

#### Indications

- Dyspnoea, asthma, chronic cough, wind cold chronic cough.
- Heat in the Spleen, steaming bone taxation consumption, carbuncles and furuncles, clove sores.
- Back pain, stiffness of the neck.

#### Commentary

The name Lingtai (Spirit Tower) is a traditional term for the Heart and has come to denote the reasoning faculty. It originated with the Spirit Tower built by the emperor Wen Wang as a vantage point to survey all that lay beneath him. Despite this name and the fact that Língtái DU-10 is located one vertebra below [Xīnshū BL-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-15), the back-shu point of the Heart, there are no indications or combinations reflecting this association in any major classic.

Língtái DU-10 is an empirical point in the treatment of furuncle and red-thread furuncle (lymphangitis) as well as for clove sores (small, hard, deep-rooted, clove-shaped purulent lesions), and is used for this purpose in modern clinical practice. It is also indicated in the Essential Questions[31](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-governing-vessel/references) for heat in the Spleen. This indication clearly fell out of favour and was not mentioned in subsequent texts.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Empirical point for furuncle, red-thread furuncle (lymphangitis) and clove sores (small, hard, deep-rooted, clove-shaped purulent lesions).

#### Combinations

- Furuncle: Língtái DU-10, [Shēnzhù DU-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-12), [Xīmén PC-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-4), [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) and [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40).


### 1. [DU10 灵台](point/DU10.md)

#### 1.1. Spirit Tower 靈台

#### 1.2. 定位

位于背部中线，第六胸椎棘突下方的凹陷中。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

此穴位于第七颈椎（C7）下方六个椎间隙。定位C7见表面解剖学。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

向上斜刺，深0.5至1寸。  
**注意：**  
1. 多部经典文献认为此穴禁针；  
2. 脊管位于皮肤表面下1.25至1.75寸，深度因体型而异。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 止咳平喘  
- 清热解毒  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 呼吸困难、哮喘、慢性咳嗽、风寒慢性咳嗽。  
- 脾热、骨蒸劳损、痈疖、丁疮。  
- 背痛、颈项僵硬。  

#### 1.7. 论述

“灵台”之名传统指心，象征理性，源于文王建灵台以俯瞰一切。尽管[DU10 灵台](point/DU10.md)位于心俞[BL15 心俞](point/BL15.md)下一椎，主要经典中无指征或配伍反映此关联。  

[DU10 灵台](point/DU10.md)是治疗疖、红丝疖（淋巴管炎）及丁疮（小型、坚硬、深根、丁香状脓疮）的经验穴，现代临床亦用之。《素问》指其用于脾热，但此指征未获后世认同，后续文献不再提及。  

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 治疗疖、红丝疖（淋巴管炎）及丁疮（小型、坚硬、深根、丁香状脓疮）的经验穴。  

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 疖疾：[DU10 灵台](point/DU10.md) + [DU12 身柱](point/DU12.md) + [PC4 郄门](point/PC4.md) + [LI4 合谷](point/LI4.md) + [BL40 委中](point/BL40.md)。
