### Window of Heaven  Tiānyǒu
- Point of the Window of Heaven

![[SJ-16 天牖.png|250]]
#### Location 

On the posterior border of the sternocleidomastoid muscle, approximately 1 cun inferior to [Wángǔ GB-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-12), on a line drawn between [Tiānzhù BL-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-10) and [Tiānróng SI-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-17).

#### Needling 

Perpendicular insertion, 0.5 to 1 cun.

#### Actions 

- Benefits the head and sense organs
- Regulates and descends qi

#### Indications 

- Sudden deafness, impaired hearing, dimness of vision, pain of the eyes with inability to see, inability to open the eyes, lacrimation, rhinitis with nosebleed, loss of sense of smell, stuffy nose.
- Wind dizziness, headache, head wind, swollen face, swelling of the submandibular region, throat painful obstruction, scrofula, swelling of the breast, malaria, confused dreaming.
- Stiffness and pain of the neck with inability to turn the head, swelling of the supraclavicular fossa.

#### Commentary 

Tiānyǒu SJ-16 is one of five points referred to in Chapter 21 of the Spiritual Pivot as points of the ['Window of Heaven'](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-categories/points-of-the-window-of-heaven "'Window of Heaven'"), and in this passage it is said "Sudden deafness with excess of qi, dimness of vision and hearing, select Tiānyǒu SJ-16". Tiānyǒu SJ-16 (Window of Heaven) in fact gave this group of points its name. In his commentary on the above passage, Zhou Zhi-cong said "The points and the orifices of the head and face are like the great windows of a high pavilion by virtue of which qi moves. When there is inversion qi below, then the channels in the upper region do not move and there is lack of clarity of vision and hearing ... ".

In common with the other points of the Window of Heaven, Tiānyǒu SJ-16 is able to regulate and balance the qi between the head and the body, and is indicated when inversion qi (chaotic and rebellious qi) rushes to the head. This gives rise to such symptoms as headache and dizziness, sudden onset of deafness, swelling of the face and disorders of the eyes and nose.

Also in common with other Window of Heaven points, Tiānyǒu SJ-16 treats scrofula.

#### Summary of clinical application 

- “Sudden deafness with excess of qi, dimness of vision and hearing, select Tiānyǒu SJ-16” (Spiritual Pivot). Window of Heaven point: regulates and balances the qi between the head and the body, for when inversion qi (chaotic and rebellious qi) rushes to the head (headache and dizziness, sudden onset of deafness, swelling of the face, disorders of the eyes and nose, scrofula).

#### Combinations 

- Sudden deafness: Tiānyǒu SJ-16 and [Sìdú SJ-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-9) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Wind dizziness and headache: Tiānyǒu SJ-16, [Fēngmén BL-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-12), [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60), [Guānchōng SJ-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-1) and [Guānyuán REN-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-4) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Swelling of the face: first needle [Yīxǐ BL-45](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-45), then Tiānyǒu SJ-16 and [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20) (Systematic Classic).
- Nasal discharge with inability to distinguish the fragrant from the foul: Tiānyǒu SJ-16 and [Rénzhōng DU-26](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-26) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Lacrimation: Tiānyǒu SJ-16 and [Xīnshū BL-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-15) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Pain of the shoulder and back: Tiānyǒu SJ-16, [Quēpén ST-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-12), [Shéndào DU-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-11), [Dàzhù BL-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-11), [Tiāntú REN-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-22), [Shuǐdào ST-28](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-28) and [Jùgǔ LI-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-16) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Stiffness of the neck with inability to turn the head: Tiānyǒu SJ-16 and [Hòuxī SI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-3) (Supplementing Life).
- Tired throat, swelling of the neck with inability to turn the head, swelling of the cheek that radiates to the ear: Tiānyǒu SJ-16, [Wángǔ GB-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-12) and [Qiángǔ SI-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-2) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).


### **[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)** (TIĀNYǑU)

* **英文名称：** Window of Heaven
* **中文名称：** 天牖
* **国际代码：** SJ-16
* **穴位属性：** **[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)** 是天窗穴之一。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于胸锁乳突肌后缘，**[GB12 完骨](/point/GB12.md)** 下方约1寸，在连接**[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)** 和**[SI17 天容](/point/SI17.md)** 的连线上。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至1寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利头目七窍
* 调理降气

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

暴聋（突然耳聋），听力障碍，视力减退，眼痛不能视物，不能睁眼，流泪，鼻炎伴鼻出血，嗅觉丧失，鼻塞。
风眩，头痛，头风，面部肿胀，颌下区肿胀，咽喉肿痛，瘰疬，乳房肿胀，疟疾，梦寐颠倒。
颈项僵硬疼痛不能转头，锁骨上窝肿胀。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)** 是《灵枢·刺节真邪》第二十一章中提到的“天窗穴”之一，该篇记载：“暴聋气实，视听不明，取**[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)**。”实际上，**[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)** (Window of Heaven) 正是这组穴位的名称来源。周纸窗对此段经文注释说：“头面穴窍，犹高阁之大窗，气由此出入。下有逆气，则上部经络不通，视听不明……”

与其他天窗穴一样，**[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)** 能够调理和平衡头与身体之间的气机，主治气逆（逆乱之气）上冲头部。这引起头痛、眩晕、暴聋、面部肿胀以及眼鼻疾病等症状。

**[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)** 也与其它天窗穴一样，治疗瘰疬。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* “暴聋气实，视听不明，取**[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)**”（《灵枢》）。**天窗穴**：调理和平衡头与身体之间的气机，用于气逆（逆乱之气）上冲头部（头痛、眩晕、暴聋、面部肿胀、眼鼻疾病，瘰疬）。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 暴聋（突然耳聋）：**[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)** 和 **[SJ9 四渎](/point/SJ9.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 风眩和头痛：**[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)**，**[BL12 风门](/point/BL12.md)**，**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)**，**[SJ1 关冲](/point/SJ1.md)** 和 **[REN4 关元](/point/REN4.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 面部水肿：先针 **[BL45 噫嘻](/point/BL45.md)**，然后 **[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)** 和 **[GB20 风池](/point/GB20.md)**（《针灸甲乙经》）。
* 鼻涕伴不辨香臭：**[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)** 和 **[DU26 人中](/point/DU26.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 流泪：**[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)** 和 **[BL15 心俞](/point/BL15.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 肩背疼痛：**[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)**，**[ST12 缺盆](/point/ST12.md)**，**[DU11 神道](/point/DU11.md)**，**[BL11 大杼](/point/BL11.md)**，**[REN22 天突](/point/REN22.md)**，**[ST28 水道](/point/ST28.md)** 和 **[LI16 巨骨](/point/LI16.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 颈项僵硬疼痛不能转头：**[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)** 和 **[SI3 后溪](/point/SI3.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 咽喉疲倦，颈部肿胀不能转头，面颊肿胀放射至耳部：**[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)**，**[GB12 完骨](/point/GB12.md)** 和 **[SI2 前谷](/point/SI2.md)**（《千金方》）。
