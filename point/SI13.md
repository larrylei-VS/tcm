### Crooked Wall QŪYUÁN
![[SI-13 曲垣.png|250]]
#### Location

In the tender depression superior to the medial end of the scapular spine, midway between [Nāoshū SI-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-10) and the spinous process of T2.

#### Location Notes

Slide a fingertip medially along the superior border of the scapular spine until it falls into the tender depression just lateral to the medial border of the scapula.

#### Needling

_i_. Perpendicular insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun; _ii_. Oblique lateral insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** this point is located close to the medial border of the scapula. Too medial an insertion or deep medial-oblique needling may puncture the lungs.

#### Actions

- Benefits the shoulder and scapula

#### Indications

- Generalised painful obstruction, painful obstruction of the shoulder and scapula, hot painful obstruction of the shoulder with contraction, pain and an oppressive sensation of the shoulder.

#### Commentary

The Great Compendium of Acupuncture and Moxibustion emphasises the use of Qūyuán SI-13 in the treatment of painful obstruction of the shoulder that has persisted and transformed into heat with contraction, pain and oppressive sensation. This transformation of wind, damp or cold into heat is a manifestation of the law of 'similar transformation' expounded by Liu Wan-su (see [Yīnlíngquán SP-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-9)).

#### Summary of clinical application

- Both Qūyuán SI-13 and [Jiānwàishū SI-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-14) are indicated for local disorders of the shoulder and scapula, with [Jiānwàishū SI-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-14) additionally indicated for chills and fever accompanied by neck rigidity and inability to turn the head, and generalised painful obstruction.

#### Combinations

- Pain of the shoulder and scapula: Qūyuán SI-13, [Tiānzōng SI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-11) and [Jiānzhēn SI-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-9).

### **[SI13 曲垣](/point/SI13.md)** (QŪYUÁN)

* **英文名称：** Crooked Wall
* **中文名称：** 曲垣
* **国际代码：** SI-13

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于肩胛冈内侧端上方的压痛凹陷中，在 **[SI10 臑俞](/point/SI10.md)** 与第二胸椎棘突之间中点。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 沿肩胛冈上缘向内侧滑动指尖，直到其落入肩胛骨内侧缘旁边的压痛凹陷中。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

i. 垂直刺0.3至0.5寸； ii. 向外侧斜刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 此穴靠近肩胛骨内侧缘。针刺过内或向内侧深斜刺可能刺破肺脏。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利肩胛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

全身痹痛，肩胛痹痛，肩部热痹伴拘急，肩部疼痛伴压迫感。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

《针灸大成》强调 **[SI13 曲垣](/point/SI13.md)** 在治疗肩部痹痛中的应用，特别是当痹痛持续并转化为热，伴有拘急、疼痛和压迫感时。这种风、湿、寒转化为热的现象是刘完素所阐发的“同气相求”原理的体现（参见 **[SP9 阴陵泉](/point/SP9.md)**）。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* **[SI13 曲垣](/point/SI13.md)** 和 **[SI14 肩外俞](/point/SI14.md)** 都主治肩部和肩胛的局部疾病，其中 **[SI14 肩外俞](/point/SI14.md)** 额外主治恶寒发热伴颈项强直不能转头以及全身痹痛。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 肩胛疼痛：**[SI13 曲垣](/point/SI13.md)**，**[SI11 天宗](/point/SI11.md)** 和 **[SI9 肩贞](/point/SI9.md)**。
