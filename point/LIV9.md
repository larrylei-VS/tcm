### Yin Wrapping YĪNBĀO
![[LIV-9 阴包.png|250]]
#### Location

Directly superior to the medial epicondyle of the femur, 4 cun superior to [Qūquán LIV-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-8), in the cleft between m. vastus medialis and m. sartorius.

#### Location Notes

Locate one patella's height (2 cun) above the superior border of the patella, in the tender depression between m. vastus medialis and m. sartorius, directly above [Qūquán LIV-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-8).

#### Needling

Perpendicular or oblique insertion 1-2 cun.

#### Actions

- Adjusts menstruation and regulates the lower jiao

#### Indications

- Irregular menstruation, disorders of menstruation, difficult urination, retention of urine, enuresis.
- Lumbo-sacral pain extending to the lower abdomen.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for irregular menstruation, disorders of menstruation, difficult urination, retention of urine, enuresis, lumbo-sacral pain extending to the lower abdomen.

#### Combinations

- Irregular menstruation: Yīnbāo LIV-9 and [Lǐgōu LIV-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-5) (Supplementing Life)


### 1. [LIV9 阴包](point/LIV9.md)

#### 1.1. Yin Wrapping 陰包

#### 1.2. 定位

位于股骨内上髁正上方，[LIV8 曲泉](point/LIV8.md)上方4寸，在股内侧肌与缝匠肌之间的裂隙中。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

定位于髌骨上缘上方一髌高（2寸），在股内侧肌与缝匠肌之间的敏感凹陷处，正对[LIV8 曲泉](point/LIV8.md)上方。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直或斜刺，深1至2寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 调经及调下焦

#### 1.6. 主治

- 月经不调、月经疾患、尿难、尿潴留、遗尿。
- 腰骶痛放射至下腹。

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 使用较少。
- 适用于月经不调、月经疾患、尿难、尿潴留、遗尿、腰骶痛放射至下腹。

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 月经不调：[LIV9 阴包](point/LIV9.md) + [LIV5 蠡沟](point/LIV5.md)（《补命》）。
