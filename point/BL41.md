### Attached Branch FÙFĒN
![[BL-41 附分.png]]
- Meeting point of the Bladder and Small Intestine channels

#### Location

3 cun lateral to the midline, level with the lower border of the spinous process of the second thoracic vertebra (T2) and level with [Fēngmén BL-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-12).

#### Location Notes

When the shoulder is relaxed, the three cun line corresponds to the medial border of the scapula.

#### Needling

Oblique insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.  
**Caution:** deep perpendicular or deep oblique needling in a medial direction carries a substantial risk of causing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Expels wind and cold

#### Indications

- Contraction of the shoulder, scapula and back, pain and stiffness of the neck, back pain radiating to the head, numbness of the elbow and upper arm.
- Wind-cold lodged in the interstices, wind-taxation.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for contraction of the shoulder, scapula and back, pain and stiffness of the neck, back pain radiating to the head, numbness of the elbow and upper arm, wind-cold lodged in the interstices, wind-taxation.

### **[BL41 附分](point/BL41.md)** (Fùfēn)

* **英文名称：** Attached Branch
* **中文名称：** 附分
* **国际代码：** BL-41
* **穴位属性：** **[BL41 附分](point/BL41.md)** 是膀胱经与小肠经的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第二胸椎（T2）棘突下缘水平，距中线旁开3寸，与**[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 平齐。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 肩部放松时，旁开3寸线对应肩胛骨内侧缘。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺0.3至0.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直深刺或向内侧深斜刺有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 通经活络，止痛
* 祛风散寒

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

肩部、肩胛和背部拘急，颈部疼痛僵硬，背痛放射至头部，肘臂麻木。
风寒留滞腠理，风劳。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL41 附分](point/BL41.md)** 临床上较少使用。其名称“附分”可能指其附着于脊柱旁，并能分清邪气。作为膀胱经与小肠经的交会穴，它能够**祛风散寒**，**通经活络，止痛**。

此穴主要用于治疗**肩、背、颈部**因风寒阻滞引起的**疼痛、僵硬和拘急**，如肩部、肩胛和背部拘急，颈部疼痛僵硬，以及背痛放射至头部。它对肘臂麻木也有一定疗效。其**祛风散寒**的功效使其适用于风寒留滞腠理、风劳等表证或虚劳证。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治肩部、肩胛和背部拘急，颈部疼痛僵硬，背痛放射至头部，肘臂麻木。
* 主治风寒留滞腠理，风劳。
