### Inner Courtyard NÈITÍNG
![[ST-44 内庭.png|250]]
- Ying-Spring and Water point of the Stomach channel
- Ma Dan-yang Heavenly Star point

#### Location

On the dorsum of the foot, between the second and third toes, 0.5 cun proximal to the margin of the web.

#### Needling

i. Perpendicular insertion 0.5 cun; ii. Oblique insertion directed proximally, 0.5 to 1 cun.

#### Actions

- Clears heat from the Stomach channel and alleviates pain
- Harmonises the intestines and clears damp-heat
- Calms the spirit

#### Indications

- Toothache, pain of the lower teeth, pain of the upper teeth, pain of the eye, pain of the face, deviation of the mouth and eye, nosebleed, throat painful obstruction, tinnitus, thirst.
- Abdominal pain, distention of the lower abdomen, borborygmus, diarrhoea, dysenteric disorder, blood in the stool, constipation.
- Febrile disease with absence of sweating, malarial disorders with no pleasure in eating, cold shivering, aversion to cold, hands and feet counterflow cold, hot inversion, urticaria, pain of the skin of the chest, blood in the urine.
- Aversion to the sound of people talking, desire for silence, frequent yawning.
- Pain and swelling of the dorsum of the foot.

#### Commentary

Nèitíng ST-44, the ying-spring and water point of the Stomach channel, was included by Ma Dan-yang, the great physician of the Jin dynasty, among his 'eleven heavenly star points'[20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-stomach-channel/references), his grouping of the most vital acupuncture points of the human body.

According to the Classic of Difficulties[41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-stomach-channel/references) ying-spring points are indicated for "heat in the body". As a distal point, the principal action of Nèitíng ST-44 is to clear heat and fire from the Stomach channel, most especially its upper portion. It is a primary point in the treatment of many heat disorders of the head and face such as toothache, pain of the face or eye, nosebleed, throat painful obstruction etc. It is frequently combined for this purpose with [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4). In the treatment of toothache, some sources recommend the use of Nèitíng ST-44 for the lower jaw and [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) for the upper jaw, whilst others consider Nèitíng ST-44 equally adept at treating both the upper and lower jaw. This is borne out by the pathway of the Stomach primary channel which descends from the eye region to enter the upper gum, joins the Governing vessel at [Rénzhōng DU-26](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-26) and then circles around the lips and descends to meet the Conception vessel at [Chéngjiāng REN-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-24). Pain of the face almost invariably involves disorder of the Stomach channel, and Nèitíng ST-44 is an essential point in the treatment of disorders such as trigeminal neuralgia, often in combination with local points such as [Dìcāng ST-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-4), [Jiáchē ST-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-6) and [Xiàguān ST-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-7). The Stomach channel also makes various connections with the throat and nose. The primary channel descends alongside the throat, the luo-connecting channel terminates at the throat, the primary channel originates at [Yíngxiāng LI-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-20) and ascends to the root of the nose and the divergent channel passes alongside the nose. Both throat painful obstruction and nosebleed can be due to heat accumulating in the yangming channel, in the latter case causing reckless movement of the blood.

The second important action of Nèitíng ST-44 is to harmonise the intestines and clear dampness and heat, and it is indicated in the treatment of such disorders as diarrhoea, dysenteric disorder, blood in the stools, constipation and abdominal pain.

One special condition for which Nèitíng ST-44 is indicated is the symptom of hands and feet counterflow cold, where only the hands and feet are cold but the body is warm. This may occur in the pattern known as 'true heat, false cold', where heat constrained in the interior prevents the yang qi from circulating to the limbs. Despite the apparent cold, the other symptoms, as well as the pulse and the tongue, are indicative of heat and constraint. This condition must be differentiated from 'cold collapse' (cold inversion) where either the whole limb or the areas distal to the elbows and knees are cold.

Finally, like many points of the Stomach channel, Nèitíng ST-44 has an action on calming the spirit. It is particularly suited to the treatment of depressive type disorders, and is indicated for aversion to the sound of people talking and a desire for silence.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Clears heat and fire from the upper reaches of the channel: toothache, pain of the lower teeth, pain of the eye, pain of the face, deviation of the mouth and eye, nosebleed, throat painful obstruction, tinnitus.
- Harmonises the intestines and clears damp-heat: abdominal pain and distention, borborygmus, diarrhoea, dysenteric disorder, bloody stools, thirst, constipation.
- For hands and feet counterflow cold (cold hands and feet with a warm body). Also pain and swelling of the dorsum of the foot.

### [ST44 内庭](/point/ST44.md) (NÈITÍNG)

* **英文名称：** Inner Courtyard
* **中文名称：** 内庭
* **国际代码：** ST-44
* **穴位属性：** [ST44 内庭](/point/ST44.md) 是胃经的荥穴和水穴，马丹阳天星穴。

#### 定位 (Location)

位于足背部，第二、第三足趾之间，趾蹼缘上方0.5寸。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

i. 垂直刺0.5寸； ii. 斜刺，针尖朝向近端，0.5至1寸。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 清泄胃经之热，止痛
* 调理肠道，清利湿热
* 宁心安神

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

牙痛，下牙痛，上牙痛，眼痛，面痛，口眼歪斜，鼻出血，咽喉肿痛，耳鸣，口渴。
腹痛，小腹胀满，肠鸣，腹泻，痢疾，便血，便秘。
发热无汗，疟疾伴食欲不振，恶寒发抖，恶寒，手足逆冷，热厥，荨麻疹，胸部皮肤疼痛，尿血。
厌恶人声，喜静，频频呵欠。
足背肿痛。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST44 内庭](/point/ST44.md)，作为胃经的荥穴和水穴，被金代名医马丹阳列入其“十一穴天星穴”中，是他认为人体最重要的穴位组合。

根据《难经》记载，荥穴主治“身热”。[ST44 内庭](/point/ST44.md) 作为远端穴位，其主要作用是清泻胃经，特别是其上部的热邪和火邪。它是治疗头面部许多热证的主要穴位，如牙痛、面部或眼部疼痛、鼻出血、咽喉肿痛等。为此目的，它常与[LI4 合谷](/point/LI4.md) 联合使用。在治疗牙痛方面，一些医家推荐[ST44 内庭](/point/ST44.md) 治疗下牙痛，[LI4 合谷](/point/LI4.md) 治疗上牙痛，而另一些医家则认为[ST44 内庭](/point/ST44.md) 上下牙痛皆可治疗。这由胃经正经的循行路径所证实：胃经从眼部下行进入上牙龈，在[DU26 人中](/point/DU26.md) 处与督脉交会，然后绕行口唇并下行至[REN24 承浆](/point/REN24.md) 处与任脉交会。面部疼痛几乎总是涉及胃经失调，[ST44 内庭](/point/ST44.md) 是治疗三叉神经痛等疾病的关键穴位，常与[ST4 地仓](/point/ST4.md)、[ST6 颊车](/point/ST6.md) 和[ST7 下关](/point/ST7.md) 等局部穴位联合使用。胃经也与咽喉和鼻部有多种联系。正经循行于咽喉旁，络脉终止于咽喉，正经起于[LI20 迎香](/point/LI20.md) 上行至鼻根，经别循行于鼻旁。咽喉肿痛和鼻出血都可能由于阳明经热积聚所致，后者导致血液妄行。

[ST44 内庭](/point/ST44.md) 的第二个重要作用是调理肠道，清利湿热，它主治腹泻、痢疾、便血、便秘和腹痛等疾病。

[ST44 内庭](/point/ST44.md) 主治的一种特殊情况是手足逆冷症状，即只有手足发冷而身体温暖。这可能发生在“真热假寒”的证型中，内热郁闭导致阳气不能循行至四肢。尽管表面发冷，但其他症状以及脉象和舌象都提示热证和郁闭。此症必须与“亡阳”（寒厥）区分开来，后者是整个肢体或肘膝远端发冷。

最后，像胃经的许多穴位一样，[ST44 内庭](/point/ST44.md) 具有宁心安神的作用。它特别适合治疗抑郁型疾病，主治厌恶人声和喜静。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 清泄经络上端的热和火：牙痛，下牙痛，眼痛，面痛，口眼歪斜，鼻出血，咽喉肿痛，耳鸣。
* 调理肠道，清利湿热：腹痛腹胀，肠鸣，腹泻，痢疾，便血，口渴，便秘。
* 治疗手足逆冷（手脚发冷而身体温暖）。也治疗足背肿痛。
