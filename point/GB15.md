### Head Governor of Tears TÓULÍNQÌ
![[GB-15 头临泣.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder and Bladder channels with the Yang Linking vessel

#### Location

On the forehead, directly above [Yángbái GB-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-14), 0.5 cun within the anterior hairline, midway between [Shéntíng DU-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-24) and [Tóuwéi ST-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-8).

#### Location Notes

The distance between the anterior and posterior hairlines in the midline is measured as 12 cun. If the anterior hairline is indistinct, the distance is measured as 15 cun between the glabella [point [Yìntáng M-HN-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/M-HN-3)] and the posterior hairline; the location of the anterior hairline would thus be defined as one fifth of this distance. If the posterior hairline is indistinct, it can be measured as 1 cun inferior to [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16) which lies immediately below the external occipital protuberance. Note that the line of the anterior hairline away from the midline usually curves somewhat posteriorly, and this should be taken into account when locating the Gall Bladder points on the scalp (Tóulínqì GB-15 to [Nǎokōng GB-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-19)).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5-1.5 cun. See note [Hànyàn GB-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-4).

#### Actions

- Eliminates wind, benefits the head and alleviates pain
- Benefits the nose and eyes

#### Indications

- Nasal congestion, nasal congestion with aversion to cold.
- Headache, head wind, visual dizziness, pain of the occiput and forehead, pain of the supraorbital ridge.
- Redness and pain of the eyes, superficial visual obstruction, lacrimation on exposure to wind, pain at the outer canthus.
- Windstroke, epilepsy, loss of consciousness, malaria, pain of the supraclavicular fossa, swelling of the axilla.

#### Commentary

As its name (Head Governor of Tears) implies, Tóulínqì GB-15 is indicated for lacrimation as well as for redness and pain of the eyes and superficial visual obstruction. In this respect it mirrors [Zulinqi Gb 41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-41) (Foot Governor of Tears) towards the distal end of the channel. Like several other points on the crown of the head (e.g. [Tōngtiān BL-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-7) and [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23)) it also has a pronounced action on the nose and is indicated for nasal congestion.

#### Summary of clinical application

- For lacrimation, redness and pain of the eyes, superficial visual obstruction, nasal congestion.

#### Combinations

- Lacrimation on exposure to wind: Tóulínqì GB-15, [Tóuwéi ST-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-8), [Jīngmíng BL-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-1) and [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20) (Great Compendium).
- Lacrimation: Tóulínqì GB-15 and [Tóuwéi ST-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-8) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Visual dizziness: Tóulínqì GB-15 and [Zhōngzhǔ SJ-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-3) (Supplementing Life).
- Superficial visual obstruction: Tóulínqì GB-15 and [Gānshū BL-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-18) (Great Compendium).
- Red eyes and bleeding from [Yíngxiāng LI-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-20) (i.e. nosebleed): Tóulínqì GB-15, [Tàichōng LIV-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-3) and [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) (Song of Points).
- Nasal congestion: Tóulínqì GB-15 and [Tōngtiān BL-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-7) (Supplementing Life).

### 1. [GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md)

#### 1.1. Head Governor of Tears 頭臨泣

- 胆经与膀胱经及阳维脉的交会穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于前额，正对[GB14 阳白](point/GB14.md)上方，前发际内0.5寸，在[DU24 神庭](point/DU24.md)与[ST8 头维](point/ST8.md)之间中点。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

前发际至后发际中线的距离定为12寸。若前发际不明显，则从眉间[MHN3 印堂](point/MHN3.md)至后发际测量为15寸，前发际位置即为此距离的五分之一。若后发际不明显，可从[DU16 风府](point/DU16.md)（位于枕外隆凸正下方）向下1寸测量。注意，中线以外的前发际线通常向后略呈弧形，定位头部的胆经穴位（[GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md)至[GB19 脑空](point/GB19.md)）时应考虑这一点。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

横刺，深0.5至1.5寸。参见[GB4 颔厌](point/GB4.md)的针刺说明。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 祛风益头止痛
- 益鼻目

#### 1.6. 主治

- 鼻塞、鼻塞伴恶寒。
- 头痛、头风、视觉眩晕、枕部及前额痛、眶上嵴痛。
- 目赤痛、视物模糊、迎风流泪、外眦痛。
- 中风、癫痫、意识丧失、疟疾、锁骨上窝痛、腋肿。

#### 1.7. 论述

正如其名称（头临泣）所示，[GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md)适用于流泪、目赤痛及视物模糊。在此方面，它对应于胆经远端的[GB41 足临泣](point/GB41.md)（足临泣）。与头顶部其他穴位（如[BL7 通天](point/BL7.md)和[DU23 上星](point/DU23.md)）类似，它对鼻部也有显著作用，适用于鼻塞。

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 适用于流泪、目赤痛、视物模糊、鼻塞。

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 迎风流泪：[GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md) + [ST8 头维](point/ST8.md) + [BL1 睛明](point/BL1.md) + [GB20 风池](point/GB20.md)（《大成》）。
- 流泪：[GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md) + [ST8 头维](point/ST8.md)（《百症赋》）。
- 视觉眩晕：[GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md) + [SJ3 中渚](point/SJ3.md)（《补命》）。
- 视物模糊：[GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md) + [BL18 肝俞](point/BL18.md)（《大成》）。
- 目赤及[LI20 迎香](point/LI20.md)出血（即鼻衄）：[GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md) + [LIV3 太冲](point/LIV3.md) + [LI4 合谷](point/LI4.md)（《穴位歌》）。
- 鼻塞：[GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md) + [BL7 通天](point/BL7.md)（《补命》）。
