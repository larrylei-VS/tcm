### Yang Intersection YÁNGJIĀO
![[GB-35 阳交.png|250]]
- Xi-Cleft point of the Yang Linking vessel

#### Location

On the lateral aspect of the lower leg, 7 cun superior to the prominence of the lateral malleolus, in the depression at the posterior border of the fibula.

#### Location Notes

i. The distance between the tip of the lateral malleolus and the popliteal crease is 16 cun; locate Yángjiāo GB-35 one cun distal to the midpoint of this line; ii. Above the region of [Xuánzhōng GB-39](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-39), the fibula is not easily palpable because it is covered by the peroneus brevis muscle; therefore feel for the posterior border of the fibula above the malleolus where it is easily palpable and extend a line towards the head of the fibula.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Regulates Gall Bladder qi and calms the spirit

#### Indications

- Swelling and pain of the knee, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limb, cold painful obstruction, contraction of the sinews in sudden turmoil disorder.
- Mania induced by fright, fullness, distention and pain of the chest and lateral costal region, swelling of the face and eyes, throat painful obstruction.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for swelling and pain of the knee, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limb, cold painful obstruction, contraction of the sinews in sudden turmoil disorder, mania induced by fright, fullness, distention and pain of the chest and lateral costal region, swelling of the face and eyes, throat painful obstruction.

#### Combinations

- Fullness of the chest: Yángjiāo GB-35 and [Zúlínqì GB-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-41) (Supplementing Life).
- Fright palpitations and pounding of the Heart: Yángjiāo GB-35 and [Jiěxī ST-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-41) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Loss of voice: Yángjiāo GB-35, [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) and [Yǒngquǎn KID-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-1) (Systematic Classic).
- Bone painful obstruction and numbness of the hip joint and knee: Yángjiāo GB-35, [Yángfǔ GB-38](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-38), and [Yánglíngquán GB-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-34) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### 1. [GB35 阳交](point/GB35.md)

#### 1.1. Yang Intersection 陽交

- 阳维脉的郄穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于小腿外侧面，外踝突出上方7寸，腓骨后缘凹陷处。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

i. 外踝尖至腘横纹的距离为16寸，[GB35 阳交](point/GB35.md)位于此连线中点下方1寸；  
ii. 在[GB39 悬钟](point/GB39.md)上方区域，腓骨不易触及，因被腓骨短肌覆盖；因此，从外踝上方易触及的腓骨后缘开始，向上延伸至腓骨头方向定位。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直刺入，深1至1.5寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 活络止痛
- 调胆气，安神

#### 1.6. 主治

- 膝肿痛、下肢萎症及痛痹、寒痛痹、卒中风病筋挛。
- 惊狂、胸胁胀满痛、面目肿、咽痹。

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 使用较少。
- 适用于膝肿痛、下肢萎症及痛痹、寒痛痹、卒中风病筋挛、惊狂、胸胁胀满痛、面目肿、咽痹。

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 胸满：[GB35 阳交](point/GB35.md) + [GB41 足临泣](point/GB41.md)（《补命》）。
- 惊悸及心悸：[GB35 阳交](point/GB35.md) + [ST41 解溪](point/ST41.md)（《百症赋》）。
- 失音：[GB35 阳交](point/GB35.md) + [LI4 合谷](point/LI4.md) + [KID1 涌泉](point/KID1.md)（《系统经典》）。
- 髋膝骨痛痹及麻木：[GB35 阳交](point/GB35.md) + [GB38 阳辅](point/GB38.md) + [GB34 阳陵泉](point/GB34.md)（《千金方》）。
