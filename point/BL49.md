### Abode of Thought YÌSHÈ
![[BL-49 意舍.png|250]]
#### Location

3 cun lateral to the midline, level with the lower border of the spinous process of the eleventh thoracic vertebra (T11) and level with [Píshū BL-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-20).

#### Location Notes

When the shoulder is relaxed, the three cun line corresponds to the medial border of the scapula.

#### Needling

Oblique insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun. Caution: deep perpendicular or deep oblique needling in a medial direction carries a substantial risk of causing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Clears damp-heat
- Regulates the Spleen and Stomach

#### Indications

- Distention and fullness of the abdomen, distention and pain of the chest and lateral costal region, slippery diarrhoea, difficult ingestion, vomiting, reddish-yellow urine, heat in the body with yellow face and eyes.
- Back pain, wasting and thirsting disorder, aversion to wind and cold.

#### Commentary

Yìshè BL-49 is level with [Píshū BL-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-20), the back-shu point of the Spleen. Despite its name (Abode of Thought), psychological and emotional indications are notable by their absence in major classical texts.

Yìshè BL-49 is able to clear damp-heat (reddish-yellow urine, heat in the body with yellow face and eyes) and regulate disharmony of the Stomach (vomiting, difficult ingestion) and Spleen (abdominal distention and fullness, slippery diarrhoea).

In the light of its proximity to the pancreas, it is interesting to note the inclusion of this point in a number of classical combinations for wasting and thirsting disorder.

According to the Essential Questions[65](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references) the five outer Bladder points level with the five zang back-shu points (i.e. [Pòhù BL-42](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-42), [Shéntáng BL-44](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-44), [Húnmén BL-47](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-47), Yìshè BL-49 and [Zhìshì BL-52](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-52)) drain heat from the five zang, an action shared by the back-shu points of the five zang.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Clears damp-heat from the three jiao and treats the Spleen and Stomach: distention and fullness of the abdomen, distention and pain of the chest and lateral costal region, slippery diarrhoea, difficult ingestion, vomiting, reddish-yellow urine, heat in the body with yellow face and eyes.

#### Combinations

- Wasting and thirsting disorder with great desire to drink: Yìshè BL-49, [Guānchōng SJ-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-1) and [Rángǔ KID-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-2) (Supplementing Life).
- Wasting and thirsting disorder with great desire to drink: Yìshè BL-49, [Chéngjiāng REN-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-24), [Rángǔ KID-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-2) and [Guānchōng SJ-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-1) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Kidney deficiency wasting and thirsting disorder, absence of sweating, difficulty in moving the lumbar spine, distention of the abdomen and pain of the lateral costal region: Yìshè BL-49 and [Zhōnglǔshū BL-29](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-29) (Supplementing Life).
- Vomiting: Yìshè BL-49, [Zhōngtíng REN-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-16) and [Shūfǔ KID-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-27) (Supplementing Life).
- Yellow eyes: Yìshè BL-49, [Yánggāng BL-48](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-48), [Nǎohù DU-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-17) and [Dǎnshū BL-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-19) (Supplementing Life).


### **[BL49 意舍](point/BL49.md)** (YÌSHÈ)

* **英文名称：** Abode of Thought
* **中文名称：** 意舍
* **国际代码：** BL-49
* **穴位属性：** **[BL49 意舍](point/BL49.md)** 是膀胱经第二侧线上的穴位，与**[BL20 脾俞](point/BL20.md)** 平齐。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第十一胸椎（T11）棘突下缘水平，距中线旁开3寸，与**[BL20 脾俞](point/BL20.md)** 平齐。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 肩部放松时，旁开3寸线对应肩胛骨内侧缘。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺0.3至0.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直深刺或向内侧深斜刺有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 清利湿热
* 调理脾胃

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

腹部胀满，胸胁胀满疼痛，滑泻，吞咽困难，呕吐，尿色赤黄，身热伴面黄目黄。
背痛，消渴病，恶风畏寒。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL49 意舍](point/BL49.md)** 与脾的背俞穴**[BL20 脾俞](point/BL20.md)** 平齐。尽管其名称（意舍，意为“思虑之居所”）引人注目，但在主要经典文献中，心理和情志主治却鲜有提及。

**[BL49 意舍](point/BL49.md)** 能够清利湿热（尿色赤黄，身热伴面黄目黄），并调理胃（呕吐，吞咽困难）和脾（腹部胀满，滑泻）的不和。

鉴于其靠近胰腺，有趣的是此穴被纳入多部经典组合中，用于治疗**消渴病**。

根据《素问·灵兰秘典论》记载，与五脏背俞穴平齐的五个膀胱经外侧穴位（即 **[BL42 魄户](point/BL42.md)**、**[BL44 神堂](point/BL44.md)**、**[BL47 魂门](point/BL47.md)**、**[BL49 意舍](point/BL49.md)** 和 **[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)**）具有清泄五脏热邪的作用，这一作用与五脏背俞穴相同。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 清利三焦湿热，并治疗脾胃疾病：腹部胀满，胸胁胀满疼痛，滑泻，吞咽困难，呕吐，尿色赤黄，身热伴面黄目黄。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 消渴病伴大渴：**[BL49 意舍](point/BL49.md)**，**[SJ1 关冲](point/SJ1.md)** 和 **[KID2 然谷](point/KID2.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 消渴病伴大渴：**[BL49 意舍](point/BL49.md)**，**[REN24 承浆](point/REN24.md)**，**[KID2 然谷](point/KID2.md)** 和 **[SJ1 关冲](point/SJ1.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 肾虚消渴病，无汗，腰脊活动不利，腹胀胁肋疼痛：**[BL49 意舍](point/BL49.md)** 和 **[BL29 中膂俞](point/BL29.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 呕吐：**[BL49 意舍](point/BL49.md)**，**[REN16 中庭](point/REN16.md)** 和 **[KID27 俞府](point/KID27.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 眼睛发黄：**[BL49 意舍](point/BL49.md)**，**[BL48 阳纲](point/BL48.md)**，**[DU17 脑户](point/DU17.md)** 和 **[BL19 胆俞](point/BL19.md)**（《资生经》）。
