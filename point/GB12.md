### Mastoid Process WÁNGǓ
![[GB-12 完骨.png|360]]
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder and Bladder channels

#### Location

In the depression just posterior and inferior to the mastoid process.

#### Location Notes

Place a finger on the prominence of the mastoid process then slide it posteriorly into the depression.

#### Needling

Oblique inferior insertion 0.5-1 cun.

#### Actions

- Eliminates wind, benefits the head and alleviates pain
- Calms the spirit

#### Indications

- Headache, head wind with pain behind the ear, shaking of the head, stiffness and pain of the neck with inability to turn the head.
- Toothache, swelling of the cheek radiating to the ear, pain of the ear, throat painful obstruction.
- Hemiplegia, deviation of the mouth and eye, clenched jaw, withering and contraction of the muscles around the mouth, weakness and flaccidity of the legs, atrophy disorder of the arms and legs, malaria, sweating with no aversion to cold.
- Epilepsy, mania, agitation of the Heart, insomnia, dark urine.

#### Commentary

Wángǔ GB-12 is able to benefit the head region and eliminate both exterior and interior wind. It is indicated for i. headache and head wind, especially when there is pain behind the ear, ii. stiffness and pain of the neck, and iii. heat, pain and swelling affecting the teeth, cheek, ear and throat.

Like many points in the neck region, Wángǔ GB-12 is also able to regulate disharmony between the head and the body. When interior wind stirs and rushes up to the head there may be hemiplegia, deviation of the mouth and eyes, shaking of the head and clenched jaw. At the same time as pathogenic wind rushes up to the head, there may be deficiency in the lower part of the body manifesting as atrophy disorder and weakness and flaccidity of the four limbs. This latter application reflects the principle expounded in the Yellow Emperor's Inner Classic[2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-gall-bladder-channel/references) "When the disease is below select [points] from above".

Another important action of Wángǔ GB-12 is to regulate and calm the spirit, and it is indicated in such disorders as mania, agitation of the Heart and insomnia. This is reflected in its close proximity to the extra point [Ānmián N-HN-54](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/N-HN-54) 'Peaceful Sleep', located just posterior and slightly superior to Wángǔ GB-12, which is much used for insomnia.

#### Summary of clinical application

- For fullness above: headache (especially when there is pain behind the ear), stiffness and pain of the neck, deviation of the mouth and eyes, shaking of the head, clenched jaw, heat, pain and swelling affecting the teeth, cheek, ear and throat.
- For deficiency below: atrophy disorder, weakness and flaccidity of the four limbs.
- Calms the spirit: mania, insomnia, agitation of the Heart, epilepsy and mania.

#### Combinations

- Head wind and pain behind the ear: Wángǔ GB-12 and [Qīmài SJ-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-18) (Supplementing Life).
- Pain of the nape of the neck: Wángǔ GB-12 and [Yùzhěn BL-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-9) (Supplementing Life).
- Tired throat, swelling of the neck with inability to turn the head, swelling of the cheek that radiates to the ear: Wángǔ GB-12, [Tiānyǒu SJ-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-16) and [Qiángǔ SI-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-2) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Toothache and tooth decay: Wángǔ GB-12 and [Fúbái GB-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-10) (Systematic Classic).
- Deviation of the mouth and face: Wángǔ GB-12 and [Lièquē LU-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-7) (Supplementing Life).
- Dark urine: Wángǔ GB-12, [Xiǎochángshū BL-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-27), [Báihuánshū BL-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-30) and [Yánggāng BL-48](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-48) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).


### 1. [GB12 完骨](point/GB12.md)

#### 1.1. Mastoid Process 完骨

- 胆经与膀胱经的交会穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于乳突后下方凹陷处。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

将手指置于乳突突出处，然后向后滑入凹陷中。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

向下斜刺，深0.5至1寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 祛风益头止痛
- 安神

#### 1.6. 主治

- 头痛、头风伴耳后痛、头部震颤、颈项僵痛不能转头。
- 牙痛、颊肿放射至耳、耳痛、咽痹。
- 偏瘫、口眼歪斜、牙关紧闭、口周肌肉萎缩挛缩、腿软无力、四肢萎症、疟疾、出汗不恶寒。
- 癫痫、躁狂、心烦、失眠、尿色深。

#### 1.7. 论述

[GB12 完骨](point/GB12.md)能益头部并祛除外风和内风，适用于：  
i. 头痛和头风，尤其是耳后痛；  
ii. 颈项僵痛；  
iii. 牙齿、颊部、耳朵和咽喉的热、痛和肿胀。

与颈部许多穴位一样，[GB12 完骨](point/GB12.md)能调节头与身体之间的失调。当内风上扰头部时，可能出现偏瘫、口眼歪斜、头部震颤和牙关紧闭。同时，当病风上冲头部时，下部可能出现虚证，如四肢萎症和软弱无力。这反映了《黄帝内经》中“病在下，取之上”的原则。

[GB12 完骨](point/GB12.md)的另一重要作用是调理和安神，适用于躁狂、心烦和失眠等疾患。这与其靠近额外穴位[NHN54 安眠](point/NHN54.md)（位于[GB12 完骨](point/GB12.md)后上方稍高处，常用于失眠）有关。

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 上实：头痛（尤其是耳后痛）、颈项僵痛、口眼歪斜、头部震颤、牙关紧闭、牙齿、颊部、耳朵和咽喉的热痛肿胀。
- 下虚：四肢萎症、软弱无力。
- 安神：躁狂、失眠、心烦、癫痫和躁狂。
