### Window of the Eye MÙCHUĀNG
![[GB-16 目窗.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder channel with the Yang Linking vessel

#### Location

Above the forehead, on a curved line drawn between [Tóulínqì GB-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-15) and [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20), following the contour of the cranium, 1.5 cun posterior to [Tóulínqì GB-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-15).

#### Location Notes

i. See location note for [Tóulínqì GB-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-15); ii. First locate [Tóulínqì GB-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-15) 0.5 cun posterior to the hairline and then locate [Chénglíng GB-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-18) directly lateral to [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20), on the line of the Gall Bladder channel. Mùchuāng GB-16 is then located one third of the distance between [Tóulínqì GB-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-15) and [Chénglíng GB-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-18).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5-1.5 cun. See note [Hànyàn GB-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-4).

#### Actions

- Benefits the eyes
- Eliminates wind and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Visual dizziness, superficial visual obstruction, redness, swelling and pain of the eyes, short sightedness, all kinds of eye diseases.
- Headache, swelling of the head and face, toothache of the upper jaw, swelling of the gums, nasal congestion, epilepsy.
- Aversion to cold, chills and fever with absence of sweating.

#### Commentary

According to _Investigation into Points Along the Channels_ by the Ming dynasty author Yan Zhen-shi, Mùchuāng GB-16 (Window of the Eye) is indicated for all kinds of eye diseases. In modern clinical practice, however, this point is rarely used.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Visual dizziness, superficial visual obstruction, redness, swelling and pain of the eyes, short sightedness, all kinds of eye diseases.
- Headache, swelling of the head and face, toothache of the upper jaw, swelling of the gums, nasal congestion, epilepsy.
- Aversion to cold, chills and fever with absence of sweating.

#### Combinations

- Red eyes: Mùchuāng GB-16 and [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-7) (Supplementing Life).
- Headache: Mùchuāng GB-16, [Tiānchōng GB-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-9) and [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20) (Systematic Classic).
- Stiffness of the lips and pain from tooth decay of the upper jaw: Mùchuāng GB-16, [Zhèngyíng GB-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-17), Duiduan DU‑27 and [Ěrmén SJ-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-21) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).


### 1. [GB16 目窗](point/GB16.md)

#### 1.1. Window of the Eye 目窗

- 胆经与阳维脉的交会穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于前额上方，沿从[GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md)到[GB20 风池](point/GB20.md)沿颅骨轮廓画出的弧线上，在[GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md)后方1.5寸处。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

i. 参见[GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md)的定位说明；  
ii. 首先定位[GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md)（发际后0.5寸），然后定位[GB18 承灵](point/GB18.md)（正对[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)的侧方，在胆经线上）。[GB16 目窗](point/GB16.md)位于[GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md)与[GB18 承灵](point/GB18.md)之间三分之一处。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

横刺，深0.5至1.5寸。参见[GB4 颔厌](point/GB4.md)的针刺说明。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 益目
- 祛风止痛

#### 1.6. 主治

- 视觉眩晕、视物模糊、目赤肿痛、近视、各种眼疾。
- 头痛、头面肿、上颌牙痛、牙龈肿、鼻塞、癫痫。
- 恶寒、寒热无汗。

#### 1.7. 论述

明代作者严真氏在《经穴考》中指出，[GB16 目窗](point/GB16.md)（目窗）适用于各种眼疾。然而，在现代临床实践中，此穴使用较少。

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 适用于视觉眩晕、视物模糊、目赤肿痛、近视、各种眼疾。
- 适用于头痛、头面肿、上颌牙痛、牙龈肿、鼻塞、癫痫。
- 适用于恶寒、寒热无汗。

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 目赤：[GB16 目窗](point/GB16.md) + [PC7 大陵](point/PC7.md)（《补命》）。
- 头痛：[GB16 目窗](point/GB16.md) + [GB9 天冲](point/GB9.md) + [GB20 风池](point/GB20.md)（《系统经典》）。
- 唇僵及上颌龋齿痛：[GB16 目窗](point/GB16.md) + [GB17 正营](point/GB17.md) + [DU27 兑端](point/DU27.md) + [SJ21 耳门](point/SJ21.md)（《千金方》）。
