### Outer Shoulder Shu JIĀNWÀISHŪ
![[SI-14 肩外俞.png|250]]
#### Location

3 cun lateral to the lower border of the spinous process of T1 ([Táodào DU-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-13)).

#### Location Notes

When the shoulder is relaxed, the three cun line corresponds to the medial border of the scapula.

#### Needling

Oblique medial insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
Caution: perpendicular insertion, especially in thin patients, carries a substantial risk of inducing a pneumo-thorax.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Expels wind and cold and benefits the shoulder and scapula

#### Indications

- Pain of the shoulder and scapula with cold sensation extending to the elbow, chills and fever accompanied by neck rigidity and inability to turn the head, generalised painful obstruction.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Both SI-13 and SI-14 are indicated for local disorders of the shoulder and scapula, with Jianwaishu SI-14 additionally indicated for chills and fever accompanied by neck rigidity and inability to turn the head, and generalised painful obstruction.

#### Combinations

- Pain of the shoulder and back: Jiānwàishū SI-14, [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60) and [Dàzhuī DU-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-14).

### **[SI14 肩外俞](point/SI14.md)** (JIĀNWÀISHŪ)

* **英文名称：** Outer Shoulder Shu
* **中文名称：** 肩外俞
* **国际代码：** SI-14

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第一胸椎棘突下缘（**[DU13 陶道](point/DU13.md)**）旁开3寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 肩部放松时，旁开3寸线对应肩胛骨内侧缘。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺，向内侧（朝向脊柱）0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直刺入，特别是在瘦弱患者中，有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 通经活络，止痛
* 祛风散寒，宣利肩胛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

肩胛疼痛伴冷感放射至肘部，恶寒发热伴颈项强直不能转头，全身痹痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SI14 肩外俞](point/SI14.md)** 位于背部肩胛区域，其主要作用是通经活络、止痛，并祛风散寒，宣利肩胛。它主治肩胛疼痛，特别是当疼痛伴有冷感并放射至肘部时。此外，其祛风散寒的功效也使其适用于恶寒发热伴颈项强直不能转头等外感病症，以及全身痹痛。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* **[SI13 曲垣](point/SI13.md)** 和 **[SI14 肩外俞](point/SI14.md)** 都主治肩部和肩胛的局部疾病，其中 **[SI14 肩外俞](point/SI14.md)** 额外主治恶寒发热伴颈项强直不能转头以及全身痹痛。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 肩背疼痛：**[SI14 肩外俞](point/SI14.md)**，**[BL60 昆仑](point/BL60.md)** 和 **[DU14 大椎](point/DU14.md)**。
