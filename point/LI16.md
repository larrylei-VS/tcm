### Great Bone JÙGǓ
![[LI-16 巨骨.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Large Intestine channel with the Yang Motility vessel

#### Location

On the upper aspect of the shoulder, in the depression medial to the acromion process and between the lateral extremity of the clavicle and the scapular spine.

#### Needling

Perpendicular or oblique insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** deep medial insertion carries a risk of causing a pneumothorax, particularly in thin patients.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel, alleviates pain and benefits the shoulder joint
- Regulates qi and blood and dissipates phlegm nodules

#### Indications

- Pain of the shoulder and back, blood stasis in the shoulder, difficulty in moving or raising the arm, pain of the upper arm.
- Fright epilepsy, vomiting of copious quantities of blood, blood stasis in the chest, scrofula, goitre.

#### Commentary

Jùgǔ LI-16 is frequently used as an adjacent point in the treatment of shoulder disorders, and in such cases should always be palpated for tenderness. The use of Jùgǔ LI-16 is particularly emphasised when the shoulder disorder is chronic and recalcitrant, reflected by the indication for blood stasis in the shoulder. This is explained by the saying "Chronic disease is frequently [due to] stasis". Its action on treating disorders of blood also extends to blood stasis in the chest and vomiting blood.

Like [Shǒuwǔlǐ LI-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-13), [Bìnào LI-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-14) and [Jiānyú LI-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-15), Jùgǔ LI-16 is indicated for scrofula.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Local point for shoulder disorders, especially when chronic and when there is blood stasis.
- Also for blood stasis in the chest and vomiting blood.

#### Combinations

- Inability to raise the arm: Jùgǔ LI-16 and [Qiángǔ SI-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-2) (Supplementing Life).
- Pain of the shoulder and back: Jùgǔ LI-16, [Tiānyǒu SJ-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-16), [Quēpén ST-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-12), [Shéndào DU-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-11), [Dàzhù BL-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-11), [Tiāntú REN-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-22) and [Shuǐdào ST-28](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-28) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### [LI16 巨骨](/point/LI16.md) (JÙGǓ)

- **英文名称：** Great Bone
- **中文名称：** 巨骨
- **国际代码：** LI-16
- **穴位属性：** [LI16 巨骨](/point/LI16.md) 是大肠经与阳跷脉的交会穴。

#### 定位 (Location)

位于肩上部，肩峰内侧凹陷中，锁骨外侧端与肩胛冈之间。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直或斜刺0.5至1寸。

- **注意 (Caution):** 向内深刺有造成气胸的风险，特别是瘦弱患者。

#### 作用 (Actions)

- 通经活络，止痛，利肩关节
- 调理气血，消散痰核

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

肩背疼痛，肩部血瘀，手臂活动不利或抬举困难，上臂疼痛。

惊痫，大量呕血，胸部血瘀，瘰疬，瘿瘤。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[LI16 巨骨](/point/LI16.md) 常作为治疗肩部疾病的邻近穴位使用，在这种情况下，应始终触诊以确定是否有压痛。当肩部疾病为慢性顽固性时，[LI16 巨骨](/point/LI16.md) 的应用尤为重要，这体现在其主治肩部血瘀。这可以用“久病多瘀”的说法来解释。它治疗血瘀的作用也延伸到胸部血瘀和呕血。

像[LI13 手五里](/point/LI13.md)、[LI14 臂臑](/point/LI14.md) 和[LI15 肩髃](/point/LI15.md) 一样，[LI16 巨骨](/point/LI16.md) 主治瘰疬。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

治疗肩部疾病的局部穴位，特别是慢性病症和有血瘀的情况。

也治疗胸部血瘀和呕血。

治疗瘰疬。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

不能抬臂：[LI16 巨骨](/point/LI16.md) 和[SI2 前谷](/point/SI2.md)（《资生经》）。

肩背疼痛：[LI16 巨骨](/point/LI16.md)，[SJ16 天牖](/point/SJ16.md)，[ST12 缺盆](/point/ST12.md)，[DU11 神道](/point/DU11.md)，[BL11 大杼](/point/BL11.md)，[REN22 天突](/point/REN22.md) 和[ST28 水道](/point/ST28.md)（《千金方》）。
