### Branch Ditch ZHĪGŌU
- Jing-River and Fire point of the Sanjiao channel
![[SJ-6 支沟.png|250]]


##### Location 

3 cun proximal to Yángchí SJ-4, in the depression between the radius and the ulna, on the radial side of the extensor digitorum communis muscle.

##### Location note 

i. Divide the distance between Yángchí SJ-4 and the lateral epicondyle into half and then halve the distance between this midpoint and Yángchí SJ-4; ii. The point is located between the radius and the extensor digitorum communis muscle, close to the border of the radius.

##### Needling 

i. Slightly oblique insertion towards the ulnar side or oblique proximal or distal insertion towards the elbow or wrist respectively, 0.5 to 1.5 cun; ii. Joined by through-needling to Jiānshǐ PC-5.  
**Caution:** movement of the patient's arm or hand after needling this point can result in a bent needle.

##### Actions 

- Regulates qi and clears heat in the three jiao
- Benefits the chest and lateral costal region
- Moves the stool
- Benefits the voice
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

##### Indications 

- Tinnitus, heat-wind tinnitus and deafness, sudden loss of voice, lockjaw, febrile disease with absence of sweating, pain of the eyes, redness, swelling and pain of the eyes, swelling and pain of the throat, scrofula.
- Pain of the lateral costal region, abdominal pain, sudden Heart pain, Heart pain and oppression of the chest, cough, cough with redness and heat of the face.
- Constipation, vomiting, sudden turmoil disorder, post-partum dizziness, blockage of the Conception vessel in women.
- Pain of the axilla, pain of the shoulder, arm and back, painful obstruction of the elbow, tremor of the hand, cold-wind hand trembling, numbness of the hand, hemiplegia.

##### Commentary 

The Sanjiao theory divides the body into three regions, the upper jiao (the area above the diaphragm), the middle jiao (the area between the diaphragm and the umbilicus) and the lower jiao (the area below the umbilicus). Zhīgōu SJ-6, the jing-river and fire point of the Sanjiao channel, is the most important point on the Sanjiao channel for moving qi and clearing heat in these three areas. According to the Song of the Primary Points of the Fourteen Channels Zhīgōu SJ-6 is able to "drain ministerial fire from the three jiao". Ministerial fire, the primal fire of the body which has its root in the ming men and Kidneys, is 'entrusted' to the Liver, Gall Bladder and Sanjiao. The quotation above emphasises the clinical importance of Zhīgōu SJ-6 in clearing heat throughout the body, and especially resolving stagnant heat of the Liver and Gall Bladder.

In the upper jiao Zhīgōu SJ-6 is able to clear heat from the Sanjiao channel, being indicated for febrile diseases, redness, pain and swelling of the eyes, tinnitus and deafness, lockjaw, swollen throat, scrofula etc. It is also able to resolve stagnation of qi in the chest giving rise to oppression of the chest and Heart pain. If stagnant qi transforms into fire and insults the Lung there may be cough with a red face.

In the middle jiao it is an essential point for treating pain of the lateral costal region, and distention due to qi stagnation or any other aetiology. According to the Ode to Elucidate Mysteries "For pain of the ribs and lateral costal region needle the Flying Tiger [Feihu, an alternative name for Zhīgōu SJ-6]" whilst the Song of the Jade Dragon says "When there is pain of the lateral costal region with obstruction and knotting, using Zhīgōu SJ-6 will give extraordinarily wonderful results". Zhīgōu SJ-6 is also indicated for vomiting and sudden turmoil disorder, and may be used in situations where there is distention and bloating in the upper and lower abdomen, often extending to the chest, with Heart pain, oppression of the chest or cough.

In the lower jiao Zhīgōu SJ-6 is a major point for moving the qi of the intestines and treating constipation, whether due to stagnation of qi, heat or any other aetiology. In fact Zhīgōu SJ-6 and Wàiguān SJ-5 are the only points on the three arm yang channels indicated for constipation, and of these two Zhīgōu SJ-6 has always been considered the pre-eminent. According to the Great Compendium of Acupuncture and Moxibustion Zhīgōu SJ-6 is also indicated for blockage of the Conception vessel in women, a further reflection of its ability to move stagnation.

According to the Spiritual Pivot [8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-sanjiao-channel/references) the jing-river points should be needled when there are "diseases manifesting in the patient's voice", and Zhīgōu SJ-6 is an important point (and included in a number of classical combinations) for sudden loss of voice.

Finally, Zhīgōu SJ-6 is indicated for various channel disorders affecting the whole upper limb, axilla, shoulder and back, including tremors, trembling and numbness of the hand. In current practice it is often combined with Xuánzhōng GB-39 in the treatment of wandering painful obstruction.

##### Summary of clinical application 

- Important point for pain and distention of the lateral costal region and distention and bloating in the upper and lower abdomen.
- Important point to treat constipation, especially due to qi stagnation.
- Clears stagnant qi and heat from the upper jiao (redness, pain and swelling of the eyes, tinnitus and deafness, lockjaw, swollen throat, Heart pain, oppression of the chest, cough with a red face).
- Treats channel disorders affecting the whole upper limb, axilla, shoulder and back, including tremors, trembling and numbness of the hand; for wandering painful obstruction.

### **[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** (ZHĪGŌU)

* **英文名称：** Branch Ditch
* **中文名称：：** 支沟
* **国际代码：** SJ-6
* **穴位属性：** **[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** 是三焦经的经穴和火穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于**[SJ4 阳池](point/SJ4.md)** 上方3寸，在桡骨和尺骨之间凹陷中，指总伸肌肌桡侧。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 将**[SJ4 阳池](point/SJ4.md)** 和肱骨外上髁之间的距离分成两半，然后取该中点与**[SJ4 阳池](point/SJ4.md)** 之间距离的一半；
    * 穴位位于桡骨和指总伸肌之间，靠近桡骨边缘。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

* i. 稍斜向尺侧进针，或斜向近端或远端（分别向肘部或腕部）进针，0.5至1.5寸；
* ii. 透刺至**[PC5 间使](point/PC5.md)**。

* **注意 (Caution):** 针刺此穴后患者手臂或手部活动可能导致弯针。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 调理三焦气机，清热
* 宣利胸胁
* 通便
* 宣利声音
* 通经活络，止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

耳鸣，热风耳鸣耳聋，暴喑（突然失音），牙关紧闭，热病无汗，眼痛，眼睛红肿疼痛，咽喉肿痛，瘰疬。
胁肋部疼痛，腹痛，心痛暴作，心痛伴胸闷，咳嗽，咳嗽伴面赤发热。
便秘，呕吐，霍乱吐泻，产后眩晕，女性任脉不通。
腋窝疼痛，肩、臂、背部疼痛，肘部痹痛，手颤，风冷手抖，手部麻木，半身不遂。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

三焦理论将身体分为上焦（膈肌以上）、中焦（膈肌与脐之间）和下焦（脐以下）。**[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)**，作为三焦经的经穴和火穴，是三焦经上调理气机和清热在这三个区域中最重要的穴位。根据《十四经穴歌》记载，**[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** 能够“泻三焦之相火”。相火是身体的元火，其根源于命门和肾脏，并“受托”于肝、胆和三焦。上述引文强调了**[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** 在清泻全身热邪，特别是化解肝胆郁热方面的临床重要性。

在上焦，**[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** 能够清泻三焦经的热邪，主治热病、眼睛红肿疼痛、耳鸣耳聋、牙关紧闭、咽喉肿痛、瘰疬等。它还能化解胸中气滞，引起胸闷和心痛。如果气滞化火犯肺，可能出现面赤咳嗽。

在中焦，它是治疗胁肋部疼痛以及气滞或任何其他病因引起的胀满的关键穴位。根据《标幽赋》记载：“肋痛胁胀，针飞虎（**[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** 的别名）立解”，而《玉龙歌》记载：“胁肋疼痛结滞者，用**[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** 效如神”。**[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** 也主治呕吐和霍乱吐泻，可用于治疗上腹部和下腹部胀满，常延伸至胸部，伴有心痛、胸闷或咳嗽。

在下焦，**[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** 是活络肠道气机、治疗便秘的主要穴位，无论其是由气滞、热邪还是任何其他病因引起。事实上，**[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** 和**[SJ5 外关](point/SJ5.md)** 是三条手部阳经上唯一主治便秘的穴位，其中**[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** 始终被认为是首要的。根据《针灸大成》记载，**[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** 也主治女性任脉不通，这进一步反映了其化解积滞的能力。

根据《灵枢·本输》记载，经穴应针刺“病在音声”的表现，**[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** 是治疗暴喑（突然失音）的重要穴位（并被纳入一些经典组合中）。

最后，**[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** 主治影响整个上肢、腋窝、肩部和背部的各种经络疾病，包括手部颤动、震颤和麻木。在当前实践中，它常与**[GB39 悬钟](point/GB39.md)** 联合用于治疗游走性痹痛。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗胁肋部疼痛和上下腹部胀满的重要穴位。
* 治疗便秘的重要穴位，特别是气滞所致者。
* 清泻上焦气滞和热邪（眼睛红肿疼痛，耳鸣耳聋，牙关紧闭，咽喉肿痛，心痛，胸闷，面赤咳嗽）。
* 治疗影响整个上肢、腋窝、肩部和背部的经络疾病，包括手部颤动、震颤和麻木；用于游走性痹痛。
