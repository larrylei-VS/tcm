### Heavenly Stream TĪANXĪ
![[SP-18 天溪.png|250]]
#### Location

On the lateral side of the chest, in the fourth intercostal space, 6 cun lateral to the midline.

#### Location Notes

i. The intercostal spaces are most reliably counted downwards from the second intercostal space which lies immediately below the sternal angle. In males, the nipple almost invariably lies in the fourth intercostal space; ii. The six cun line is located two cun lateral to the mamillary line.

#### Needling

Transverse-oblique insertion along the intercostal space, 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** perpendicular insertion, especially in thin patients, carries a substantial risk of inducing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Regulates and descends qi
- Benefits the breasts and promotes lactation

#### Indications

- Fullness and pain of the chest and lateral costal region, shortness of breath, cough, rattling of phlegm in the throat, running piglet qi, hiccup.
- Breast abscess, failure of breast milk to flow, insufficient lactation.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Tianxi SP-18 is indicated for fullness and pain of the chest and lateral costal region, shortness of breath, cough, rattling of phlegm in the throat, running piglet qi, hiccup, breast abscess, failure of breast milk to flow, insufficient lactation.

#### Combinations

- Abscess, ulceration and swelling of the breast: Tīanxī SP-18 and [Xiáxī GB-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-43) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[SP18 天溪](/point/SP18.md)** (TIĀNXĪ)

* **英文名称：** Heavenly Stream
* **中文名称：** 天溪
* **国际代码：** SP-18

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于胸部外侧，第四肋间隙中，距前正中线旁开6寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 肋间隙最可靠的计数方法是从胸骨角下方的第二肋间隙向下数。男性乳头几乎总位于第四肋间隙；
    * 旁开6寸线位于乳头线旁开2寸处。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

沿肋间隙横斜刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直刺入，特别是在瘦弱患者中，有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 调理降气
* 宣利乳房，促进泌乳

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

胸部和胁肋部胀满疼痛，气短，咳嗽，喉中痰鸣，奔豚气，呃逆。
乳痈，乳汁不下，乳汁量少。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SP18 天溪](/point/SP18.md)** 临床上较少使用。其名称“天溪”可能暗示其在胸部气机和乳汁分泌方面的作用，如同溪流般通畅。

此穴主治胸部和胁肋部的胀满疼痛，以及气短、咳嗽、喉中痰鸣、呃逆等气机上逆和痰饮阻滞的症状。此外，**[SP18 天溪](/point/SP18.md)** 尤其擅长治疗乳房疾病，如乳痈、乳汁不下和乳汁量少，这与其调理气血、宣利乳房的功效密切相关。它对奔豚气的治疗也显示了其调理肝脾气机的能力。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治胸部和胁肋部胀满疼痛，气短，咳嗽，喉中痰鸣，奔豚气，呃逆。
* 治疗乳痈，乳汁不下，乳汁量少。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 乳房痈肿溃烂：**[SP18 天溪](/point/SP18.md)** 和 **[GB43 侠溪](/point/GB43.md)**（《千金方》）。
