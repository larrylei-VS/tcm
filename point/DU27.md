### DUÌDUĀN
![[DU-27 兑端.png|250]]
#### Location

On the midline, at the junction of the margin of the upper lip and the philtrum.

#### Location Notes

The philtrum is the marked indentation found on the midline between the root of the nose and the margin of the upper lip.

#### Needling

Oblique insertion directed superiorly 0.2 to 0.3 cun.

#### Actions

- Clears heat, generates fluid and benefits the mouth
- Calms the spirit

#### Indications

- Ulceration of the mouth, foul breath, pain of the gums, dry tongue, wasting and thirsting disorder with much drinking, swelling and stiffness of the lips, ceaseless nosebleed, nasal congestion.
- Mania-depression, epilepsy, lockjaw.
- Dark urine, vomiting of foam.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for ulceration of the mouth, foul breath, pain of the gums, dry tongue, wasting and thirsting disorder with much drinking, swelling and stiffness of the lips, ceaseless nosebleed, nasal congestion, mania-depression, epilepsy, lockjaw, dark urine, vomiting of foam.

#### Combinations

- Stiffness of the lips and pain from tooth decay of the upper jaw: Duìduān DU-27, [Mùchuāng GB-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-16), [Zhèngyíng GB-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-17) and [Ěrmén SJ-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-21) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Vomiting of foam: Duìduān DU-27, [Shàohǎi HE-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-3) and [Běnshén GB-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-13) (Supplementing Life).
- Incessant nosebleed: Duìduān DU-27, [Kǒuhéliáo LI-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-19) and [Láogōng PC-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-8) (Supplementing Life).

### 1. [DU27 兑端](/point/DU27.md)

#### 1.1. Extremity of the Mouth 兌端

#### 1.2. 定位

位于中线，上唇缘与人中交界处。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

人中为鼻根与上唇缘间的中线明显凹陷。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

向上斜刺，深0.2至0.3寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 清热生津，利口  
- 定神  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 口疮、口臭、牙龈痛、舌干、消渴多饮、唇肿僵、鼻衄不止、鼻塞。  
- 躁郁症、癫痫、牙关紧闭。  
- 尿色深、吐沫。  

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 使用较少。  
- 用于口疮、口臭、牙龈痛、舌干、消渴多饮、唇肿僵、鼻衄不止、鼻塞、躁郁症、癫痫、牙关紧闭、尿色深、吐沫。  

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 上唇僵及上颌龋痛：[DU27 兑端](/point/DU27.md) + [GB16 目窗](/point/GB16.md) + [GB17 正营](/point/GB17.md) + [SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)（《千金方》）。  
- 吐沫：[DU27 兑端](/point/DU27.md) + [HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md) + [GB13 本神](/point/GB13.md)（《补命》）。  
- 鼻衄不止：[DU27 兑端](/point/DU27.md) + [LI19 口禾髎](/point/LI19.md) + [PC8 劳宫](/point/PC8.md)（《补命》）。
