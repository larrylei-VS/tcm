### Water Prominence SHUǏTÚ
![[ST-10 水突.png|250]]
#### Location

On the neck, at the anterior border of the sternocleidomastoid muscle, midway between [Rényíng ST-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-9) and [Qìshè ST-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-11).

#### Location Notes

To identify the anterior border of the muscle, ask the patient to turn their head away from the side to be needled, whilst you apply resistance at the chin.

#### Needling

Perpendicular-oblique insertion directed medially to avoid the carotid artery, 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** see [Rényíng ST-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-9).

#### Actions

- Benefits the throat and neck
- Descends Lung qi

#### Indications

- Swelling and pain of the throat, goitre, scrofula.
- Cough, whooping cough, shortness of breath, dyspnoea, swelling and pain of the shoulder.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Both Shuǐtú ST-10 and [Qìshè ST-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-11) are indicated for swelling and pain of the throat, goitre, scrofula and symptoms such as cough and dyspnoea.

#### Combinations

- Swelling of the throat: Shuǐtú ST-10 and [Qìshè ST-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-11) (Supplementing Life).

### [ST10 水突](point/ST10.md) (SHUǏTÚ)

- **英文名称：** Water Prominence
- **中文名称：** 水突
- **国际代码：** ST-10
#### 定位 (Location)

位于颈部，胸锁乳突肌前缘，[ST9 人迎](point/ST9.md) 和[ST11 气舍](point/ST11.md) 之间中点。
- **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    - 为辨别胸锁乳突肌前缘，请患者头部转向针刺侧的对侧，同时施加阻力于下巴。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直斜刺，向内侧进针以避开颈总动脉，0.5至1寸。
- **注意 (Caution):** 参见[ST9 人迎](point/ST9.md) 的注意事项。
#### 作用 (Actions)
- 宣利咽喉颈部
- 肃降肺气

#### 主治病症 (Indications)
咽喉肿痛，瘿瘤，瘰疬。
咳嗽，百日咳，气短，呼吸困难，肩部肿痛。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)
[ST10 水突](point/ST10.md) 临床上较少使用。[ST10 水突](point/ST10.md) 和[ST11 气舍](point/ST11.md) 都主治咽喉肿痛、瘿瘤、瘰疬以及咳嗽、呼吸困难等症状。
#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)
临床上较少使用。
主治咽喉肿痛，瘿瘤，瘰疬以及咳嗽，呼吸困难等症状。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

- 咽喉肿胀：[ST10 水突](point/ST10.md) 和[ST11 气舍](point/ST11.md)（《资生经》）。
