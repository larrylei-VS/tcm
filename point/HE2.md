### Green Spirit QĪNGLÍNG
![[HE-2 青灵.png|250]]
#### Location

3 cun proximal to the medial end of the transverse cubital crease, on the line connecting [Jíquán HE-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-1) and [Shàohǎi HE-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-3).

#### Location Notes

i. Locate with the elbow flexed; ii.. This point is located in the groove medial to the biceps brachii muscle, one handbreadth proximal to [Shàohǎi HE-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-3).

#### Needling

Oblique distal or proximal insertion, 0.5 to 1 cun, avoiding the brachial artery. Note: many early classics only discuss moxibustion in relation to this point, implying that needling was contraindicated, perhaps because of the danger of damaging the brachial artery, whilst the Introduction to Medicine specifically contraindicates needling at this point.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Inability to raise the shoulder and arm, swelling, pain and redness of the shoulder, pain of the axilla, headache with cold shivering, yellow eyes, pain of the lateral costal region, scrofula.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Inability to raise the shoulder and arm, swelling, pain and redness of the shoulder, pain of the axilla, headache with cold shivering, yellow eyes, pain of the lateral costal region, scrofula.

#### Combinations

- Pain of the shoulder and upper arm: Qīnglíng HE-2, [Jiānyú LI-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-15) and [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11).

### **[HE2 青灵](/point/HE2.md)** (QĪNGLÍNG)

* **英文名称：** Green Spirit
* **中文名称：** 青灵
* **国际代码：** HE-2

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于肘横纹内侧端上方3寸，在连接 **[HE1 极泉](/point/HE1.md)** 与 **[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)** 的连线上。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 屈肘时定位；
    * 此穴位于肱二头肌内侧沟中，距 **[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)** 上方一横掌处。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺，向远端或近端，0.5至1寸，避开肱动脉。
注意：许多早期经典只讨论此穴的艾灸，暗示针刺禁忌，可能因为有损伤肱动脉的危险；《医学入门》明确指出此穴禁针。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 通经活络，止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

不能抬肩抬臂，肩部肿痛红赤，腋窝疼痛，头痛伴恶寒发抖，眼睛发黄，胁肋部疼痛，瘰疬。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[HE2 青灵](/point/HE2.md)** 位于心经循行路线上，其名称“青灵”可能暗示其在肝（青色）与心神（灵）之间的联系，或在疏通经络、活血止痛方面的作用。

此穴主要用于治疗肩臂部经络循行所过之处的疼痛、肿胀和活动不利。它主治不能抬肩抬臂，肩部肿痛红赤，以及腋窝疼痛。此外，它对头痛伴恶寒发抖、眼睛发黄和胁肋部疼痛也有一定的治疗作用，这些都可能与经络气血不畅或热邪相关。传统上，它也被用于治疗瘰疬。

值得注意的是，由于此穴附近有重要的血管，针刺操作需格外谨慎。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 主治不能抬肩抬臂，肩部肿痛红赤，腋窝疼痛，头痛伴恶寒发抖，眼睛发黄，胁肋部疼痛，瘰疬。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 肩臂疼痛：**[HE2 青灵](/point/HE2.md)**，**[LI15 肩髃](/point/LI15.md)** 和 **[LI11 曲池](/point/LI11.md)**。
