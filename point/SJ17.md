### Wind Screen  Yìfēng
- Meeting point of the Sanjiao and Gall Bladder channels
![[SJ-17 翳风.png|250]]
#### Location 

Behind the earlobe, between the ramus of the mandible and the mastoid process, in the depression just superior to the palpable transverse process of the first cervical vertebra.

#### Location note 

Fold the earlobe forwards to reveal this point.

#### Needling 

Perpendicular insertion, directed towards the opposite ear, 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Note:** if the needle is directed too anteriorly or posteriorly, pain will ensue and may cause discomfort on opening and closing the mouth for some while after treatment.

#### Actions 

- Benefits the ears
- Eliminates wind
- Clears heat
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications 

- Deafness, tinnitus, discharge from the ear, damp itching of the inside of the ear, redness pain and swelling of the ear, earache.
- Deviation of the mouth and eye, loss of speech, tetany with inability to speak, lockjaw, pain and swelling of the cheek, toothache of the lower jaw, pain of the teeth and gums, mumps, scrofula, superficial visual obstruction with dimness of vision.
- Mania disorder, clonic spasm.

#### Commentary 

Yìfēng SJ-17 is a meeting point of the Sanjiao and Gall Bladder channels, both of which enter the ear, and is an essential local point in the treatment of all ear disorders including tinnitus, deafness, discharge, itching, redness, swelling and pain.

As the name of this point (Wind Screen) indicates, Yìfēng SJ-17 has an action on expelling exterior pathogenic wind and treating the manifestations of internal wind, and is used widely for facial paralysis due to windstroke or Bell's palsy, trigeminal neuralgia, spasm and lockjaw.

Through its action of clearing heat and circulating the channel, Yìfēng SJ-17 is also indicated for local disorders such as mumps, scrofula, swelling of the cheek and toothache.

#### Summary of clinical application 

- Important local point in the treatment of all ear disorders including tinnitus, deafness, discharge, itching, redness and swelling and pain.
- Eliminates exterior and interior wind: facial paralysis due to windstroke or Bell’s palsy, trigeminal neuralgia, spasm and lockjaw.
- Treats local disorders such as mumps, scrofula, swelling of the cheek and toothache.

### **[SJ17 翳风](point/SJ17.md)** (Yìfēng)

* **英文名称：** Wind Screen
* **中文名称：** 翳风
* **国际代码：** SJ-17
* **穴位属性：** **[SJ17 翳风](point/SJ17.md)** 是三焦经与胆经的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于耳垂后方，下颌支与乳突之间，第一颈椎横突上方凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 向前折叠耳垂以显露出此穴。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺，针尖朝向对侧耳朵，0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Note):** 如果针尖刺入过前或过后，会引起疼痛，并可能在治疗后一段时间内导致开口和闭口不适。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利耳部
* 祛风
* 清热
* 通经活络，止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

耳聋，耳鸣，耳部流脓，耳内湿痒，耳部红肿疼痛，耳痛。
口眼歪斜，失语，破伤风不能言语，牙关紧闭，面颊疼痛肿胀，下颌牙痛，牙齿牙龈疼痛，痄腮（腮腺炎），瘰疬，外障眼病（翳膜）伴视力减退。
癫狂，痉挛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SJ17 翳风](point/SJ17.md)** 是三焦经与胆经的交会穴，这两条经脉都入耳，是治疗耳鸣、耳聋、流脓、瘙痒、红肿疼痛等所有**耳部疾病**的关键局部穴位。

正如其名称“翳风”所示，**[SJ17 翳风](point/SJ17.md)** 具有**祛除外感风邪**和**治疗内风表现**的作用，广泛用于中风或贝尔氏麻痹引起的**面瘫**、**三叉神经痛**、**痉挛**和**牙关紧闭**。

通过其**清热**和**活络经脉**的作用，**[SJ17 翳风](point/SJ17.md)** 也主治痄腮、瘰疬、面颊肿胀和牙痛等局部疾病。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗所有**耳部疾病**的重要局部穴位，包括耳鸣、耳聋、流脓、瘙痒、红肿和疼痛。
* **祛除外风和内风**：中风或贝尔氏麻痹引起的面瘫，三叉神经痛，痉挛和牙关紧闭。
* 治疗**局部疾病**，如痄腮、瘰疬、面颊肿胀和牙痛。
