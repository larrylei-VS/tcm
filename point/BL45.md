### Yi Xi YĪXǏ
![[BL-45 譩譆.png|250]]
#### Location

3 cun lateral to the midline, level with the lower border of the spinous process of the sixth thoracic vertebra (T6) and level with [Dūshū BL-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-16).

#### Location Notes

When the shoulder is relaxed, the three cun line corresponds to the medial border of the scapula.

#### Needling

Oblique insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.  
**Caution:** deep perpendicular or deep oblique needling in a medial direction carries a substantial risk of causing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Expels wind, clears heat and descends Lung qi
- Invigorates qi and blood and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Cough, dyspnoea, febrile disease with absence of sweating, attack by wind with absence of sweating, visual dizziness, eye pain, nosebleed, malaria, headache in children whilst eating, five palms agitated and hot.
- Pain of the shoulder, scapula and back, chest pain that radiates to the lumbar region, pain of the lateral costal region accompanied by distention and pain of the hypogastrium, pain of the lateral costal region radiating to the Heart and Lung, abdominal distention, contraction of the axilla.

#### Commentary

Yīxǐ BL-45 is level with [Dūshū BL-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-16) which is listed in some classical texts as the back-shu point of the Governing vessel. Whilst [Dūshū BL-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-16) has few indications which reflect disorder of the Governing vessel, some of the indications of Yīxǐ BL-45, for example visual dizziness, eye pain, nosebleed, malaria, headache and chest pain radiating to the lumbar region, may be understood in this way.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Lies level with [Dūshū BL-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-16) (listed in some classics as the backshu point of the Governing vessel) and has some Governing vessel indications: visual dizziness, eye pain, nosebleed, malaria, headache and chest pain radiating to the lumbar region.
- Also indicated for cough, dyspnoea, febrile disease with absence of sweating, attack by wind with absence of sweating, headache in children whilst eating, five palms agitated and hot, pain of the shoulder, scapula and back, pain of the lateral costal region accompanied by distention and pain of the hypogastrium, pain of the lateral costal region radiating to the Heart and Lung, abdominal distention, contraction of the axilla.

#### Combinations

- Abdominal fullness: Yīxǐ BL-45 and [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) (Supplementing Life).
- Cough with rebellious qi, dyspnoea, vomiting of foam and clenched teeth: Yīxǐ BL-45, [Fútú LI-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-18), [Tiānróng SI-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-17), [Liánquán REN-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-23), [Pòhù BL-42](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-42) and [Qìshè ST-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-11) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Swelling of the face: first needle Yīxǐ BL-45, then [Tiānyǒu SJ-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-16) and [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20) (Systematic Classic).
- Visual dizziness: Yīxǐ BL-45, [Shéntíng DU-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-24), [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23), [Yǒngquǎn KID-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/points/KID-1), [Yújì LU-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-10) and [Dàdū SP-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-2) (Supplementing Life).
- Contraction and cold of the shoulder and back, with pain of the inner aspect of the scapula: Yīxǐ BL-45, [Chǐzé LU-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-5), [Géshū BL-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-17) and [Jīnmén BL-63](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-63) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[BL45 噫嘻](point/BL45.md)** (YĪXĪ)

* **英文名称：** Yi Xi
* **中文名称：** 噫嘻
* **国际代码：** BL-45
* **穴位属性：** **[BL45 噫嘻](point/BL45.md)** 是膀胱经第二侧线上的穴位，与**[BL16 督俞](point/BL16.md)** 平齐。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第六胸椎（T6）棘突下缘水平，距中线旁开3寸，与**[BL16 督俞](point/BL16.md)** 平齐。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 肩部放松时，旁开3寸线对应肩胛骨内侧缘。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺0.3至0.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直深刺或向内侧深斜刺有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 祛风清热，肃降肺气
* 活气血，止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

咳嗽，呼吸困难，热病无汗，伤风无汗，视物眩晕，眼痛，鼻出血，疟疾，小儿食中头痛，五心烦热。
肩胛疼痛，肩背疼痛，胸痛放射至腰部，胁肋部疼痛伴小腹胀痛，胁肋部疼痛放射至心肺，腹胀，腋窝挛缩。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL45 噫嘻](point/BL45.md)** 与**[BL16 督俞](point/BL16.md)** 平齐，后者在一些经典中被列为督脉的背俞穴。尽管**[BL16 督俞](point/BL16.md)** 很少有反映督脉疾病的主治，但**[BL45 噫嘻](point/BL45.md)** 的一些主治，例如视物眩晕、眼痛、鼻出血、疟疾、头痛和胸痛放射至腰部，可以从这个角度来理解。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 与**[BL16 督俞](point/BL16.md)**（在某些经典中被列为督脉的背俞穴）平齐，并具有一些督脉主治：视物眩晕，眼痛，鼻出血，疟疾，头痛和胸痛放射至腰部。
* 也主治咳嗽，呼吸困难，热病无汗，伤风无汗，小儿食中头痛，五心烦热，肩胛疼痛，肩背疼痛，胁肋部疼痛伴小腹胀痛，胁肋部疼痛放射至心肺，腹胀，腋窝挛缩。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 腹胀：**[BL45 噫嘻](point/BL45.md)** 和 **[ST36 足三里](point/ST36.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 咳嗽气逆，呼吸困难，呕吐泡沫，牙关紧闭：**[BL45 噫嘻](point/BL45.md)**，**[LI18 扶突](point/LI18.md)**，**[SI17 天容](point/SI17.md)**，**[REN23 廉泉](point/REN23.md)**，**[BL42 魄户](point/BL42.md)** 和 **[ST11 气舍](point/ST11.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 面部水肿：先针 **[BL45 噫嘻](point/BL45.md)**，然后 **[SJ16 天牖](point/SJ16.md)** 和 **[GB20 风池](point/GB20.md)**（《针灸甲乙经》）。
* 视物眩晕：**[BL45 噫嘻](point/BL45.md)**，**[DU24 神庭](point/DU24.md)**，**[DU23 上星](point/DU23.md)**，**[KID1 涌泉](point/KID1.md)**，**[LU10 鱼际](point/LU10.md)** 和 **[SP2 大都](point/SP2.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 肩背挛缩寒冷，肩胛内侧疼痛：**[BL45 噫嘻](point/BL45.md)**，**[LU5 尺泽](point/LU5.md)**，**[BL17 膈俞](point/BL17.md)** 和 **[BL63 金门](point/BL63.md)**（《千金方》）。
