### Hold and Support CHÉNGFÚ
![[BL-36 承扶.png|250]]
#### Location

Just below the buttock, on a line directly superior to [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40), in the centre of the transverse gluteal crease in a depression between the hamstring muscles.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 2 cun.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel, relaxes the sinews and alleviates pain
- Regulates the lower jiao and alleviates pain
- Treats haemorrhoids

#### Indications

- Lumbar pain, pain of the sacrum and coccyx, pain of the buttock, sciatica, painful obstruction and atrophy disorder of the lower limb.
- Difficult defecation, difficult urination, pain of the genitals, seminal emission, cold in the uterus, swelling of the axilla.
- Chronic haemorrhoids, bleeding haemorrhoids.

#### Commentary

Chéngfú BL-36 is much used for pain radiating down the Bladder channel from the lumbar region and buttock. It is especially indicated for pain of the buttocks and sciatic pain that is aggravated by sitting. Its secondary application is as an adjacent point in the treatment of haemorrhoids.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Commonly used for sciatica and pain radiating down the Bladder channel from the lumbar region and buttock. Also for painful obstruction and atrophy disorder of the lower limb.
- Also indicated for disorders of the lower jiao such as difficult defecation or urination, pain of the genitals, seminal emission, cold in the uterus, chronic or bleeding haemorrhoids.

#### Combinations

- Difficult urination and seminal emission: Chéngfú BL-36, [Lòugǔ SP-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-7), [Zhōngjí REN-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-3), [Lǐgōu LIV-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-5) and [Zhìyīn BL-67](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-67) (Supplementing Life).
- Haemorrhoids, swelling of the axilla: Chéngfú BL-36, [Chéngjīn BL-56](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-56), [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) and [Yánggǔ SI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-5) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Sciatica: Chéngfú BL-36, [Cìliáo BL-32](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-32), [Zhìbiān BL-54](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-54), [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) and [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60).

### **[BL36 承扶](/point/BL36.md)** (CHÉNGFÚ)

* **英文名称：** Hold and Support
* **中文名称：** 承扶
* **国际代码：** BL-36

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于臀部下方，**[BL40 委中](/point/BL40.md)** 直上方，臀横纹中央，股后肌群之间的凹陷中。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺1至2寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 通经活络，舒筋止痛
* 调理下焦，止痛
* 治疗痔疮

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

腰痛，骶骨和尾骨疼痛，臀部疼痛，坐骨神经痛，下肢痹证和痿证。
排便困难，小便困难，生殖器疼痛，遗精，子宫虚寒，腋窝肿胀。
慢性痔疮，痔疮出血。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL36 承扶](/point/BL36.md)** 广泛用于治疗沿膀胱经从腰部和臀部向下放射的疼痛。它特别主治臀部疼痛和坐骨神经痛（久坐后加重）。其次要的应用是作为治疗痔疮的邻近穴位。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 常用于坐骨神经痛和沿膀胱经从腰部和臀部向下放射的疼痛。也用于下肢痹证和痿证。
* 也主治下焦疾病，如排便或小便困难，生殖器疼痛，遗精，子宫虚寒，慢性痔疮或痔疮出血。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 小便不利伴遗精：**[BL36 承扶](/point/BL36.md)**，**[SP7 漏谷](/point/SP7.md)**，**[REN3 中极](/point/REN3.md)**，**[LIV5 蠡沟](/point/LIV5.md)** 和 **[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 痔疮，腋窝肿胀：**[BL36 承扶](/point/BL36.md)**，**[BL56 承筋](/point/BL56.md)**，**[BL40 委中](/point/BL40.md)** 和 **[SI5 阳谷](/point/SI5.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 坐骨神经痛：**[BL36 承扶](/point/BL36.md)**，**[BL32 次髎](/point/BL32.md)**，**[BL54 秩边](/point/BL54.md)**，**[BL40 委中](/point/BL40.md)** 和 **[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)**。
