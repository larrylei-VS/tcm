### Declining Connection LUÒQUÈ
![[BL-8 络却.png|250]]
#### Location

1.5 cun posterior to [Tōngtiān BL-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-7), and 5.5 cun within the anterior hairline, 1.5 cun lateral to the midline.

#### Location Notes

i. Luòquè BL-8 is most easily located 1.5 cun lateral to and 0.5 cun posterior to [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20); ii. To locate the anterior hairline if indistinct, see location note for [Méichōng BL-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-3); iii. Note that the distance from the anterior hairline at the midline to [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) is 5 cun.

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.

#### Actions

- Benefits the sense organs
- Pacifies wind, transforms phlegm and calms the spirit

#### Indications

- Dizziness, tinnitus, nasal congestion, deviation of the mouth, blurred vision.
- Mania-depression, mad walking, epilepsy, disorientation, goitre, vomiting, abdominal distention, collapse.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for dizziness, tinnitus, nasal congestion, deviation of the mouth, blurred vision, mania-depression, mad walking, epilepsy, disorientation, goitre, vomiting, abdominal distention, collapse.

#### Combinations

- Madness with vomiting: Luòquè BL-8, [Zhúbīn KID-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-9), [Yánggǔ SI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-5), [Hòudǐng DU-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-19), [Qiángjiān DU-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-18), [Nǎohù DU-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-17) and [Yùzhěn BL-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-9) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Collapse on sudden standing: Luòquè BL-8 and [Tōngtiān BL-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-7) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Tinnitus: Luòquè BL-8, [Tīnggōng SI-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-19), [Tīnghuì GB-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-2), [Ěrmén SJ-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-21), [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20), [Yángxī LI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-5), [Qiángǔ SI-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-2), [Hòuxī SI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-3), [Wàngǔ SI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-4), [Zhōngzhǔ SJ-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-3), [Yèmén SJ-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-2), [Shāngyáng LI-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-1) and [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23) (Great Compendium).

### **[BL8 络却](point/BL8.md)** (LUÒQUÈ)

* **英文名称：** Declining Connection
* **中文名称：** 络却
* **国际代码：** BL-8

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于**[BL7 通天](point/BL7.md)** 后方1.5寸，前发际内5.5寸，距中线旁开1.5寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * **[BL8 络却](point/BL8.md)** 最易定位在**[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)** 旁开1.5寸，后方0.5寸处；
    * 如果前发际不明显，请参见**[BL3 眉冲](point/BL3.md)** 的定位要点；
    * 请注意，中线前发际到**[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)** 的距离为5寸。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

横刺0.5至1寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利七窍
* 息风化痰，宁心安神

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

眩晕，耳鸣，鼻塞，口歪，视物模糊。
癫狂，循衣摸床（或行走无度），癫痫，神志不清，瘿瘤，呕吐，腹胀，昏厥。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL8 络却](point/BL8.md)** 临床上较少使用。其名称“络却”可能暗示其在经络气血运行受阻时，对头部和七窍的治疗作用。

此穴主要作用于头部和七窍，能够宣利七窍，平息风邪，化痰宁神。它主治眩晕、耳鸣、鼻塞、口歪和视物模糊等症状。此外，**[BL8 络却](point/BL8.md)** 也辅助治疗癫狂、循衣摸床、癫痫、神志不清和瘿瘤等精神情志及颈部病症。其对呕吐、腹胀和昏厥的治疗也显示了其调气降逆的功效。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治眩晕，耳鸣，鼻塞，口歪，视物模糊，癫狂，循衣摸床（或行走无度），癫痫，神志不清，瘿瘤，呕吐，腹胀，昏厥。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 狂病伴呕吐：**[BL8 络却](point/BL8.md)**，**[KID9 筑宾](point/KID9.md)**，**[SI5 阳谷](point/SI5.md)**，**[DU19 后顶](point/DU19.md)**，**[DU18 强间](point/DU18.md)**，**[DU17 脑户](point/DU17.md)** 和 **[BL9 玉枕](point/BL9.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 突然站立时昏厥：**[BL8 络却](point/BL8.md)** 和 **[BL7 通天](point/BL7.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 耳鸣：**[BL8 络却](point/BL8.md)**，**[SI19 听宫](point/SI19.md)**，**[GB2 听会](point/GB2.md)**，**[SJ21 耳门](point/SJ21.md)**，**[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)**，**[LI5 阳溪](point/LI5.md)**，**[SI2 前谷](point/SI2.md)**，**[SI3 后溪](point/SI3.md)**，**[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)**，**[SJ3 中渚](point/SJ3.md)**，**[SJ2 液门](point/SJ2.md)**，**[LI1 商阳](point/LI1.md)** 和 **[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)**（《针灸大成》）。
