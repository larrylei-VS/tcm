### Suspended Skull   XUÁNLÚ
![[GB-5 悬颅.png|250]]

- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder, Stomach, Sanjiao and Large Intestine channels

#### Location

In the temporal region, within the hairline, half the distance between [Tóuwéi ST-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-8) and [Qūbìn GB-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-7).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5-1.5 cun. See note [Hànyàn GB-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-4).

#### Actions

- Expels wind and clears heat
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- One-sided headache extending to the outer canthus, pain of the outer canthus, headache.
- Toothache, pain, swelling and redness of the skin of the face, nosebleed, incessant turbid nasal discharge, rhinitis, febrile disease with agitation and fullness and absence of sweating.

#### Summary of clinical application

- One sided headache extending to the outer canthus, pain of the outer canthus, headache.
- Toothache, pain, swelling and redness of the skin of the face, nosebleed, incessant turbid nasal discharge, rhinitis, febrile disease with agitation and fullness and absence of sweating.

#### Combinations

- One-sided headache: Xuánlú GB-5 and [Hànyàn GB-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-4) (One Hundred Symptoms).

### 1. [GB5 悬颅](point/GB5.md)


- 胆经、胃经、三焦经和大肠经的交会穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于颞区，发际内，在[ST8 头维](point/ST8.md)与[GB7 曲鬓](point/GB7.md)之间中点。

#### 1.3. 针刺方法

横刺，深0.5至1.5寸。参见[GB4 颔厌](point/GB4.md)的针刺说明。

#### 1.4. 作用

- 祛风清热
- 活络止痛

#### 1.5. 主治

- 偏头痛延伸至外眦、外眦痛、头痛。
- 牙痛、面部皮肤疼痛肿红、鼻衄、持续浊涕、鼻炎、热病伴烦满无汗。

#### 1.6. 临床应用总结

- 适用于偏头痛延伸至外眦、外眦痛、头痛。
- 适用于牙痛、面部皮肤疼痛肿红、鼻衄、持续浊涕、鼻炎、热病伴烦满无汗。

#### 1.7. 配伍

- 偏头痛：[GB5 悬颅](point/GB5.md) + [GB4 颔厌](point/GB4.md)（《百症赋》）。
