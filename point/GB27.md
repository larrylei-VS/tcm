### Five Pivots WǓSHŪ
![[GB-27 五枢.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder channel with the Girdling vessel

#### Location

In the depression just anterior to the anterior superior iliac spine, approximately level with [Guānyuán REN-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-4) (3 cun below the umbilicus).

#### Location Notes

To locate the anterior superior iliac spine (ASIS), place the hand on the lateral part of the lower abdomen, below the level of the umbilicus. The ASIS is then readily palpated as the pronounced bony prominence. Alternatively, follow the ridge of the iliac crest and palpate the ASIS as its anterior prominence.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Regulates the Girdling vessel
- Regulates the lower jiao and transforms stagnation

#### Indications

- Uterine prolapse, red and white leucorrhoea, irregular menstruation.
- Lower abdominal pain, cold shan disorder in men, abdominal pain due to retraction of the testicles, constipation, tenesmus, pain of the back, lumbar region and ilium, clonic spasm.

#### Commentary

Like [Dàimài GB-26](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-26), Wǔshū GB-27 (a coalescent point of the Girdling vessel) regulates the Girdling vessel, treats menstrual disorders and leucorrhoea, and spreads the Liver qi in the lower abdomen (lower abdominal pain and constipation).

In comparing the two points, however, it is interesting to note that the traditional qi moving and pain relieving indications of this point lean more towards male disorders such as cold shan disorder in men and abdominal pain due to retraction of the testicles, whilst those of [Dàimài GB-26](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-26) lean more towards female disorders such as hardness of the hypogastrium in women and lower abdominal pain in women. This is reflected in Zhu Dan-xi's statement "Serious shan qi is acute pain in the testicles extending to the lower abdomen. The pain may be in the testicles or in the neighbourhood of the point Five Pivots (Wǔshū GB-27). In either case it involves the foot jueyin channel"[8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-gall-bladder-channel/references).

#### Summary of clinical application

- Treats cold shan disorder in men and abdominal pain due to retraction of the testicles. “Serious shan qi is acute pain in the testicles extending to the lower abdomen. The pain may be in the testicles or in the neighbourhood of the point Five Pivots (Wushu GB-27). In either case it involves the foot jueyin channel" (Zhu Dan-xi).
- Also uterine prolapse, red and white leucorrhoea, irregular menstruation.

#### Combinations

- Retracted testicle: Wǔshū GB-27 and [Guīlái ST-29](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-29) (Supplementing Life).

### 1. [GB27 五枢](point/GB27.md)

#### 1.1. Five Pivots 五樞

- 胆经与带脉的交会穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于髂前上棘前方的凹陷处，约与[REN4 关元](point/REN4.md)（脐下3寸）平齐。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

为定位髂前上棘（ASIS），将手置于下腹部侧面，脐水平下方。髂前上棘为可明显触及的骨性突起。或者，沿髂嵴探查，髂前上棘为其前方突起。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直刺入，深1至1.5寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 调带脉
- 调下焦，化滞

#### 1.6. 主治

- 子宫脱垂、赤白带下、不规则月经。
- 下腹痛、男子寒疝、睾丸上缩引起的腹痛、便秘、里急后重、背腰髂痛、痉挛。

#### 1.7. 论述

与[GB26 带脉](point/GB26.md)类似，[GB27 五枢](point/GB27.md)（带脉的交会穴）调理带脉，治疗月经失调和带下病，并疏通下腹部的肝气（下腹痛和便秘）。

然而，比较两穴时，值得注意的是，[GB27 五枢](point/GB27.md)传统的疏气止痛适应症更倾向于男性疾患，如男子寒疝及睾丸上缩引起的腹痛；而[GB26 带脉](point/GB26.md)更倾向于女性疾患，如女子脐下硬痛及下腹痛。这反映在朱丹溪的论述中：“重疝气为睾丸急痛延及下腹，痛可能在睾丸或[GB27 五枢](point/GB27.md)附近，无论何处，皆涉足厥阴经。”

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 治疗男子寒疝及睾丸上缩引起的腹痛。“重疝气为睾丸急痛延及下腹，痛可能在睾丸或[GB27 五枢](point/GB27.md)附近，无论何处，皆涉足厥阴经”（朱丹溪）。
- 也适用于子宫脱垂、赤白带下、不规则月经。

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 睾丸上缩：[GB27 五枢](point/GB27.md) + [ST29 归来](point/ST29.md)（《补命》）。
