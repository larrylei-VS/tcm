### Qi Door QÌHÙ
![[ST-13 气户.png|250]]
#### Location

At the top of the chest, directly below [Quēpén ST-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-12), on the inferior border of the clavicle, 4 cun lateral to the midline, on the mamillary line.

#### Needling

Transverse-oblique insertion laterally or medially, 0.5 to 0.8 cun, or transverse insertion inferiorly along the channel.  
**Caution:** deep or perpendicular insertion carries a substantial risk of puncturing the lung or injuring the subclavian vessels.

#### Actions

- Descends rebellious qi and unbinds the chest

#### Indications

- Fullness and distention of the chest and lateral costal region, pain of the chest and upper back, cough, dyspnoea, asthma, wheezing, vomiting blood, hiccup, inability to taste food, stiffness of the neck with inability to turn the head.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Indicated for fullness and distention of the chest and lateral costal region, pain of the chest and upper back, cough, dyspnoea, asthma, wheezing, vomiting blood, hiccup, inability to taste food, stiffness of the neck with inability to turn the head.

#### Combinations

- Chronic pain of the lateral costal region: Qìhù ST-13 and [Huágài REN-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-20) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Dyspnoea with rebellious qi, breathing with raised shoulders, inability to taste food: Qìhù ST-13, [Yúnmén LU-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-2), [Tiānfǔ LU-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-3) and [Shénmén HE-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-7) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### [ST13 气户](/point/ST13.md) (QÌHÙ)

* **英文名称：** Qi Door
* **中文名称：** 气户
* **国际代码：** ST-13

#### 定位 (Location)

位于胸部上方，[ST12 缺盆](/point/ST12.md) 直下方，锁骨下缘，距前正中线旁开4寸，在乳头线上。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

横斜刺，向外侧或内侧刺0.5至0.8寸，或沿经络向下横刺。

* **注意 (Caution):** 深刺或垂直刺入有很大风险刺破肺脏或损伤锁骨下血管。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 降逆气，宽胸散结

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

胸部和胁肋部胀满，胸部和上背部疼痛，咳嗽，呼吸困难，哮喘，喘息，咳血，呃逆，食不知味，颈项强直不能转头。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST13 气户](/point/ST13.md) 位于胸部上方，是胃经的重要穴位。其主要作用是降逆气、宽胸散结，因此擅长治疗各种因气机不畅或邪气阻滞胸部所引起的症状。这包括胸部和胁肋部的胀满、疼痛，以及咳嗽、呼吸困难、哮喘、喘息等肺系疾患。此外，它也主治咳血、呃逆等气逆上冲的表现，以及食不知味、颈项强直不能转头等病症。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 主治胸部和胁肋部胀满，胸部和上背部疼痛，咳嗽，呼吸困难，哮喘，喘息，咳血，呃逆，食不知味，颈项强直不能转头。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 慢性胁肋部疼痛：[ST13 气户](/point/ST13.md) 和[REN20 华盖](/point/REN20.md)（《百症赋》）。
* 气逆喘息，耸肩呼吸，食不知味：[ST13 气户](/point/ST13.md)，[LU2 云门](/point/LU2.md)，[LU3 天府](/point/LU3.md) 和[HE7 神门](/point/HE7.md)（《千金方》）。
