### Sanjiao Shu SĀNJIĀOSHU
![[BL-22 三焦俞.png|250]]
- Back-Shu point of the Sanjiao

#### Location

1.5 cun lateral to the lower border of the spinous process of the first lumbar vertebra (L1).

#### Location Notes

Locate at the visible highest point of the paraspinal muscles.

#### Needling

Oblique or perpendicular-oblique insertion towards the spine, 1 to 1.5 cun.  
Caution: deep perpendicular needling carries a risk of injuring the kidney.

#### Actions

- Moves and regulates the Sanjiao
- Regulates the Spleen and Stomach and resolves dampness
- Regulates the water passages and promotes urination
- Resolves masses

#### Indications

- Borborygmus, diarrhoea, undigested food (in the stool), dysenteric disorder, oesophageal constriction, vomiting, difficult ingestion.
- Abdominal distention with emaciation, abdominal (zheng ju) masses and emaciation in women, zangfu (ji ju) masses, large and hard hypogastric (ji ju) masses.
- Oedema, difficult urination, white turbidity, blood in the urine, deficiency-taxation.
- Headache, visual dizziness, alternating chills and fever, heat in the body, bitter taste in the mouth and cracked lips, jaundice.
- Stiffness and pain of the lumbar spine, inflexibility and stiffness of the shoulder and back.

#### Commentary

Sanjiaoshu BL-22 is the back-shu point of the Sanjiao, where the qi of the Sanjiao emanates from the interior to the body surface. The Sanjiao integrates and harmonises the upper, middle and lower jiao and dominates the free flow of qi and fluids through these three areas. Located between [Weishu BL-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-21) above and [Shenshu BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23) below, Sanjiaoshu BL-22 however has the action of regulating the qi of the middle and lower jiao only. It integrates the activities of the Stomach and Spleen in the middle jiao, and the Kidneys, Bladder and intestines in the lower jiao, and hence the transformation, transportation and excretion of both food and fluid.

The _Classic of Difficulties_[35](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references) states that “the Sanjiao is the pathway of water and grain”, the _Essential Questions_[36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references) says “the Sanjiao is the official in charge of drainage and controls the water passages” and the _Introduction to Medicine_ says “The middle jiao dominates transformation of the flavours of water and grain ... therefore it is said the middle jiao is like foam. The lower jiao dominates moving and draining the urine and faeces and at the right time discharging them ... therefore it is said the lower jiao is like a drain”.

In cases where there is loss of harmony between the Stomach and Spleen in the middle jiao and the intestines in the lower jiao, there are symptoms such as abdominal distention, borborygmus, diarrhoea or dysenteric disorder, undigested food (in the stool), difficult ingestion, oesophageal constriction and vomiting. In severe cases where there is obstruction and accumulation there may be abdominal masses or hypogastric lumps. Sanjiaoshu BL-22 is especially effective to activate and harmonise the qi of the middle and lower jiao, to resolve damp and to dispel stasis, and is one of the most important classically indicated points for such abdominal masses.

In cases where there is loss of harmony between the Spleen in the middle jiao and the Kidneys and Bladder in the lower jiao there may be oedema with difficult urination, blood in the urine and white turbidity due to deficiency and exhaustion. Sanjiaoshu BL-22 is able to treat such disorders by its ability to move the fluids and activate the qi transformation function of the Bladder.

Finally the Sanjiao channel belongs to shaoyang, and although not a point of the Sanjiao channel, Sanjiaoshu BL-22 is indicated for the treatment of alternating chills and fever, heat in the body, bitter taste in the mouth, headache and visual dizziness, all manifestations of shaoyang pattern.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Regulates the middle jiao: borborygmus, diarrhoea, undigested food (in the stool), dysenteric disorder, vomiting, difficult ingestion, abdominal distention with emaciation.
- Disperses masses: abdominal masses and emaciation in women, zangfu masses, large and hard hypogastric masses.
- Regulates urination: oedema, difficult urination, white turbidity, blood in the urine.

#### Combinations

- Borborygmus, abdominal distention and watery diarrhoea: Sānjiāoshu BL-22, [Xiǎochángshū BL-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-27), [Xiàliáo BL-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-34), [Yìshè BL-49](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-49) and [Zhāngmén LIV-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-13) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Borborygmus: Sānjiāoshu BL-22, [Dàchángshū BL-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-25), [Tàibái SP-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-3) and [Gōngsūn SP-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-4) (Supplementing Life).
- Undigested food (in the stool): Sānjiāoshu BL-22, [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36), [Dàchángshū BL-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-25), [Sānyīnjiāo SP-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-6), [Xiàwǎn REN-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-10), [Xuánshū DU-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-5) and [Liángmén ST-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-21) (Supplementing Life).
- Hypogastric (ji ju) masses that are hard and big like a plate, with epigastric distention and undigested food (in the stool): Sānjiāoshu BL-22 and [Zhōngwǎn REN-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-12) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[BL22 三焦俞](point/BL22.md)** (SĀNJIĀOSHŪ)

* **英文名称：** Sanjiao Shu
* **中文名称：** 三焦俞
* **国际代码：** BL-22
* **穴位属性：** **[BL22 三焦俞](point/BL22.md)** 是三焦的背俞穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第一腰椎（L1）棘突下缘旁开1.5寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 位于脊柱旁肌肉的最高点。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺或垂直斜刺，向脊柱方向1至1.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直深刺有损伤肾脏的风险。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 调理三焦气机
* 调理脾胃，化湿
* 通调水道，促进排尿
* 消散积聚

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

肠鸣，腹泻，大便完谷不化，痢疾，噎膈（吞咽困难），呕吐，吞咽困难。
腹胀伴消瘦，女性腹部癥瘕积聚伴消瘦，脏腑癥瘕积聚，下腹部硬大癥瘕积聚。
水肿，小便不利，白浊，尿血，虚劳。
头痛，视物眩晕，寒热往来，身热，口苦唇裂，黄疸。
腰脊僵硬疼痛，肩背活动不利。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL22 三焦俞](point/BL22.md)** 是三焦的背俞穴，三焦之气从内向体表输布。三焦整合和调和上、中、下三焦，主宰气和水液在这三个区域的自由流通。**[BL22 三焦俞](point/BL22.md)** 位于上方的**[BL21 胃俞](point/BL21.md)** 和下方的**[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)** 之间，但其作用仅限于调理中焦和下焦的气机。它整合了中焦脾胃以及下焦肾、膀胱和肠道的功能，从而影响水谷的运化、输布和排泄。

《难经》记载：“三焦者，水谷之道路也”，《素问·灵兰秘典论》记载：“三焦者，决渎之官，水道出焉”，《医学入门》记载：“中焦者，主水谷之变化……故曰中焦如沤。下焦者，主出水谷之道路……故曰下焦如渎”。

当脾胃在中焦与肠道在下焦之间失去协调时，会出现腹胀、肠鸣、腹泻或痢疾、大便完谷不化、吞咽困难、噎膈和呕吐等症状。在严重情况下，若有阻塞和积聚，则可能出现腹部肿块或下腹部结块。**[BL22 三焦俞](point/BL22.md)** 特别善于活化和调和中下焦气机，化湿并消散瘀滞，是治疗此类腹部肿块最重要的经典穴位之一。

当脾在中焦与肾、膀胱在下焦之间失去协调时，可能出现水肿伴小便不利、尿血和白浊，这通常是由于虚劳耗损所致。**[BL22 三焦俞](point/BL22.md)** 能够通过其运行水液和活化膀胱气化功能的能力来治疗这些疾病。

最后，三焦经属少阳，尽管**[BL22 三焦俞](point/BL22.md)** 并非三焦经上的穴位，但它主治寒热往来、身热、口苦唇裂、头痛和视物眩晕，这些都是少阳证的表现。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 调理中焦：肠鸣，腹泻，大便完谷不化，痢疾，呕吐，吞咽困难，腹胀伴消瘦。
* 消散积聚：女性腹部癥瘕积聚伴消瘦，脏腑癥瘕积聚，下腹部硬大癥瘕积聚。
* 调理小便：水肿，小便不利，白浊，尿血。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 肠鸣，腹胀，水泻：**[BL22 三焦俞](point/BL22.md)**，**[BL27 小肠俞](point/BL27.md)**，**[BL34 次髎](point/BL34.md)**，**[BL49 意舍](point/BL49.md)** 和 **[LIV13 章门](point/LIV13.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 肠鸣：**[BL22 三焦俞](point/BL22.md)**，**[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)**，**[SP3 太白](point/SP3.md)** 和 **[SP4 公孙](point/SP4.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 大便完谷不化：**[BL22 三焦俞](point/BL22.md)**，**[ST36 足三里](point/ST36.md)**，**[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)**，**[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md)**，**[REN10 下脘](point/REN10.md)**，**[DU5 悬枢](point/DU5.md)** 和 **[ST21 梁门](point/ST21.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 腹部硬大癥瘕积聚如盘，伴胃脘胀满，大便完谷不化：**[BL22 三焦俞](point/BL22.md)** 和 **[REN12 中脘](point/REN12.md)**（《千金方》）。
