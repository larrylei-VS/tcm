### Skull’s Rest Lúxí
![[Pasted image 20250314160209.png|250]]
#### Location 

Posterior to the ear, in a small depression two thirds of the distance along a curved line drawn from [Yìfēng SJ-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-17) to [Jiǎosūn SJ-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ/-20) following the line of the rim of the ear.

#### Needling 

Subcutaneous insertion along the course of the channel 0.3 to 0.5 cun, or prick to bleed.  
**Note:** according to several classical texts, this point is contraindicated to bleeding.

#### Actions 

- Benefits the ears and clears heat
- Calms fright and relieves tetany

#### Indications 

- Deafness, tinnitus, ear pain, discharge of pus from the ear, itching of the face, redness and swelling of the corner of the forehead (in the region of [Tóuwéi ST-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-8)).
- Headache, heavy head, one-sided headache, heat in the body with headache and inability to sleep, dizziness, childhood epilepsy, tetany, fright and fear, childhood vomiting of foamy (watery) saliva, vomiting and drooling, pain of the chest and lateral costal region, dyspnoea, seminal emission.

#### Commentary 

Although this point is rarely used clinically, the Ode to the One Hundred Symptoms stated unequivocally "tetany cannot be treated successfully without Lúxí SJ-19. Tetany is a disorder characterised by stiffness and rigidity of the neck and back, lockjaw, twitching of the limbs, opisthotonos etc.

#### Summary of clinical application 

- Rarely used.
- Deafness, tinnitus, ear pain, discharge of pus from the ear, itching of the face, redness and swelling of the corner of the forehead (in the region of [Tóuwéi ST-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-8)).
- Headache, heavy head, one-sided headache, heat in the body with headache and inability to sleep, dizziness, childhood epilepsy, tetany, fright and fear, childhood vomiting of foamy (watery) saliva, vomiting and drooling, pain of the chest and lateral costal region, dyspnoea, seminal emission.

#### Combinations 

- Pain of the lateral costal region with inability to turn the body: Lúxí SJ-19 and [Běnshén GB-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-13) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- One-sided headache: Lúxí SJ-19, [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20), [Tàiyáng M-HN-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/M-HN-9), [Wàiguān SJ-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-5) and [Jiānjǐng GB-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-21).

### **[SJ19 颅息](point/SJ19.md)** (LÚXĪ)

* **英文名称：** Skull’s Rest
* **中文名称：** 颅息
* **国际代码：** SJ-19

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于耳后，乳突上小凹陷中，沿耳轮边缘，从**[SJ17 翳风](point/SJ17.md)** 画一弧线至**[SJ20 角孙](point/SJ20.md)**，此线的三分之二处。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

沿经络皮下浅刺0.3至0.5寸，或点刺出血。

* **注意 (Note):** 根据几部经典文献，此穴禁忌放血。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利耳部，清热
* 镇惊，解痉

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

耳聋，耳鸣，耳痛，耳部流脓，面部瘙痒，额角红肿（**[ST8 头维](point/ST8.md)** 区域）。
头痛，头重，偏头痛，身热伴头痛不能入睡，眩晕，小儿癫痫，破伤风，惊恐，小儿呕吐泡沫（清水）唾液，呕吐流涎，胸胁疼痛，呼吸困难，遗精。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

尽管此穴临床上较少使用，但《百症赋》明确指出“破伤风非**[SJ19 颅息](point/SJ19.md)** 不可治”。破伤风是一种以颈背僵硬、牙关紧闭、四肢抽搐、角弓反张等为特征的疾病。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治耳聋，耳鸣，耳痛，耳部流脓，面部瘙痒，额角红肿（**[ST8 头维](point/ST8.md)** 区域）。
* 主治头痛，头重，偏头痛，身热伴头痛不能入睡，眩晕，小儿癫痫，破伤风，惊恐，小儿呕吐泡沫（清水）唾液，呕吐流涎，胸胁疼痛，呼吸困难，遗精。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 胁肋部疼痛不能转侧：**[SJ19 颅息](point/SJ19.md)** 和 **[GB13 本神](point/GB13.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 偏头痛：**[SJ19 颅息](point/SJ19.md)**，**[GB20 风池](point/GB20.md)**，太阳（经外穴 **[MHN9 太阳](point/MHN9.md)**），**[SJ5 外关](point/SJ5.md)** 和 **[GB21 肩井](point/GB21.md)**。
