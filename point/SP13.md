### Abode of the Fu FǓSHÈ
![[SP-13 府舍.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Spleen and Liver channels with the Yin Linking vessel

#### Location

On the lower abdomen, 0.7 cun superior and 0.5 cun lateral to [Chōngmén SP-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-12), 4 cun lateral to the midline. This point is also defined as lying 4.3 cun inferior to [Dàhéng SP-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-21).

#### Location Notes

The 4 cun line is determined by the palpable lateral border of the rectus abdominis muscle at the level of the umbilicus.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion, 1 to 1.5 cun.  
**Caution:** in thin patients deep needling may penetrate the peritoneal cavity.

#### Actions

- Regulates qi and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Abdominal fullness and pain, painful abdominal (ji ju) masses, shan disorder, constipation, pain of the lateral costal region, sudden turmoil disorder, pain of the thigh.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for abdominal fullness and pain, painful abdominal (ji ju) masses, shan disorder, constipation, pain of the lateral costal region, sudden turmoil disorder, pain of the thigh.

#### Combinations

- Abdominal fullness and abdominal (ji ju) masses: Fǔshè SP-13 and [Chōngmén SP-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-12) (Supplementing Life).

### **[SP13 府舍](point/SP13.md)** (FǓSHÈ)

* **英文名称：** Abode of the Fu
* **中文名称：** 府舍
* **国际代码：** SP-13
* **穴位属性：** **[SP13 府舍](point/SP13.md)** 是脾经与肝经、阴维脉的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于下腹部，**[SP12 冲门](point/SP12.md)** 上方0.7寸，旁开0.5寸，距前正中线旁开4寸。此穴也可定义为距 **[SP15 大横](point/SP15.md)** 下方4.3寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 旁开4寸线由脐水平的腹直肌可触及的外缘确定。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺，1至1.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 瘦弱患者深刺可能刺穿腹膜腔。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 理气止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

腹部胀满疼痛，腹部癥瘕积聚疼痛，疝气，便秘，胁肋部疼痛，霍乱吐泻，大腿疼痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SP13 府舍](point/SP13.md)** 位于腹部，脾经和肝经在此交会，并与阴维脉相通。其名称“府舍”意为腑的居所，暗示此穴在调理腹部脏腑功能、特别是气机方面的重要作用。

此穴主要用于治疗腹部气机郁滞、疼痛和积聚类疾病。它主治腹部胀满疼痛、癥瘕积聚疼痛，以及疝气和便秘。这些症状通常与气滞、血瘀或湿邪阻滞中下焦有关。**[SP13 府舍](point/SP13.md)** 通过理气止痛的功效，有助于缓解这些不适。此外，它也用于治疗胁肋部疼痛和大腿疼痛，这与脾经和肝经在这些区域的循行有关。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治腹部胀满疼痛，腹部癥瘕积聚疼痛，疝气，便秘，胁肋部疼痛，霍乱吐泻，大腿疼痛。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 腹部胀满和腹部癥瘕积聚：**[SP13 府舍](point/SP13.md)** 和 **[SP12 冲门](point/SP12.md)**（《资生经》）。
