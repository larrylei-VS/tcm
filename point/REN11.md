### Strengthen the Interior JIÀNLǏ
![[REN-11 建里.png|250]]
#### Location

On the midline of the abdomen, 3 cun above the umbilicus and 5 cun below the sternocostal angle.

#### Location Notes

To locate the sternocostal angle see [Surface Anatomy](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-location-needling/surface-anatomy "Surface Anatomy").

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.8 to 1.5 cun.  
**Caution:** in thin patients deep needling may penetrate the peritoneal cavity.

#### Actions

- Harmonises the middle jiao and regulates qi

#### Indications

- Abdominal distention, pain of the intestines, vomiting, no pleasure in eating, sudden turmoil disorder, Heart pain, oedema of the body.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for abdominal distention, pain of the intestines, vomiting, no pleasure in eating, sudden turmoil disorder, Heart pain, oedema of the body.

#### Combinations

- Oppression of the chest: Jiànlǐ REN-11 and [Nèiguān PC-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-6) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Oedema of the abdomen with drum-like distention: Jiànlǐ REN-11 (reduce) and [Shuǐfēn REN-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-9) (Secrets of the Celestial Star).


### 1. [REN11 建里](point/REN11.md)

#### 1.1. Strengthen the Interior 建里

#### 1.2. 定位

位于腹部中线，脐上3寸，胸肋角下5寸。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

定位胸肋角见表面解剖学。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直刺入，深0.8至1.5寸。  
**注意：** 瘦弱者深刺可能穿透腹膜腔。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 和中焦，调气  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 腹胀、肠痛、呕吐、食不甘味、卒中风病、心痛、全身水肿。  

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 使用较少。  
- 适用于腹胀、肠痛、呕吐、食不甘味、卒中风病、心痛、全身水肿。  

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 胸闷：[REN11 建里](point/REN11.md) + [PC6 内关](point/PC6.md)（《百症赋》）。  
- 腹部水肿胀如鼓：[REN11 建里](point/REN11.md)（泻）+ [REN9 水分](point/REN9.md)（《天星秘诀》）。
