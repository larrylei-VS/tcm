### Three Yin Intersection SĀNYĪNJIĀO
![[SP-6 三阴交.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Spleen, Liver and Kidney channels

#### Location

On the medial side of the lower leg, 3 cun superior to the prominence of the medial malleolus, in a depression close to the medial crest of the tibia.

#### Location Notes

This point is most readily located one handbreadth superior to the prominence of the medial malleolus.

#### Needling

Perpendicular or oblique proximal insertion, 1 to 1.5 cun.  
**Caution:** contraindicated in pregnancy.

#### Actions

- Tonifies the Spleen and Stomach
- Resolves dampness
- Harmonises the Liver and tonifies the Kidneys
- Regulates menstruation and induces labour
- Harmonises the lower jiao
- Regulates urination and benefits the genitals
- Calms the spirit
- Invigorates blood
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Spleen and Stomach deficiency, Spleen deficiency with heavy body, heavy body with heaviness of the four limbs, oedema, borborygmus, diarrhoea, undigested food (in the stool), abdominal distention, cold abdomen, unbearable pain below the umbilicus, pain of the Spleen, fullness and distention of the Heart and abdomen, no desire to eat and drink, vomiting of fluid after eating, sudden turmoil disorder.
- Irregular menstruation, uterine bleeding, uterine bleeding with dizziness, menorrhagia, amenorrhoea, dysmenorrhoea, abdominal (zheng jia) masses in women, leucorrhoea, uterine prolapse.
- Infertility, restless foetus syndrome, transverse presentation, delayed labour, prolonged or difficult labour, retention of lochia, retention of dead foetus, post-partum dizziness.
- Seminal emission, seminal emission with dreaming, sexual hyperactivity in men, impotence, pain of the genitals, pain of the penis, contracted testicles, shan disorder, pain due to shan disorder.
- Difficult urination, enuresis, the five types of painful urinary dysfunction, cloudy urine, white turbidity.
- Palpitations, insomnia, Gall Bladder deficiency, sudden fright disorder in children.
- Dizziness, blurred vision, tinnitus, yawning, hypertension.
- Leg pain, crane's knee, damp painful obstruction, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limbs, hemiplegia, heat in the soles of the feet, shin pain, eczema, urticaria, counterflow cold of the foot and hand.

#### Commentary

Sānyīnjiāo SP-6 (Three Yin Intersection) is the meeting point of the three yin channels of the leg (Spleen, Liver and Kidney) and is one of the most important and widely used of the acupuncture points. Its actions and indications are extraordinarily broad, and it is a primary point in the treatment of many digestive, gynaecological, sexual, urinary and emotional disorders.

Located on the Spleen channel, its strongest action is on harmonising all the functions of the Spleen. The Spleen dominates transportation and transformation and is thus the primary zang responsible for the formation of qi and blood. When Spleen qi or yang is deficient there may be: i. impairment of the transportation and transformation function (undigested food in the stool, diarrhoea, abdominal distention and fullness, borborygmus etc.), ii. inadequate formation of blood (palpitations, blurred vision, amenorrhoea, post-partum dizziness, dizziness associated with uterine bleeding etc.), iii. failure of the Spleen to hold the blood (uterine bleeding, menorrhagia), and iv. sinking of central qi (uterine prolapse). Furthermore, deficiency of Spleen qi and incomplete transportation and transformation may lead to the formation of excess dampness. According to the Essential Questions[11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-spleen-channel/references) "The lower is the first to suffer from dampness". Dampness, whether damp-heat or cold damp, may lead to heaviness of the body and limbs and oedema, or pour down to the lower jiao giving rise to such symptoms as diarrhoea or leucorrhoea. Sānyīnjiāo SP-6, exceptionally balanced in its action, is unique among points of the Spleen channel in being able to treat all these different manifestations of Spleen disharmony.

Although its primary action is on the Spleen, Sānyīnjiāo SP-6 is also an important point to treat disorders of the Liver and Kidneys. It is able to soften and harmonise the Liver, by both spreading the Liver qi and nourishing Liver blood, and at the same time to benefit the Kidney qi. This threefold action of harmonising the Spleen, Liver and Kidneys finds expression in the forte of Sānyīnjiāo SP-6 in treating all disorders of the lower jiao.

The three leg yin channels dominate menstruation, conception, pregnancy, leucorrhoea and the external genitalia. Sānyīnjiāo SP-6 is the single most important distal point in the treatment of any gynaecological, obstetrical or post-partum disorder whether characterised by deficiency of qi, blood, yin, yang or Kidney essence, failure of Spleen qi to hold the blood, or stagnation of qi, blood, dampness, damp-heat or phlegm. So wide is its range of action, and so universal its indications, that Sānyīnjiāo SP-6 appears in classical combinations for the treatment of virtually any disorder of the reproductive system. Sānyīnjiāo SP-6 is also an essential point to induce labour, assist in transverse presentation and alleviate the pain of childbirth. Despite its indication for restless foetus syndrome, however, its ability to expedite delivery of the live or dead foetus, and the lochia, means that Sānyīnjiāo SP-6 is generally contraindicated in pregnancy.

According to the Essential Questions[12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-spleen-channel/references) "The genitals are the gathering place of the sinews". The Spleen sinew channel binds at the genitals and Sānyīnjiāo SP-6 is an essential point in the treatment of sexual and genital disorders in both men and women, including pain and contraction, seminal emission, impotence, and excessive sexual drive in men. Although impotence is generally related to deficiency of Kidney fire, two classics help to explain its relationship to the Spleen. The Complete Works of Jing-yue says "Ming men [the fire of the gate of life stored in the Kidneys] is the sea of the essence and blood, the Spleen is the sea of water and grain; the two together form the foundation of the five zang and six fu", whilst the Essential Questions[13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-spleen-channel/references) explains "The Kidneys dominate water; they receive essence from the five zang and six fu and store it, therefore only when the five zang are flourishing is ejaculation possible".

Disharmony of the Liver, Spleen or Kidneys is responsible for the majority of urinary disorders, and the principal patterns are accumulation of damp-heat or cold damp, Kidney deficiency, qi deficiency, stagnation of Liver qi or Liver fire. Due to its ability to treat all these pathologies, Sānyīnjiāo SP-6 is an essential point for the treatment of urinary disorders, including difficult urination, retention of urine, enuresis, painful urinary dysfunction and cloudy urine.

The actions of Sānyīnjiāo SP-6 are not, however, confined to the middle and lower jiao. Its inclusion in many prescriptions for the treatment of any kind of insomnia (usually in combination with [Shénmén HE-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-7)) dramatically illustrates its wide-ranging actions. Insomnia is normally differentiated into five main patterns: i. deficiency of Heart and Spleen (qi and blood deficiency), ii. disharmony of Heart and Kidneys (yin deficiency), iii. disharmony of Heart and Liver (Liver blood and yin deficiency or Liver fire), iv. deficiency of Heart and Gall Bladder (qi deficiency), v. disharmony of Heart and Stomach (accumulation of food or retention of phlegm-heat). Since Sānyīnjiāo SP-6 is able to fortify the Spleen, tonify qi and blood, nourish Liver and Kidney yin, spread the Liver qi and harmonise the digestion it may be used in any of these patterns.

The especial importance of treating the Spleen in cases of disharmony of the Heart and Kidneys is emphasised in a number of classics. The Standards of Patterns and Treatments says "In order to tonify the Kidneys it is best to tonify the Spleen; use the Spleen to connect with the Heart in the upper and with the Kidneys in the lower ..." and "The essence of the five zang is all transported from the Spleen; when the Spleen is flourishing then the Heart and Kidneys are in communication". The Helpful Questions in Medical Cases says "If you desire to establish communication between the Heart and the Kidneys it is necessary to use the Spleen earth as an intermediary".

Along with [Géshū BL-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-17), [Xuèhǎi SP-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-10) and [Dìjī SP-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-8), Sānyīnjiāo SP-6 is considered one of the most important acupuncture points to harmonise and cool blood and to promote and invigorate its circulation. This action finds its most important application in the treatment of gynaecological disorders, but extends to other disorders where blood disharmony plays an important role, for example skin diseases such as eczema and urticaria.

Finally Sānyīnjiāo SP-6 is cited in the Song of the Song of the Nine Needles for Returning the Yang, for the treatment of collapse of yang characterised by loss of consciousness, aversion to cold, cold counterflow of the limbs, purple lips etc.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Tonifies the middle jiao and resolves dampness: heavy body, heaviness of the four limbs, oedema, borborygmus, diarrhoea, undigested food (in the stool), abdominal distention, cold abdomen, no desire to eat and drink, vomiting, damp painful obstruction, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limbs, eczema.
- Regulates the uterus and menstruation: irregular menstruation, uterine bleeding, menorrhagia, amenorrhoea, dysmenorrhoea, abdominal masses, leucorrhoea, uterine prolapse, infertility, restless foetus syndrome, transverse presentation, difficult labour, retention of lochia, post-partum dizziness.
- Regulates the sexual function and genitals: seminal emission, sexual hyperactivity in men, impotence, pain of the genitals, pain of the penis, contracted testicles, shan disorder.
- Regulates urination: difficult urination, enuresis, painful urinary dysfunction, cloudy urine.
- Calms the spirit: palpitations, insomnia, Gall Bladder deficiency.

### [SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) (SĀNYĪNJIĀO)

* **英文名称：** Three Yin Intersection
* **中文名称：** 三阴交
* **国际代码：** SP-6
* **穴位属性：** [SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 是足太阴脾经、足厥阴肝经、足少阴肾经三条阴经的交会穴。

#### 定位 (Location)

位于小腿内侧，内踝尖上方3寸，胫骨内侧缘近旁的凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 此穴最易定位在内踝尖上方一横掌处。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直或向近端斜刺1至1.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 孕妇禁针。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 健脾益胃
* 化湿利水
* 调和肝肾，益肾固精
* 调经催产
* 调和下焦
* 调理小便，益生殖器
* 宁心安神
* 活血
* 通经活络，止痛

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

脾胃虚弱，身体沉重伴脾虚，身体沉重伴四肢沉重，水肿，肠鸣，腹泻，大便完谷不化，腹胀，腹部冷痛，脐下剧烈疼痛，脾痛，心腹胀满，不思饮食，食后呕吐，霍乱吐泻。
月经不调，崩漏（子宫出血），崩漏伴眩晕，月经过多，闭经，痛经，女性腹部癥瘕积聚，带下，子宫脱垂。
不孕症，胎动不安，胎位不正，滞产，难产，恶露不尽，死胎不下，产后眩晕。
遗精，梦遗，男性性欲亢进，阳痿，生殖器疼痛，阴茎疼痛，睾丸挛缩，疝气，疝气疼痛。
小便不利，遗尿，五淋（小便涩痛），小便混浊，白浊。
心悸，失眠，胆虚，小儿急慢惊风。
眩晕，视物模糊，耳鸣，呵欠，高血压。
腿痛，鹤膝风，湿痹，下肢痿证和痹证，半身不遂，足底发热，胫部疼痛，湿疹，荨麻疹，手足逆冷。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) (Three Yin Intersection) 是足部三条阴经（脾经、肝经和肾经）的交会穴，是针灸穴位中最重要的、应用最广泛的穴位之一。其作用和主治范围极其广泛，是治疗许多消化、妇科、生殖、泌尿和情志疾病的主要穴位。

[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 位于脾经上，其最强的作用是调和脾脏的所有功能。脾脏主运化，因此是气血生化的主要脏腑。当脾气或脾阳不足时，可能出现：i. 运化功能失调（大便完谷不化、腹泻、腹胀满、肠鸣等）；ii. 气血生化不足（心悸、视物模糊、闭经、产后眩晕、伴子宫出血的眩晕等）；iii. 脾不摄血（崩漏、月经过多）；以及 iv. 中气下陷（子宫脱垂）。此外，脾气不足和运化不全可能导致湿邪过盛的形成。《素问·水热穴论》记载：“其在下者，先受其湿”。湿邪，无论是湿热还是寒湿，都可能导致身体和四肢沉重以及水肿，或下注下焦，引起腹泻或带下等症状。[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 作用平衡，在脾经穴位中独一无二，能够治疗脾脏失调的所有这些不同表现。

虽然[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 的主要作用在脾，但它也是治疗肝肾疾病的重要穴位。它能够柔肝，通过疏肝理气和滋养肝血，同时益肾气。这种调和脾、肝、肾的三重作用体现在[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 治疗所有下焦疾病的特长。

足部三条阴经主宰月经、受孕、妊娠、带下和外生殖器。[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 是治疗任何妇科、产科或产后疾病最重要的远端穴位，无论其特点是气、血、阴、阳或肾精不足，脾气不能摄血，或气滞、血瘀、湿、湿热或痰湿阻滞。其作用范围之广，主治之普遍，以至于[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 出现在几乎所有生殖系统疾病的经典组合中。[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 也是催产、纠正胎位不正和缓解分娩疼痛的关键穴位。然而，尽管它主治胎动不安，但其促进活胎或死胎及恶露排出的能力意味着[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 通常在妊娠期禁针。

根据《素问·骨空论》记载：“宗筋之所聚，生殖器也”。脾经筋结于生殖器，[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 是治疗男女生殖和性功能障碍的关键穴位，包括疼痛和挛缩、遗精、阳痿和男性性欲亢进。尽管阳痿通常与肾火不足有关，但有两部经典有助于解释其与脾脏的关系。《景岳全书》记载：“命门者，精血之海也，脾者，水谷之海也；二者并为五脏六腑之根本”，而《素问·经脉别论》解释“肾主水，受五脏六腑之精而藏之，故五脏盛乃能泻”。

肝、脾、肾的失调是大多数泌尿系统疾病的原因，主要证型是湿热或寒湿积聚，肾虚，气虚，肝气郁结或肝火。由于[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 能够治疗所有这些病理，它是治疗泌尿系统疾病的关键穴位，包括小便不利、尿潴留、遗尿、淋证和小便混浊。

然而，[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 的作用不仅限于中焦和下焦。它被纳入许多治疗各种失眠的处方中（通常与[HE7 神门](point/HE7.md) 联合使用），这生动地说明了其广泛的作用。失眠通常分为五种主要证型：i. 心脾两虚（气血不足），ii. 心肾不交（阴虚），iii. 心肝不和（肝血肝阴不足或肝火），iv. 心胆气虚，v. 胃不和（食积或痰热内停）。由于[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 能够健脾、补益气血、滋养肝肾阴、疏肝理气和调和消化，因此可用于任何这些证型。

在心肾不交的情况下，治疗脾脏的特殊重要性在多部经典中都有强调。《症治准绳》记载：“凡补肾者，以补脾为先；用脾者，以上通于心，下交于肾……”，“五脏之精，皆禀于脾，脾盛则心肾交通”。《医宗金鉴》记载：“欲使心肾交通，必赖脾土为中介”。

与[BL17 膈俞](point/BL17.md)、[SP10 血海](point/SP10.md) 和[SP8 地机](point/SP8.md) 一起，[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 被认为是调和凉血、促进和活血化瘀最重要的穴位之一。这一作用在妇科疾病的治疗中发挥最重要作用，但也扩展到血瘀在其中发挥重要作用的其他疾病，例如湿疹和荨麻疹等皮肤病。

最后，《回阳九针歌》中列举了[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) 用于治疗亡阳证，表现为昏厥、恶寒、四肢厥逆、口唇青紫等。

三阴交（SP6，Sanyinjiao）是针灸临床中最常用的穴位之一。它的名字直观地揭示了它的独特性：**足太阴脾经、足厥阴肝经、足少阴肾经**三条阴经在此交会。

如果说太溪是“补肾水”的专才，那么**三阴交就是“调节阴液与内分泌”的全才**。在现代科研中，三阴交的研究主要集中在**盆腔脏器调节（生殖/泌尿）、自主神经平衡以及内分泌轴的重塑**。

以下是三阴交的深度科学解析：

---

### 一、 神经解剖基础：骶丛神经的“外周入口”

三阴交位于内踝尖上3寸，胫骨内侧缘后方。

- **关键神经**：深层布有**胫神经（Tibial Nerve）**。
    
- **信号路径**：针刺三阴交的电信号沿胫神经上行，进入脊髓的**骶段（S2-S4）**。
    
- **为什么重要**：骶髓是全身**副交感神经**的核心中枢之一，主管盆腔内脏（子宫、膀胱、直肠）的神经支配。这解释了为什么三阴交能通过“神经节段反射”精准调节生殖和泌尿系统。
    

---

### 二、 滋阴的激素证据：调节“HPO/HPG轴”

针对你提到的“阴虚火旺”和内分泌紊乱，三阴交被证明是调节性激素的关键点。

1. 双向调节雌、孕激素：
    
    大量针对围绝经期综合征（更年期）和痛经的研究显示，针刺三阴交能调节雌二醇（E2）、**孕酮（P）**以及促性腺激素（FSH/LH）的分泌。
    
    - **滋阴体现**：对于阴虚（激素水平下降、阴道干涩、潮热）的患者，针刺能提升 E2 水平，通过激素的生理效应实现“滋润”和“降火”。
        
2. 调节催产素（Oxytocin）释放：
    
    三阴交在助产和缓解痛经方面有显著效果，其机理与促进大脑下丘脑释放催产素有关。催产素不仅收缩子宫，还具有极强的抗焦虑和抗应激作用，能中和皮质醇的毒性。
    

---

### 三、 平抑“肝阳”：对边缘系统的抑制作用

对于“肝阳上亢”导致的焦虑和烦躁，三阴交通过脑中枢发挥作用：

- 抑制杏仁核（Amygdala）：
    
    fMRI研究发现，针刺三阴交可以显著降低杏仁核（大脑的情绪恐惧中心）的活跃度。
    
- 提升 5-羟色胺 (5-HT)：
    
    它能提升脑干缝际核产生 5-HT 的能力， 5-HT 这种“阴性”递质的增多，可以直接抑制肝阳上亢引发的攻击性和兴奋感。

### 五、 给针灸学生的实操Tips（基于解剖与科研）

1. 进针深度与“得气”：
    
    研究发现，针对妇科或内分泌问题，三阴交需要深刺（1-1.5寸）。因为胫神经分支位于深层，只有产生明显的酸胀感并向足底传导时，才能最有效地激活骶髓反射。
    
2. **三阴交 vs. 太溪**：
    
    - 如果患者以**“肾水不足”**（腰酸、耳鸣、记忆力减退）为主，重用**太溪**。
        
    - 如果患者以**“血虚、情绪波动、内分泌失调”**（焦虑、月经问题、失眠）为主，重用**三阴交**。
        
3. **配穴逻辑**：
    
    - **三阴交 + 神门**：侧重于安神、滋阴、治疗焦虑。
        
    - **三阴交 + 足三里**：侧重于补血、化生气血、增强免疫。
        

### 总结

### 1. 神经解剖学基础：底层布线（Hardware Wiring）

三阴交之所以能调节盆腔脏器（生殖、泌尿系统），是因为其物理位置处于神经分布的交叉区域：

- **周围神经**：三阴交穴区分布有**胫神经**（Tibial nerve，来自 $L_4$ 至 $S_3$ 脊神经节）和**隐神经**（Saphenous nerve）。
    
- **节段性联系（Segmental Convergence）**：
    
    - 支配盆腔器官（如子宫、膀胱、前列腺）的副交感神经主要源自骶髓（$S_2$ - $S_4$）。
        
    - 三阴交的输入信号通过胫神经进入脊髓，在脊髓节段上与盆腔脏器的传入神经存在**“汇聚”**现象。
        
    - **逻辑类比**：这就像是两个不同的传感器挂在了同一条总线（脊髓节段）上，刺激三阴交（SP 6）产生的电信号可以“调制”同一网段内脏器的生物电状态。
        

### 2. 中枢神经调节：脑部协议（System Protocols）

通过功能性磁共振成像（fMRI）技术，科学家观察到针刺三阴交会触发大脑内特定的响应模式：

- **显著性网络（Salience Network）**：研究发现针刺三阴交能激活**前扣带回（ACC）**、**岛叶（Insula）**和**基底节区**。这些区域负责处理躯体感觉、情绪和自主神经调节。
    
- **默认模式网络（DMN）的调制**：对于失眠或长期焦虑的患者，三阴交能调节 DMN 的连接性，这解释了它在**改善睡眠质量**和**缓解经前综合征（PMS）**中的“重置”作用。
    

### 3. 神经-内分泌-免疫（NEI）网络：全局管理

三阴交被认为是一个调节**下丘脑-垂体-性腺轴（HPG Axis）**的强效开关：

- **内分泌调节**：现代临床研究显示，刺激三阴交能调节雌激素、孕激素以及前列腺素（$PGF_{2\alpha}$）的水平。这也是它能有效治疗原发性痛经（通过减少子宫平滑肌痉挛）的生化基础。
    
- **免疫抑制/激活**：在抗炎研究中，三阴交被发现能调节细胞因子（如 $IL-4$，$IL-6$）的释放，平衡 $Th1/Th2$ 细胞，从而缓解盆腔炎症。
