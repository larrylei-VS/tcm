### Support the Prominence FÚTÚ
![[LI-18 扶突.png|250]]
- Point of the Window of Heaven

#### Location

On the lateral side of the neck, level with the tip of the laryngeal prominence, between the sternal and clavicular heads of the sternocleidomastoid muscle.

#### Location Notes

i. Palpation of the sternal and clavicular heads is made easier if the patient turns their head away from the side to be needled, whilst you apply resistance at the chin; ii. In females the laryngeal prominence is not as pronounced as in males. If it is indistinct, palpate the depression formed by the lower border of the hyoid bone and the upper border of the thyroid cartilage at the midline. The laryngeal prominence lies just below this; iii. This point may be located approximately 3 cun (one handbreadth) lateral to the laryngeal prominence.

#### Needling

i. Perpendicular insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun; ii. Oblique insertion 0.5 to 0.8 cun.  
**Caution:** deeper needling may puncture the carotid artery or jugular vein.

#### Actions

- Benefits the throat and voice
- Alleviates cough and wheezing

#### Indications

- Swelling and pain of the throat, sudden loss of voice, rattling sound in the throat, goitre, scrofula, difficulty in swallowing.
- Cough, coughing with much spittle, wheezing, asthma, hypotension.

#### Commentary

In Chapter 21 of the Spiritual Pivot Fútú LI-18 is included in a list of five points known as 'Window of Heaven' (literally 'five regions of the Window of Heaven') points. This passage states "Sudden loss of voice with obstructed qi in the throat, choose Fútú LI-18 and bleed the root of the tongue". Fútú LI-18 shares certain important characteristics of this grouping in that it treats goitre and scrofula due to stagnation of qi and phlegm in the pivotal neck region, and sudden loss of voice, coughing and wheezing due to inversion qi. See '[Window of Heaven](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-categories/points-of-the-window-of-heaven)' for a fuller discussion of these points.

In clinical practice, Fútú LI-18 is frequently used as an alternative to (or in combination with) [Rényíng ST-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-9), in the treatment of difficulty in swallowing (especially following windstroke) and chronic disorders of the vocal cords.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Local point for disorders of the throat region: swelling and pain, sudden loss of voice, rattling sound, difficulty in swallowing.
- Window of Heaven point hence treats the throat region (scrofula and goitre), sudden onset of symptoms (sudden loss of voice) and rebellion of qi manifesting as coughing and wheezing.

#### Combinations

- Sudden loss of voice: Fútú LI-18, [Tiānchuāng SI-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-16), [Zhīgōu SJ-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-6), [Qūbìn GB-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-7) and [Língdào HE-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-4) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Bleeding from the root of the tongue: Fútú LI-18, [Dàzhōng KID-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-4) and [Tóuqiàoyīn GB-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-11) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Cough with rebellious qi, dyspnoea, vomiting of foam and clenched teeth: Fútú LI-18, [Tiānróng SI-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-17), [Liánquán REN-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-23), [Pòhù BL-42](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-42), [Qìshè ST-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-11) and [Yīxǐ BL-45](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-45) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### [LI18 扶突](point/LI18.md) (FÚTÚ)

- **英文名称：** Support the Prominence
- **中文名称：** 扶突
- **国际代码：** LI-18
- **穴位属性：** [LI18 扶突](point/LI18.md) 是天窗穴之一。

#### 定位 (Location)

位于颈部外侧，平喉结旁，在胸锁乳突肌胸骨头和锁骨头之间。

- **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    - 让患者头部转向对侧（与针刺侧相反），在下巴处施加阻力，可更容易触及胸锁乳突肌的胸骨头和锁骨头；
    - 女性喉结不像男性那样突出。如果不明显，可在正中线触诊舌骨下缘与甲状软骨上缘形成的凹陷，喉结就在此下方；
    - 此穴大致位于喉结旁开3寸（一横掌）处。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

i. 垂直刺0.3至0.5寸； ii. 斜刺0.5至0.8寸。

- **注意 (Caution):** 深刺可能刺破颈总动脉或颈内静脉。

#### 作用 (Actions)

- 宣利咽喉，改善声音
- 止咳平喘

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

咽喉肿痛，暴喑（突然失音），喉中痰鸣，瘿瘤，瘰疬，吞咽困难。

咳嗽，咳痰多，喘息，哮喘，低血压。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

在《灵枢·热病论》第二十一章中，[LI18 扶突](point/LI18.md) 被列入“五处天窗”的五个穴位之一。该段经文记载“暴喑气梗，取扶突，舌根出血。”[LI18 扶突](point/LI18.md) 与此组穴位具有一些重要的共同特征，即主治枢纽颈部气滞痰凝所致的瘿瘤和瘰疬，以及气上逆所致的暴喑、咳嗽和喘息。关于这些穴位的更详细讨论，请参阅“天窗穴”。

临床实践中，[LI18 扶突](point/LI18.md) 常作为[ST9 人迎](point/ST9.md) 的替代（或与其联合使用），治疗吞咽困难（特别是中风后）和声带慢性疾病。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

治疗咽喉部疾病的局部穴位：肿痛，暴喑，喉中痰鸣，吞咽困难。

天窗穴，故治疗咽喉部病症（瘰疬、瘿瘤），症状突然发作（暴喑），以及气上逆导致的咳嗽和喘息。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

言语中止，失音：[LI18 扶突](point/LI18.md) 和[SI16 天窗](point/SI16.md)，[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)，[GB7 曲鬓](point/GB7.md) 和[HE4 灵道](point/HE4.md)（《千金方》）。

舌根出血：[LI18 扶突](point/LI18.md)，[KID4 大钟](point/KID4.md) 和[GB11 头窍阴](point/GB11.md)（《千金方》）。

咳嗽气逆，呼吸困难，呕吐泡沫，牙关紧闭：[LI18 扶突](point/LI18.md)，[SI17 天容](point/SI17.md)，[REN23 廉泉](point/REN23.md)，[BL42 魄户](point/BL42.md)，[ST11 气舍](point/ST11.md) 和[BL45 噫嘻](point/BL45.md)（《千金方》）。
