### Reaching Yin ZHÌYĪN
![[BL-67 至阴.png|250]]
- Jing-Well and Metal point of the Bladder channel

#### Location

On the dorsal aspect of the little toe, at the junction of lines drawn along the lateral border of the nail and the base of the nail, approximately 0.1 cun from the corner of the nail.

#### Needling

Perpendicular or oblique insertion directed proximally 0.1 to 0.2 cun, or prick to bleed.

#### Actions

- Expels wind and clears the head and eyes
- Turns the foetus and facilitates labour

#### Indications

- Vertex headache, occipital headache, heaviness of the head, neck pain, nasal congestion, nosebleed, eye pain, pain of the inner canthus, superficial visual obstruction, deafness and tinnitus, pain of the lateral costal region and chest, agitation of the Heart.
- Retention of placenta, malposition of foetus, delayed labour, prolonged or difficult labour.
- Difficult urination, painful urinary dysfunction, seminal emission, cold malaria, wind cold starting from the little toe, absence of sweating, heat in the soles of the feet, cramps, clonic spasm, swelling of the knees.

#### Commentary

Zhìyīn BL-67 is the jing-well and metal point of the Bladder channel. Unlike most of the other eleven jing- well points, however, it has no apparent classically recorded action of restoring consciousness in case of coma or collapse and its similarity to the other jing-well points is limited to its ability to clear fullness and heat from the opposite end of the channel in the head.

The Spiritual Pivot[76](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references) states "for diseases of the head select [points from] the feet", whilst Song to Keep Up Your Sleeve says that Zhìyīn BL-67 is indicated for "disorders of the face and head". Zhìyīn BL-67 is particularly applicable for vertex and occipital headache, whether due to exterior pathogenic wind or to internal disharmony, as well as for disorders of the neck, eyes, nose and ears. As the terminal point of the Bladder channel, Zhìyīn BL-67 is especially dynamic in its action, and in clinical practice would be considered when these disorders are acute rather than chronic in nature.

The principal application of Zhìyīn BL-67, however, is in the treatment of malposition of the foetus, for which it is renowned. For this purpose it is treated by stick moxibustion for fifteen to twenty minutes bilaterally, or by moxa cones (five to ten cones at each point) once or twice a day. The woman should loosen her clothes and sit in a comfortable semi-reclining position. It is common practice in China to demonstrate this method to the pregnant woman who is then supplied with moxa sticks for self-treatment at home. Best results are achieved if this treatment is started in the 34th week. It is important to note that once the foetus has turned, moxibustion should be discontinued, otherwise further malposition may be induced. The effect of Zhìyīn BL-67 on the uterus extends further to inducing delayed labour, hastening delivery once labour has commenced and promoting the expulsion of the placenta after childbirth.

It is interesting to note that despite the intimate relationship between the Kidneys and the uterus, it is points of its coupled Bladder channel, especially Zhìyīn BL-67 and [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60), rather than points of the Kidney channel, which have the action of promoting labour. According to yin yang theory "yang is activity, yin is quiescence; yang brings forth and yin develops" and "When yin reaches its maximum it will necessarily transform into yang". The yin of the Kidney nourishes and dominates the development and growth of the foetus through the long months of pregnancy. As the birth date approaches and yin reaches its zenith, yang must begin to grow in order to turn the foetus and prepare for the intense activity of birth. If, towards the time of delivery, there is insufficient yang activity of the uterus, due either to deficiency or stagnation, then yang must be stimulated. Zhìyīn BL-67 is the terminal point of the yang Bladder channel, where the qi changes polarity and enters its coupled yin Kidney channel, reflected in its name 'Reaching Yin'. It is the most dynamic point to activate the uterus and hence turn the foetus and promote labour, especially when stimulated by the yang heat of moxibustion.

Finally Zhìyīn BL-67 is one of the few distal points on the Bladder channel indicated for disorders of urination, in this case painful urinary dysfunction and difficult urination. Clinically however, distal points of the Bladder channel are rarely used for this purpose.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Clears the head: as the terminal point of the Bladder channel Zhiyin BL-67 is indicated for acute headache at the vertex or occiput, and disorders of the neck (pain), eyes (eye pain, pain of the inner canthus, superficial visual obstruction), nose (nasal congestion, nosebleed) and ears (deafness and tinnitus).
- Turns the foetus and facilitates labour: for malposition of the foetus, difficult delivery and retention of the placenta.For malposition of the foetus, moxibustion with cones or stick is applied daily, preferably around the 34th week, until the foetus turns. It is important to note that once the foetus has turned, moxibustion should be discontinued, otherwise further malposition may be induced.

### **[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)** (Zhìyīn)

* **英文名称：** Reaching Yin
* **中文名称：** 至阴
* **国际代码：** BL-67
* **穴位属性：** **[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)** 是膀胱经的井穴和金穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于小趾背侧，在趾甲外侧缘与趾甲根部交界处，距趾甲角约0.1寸。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直或向近端斜刺0.1至0.2寸，或点刺出血。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 祛风，清头明目
* 转胎，催产

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

头顶痛，枕部头痛，头重，颈痛，鼻塞，鼻出血，眼痛，内眼角痛，外障眼病（翳膜），耳聋耳鸣，胁肋部和胸部疼痛，心烦。
胞衣不下，胎位不正，滞产，难产。
小便不利，淋证（小便涩痛），遗精，寒疟，小趾起始风寒，无汗，足底热感，痉挛，抽搐，膝关节肿胀。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)** 是膀胱经的井穴和金穴。然而，与大多数其他十一个井穴不同，它没有明确的经典记载用于昏迷或虚脱时开窍醒神的作用，其与井穴的相似性仅限于其清泻经络另一端（头部）胀满和热邪的能力。

《灵枢·病本》记载：“病在头者取之足”，而《袖珍赋》记载**[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)** 主治“面部和头部疾病”。**[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)** 特别适用于头顶和枕部头痛，无论是外感风邪还是内部失调所致，也适用于颈部、眼睛、鼻子和耳朵的疾病。作为膀胱经的末端穴位，**[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)** 作用尤其迅速，临床实践中当这些疾病是急性而非慢性时，会考虑使用此穴。

然而，**[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)** 的主要应用是治疗胎位不正，对此它闻名遐迩。为此，可双侧艾条灸15至20分钟，或艾炷灸（每穴5至10壮），每日一至两次。孕妇应放松衣物，采取舒适的半卧位。在中国，通常会向孕妇演示此方法，然后提供艾条供其在家自行治疗。在怀孕第34周开始治疗效果最佳。重要的是要注意，一旦胎位转正，应停止艾灸，否则可能引起进一步的胎位不正。**[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)** 对子宫的作用进一步延伸至催产，一旦分娩开始可加速分娩，并促进产后胞衣排出。

有趣的是，尽管肾脏与子宫关系密切，但却是其相表里的膀胱经穴位，特别是**[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)** 和**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)**，而非肾经穴位，具有催产作用。根据阴阳理论：“阳主动，阴主静；阳主生发，阴主生长”，以及“阴极则阳生”。肾阴滋养和主宰胎儿在漫长孕期的发育和生长。随着预产期临近，阴气达到极致，阳气必须开始生长以转正胎位，并为分娩的剧烈活动做准备。如果在临产前，子宫阳气活动不足，无论是虚证或气滞，则必须刺激阳气。**[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)** 是阳经膀胱经的末端穴位，在此处气机改变极性，进入其相表里的阴经肾经，这体现在其名称“至阴”中。它是活化子宫、从而转正胎位和催产最具活力的穴位，特别是通过艾灸的阳热刺激时。

最后，**[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)** 是膀胱经上少数主治泌尿系统疾病的远端穴位之一，此处指淋证（小便涩痛）和小便不利。然而，临床上膀胱经的远端穴位很少用于此目的。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 清头：作为膀胱经的末端穴位，**[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)** 主治急性头顶痛或枕部头痛，以及颈部（疼痛）、眼睛（眼痛，内眼角痛，外障眼病）、鼻子（鼻塞，鼻出血）和耳朵（耳聋耳鸣）的疾病。
* 转胎，催产：用于胎位不正，难产和胞衣不下。对于胎位不正，可每日艾炷灸或艾条灸双侧，最好在怀孕第34周左右开始，直至胎位转正。重要的是要注意，一旦胎位转正，应停止艾灸，否则可能引起进一步的胎位不正。
