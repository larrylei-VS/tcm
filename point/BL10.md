### Celestial Pillar TIĀNZHÙ
![[BL-10 天柱.png|250]]
- Point of the Window of Heaven

#### Location

On the lateral aspect of the trapezius muscle, 1.3 cun lateral to [Yǎmén DU-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-15).

#### Location Notes

First locate [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16) directly below the occipital protuberance in a depression between the trapezius on both sides. Then locate [Yǎmén DU-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-15) which is 0.5 cun below [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16) in the depression 0.5 cun within the hairline. Now locate Tiānzhù BL-10, 1.3 cun lateral to [Yǎmén DU-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-15).

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 0.8 cun.

#### Actions

- Regulates qi and pacifies wind
- Benefits the head and sensory orifices
- Calms the spirit
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Dizziness, inability of the legs to support the body, sudden muscular contractions, pain of the body.
- Pain and heaviness of the head, headache, head wind, stiffness of the neck with inability to turn the head, pain of the shoulder and back, bursting eye pain, redness of the eyes, blurred vision, lacrimation, swelling of the throat with difficulty in speaking, nasal congestion, loss of sense of smell, febrile disease with absence of sweating.
- Mania, incessant talking, seeing ghosts, epilepsy, childhood epilepsy, upward staring eyes.

#### Commentary

Tiānzhù BL-10 is one of five points referred to in Chapter 21 of the Spiritual Pivot as points of the 'Window of Heaven' (see [Point Categories](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-categories/points-of-the-window-of-heaven "Point Categories") for a fuller discussion), and in this passage it is said "Sudden contraction, epilepsy and dizziness, with inability of the legs to support the body, select Tiānzhù BL-10". Chapter 24 of the Spiritual Pivot says "inversion headache with neck pain followed by lumbar pain select Tiānzhù BL-10"

In common with the other Window of Heaven points, Tiānzhù BL-10 is indicated when inversion qi (chaotic and rebellious qi) rises to the head. This may give rise to dizziness, headache, heaviness of the head, stiff neck and epilepsy, as well as congestion, pain and swelling of the throat, eyes and nose, whilst at the same time in the lower part the legs are unable to support the body due to unbalanced distribution of qi.

The use of Tiānzhù BL-10 for disorders such as inability of the legs to support the body, pain of the body, and headache accompanied by lumbar pain is a reflection of the principle of selecting points above to treat disorders below.

Like [Tiānfǔ LU-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-3) and [Tiānchuāng SI-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-16) (also points of the Window of Heaven), Tiānzhù BL-10 is further indicated for mental disorders characterised by mania and incessant talking, as well as for disorders characterised in the classical texts as seeing or 'communicating with ghosts' and therefore attributed to some form of demonic possession. In terms of modern medicine these indications refer to various forms of severe mental disorder including schizophrenia.

Finally there is some similarity between the indications of Tiānzhù BL-10 and [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20), both points being located close to each other. [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20) however is much used clinically both to expel exterior pathogenic wind and to pacify interior wind, whilst the clinical use of Tiānzhù BL-10 is primarily confined to pacifying interior wind.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Window of Heaven point for qi rebelling to the head: dizziness, headache, heavy head, stiff neck, pain and swelling of the throat, eyes and nose, inability of the legs to support the body.
- For mania, incessant talking, ‘seeing ghosts’ and epilepsy.

#### Combinations

- Headache: Tiānzhù BL-10, [Táodào DU-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-13), [Dàzhù BL-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-11), [Kǒngzuì LU-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-6) and [Hòuxī SI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-3) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Head wind: Tiānzhù BL-10, [Nǎokōng GB-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-19) and [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) (Supplementing Life).
- Dizziness: Tiānzhù BL-10, [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23) and [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20) (Glorious Anthology).
- Visual dizziness, dimness of vision with bursting eye pain: Tiānzhù BL-10, [Táodào DU-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-13) and [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60) (Supplementing Life).
- Blurring of vision: Tiānzhù BL-10 and [Yǎnglǎo SI-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-6) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Stiff neck with great aversion to wind: Tiānzhù BL-10 and [Shùgǔ BL-65](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-65) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Inability of the legs to support the body: Tiānzhù BL-10 and [Xíngjiān LIV-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-2) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Childhood fright epilepsy: Tiānzhù BL-10, [Běnshén GB-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-13), [Qiándǐng DU-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-21) and [Xìnhuì DU-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-22) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Pain of the shoulder as if it were broken: Tiānzhù BL-10 and [Yǎnglǎo SI-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-6) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)** (TIĀNZHÙ)

* **英文名称：** Celestial Pillar
* **中文名称：** 天柱
* **国际代码：** BL-10
* **穴位属性：** **[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)** 是天窗穴之一。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于斜方肌外侧缘，距**[DU15 哑门](/point/DU15.md)** 旁开1.3寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 首先定位枕外隆突正下方，两侧斜方肌之间凹陷中的**[DU16 风府](/point/DU16.md)**。
    * 然后定位**[DU15 哑门](/point/DU15.md)**，它在**[DU16 风府](/point/DU16.md)** 下方0.5寸，发际内0.5寸的凹陷中。
    * 现在，**[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)** 位于**[DU15 哑门](/point/DU15.md)** 旁开1.3寸处。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至0.8寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 调气，息风
* 宣利头目七窍
* 宁心安神
* 通经活络，止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

眩晕，两腿不能支撑身体，肌肉突然抽搐，身体疼痛。
头重疼痛，头痛，头风，颈项强直不能转头，肩背疼痛，眼痛如裂，眼睛红赤，视物模糊，流泪，咽喉肿胀伴言语困难，鼻塞，嗅觉丧失，热病无汗。
癫狂，言语不止，见鬼，癫痫，小儿癫痫，眼睛上视。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)** 是《灵枢·本输》第二十一章中提到的“天窗穴”之一（更详细的讨论请参阅“穴位分类”），该篇记载：“卒挛，癫疾，眩仆，足不任地，取**[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)**。”《灵枢·刺节真邪》第二十四章记载：“逆头痛，项痛而转，腰痛，取**[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)**。”

与其他天窗穴一样，当逆乱之气上冲头部时，**[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)** 可用于治疗。这可能引起眩晕、头痛、头重、颈项强直和癫痫，同时下肢因气机分布不均而不能支撑身体。

**[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)** 治疗两腿不能支撑身体、身体疼痛以及伴有腰痛的头痛等疾病，是“取上治下”原则的体现。

像**[LU3 天府](/point/LU3.md)** 和**[SI16 天窗](/point/SI16.md)**（也属天窗穴）一样，**[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)** 还主治以癫狂和言语不止为特征的精神疾病，以及经典文献中描述为“见鬼”或“与鬼交流”并归因于某种魔邪附体的疾病。在现代医学中，这些主治指代各种形式的严重精神疾病，包括精神分裂症。

最后，**[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)** 和**[GB20 风池](/point/GB20.md)** 在主治上有一些相似之处，这两个穴位彼此靠近。然而，**[GB20 风池](/point/GB20.md)** 临床上主要用于驱散外感风邪和平息内风，而**[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)** 的临床应用主要限于平息内风。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 天窗穴，用于治疗气逆上冲头部的病症：眩晕，头痛，头重，颈项强直，咽喉、眼睛和鼻子疼痛肿胀，两腿不能支撑身体。
* 治疗癫狂，言语不止，“见鬼”和癫痫。

* 眩晕：**[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)**，**[DU23 上星](/point/DU23.md)** 和**[GB20 风池](/point/GB20.md)**（《针灸大成》）。
* 视物眩晕，视力模糊伴眼痛如裂：**[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)**，**[DU13 陶道](/point/DU13.md)** 和**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)**（《资生经》）。

* 小儿惊痫：**[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)**，**[GB13 本神](/point/GB13.md)**，**[DU21 前顶](/point/DU21.md)** 和**[DU22 囟会](/point/DU22.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 肩痛如折：**[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)** 和**[SI6 养老](/point/SI6.md)**（《千金方》）。
