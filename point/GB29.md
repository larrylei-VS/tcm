### Stationary Crevice JŪLIÁO
![[GB-29 居髎.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder channel with the Yang Motility vessel

#### Location

On the lateral aspect of the hip joint, at the midpoint of a line drawn between the anterior superior iliac spine and the prominence of the greater trochanter.

#### Location Notes

i. To locate the anterior superior iliac spine see [Wǔshū GB-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-27); ii. To accurately palpate the prominence of the greater trochanter place one hand over the lateral aspect of the hip joint at the level of the perineum, and with the other hand rotate the foot in order to feel the movement of the prominence of the greater trochanter; iii. Alternatively, flex the hip joint and locate Jūliáo GB-29 at the lateral end of the crease thus formed.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 2 cun, or oblique insertion inferiorly 2 to 3 cun.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Benefits the hip joint

#### Indications

- Pain of the back and leg, lumbar pain radiating to the lower abdomen, hip pain radiating to the groin, weakness and paralysis of the leg, sciatica, shan disorder, pain of the front of the shoulder radiating to the chest, inability to raise the arm.
- Oedema, vomiting, diarrhoea.

#### Commentary

Jūliáo GB-29 is very commonly used in clinical practice in the treatment of hip disorders, and is especially indicated when pain radiates from the hip to the groin and lower abdomen. Correct needling will ensure radiation of sensation to these areas.

In the cross-needling method of point selection, the shoulder joint in the upper body corresponds to the hip joint in the lower body, and Jūliáo GB-29 is not only indicated for hip pain radiating forward to the groin, but also for shoulder pain radiating forward to the chest.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Important local point for disorders of the hip joint, especially when pain radiates from the hip to the groin and lower abdomen.
- Also for pain of the back and leg, lumbar pain radiating to the lower abdomen, weakness and paralysis of the leg, sciatica, shan disorder, pain of the front of the shoulder radiating to the chest, inability to raise the arm.

#### Combinations

- Wind-damp leg pain: Jūliáo GB-29, [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30) and [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) (Song of the Jade Dragon).

### 1. [GB29 居髎](/point/GB29.md)

#### 1.1. Stationary Crevice 居髎

- 胆经与阳跷脉的交会穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于髋关节外侧面，髂前上棘与股骨大转子突出点连线的中点。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

i. 定位髂前上棘参见[GB27 五枢](/point/GB27.md)；  
ii. 为准确探查股骨大转子突出点，将一只手置于髋关节外侧，会阴水平处，另一只手旋转足部以感受大转子突起的移动；  
iii. 或者，屈髋关节，[GB29 居髎](/point/GB29.md)位于由此形成的皱褶外侧端。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直刺入，深1至2寸，或向下斜刺，深2至3寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 活络止痛
- 益髋关节

#### 1.6. 主治

- 背腿痛、腰痛放射至下腹、髋痛放射至腹股沟、腿软瘫痪、坐骨神经痛、疝病、肩前痛放射至胸、不能举臂。
- 水肿、呕吐、腹泻。

#### 1.7. 论述

[GB29 居髎](/point/GB29.md)在临床实践中常用于治疗髋关节疾患，特别适用于髋痛放射至腹股沟和下腹的情况。正确的针刺可确保针感放射至这些区域。

在交叉取穴法中，上半身的肩关节对应下半身的髋关节，[GB29 居髎](/point/GB29.md)不仅适用于髋痛向前放射至腹股沟，也适用于肩前痛向前放射至胸部。

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 治疗髋关节疾患的重要局部穴位，尤其是髋痛放射至腹股沟和下腹时。
- 也适用于背腿痛、腰痛放射至下腹、腿软瘫痪、坐骨神经痛、疝病、肩前痛放射至胸、不能举臂。

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 风湿腿痛：[GB29 居髎](/point/GB29.md) + [GB30 环跳](/point/GB30.md) + [BL40 委中](/point/BL40.md)（《玉龙歌》）。
