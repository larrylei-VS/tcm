### Mouth Grain Crevice KǑUHÉLIÁO
![[LI-19 口禾髎.png|250]]
#### Location

Below the lateral margin of the nostril, 0.5 cun lateral to [Rénzhōng DU-26](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-26).

#### Location Notes

[Rénzhōng DU-26](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-26) is located above the upper lip on the midline, at the junction of the upper third and lower two thirds of the philtrum.

#### Needling

Oblique insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.  
**Note:** according to some classical and modern texts, this point is contraindicated to moxibustion.

#### Actions

- Eliminates wind and opens the nasal passages

#### Indications

- Nasal congestion and discharge, loss of sense of smell, nasal sores, nasal polyps, rhinitis with nosebleed.
- Lockjaw, deviation of the mouth, loss of consciousness.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for nasal congestion and discharge, loss of sense of smell, nasal sores, nasal polyps, rhinitis with nosebleed, lockjaw, deviation of the mouth, loss of consciousness.

#### Combinations

- Nosebleed: Kǒuhéliáo LI-19 and [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23) (Miscellaneous Diseases).
- Incessant nosebleed: Kǒuhéliáo LI-19, [Duìduān DU-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-27) and [Láogōng PC-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-8) (Supplementing Life).
- Nasal congestion with inability to distinguish the fragrant from the foul: Kǒuhéliáo LI-19, [Yíngxiāng LI-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-20), [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23) and [Wǔchù BL-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-5) (Great Compendium).


### [LI19 口禾髎](/point/LI19.md) (KǑUHÉLIÁO)

- **英文名称：** Mouth Grain Crevice
- **中文名称：** 口禾髎
- **国际代码：** LI-19

#### 定位 (Location)

位于鼻孔外缘下方，距[DU26 人中](/point/DU26.md) 旁开0.5寸。

- **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    - [DU26 人中](/point/DU26.md) 位于上唇人中沟中线上，人中沟上1/3与下2/3交界处。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

斜刺0.3至0.5寸。

- **注意 (Note):** 根据一些经典和现代文献，此穴禁灸。

#### 作用 (Actions)

- 祛风，通利鼻窍

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

鼻塞流涕，嗅觉丧失，鼻疮，鼻息肉，鼻炎伴鼻出血。

牙关紧闭，口歪，昏厥。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[LI19 口禾髎](/point/LI19.md) 临床上较少使用。主治鼻塞流涕，嗅觉丧失，鼻疮，鼻息肉，鼻炎伴鼻出血，牙关紧闭，口歪，昏厥。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

临床上较少使用。

主治鼻塞流涕，嗅觉丧失，鼻疮，鼻息肉，鼻炎伴鼻出血，牙关紧闭，口歪，昏厥。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

鼻出血：[LI19 口禾髎](/point/LI19.md) 和[DU23 上星](/point/DU23.md)（《杂病穴法歌》）。

鼻出血不止：[LI19 口禾髎](/point/LI19.md)，[DU27 兑端](/point/DU27.md) 和[PC8 劳宫](/point/PC8.md)（《资生经》）。

鼻塞不辨香臭：[LI19 口禾髎](/point/LI19.md)，[LI20 迎香](/point/LI20.md)，[DU23 上星](/point/DU23.md) 和[BL5 五处](/point/BL5.md)（《针灸大成》）。
