### Lower Crevice XIÀLIÁO
![[BL-34 下髎.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Bladder, and Gall Bladder channels

#### Location

Over the fourth posterior sacral foramen.

#### Location Notes

For guidance on locating the sacral foramen see [Surface Anatomy](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-location-and-needling/surface-anatomy "Surface Anatomy").

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun, or 1.5 to 2 cun through the foramen.

#### Actions

- Regulates the lower jiao and facilitates urination and defecation
- Regulates menstruation and stops leucorrhoea
- Benefits the lumbar region and legs

#### Indications

- Lower abdominal pain, borborygmus, diarrhoea, difficult defecation, constipation, blood in the stool, difficult urination, painful urinary dysfunction, shan disorder.
- Dysmenorrhoea, itching, swelling and pain of the genitals, incessant green leucorrhoea leading to vaginal pain and pain radiating to the lower abdomen.
- Pain of the sacrum and coccyx, lumbar pain, lumbar pain radiating to the testicles.

#### Commentary

See [Cìliáo BL-32](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-32).

#### Summary of clinical application

- Lower abdominal pain, borborygmus, diarrhoea, difficult defecation, constipation, blood in the stool, difficult urination, painful urinary dysfunction, shan disorder.
- Dysmenorrhoea, itching, swelling and pain of the genitals, incessant green leucorrhoea leading to vaginal pain and pain radiating to the lower abdomen.
- Pain of the sacrum and coccyx, lumbar pain, lumbar pain radiating to the testicles.

#### Combinations

- Borborygmus, abdominal distention and watery diarrhoea: Xiàliáo BL-34, [Sānjiāoshu BL-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-22), [Xiǎochángshū BL-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-27), [Yìshè BL-49](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-49) and [Zhāngmén LIV-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-13) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[BL34 下髎](/point/BL34.md)** (XIÀLIÁO)

* **英文名称：** Lower Crevice
* **中文名称：** 下髎
* **国际代码：** BL-34
* **穴位属性：** **[BL34 下髎](/point/BL34.md)** 是膀胱经与胆经的交会穴。

####   **定位 (Location)**

位于第四骶后孔处。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 骶孔的定位请参考“Surface Anatomy”（此处引用原文，请根据实际参考资料定位）。

####   **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至1寸，或通过骶孔透刺1.5至2寸。

####   **作用 (Actions)**

* 调理下焦，通利二便
* 调经止带
* 宣利腰腿

####   **主治病症 (Indications)**

小腹疼痛，肠鸣，腹泻，排便困难，便秘，便血，小便不利，淋证（小便涩痛），疝气。
痛经，生殖器瘙痒、肿胀、疼痛，绿色带下不止导致阴道疼痛并放射至小腹。
骶骨和尾骨疼痛，腰痛，腰痛放射至睾丸。

####   **阐释 (Commentary)**

请参阅**[BL32 次髎](/point/BL32.md)**。

####   **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 主治小腹疼痛，肠鸣，腹泻，排便困难，便秘，便血，小便不利，淋证（小便涩痛），疝气。
* 主治痛经，生殖器瘙痒、肿胀、疼痛，绿色带下不止导致阴道疼痛并放射至小腹。
* 主治骶骨和尾骨疼痛，腰痛，腰痛放射至睾丸。

####   **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 肠鸣，腹胀，水泻：**[BL34 下髎](/point/BL34.md)**，**[BL22 三焦俞](/point/BL22.md)**，**[BL27 小肠俞](/point/BL27.md)**，**[BL49 意舍](/point/BL49.md)** 和 **[LIV13 章门](/point/LIV13.md)**（《千金方》）。
