### Ear Gate ĚRMÉN
![[SJ-21 耳门.png|250]]
#### Location

In the depression anterior to the supratragic notch and slightly superior to the condyloid process of the mandible.

#### Location Notes

In order to locate this point, ask the patient to open the mouth so that the condyloid process of the mandible slides forwards to reveal the depression. This point is needled with the mouth open. Following needling the mouth can be closed.

#### Needling

Inferior oblique insertion, slightly posteriorly, 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Note:** many classical sources prohibit moxibustion at this point in cases of discharge of pus from the ear.

#### Actions

- Benefits the ears
- Clears heat

#### Indications

- Tinnitus, deafness, impaired hearing, earache, discharge of pus from the ear, swelling of the ear, ear sores.
- Toothache, tooth decay, swelling and pain of the submandibular region, stiffness of the lips, headache, lockjaw, neck pain.

#### Commentary

Ěrmén SJ-21 is the uppermost of three points anterior to the ear, the others being [Tīnggōng SI-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-19) and [Tīnghuì GB-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-2). All are frequently employed for the treatment of a wide variety of ear disorders including tinnitus, deafness, pain, itching and discharge. Due to the close proximity of these points and the similar indications for each, it is difficult to distinguish between them clinically. If it is necessary to needle points around the ear regularly, then these three points should be alternated.

Like [Jiǎosūn SJ-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-20), Ěrmén SJ-21 is also indicated for toothache, tooth decay and stiffness of the lips.

Finally it is interesting to note that according to the arrangement of the Sanjiao channel points in the Great Compendium of Acupuncture and Moxibustion Ěrmén SJ-21 is the last point of the channel.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Important point in the treatment of a wide variety of ear disorders, including tinnitus, deafness, impaired hearing, swelling of the ear, ear sores, ear pain or itching, discharge from the ear.
- Also indicated for toothache, tooth decay and stiffness of the lips.

#### Combinations

- Purulent ear sores with discharge: Ěrmén SJ-21, [Yìfēng SJ-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-17) and [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) (Great Compendium).
- Impaired hearing and deafness: Ěrmén SJ-21, [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB/108), [Xiáxī GB-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-43), [Tīnghuì GB-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-2) and Tinggong SI‑19 (Great Compendium).
- Tinnitus with lumbar pain: first needle [Rényíng ST-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-9), then needle Ěrmén SJ-21 and [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) (Secrets of the Celestial Star).
- Tinnitus: Ěrmén SJ-21, [Tīnggōng SI-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-19), Tinghui GB‑2, [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20), [Luòquè BL-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-8), [Yángxī LI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-5), Qiangu SI‑2, [Hòuxī SI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-3), [Wàngǔ SI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-4), [Zhōngzhǔ SJ-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-3), [Yèmén SJ-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-2), [Shāngyáng LI-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-1) and [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23) (Great Compendium).
- Stiffness of the lips and pain from tooth decay of the upper jaw: Ěrmén SJ-21, [Duìduān DU-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-27), [Mùchuāng GB-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-16) and [Zhèngyíng GB-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-17) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Toothache: Ěrmén SJ-21 and [Sīzhúkōng SJ-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-23) (One Hundred Symptoms).


### **[SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)** (Ěrmén)

* **英文名称：** Ear Gate
* **中文名称：** 耳门
* **国际代码：** SJ-21

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于耳屏上切迹前方，下颌骨髁状突稍上方凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 为了定位此穴，请患者张口，使下颌骨髁状突向前滑动，以显露出凹陷。此穴在患者张口时针刺。针刺后患者可闭口。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

向下斜刺，稍向后方，0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Note):** 许多经典文献禁止在耳部流脓的病例中对此穴施灸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利耳部
* 清热

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

耳鸣，耳聋，听力障碍，耳痛，耳部流脓，耳部肿胀，耳疮。
牙痛，龋齿，颌下区肿痛，口唇僵硬，头痛，牙关紧闭，颈痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)** 位于耳屏前方三个穴位中的最上方，其他两个是**[SI19 听宫](/point/SI19.md)** 和**[GB2 听会](/point/GB2.md)**。所有这些穴位都常用于治疗各种耳部疾病，包括耳鸣、耳聋、疼痛、瘙痒和流脓。由于这些穴位距离很近，且主治相似，临床上很难区分。如果需要定期针刺耳周穴位，这三个穴位应交替使用。

像**[SJ20 角孙](/point/SJ20.md)** 一样，**[SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)** 也主治牙痛、龋齿和口唇僵硬。

最后值得注意的是，根据《针灸大成》对三焦经穴位的排列，**[SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)** 是该经脉的最后一个穴位。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗各种耳部疾病的重要穴位，包括耳鸣、耳聋、听力障碍、耳部肿胀、耳疮、耳痛或瘙痒、耳部流脓。
* 也主治牙痛、龋齿和口唇僵硬。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 耳部化脓疮疡伴流脓：**[SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)**，**[SJ17 翳风](/point/SJ17.md)** 和 **[LI4 合谷](/point/LI4.md)**（《针灸大成》）。
* 听力障碍和耳聋：**[SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)**，**[GB20 风池](/point/GB20.md)**，**[GB43 侠溪](/point/GB43.md)**，**[GB2 听会](/point/GB2.md)** 和 **[SI19 听宫](/point/SI19.md)**（《针灸大成》）。
* 腰痛伴耳鸣：先针 **[ST9 人迎](/point/ST9.md)**，后针 **[SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)** 和 **[ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md)**（《天星秘诀》）。
* 耳鸣：**[SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)**，**[SI19 听宫](/point/SI19.md)**，**[GB2 听会](/point/GB2.md)**，**[DU20 百会](/point/DU20.md)**，**[BL8 络却](/point/BL8.md)**，**[LI5 阳溪](/point/LI5.md)**，**[SI2 前谷](/point/SI2.md)**，**[SI3 后溪](/point/SI3.md)**，**[SI4 腕骨](/point/SI4.md)**，**[SJ3 中渚](/point/SJ3.md)**，**[SJ2 液门](/point/SJ2.md)**，**[LI1 商阳](/point/LI1.md)** 和 **[BL23 肾俞](/point/BL23.md)**（《针灸大成》）。
* 口唇僵硬和上颌龋齿痛：**[SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)**，**[DU27 兑端](/point/DU27.md)**，**[GB16 目窗](/point/GB16.md)** 和 **[GB17 正营](/point/GB17.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 牙痛：**[SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)** 和 **[SJ23 丝竹空](/point/SJ23.md)**（《百症赋》）。
