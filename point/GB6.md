### Suspended Hair XUÁNLÍ
![[GB-6 悬厘.png|250]]

- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder, Stomach, Sanjiao and Large Intestine channels

#### Location

In the temporal region, within the hairline, three quarters of the distance between [Tóuwéi ST-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-8) and [Qūbìn GB-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-7).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5-1.5 cun. See note [Hànyàn GB-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-4).

#### Actions

- Expels wind and clears heat
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- One-sided headache, one-sided headache extending to the outer canthus, pain of the outer canthus, sneezing, tinnitus, swelling and redness of the skin of the face.
- Febrile disease with absence of sweating, agitation of the Heart with no desire to eat, heat in the middle jiao.

#### Summary of clinical application

- One-sided headache, one-sided headache extending to the outer canthus, pain of the outer canthus, sneezing, tinnitus, swelling and redness of the skin of the face.
- Febrile disease with absence of sweating, agitation of the Heart with no desire to eat, heat in the middle jiao.

#### Combinations

- Fieberhafte Erkrankung mit einseitigem Kopfschmerz: Gb 6 Xuanli und Ren 15 Jiuwei (_Thousand Ducat Formulas_).

### 1. [GB6 悬厘](/point/GB6.md)

#### 1.1. Suspended Hair 懸厘

- 胆经、胃经、三焦经和大肠经的交会穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于颞区，发际内，在[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md)与[GB7 曲鬓](/point/GB7.md)之间四分之三处。

#### 1.3. 针刺方法

横刺，深0.5至1.5寸。参见[GB4 颔厌](/point/GB4.md)的针刺说明。

#### 1.4. 作用

- 祛风清热
- 活络止痛

#### 1.5. 主治

- 偏头痛、偏头痛延伸至外眦、外眦痛、喷嚏、耳鸣、面部皮肤肿红。
- 热病无汗、心烦不欲食、中焦热。

#### 1.6. 临床应用总结

- 适用于偏头痛、偏头痛延伸至外眦、外眦痛、喷嚏、耳鸣、面部皮肤肿红。
- 适用于热病无汗、心烦不欲食、中焦热。

#### 1.7. 配伍

- 热病伴偏头痛：[GB6 悬厘](/point/GB6.md) + [REN15 鸠尾](/point/REN15.md)（《千金方》）。
