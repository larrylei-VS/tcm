### Root of the Spirit BĚNSHÉN
![[GB-13 本神.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder channel with the Yang Linking vessel

#### Location

On the forehead, 0.5 cun within the anterior hairline, two thirds of the distance between [Shéntíng DU-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-24) and [Tóuwéi ST-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-8).

#### Location Notes

Some sources locate this point on the line directly above the outer canthus of the eye.

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5-1.5 cun. See note [Hànyàn GB-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-4).

#### Actions

- Eliminates wind, resolves phlegm and treats epilepsy

#### Indications

- Headache, visual dizziness, stiffness and pain of the neck, pain of the chest and lateral costal region with inability to turn the body.
- Epilepsy, childhood fright epilepsy, vomiting of foamy saliva, windstroke, hemiplegia, deviation of the mouth and eyes.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for headache, visual dizziness, stiffness and pain of the neck, pain of the chest and lateral costal region with inability to turn the body, epilepsy, childhood fright epilepsy, vomiting of foamy saliva, windstroke, hemiplegia, deviation of the mouth and eyes.

#### Combinations

- Madness: Běnshén GB-13 and [Shēnzhù DU-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-12) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Childhood fright epilepsy: Běnshén GB-13, [Qiándǐng DU-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-21), [Xìnhuì DU-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-22) and [Tiānzhù BL-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-10) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Vomiting of foam: Běnshén GB-13, [Shàohǎi HE-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-3) and [Duìduān DU-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-27) (Supplementing Life).
- Epilepsy with vomiting of foam: Běnshén GB-13 and [Duìduān DU-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-27) (Supplementing Life).
- Pain of the lateral costal region with inability to turn the body: Běnshén GB-13 and [Lúxí SJ-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-19) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### 1. [GB13 本神](/point/GB13.md)

#### 1.1. Root of the Spirit 本神

- 胆经与阳维脉的交会穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于前额，前发际内0.5寸，在[DU24 神庭](/point/DU24.md)与[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md)之间三分之二处。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

一些资料将此穴定位于外眦正上方线上。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

横刺，深0.5至1.5寸。参见[GB4 颔厌](/point/GB4.md)的针刺说明。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 祛风化痰，治疗癫痫

#### 1.6. 主治

- 头痛、视觉眩晕、颈项僵痛、胸胁痛伴不能转体。
- 癫痫、小儿惊痫、吐泡沫涎、中风、偏瘫、口眼歪斜。

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 使用较少。
- 适用于头痛、视觉眩晕、颈项僵痛、胸胁痛伴不能转体、癫痫、小儿惊痫、吐泡沫涎、中风、偏瘫、口眼歪斜。

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 癫狂：[GB13 本神](/point/GB13.md) + [DU12 身柱](/point/DU12.md)（《百症赋》）。
- 小儿惊痫：[GB13 本神](/point/GB13.md) + [DU21 前顶](/point/DU21.md) + [DU22 囟会](/point/DU22.md) + [BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)（《千金方》）。
- 吐泡沫：[GB13 本神](/point/GB13.md) + [HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md) + [DU27 兑端](/point/DU27.md)（《补命》）。
- 癫痫伴吐泡沫：[GB13 本神](/point/GB13.md) + [DU27 兑端](/point/DU27.md)（《补命》）。
- 胁痛伴不能转体：[GB13 本神](/point/GB13.md) + [SJ19 颅息](/point/SJ19.md)（《千金方》）。
