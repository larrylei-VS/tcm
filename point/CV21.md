### Jade Pivot XUÁNJĪ
![[CV-21 璇玑.png|250]]
#### Location

On the midline of the manubrium of the sternum, midway between [Huágài CV-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/CV-20) and [Tiāntú CV-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/CV-22).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion directed inferiorly, 0.5 to 1 cun.

#### Actions

- Descends Stomach qi and dispels food accumulation
- Unbinds the chest and descends Lung qi
- Benefits the throat

#### Indications

- Accumulation in the Stomach, difficult ingestion.
- Pain and fullness of the chest and lateral costal region, cough, rebellious qi, wheezing, dyspnoea with inability to speak.
- Rattling sound in the throat, throat painful obstruction.

#### Commentary

The combination of Xuánjī CV-21 and [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) has long been considered pre-eminent for treating accumulation of food in the Stomach. Whilst Xuánjī CV-21 shares with many points of the Conception vessel the ability to regulate the Stomach, it is interesting to note the statement in the Ode to Elucidate Mysteries, "Heaven, earth and man are the three powers. [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) ... echoes heaven, Xuánjī CV-21 ... echoes man and [Yǒngquǎn KID-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-1) ... echoes the earth". When this threefold division is applied to the body, heaven corresponds to the upper jiao which absorbs the heavenly qi, earth corresponds to the lower jiao and especially the Kidneys, whilst 'man', lying in-between heaven and earth, corresponds to the middle jiao and hence the Stomach.

The strong descending action of this point extends also to the Lung and it is indicated for rebellion of Lung qi giving rise to cough, wheezing and dyspnoea.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Traditionally indicated for accumulation of food in the Stomach, especially when combined with Zusanli ST-36.
- Also pain and fullness of the chest and lateral costal region, cough, rebellious qi, wheezing, dyspnoea with inability to speak, rattling sound in the throat.

#### Combinations

- Internal injury by accumulation of food in the Stomach: Xuánjī CV-21 and [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) (Miscellaneous Diseases).
- Accumulation in the Stomach: Xuánjī CV-21 and [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) (Ode of Xi-hong).
- Obstruction of food in the Stomach: Xuánjī CV-21 and [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) (Heavenly Star Points).
- Distressed, rapid dyspnoea: Xuánjī CV-21 and [Qìhǎi CV-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/CV-6) (Ode of the Jade Dragon).
- Fullness of the chest with stiffness of the neck: Xuánjī CV-21 and [Shéncáng KID-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-25) (One Hundred Symptoms).


### 1. [CV21 璇玑](/point/CV21.md)

#### 1.1. Jade Pivot 璇璣

#### 1.2. 定位

位于胸骨柄中线，[CV20 华盖](/point/CV20.md)与[CV22 天突](/point/CV22.md)之间中点。

#### 1.3. 针刺方法

向下平刺，深0.5至1寸。

#### 1.4. 作用

- 降胃气，消食积  
- 宽胸，降肺气  
- 利咽喉  

#### 1.5. 主治

- 胃积、吞咽难。  
- 胸胁痛满、咳嗽、气逆、喘鸣、气促不能言。  
- 喉中鸣响、喉痹。  

#### 1.6. 论述

[CV21 璇玑](/point/CV21.md)与[ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md)的组合长期被视为治疗胃食积的首选。[CV21 璇玑](/point/CV21.md)与许多任脉穴一样能调胃，《阐秘颂》称：“天地人三才，[DU20 百会](/point/DU20.md)应天，[CV21 璇玑](/point/CV21.md)应人，[KID1 涌泉](/point/KID1.md)应地。”以此三分法，上焦应天吸天之气，下焦应地尤指肾，中焦居天地间应人，故对应胃。  

此穴强效降气亦延至肺，适用于肺气上逆引起的咳嗽、喘鸣及呼吸困难。  

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 传统用于胃食积，尤与[ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md)配用。  
- 亦治胸胁痛满、咳嗽、气逆、喘鸣、气促不能言、喉中鸣响。  

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 胃食积内伤：[CV21 璇玑](/point/CV21.md) + [ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md)（《杂病》）。  
- 胃积：[CV21 璇玑](/point/CV21.md) + [ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md)（《西弘颂》）。  
- 胃食阻滞：[CV21 璇玑](/point/CV21.md) + [ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md)（《天星穴》）。  
- 急促呼吸困难：[CV21 璇玑](/point/CV21.md) + [CV6 气海](/point/CV6.md)（《玉龙颂》）。  
- 胸满伴颈僵：[CV21 璇玑](/point/CV21.md) + [KID25 神藏](/point/KID25.md)（《百症赋》）。
