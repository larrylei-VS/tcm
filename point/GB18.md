### Support Spirit CHÉNGLÍNG
![[GB-18 承灵.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder channel with the Yang Linking vessel

#### Location

In the parietal region, on a curved line drawn between [Tóulínqì GB-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-15) and [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20), following the contour of the cranium, 1.5 cun posterior to [Zhèngyíng GB-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-17).

#### Location Notes

Locate directly lateral to [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20), on the line of the Gall Bladder channel (2.25 cun from the midline).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5-1.5 cun. See note [Hànyàn GB-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-4).

#### Actions

- Benefits the head and alleviates pain
- Benefits the nose and descends Lung qi

#### Indications

- Headache, brain wind, dizziness, eye pain.
- Rhinitis and nosebleed, nasal congestion, dyspnoea, cough, aversion to wind and cold.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for headache, brain wind, dizziness, eye pain, rhinitis and nosebleed, nasal congestion, dyspnoea, cough, aversion to wind and cold.

#### Combinations

- Nosebleed with stifled breathing: Chénglíng GB-18, [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20), [Fēngmén BL-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-12), [Yīxǐ BL-45](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-45) and [Hòuxī SI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-3) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### 1. [GB18 承灵](point/GB18.md)

#### 1.1. Support Spirit 承靈

- 胆经与阳维脉的交会穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于顶区，沿从[GB15 头临泣](point/GB15.md)到[GB20 风池](point/GB20.md)沿颅骨轮廓画出的弧线上，在[GB17 正营](point/GB17.md)后方1.5寸处。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

定位于[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)正侧方，在胆经线上（距中线2.25寸）。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

横刺，深0.5至1.5寸。参见[GB4 颔厌](point/GB4.md)的针刺说明。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 益头止痛
- 益鼻降肺气

#### 1.6. 主治

- 头痛、脑风、眩晕、目痛。
- 鼻炎及鼻衄、鼻塞、呼吸困难、咳嗽、恶风寒。

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 使用较少。
- 适用于头痛、脑风、眩晕、目痛、鼻炎及鼻衄、鼻塞、呼吸困难、咳嗽、恶风寒。

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 鼻衄伴呼吸憋闷：[GB18 承灵](point/GB18.md) + [GB20 风池](point/GB20.md) + [BL12 风门](point/BL12.md) + [BL45 夷希](point/BL45.md) + [SI3 后溪](point/SI3.md)（《千金方》）。
