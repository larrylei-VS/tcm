### Lines Opening TIÁOKǑU
![[ST-38 条口.png|250]]
#### Location

On the lower leg, midway between the tibio-femoral joint line (level with the popliteal crease) and the prominence of the lateral malleolus, one finger-breadth lateral to the anterior crest of the tibia.

#### Needling

Perpendicular or oblique insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Expels wind-damp and alleviates pain
- Benefits the shoulder

#### Indications

- Atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limb, damp painful obstruction, numbness, coldness, swelling and pain of the shin, inability to stand for long, swelling of the thigh and knee, heat in the soles of the feet.
- Pain and stiffness of the shoulder, abdominal pain.

#### Commentary

Tiáokǒu ST-38 is either used as a local point in the treatment of a variety of disorders of the leg, or more commonly as an important empirical point in the treatment of shoulder disorders. For this latter use, Tiáokǒu ST-38 is normally needled on the affected side, with the patient sitting. After qi is obtained, the patient is asked to move the shoulder around the area of pain whilst the needle is manipulated at Tiáokǒu ST-38. In most cases, pain will be relieved and mobility increased immediately. This procedure is normally followed by needling of appropriate local and distal points. Although the ability of this point to treat shoulder disorders with great effectiveness may be partially explained by the close link between the Stomach and Large Intestine channels, this is first and foremost an example of the empirical use of acupuncture points.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Principally used as a distal point for the treatment of shoulder disorders: needle the affected side whilst the patient rotates the shoulder joint around the area of pain.
- Also for atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limb, damp painful obstruction, numbness, coldness, swelling and pain of the shin, inability to stand for long, swelling of the thigh and knee, heat in the soles of the feet.

#### Combinations

- Heat in the sole of the foot with inability to stand for long: Tiáokǒu ST-38, [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36), [Chéngshān BL-57](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-57) and [Chéngjīn BL-56](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-56) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Flaccidity of the legs with difficulty in walking: first needle [Xuánzhōng GB-39](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-39) then needle Tiáokǒu ST-38 and [Chōngyáng ST-42](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-42) (Secrets of the Heavenly Star).
- Shoulder pain: Tiáokǒu ST-38 and local points.

### [ST38 条口](point/ST38.md) (TIÁOKǑU)

* **英文名称：** Lines Opening
* **中文名称：** 条口
* **国际代码：** ST-38

#### 定位 (Location)

位于小腿部，胫股关节线（与腘横纹平齐）与外踝尖之间的中点，胫骨前嵴旁开一横指。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直或斜刺1至1.5寸。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 祛风湿，止痛
* 宣利肩部

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

下肢痿证和痹证，湿痹，胫部麻木、发冷、肿痛，不能久站，大腿和膝关节肿胀，足底发热。
肩部疼痛僵硬，腹痛。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST38 条口](point/ST38.md) 既可作为治疗多种腿部疾病的局部穴位使用，更常作为治疗肩部疾病的重要经验穴。对于后一种用法，[ST38 条口](point/ST38.md) 通常针刺患侧，患者取坐位。得气后，患者在疼痛区域活动肩部，同时在[ST38 条口](point/ST38.md) 处行针。大多数情况下，疼痛会立即缓解，活动度增加。此操作通常随后针刺相应的局部穴位和远端穴位。尽管此穴治疗肩部疾病的显著疗效部分可由胃经与大肠经的密切联系来解释，但这首先是针灸经验应用的一个例子。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 主要作为远端穴位治疗肩部疾病：针刺患侧，同时患者围绕疼痛区域旋转肩关节。
* 也主治下肢痿证和痹证，湿痹，胫部麻木、发冷、肿胀疼痛，不能久站，大腿和膝关节肿胀，足底发热。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 足底发热不能久站：[ST38 条口](point/ST38.md)，[ST36 足三里](point/ST36.md)，[BL57 承山](point/BL57.md) 和[BL56 承筋](point/BL56.md)（《千金方》）。
* 腿部痿弱，行走困难：先针[GB39 悬钟](point/GB39.md)，后针[ST38 条口](point/ST38.md) 和[ST42 冲阳](point/ST42.md)（《天星秘诀》）。
* 肩痛：[ST38 条口](point/ST38.md) 和局部穴位。
