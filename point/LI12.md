### Elbow Crevice ZHǑULIÁO
![[LI-12 肘髎.png|250]]
#### Location

When the elbow is flexed, this point is located in the depression 1 cun proximal to and 1 cun lateral to [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11).

#### Location Notes

When the elbow is flexed, this point may be found directly above the lateral epicondyle of the humerus, just anterior to the lateral supracondylar ridge.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Benefits the elbow joint

#### Indications

- Wind-taxation with somnolence, contraction, numb-ness and immobility of the upper arm, pain and stiffness of the elbow.

#### Commentary

Zhǒuliáo LI-12 is primarily used as a local point for disorders of the elbow (for example in the treatment of tennis elbow when pain radiates upwards towards the shoulder).

#### Summary of clinical application

- Primarily used as a local point for disorders of the elbow (for example in the treatment of tennis elbow when pain radiates upwards towards the shoulder).
- Indicated for wind-taxation with somnolence, contraction, numbness and immobility of the upper arm, pain and stiffness of the elbow.

#### Combinations

- Swelling and redness of the arm with elbow pain: Zhǒuliáo LI-12, [Jiānyú LI-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-15) and [Wàngǔ SI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-4) (Great Compendium).
- Pain and inability to raise the arm: Zhǒuliáo LI-12 and [Bìnào LI-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-14) (Preserving Life).


### [LI12 肘髎](/point/LI12.md) (ZHǑULIÁO)

- **英文名称：** Elbow Crevice
- **中文名称：** 肘髎
- **国际代码：** LI-12

#### 定位 (Location)

屈肘时，此穴位于[LI11 曲池](/point/LI11.md) 上方1寸，外侧1寸的凹陷中。

- **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    - 屈肘时，此穴可能位于肱骨外上髁正上方，肱骨外上髁嵴的前方。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直刺0.5至1寸。

#### 作用 (Actions)

- 通经活络，止痛
- 利肘关节

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

风劳伴嗜睡，上臂拘急、麻木、活动不利，肘部疼痛僵硬。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[LI12 肘髎](/point/LI12.md) 主要作为治疗肘部疾病的局部穴位使用（例如在治疗网球肘时，当疼痛向上臂放射时）。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

主要作为治疗肘部疾病的局部穴位使用（例如在治疗网球肘时，当疼痛向上臂放射时）。

主治风劳伴嗜睡，上臂拘急、麻木、活动不利，肘部疼痛僵硬。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

手臂红肿伴肘痛：[LI12 肘髎](/point/LI12.md)，[LI15 肩髃](/point/LI15.md) 和[SI4 腕骨](/point/SI4.md)（《针灸大成》）。

手臂疼痛无力抬起：[LI12 肘髎](/point/LI12.md) 和[LI14 臂臑](/point/LI14.md)（《保命集》）。
