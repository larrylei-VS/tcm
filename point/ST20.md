### Supporting Fullness CHÉNGMǍN
![[ST-20 承满.png|250]]
#### Location

On the abdomen, 2 cun lateral to the midline and 5 cun superior to the umbilicus, level with [Shàngwǎn REN-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-13).

#### Location Notes

i. The 2 cun line is located halfway between the midline and the palpable lateral border of the rectus abdominis muscle; ii. In some patients with a narrow subcostal angle, this point may fall on the costal margin. The options then are to a. locate more medially, b. needle transversely on the costal margin, c. select a different point.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** i. in thin subjects, deep needling may penetrate the peritoneal cavity; ii. deep needling at right Chéngmǎn ST-20 may penetrate an enlarged liver.

#### Actions

- Harmonises the middle jiao
- Descends rebellion of the Lung and Stomach

#### Indications

- Epigastric pain, vomiting, vomiting blood, spitting blood, hiccup, difficult ingestion, poor appetite, abdominal distention, borborygmus, diarrhoea, hardness and pain of the lateral costal region.
- Shortness of breath, wheezing, difficult breathing with raised shoulders.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for epigastric pain, vomiting, vomiting blood, spitting blood, hiccup, difficult ingestion, poor appetite, abdominal distention, borborygmus, diarrhoea, hardness and pain of the lateral costal region, shortness of breath, wheezing, difficult breathing with raised shoulders.

#### Combinations

- Difficult ingestion: Chéngmǎn ST-20, [Zhōngfǔ LU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-1), [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60), [Yújì LU-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-10) and [Zhōuróng SP-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-20) (Supplementing Life).
- Hardness and pain of the lateral costal region: Chéngmǎn ST-20 and [Zhōngwǎn REN-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-12) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### [ST20 承满](point/ST20.md) (CHÉNGMǍN)

* **英文名称：** Supporting Fullness
* **中文名称：** 承满
* **国际代码：** ST-20

#### 定位 (Location)

位于腹部，距前正中线旁开2寸，脐上5寸，与[REN13 上脘](point/REN13.md) 平齐。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 旁开2寸线位于前正中线与可触及的腹直肌外缘之间中点；
    * 对于一些剑突下角较窄的患者，此穴可能落在肋缘上。此时可选择：a. 向内侧定位； b. 在肋缘处横刺； c. 选择其他穴位。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):**
    * 瘦弱患者深刺可能刺穿腹膜腔；
    * 右侧[ST20 承满](point/ST20.md) 深刺可能刺穿肿大的肝脏。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 调和中焦
* 肃降肺胃之逆气

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

胃脘痛，呕吐，呕血，咯血，呃逆，吞咽困难，食欲不振，腹胀，肠鸣，腹泻，胁肋部硬满疼痛。
气短，喘息，耸肩呼吸困难。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST20 承满](point/ST20.md) 临床上较少使用。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治胃脘痛，呕吐，呕血，咯血，呃逆，吞咽困难，食欲不振，腹胀，肠鸣，腹泻，胁肋部硬满疼痛，气短，喘息，耸肩呼吸困难。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 吞咽困难：[ST20 承满](point/ST20.md)，[LU1 中府](point/LU1.md)，[BL60 昆仑](point/BL60.md)，[LU10 鱼际](point/LU10.md) 和[SP20 周荣](point/SP20.md)（《资生经》）。
* 胁肋部硬满疼痛：[ST20 承满](point/ST20.md) 和[REN12 中脘](point/REN12.md)（《千金方》）。
