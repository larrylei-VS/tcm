### Branch of the Upright ZHĪZHÈNG
![[SI-7 支正.png|250]]
- Luo-Connecting point of the Small Intestine channel

#### Location

On a line connecting [Yánggǔ SI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-5) and [Xiǎohǎi SI-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-8), 5 cun proximal to [Yánggǔ SI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-5), in the groove between the anterior border of the ulna and the muscle belly of flexor carpi ulnaris.

#### Location Notes

This point should be located and needled with the patient lying on their back and with their arm either in the supine position or resting on the chest.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.

#### Actions

- Clears heat and releases the exterior
- Calms the spirit
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Benefits the finger joints

#### Indications

- Febrile disease, cold shivering and fever, fever with neck and lumbar pain and desire to drink.
- Mania-depression, fear and fright, sadness and anxiety, restless zang disorder.
- Headache, dizziness, visual dizziness, blurred vision, superficial visual obstruction.
- Feebleness of the four limbs, the five taxations, stiffness of the neck, contraction of the elbow, slackness of the joints and inability to move the elbow, severe pain of all the fingers, inability to grip firmly, inability to make a fist, warts.

#### Commentary

Zhīzhèng SI-7 has three main actions. Firstly, since the Small Intestine belongs to taiyang channel (the most exterior of the six channels and thus the first to be attacked by exterior pathogenic factors) Zhīzhèng SI-7 in common with many points of the Small Intestine channel is able to release the exterior. It is primarily used in cases of exterior heat pathogen giving rise to febrile disease, and is especially indicated when the onset is characterised by neck and lumbar pain.

Secondly, Zhīzhèng SI-7 is the luo-connecting point of the Small Intestine channel from where the Small Intestine luo-connecting channel spreads to join with the Heart channel. This is reflected in the name of this point 'Branch of the Upright', the 'upright' being the Heart channel. The Guide to the Classics of Acupuncture states "the luo-connecting points are located between two channels ... if they are punctured, symptoms of the interiorly-exteriorly related channels can be treated"[5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-small-intestine-channel/references). Zhīzhèng SI-7 has a pronounced effect on regulating and calming the spirit and is indicated for mania-depression, fear and fright, and sadness and anxiety. The Methods of Acupuncture and Moxibustion from the Golden Mirror of Medicine more specifically recommends Zhīzhèng SI-7 for depression and knotting of all the seven emotions.

Thirdly Zhīzhèng SI-7 is able to regulate the Small Intestine channel in the region of the head (headache and dizziness), eyes (blurred vision, superficial visual obstruction, visual dizziness), elbow (contraction of the elbow), and especially the fingers (severe pain of all the fingers, inability to grip firmly, inability to make a fist). The principle of selecting distal points to treat disorders of the limbs, trunk and head is fundamental to acupuncture practice, and since the twelve primary channels all flow to or from the hands and feet, there is a profusion of distal points to choose from. In the treatment of disorders of the hands and feet themselves, however, there are by definition no distal points, and there are relatively few proximal points that affect these areas. Zhīzhèng SI-7 is therefore notable for its action on the fingers and hand.

Finally the Great Compendium of Acupuncture and Moxibustion gives specific indications for excess and deficiency of the luo-connecting points. In the case of Zhīzhèng SI-7, these are slackness of the joints and inability to move the elbow (excess); warts (deficiency).

#### Summary of clinical application

- Principally used to treat the Heart and spirit (luo-connecting point joining with the Heart channel): mania-depression, fear and fright, sadness and anxiety, restless zang disorder.
- Dispels fever: febrile disease, cold shivering and fever, fever with neck and lumbar pain and desire to drink.
- Treats the head and eyes: headache, dizziness, visual dizziness, blurred vision, superficial visual obstruction.
- Proximal point for the hand and fingers: severe pain of all the fingers, inability to grip firmly, inability to make a fist.

#### Combinations

- Manic raving, fear and fright: Zhīzhèng SI-7, [Yújì LU-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-10), [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4), [Shàohǎi HE-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-3), [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11) and [Wàngǔ SI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-4) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Visual dizziness and headache: Zhīzhèng SI-7 and [Sānjiāoshu BL-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-22) (Supplementing Life).
- Visual dizziness: Zhīzhèng SI-7 and [Fēiyáng BL-58](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-58) (One Hundred Symptoms).

### **[SI7 支正](/point/SI7.md)** (ZHĪZHÈNG)

* **英文名称：** Branch of the Upright
* **中文名称：** 支正
* **国际代码：** SI-7
* **穴位属性：** **[SI7 支正](/point/SI7.md)** 是小肠经的络穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于连接 **[SI5 阳谷](/point/SI5.md)** 与 **[SI8 小海](/point/SI8.md)** 的连线上，距 **[SI5 阳谷](/point/SI5.md)** 上方5寸，在尺骨前缘与尺侧腕屈肌肌腹之间的沟中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 患者仰卧，手臂仰卧位或放在胸部时定位和针刺此穴。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至1寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 清热解表
* 宁心安神
* 通经活络，止痛
* 宣利指关节

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

热病，恶寒发热，发热伴颈腰痛且口渴欲饮。
癫狂，恐惧惊吓，悲伤焦虑，脏躁。
头痛，眩晕，视物眩晕，视物模糊，外障眼病（翳膜）。
四肢无力，五劳，颈项僵硬，肘部挛缩，关节松弛且肘部不能活动，所有手指剧烈疼痛，不能牢固握持，不能握拳，疣。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SI7 支正](/point/SI7.md)** 主要有三个作用。首先，由于小肠经属太阳经（六经中最表层的经脉，因此最易受外邪侵袭），**[SI7 支正](/point/SI7.md)** 与小肠经许多穴位一样，能够解表。它主要用于外感热邪引起的热病，特别主治起病时伴有颈腰痛者。

其次，**[SI7 支正](/point/SI7.md)** 是小肠经的络穴，络脉由此散布与心经相连。这反映在穴位名称“支正”中，“正”即指心经。《针灸大成》记载：“络穴位于两经之间……若刺之，可治表里经脉的病症。” **[SI7 支正](/point/SI7.md)** 在调理和宁心安神方面有显著作用，主治癫狂、恐惧惊吓以及悲伤焦虑。
《医宗金鉴·针灸法》更具体地推荐 **[SI7 支正](/point/SI7.md)** 治疗七情郁结。

第三，**[SI7 支正](/point/SI7.md)** 能够调理头部（头痛眩晕）、眼睛（视物模糊、外障眼病、视物眩晕）、肘部（肘部挛缩）以及特别是手指（所有手指剧烈疼痛，不能牢固握持，不能握拳）的小肠经病症。远端取穴治疗肢体、躯干和头部疾病是针灸实践的基本原则，由于十二正经都流向或源于手足，因此有大量远端穴位可供选择。然而，在治疗手足本身的疾病时，按定义没有远端穴位，且影响这些区域的近端穴位相对较少。因此，**[SI7 支正](/point/SI7.md)** 因其对指部和手部的作用而引人注目。

最后，《针灸大成》列出了络穴虚实证的具体主治。**[SI7 支正](/point/SI7.md)** 在实证时主治关节松弛且肘部不能活动；虚证时主治疣。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 主要用于治疗心神（络穴与心经相连）：癫狂，恐惧惊吓，悲伤焦虑，脏躁。
* 解热退热：热病，恶寒发热，发热伴颈腰痛且口渴欲饮。
* 治疗头部和眼睛：头痛，眩晕，视物眩晕，视物模糊，外障眼病（翳膜）。
* 治疗手部和手指的近端穴位：所有手指剧烈疼痛，不能牢固握持，不能握拳。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 狂言谵语，恐惧惊吓：**[SI7 支正](/point/SI7.md)**，**[LU10 鱼际](/point/LU10.md)**，**[LI4 合谷](/point/LI4.md)**，**[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)**，**[LI11 曲池](/point/LI11.md)** 和 **[SI4 腕骨](/point/SI4.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 视物眩晕和头痛：**[SI7 支正](/point/SI7.md)** 和 **[BL22 三焦俞](/point/BL22.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 视物眩晕：**[SI7 支正](/point/SI7.md)** 和 **[BL58 飞扬](/point/BL58.md)**（《百症赋》）。
