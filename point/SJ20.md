### Minute Angle JIǍOSŪN
- Meeting point of the Sanjiao, Small Intestine and Gall Bladder channels
![[SJ-20 角孙.png|250]]
#### Location

On the side of the head, directly level with the apex of the ear when the ear is folded forwards.

#### Location Notes

Fold the ear so that the posterior part of the upper helix directly covers the anterior part of the upper helix. Take care not to push the whole of the ear forwards.

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5-1.5 cun.  
**Note:** this point, in common with all points within the hairline, may be needled anteriorly, posteriorly, inferiorly or superiorly. Direction depends on symptomatology, in other words direct needle either towards where the headache or other pain radiates, or join up to connect with other head points. The needle should be inserted within the subcutaneous layers close to the bone of the skull, rather than more shallowly.

#### Actions

- Benefits the ears
- Benefits the teeth, gums and lips
- Clears heat

#### Indications

- Tinnitus, deafness, discharge of pus from the ear, redness and swelling of the back of the ear, redness and swelling of the auricle.
- Toothache, tooth decay, swelling and pain of the gums with inability to masticate, stiffness of the lips, dryness of the lips, superficial visual obstruction, stiffness of the nape of the neck with inability to turn the head.

#### Commentary

Although rarely used clinically, it is interesting to note that Jiǎosūn SJ-20 was traditionally indicated, and appeared in combination, for swelling and pain of the gums, dryness of the lips, inability to chew, toothache and tooth decay, and as such may be viewed as a useful adjacent point for such disorders. As far as stiffness of the lips is concerned, it is notable that of the few points that are traditionally said to treat these disorders, the majority (for example Jiǎosūn SJ-20, [Ěrmén SJ-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-21), [Shàngguān GB-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-3), [Zhèngyíng GB-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-17)) are in the region of the ear.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Traditionally indicated for swelling and pain of the gums, dryness of the lips, inability to chew, toothache and tooth decay.
- Also for ear disorders: tinnitus, deafness, discharge of pus from the ear, redness and swelling of the back of the ear, redness and swelling of the auricle.

#### Combinations

- Pain of the gums: Jiǎosūn SJ-20 and [Xiǎohǎi SI-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-8) (Great Compendium).
- Inability to chew: Jiǎosūn SJ-20 and [Jiáchē ST-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-6) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[SJ20 角孙](/point/SJ20.md)** (Jiǎosūn)

* **英文名称：** Minute Angle
* **中文名称：** 角孙
* **国际代码：** SJ-20
* **穴位属性：** **[SJ20 角孙](/point/SJ20.md)** 是三焦经、小肠经和胆经的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于头部侧面，耳廓折叠向前时，平耳尖处。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 折叠耳廓，使耳上部耳轮的后缘直接覆盖前缘。注意不要将整个耳朵向前推。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

横刺0.5至1.5寸。

* **注意 (Note):** 此穴与发际线内的所有穴位一样，可向前、向后、向下或向上针刺。方向取决于症状，即针尖直接指向头痛或其他疼痛放射的部位，或连接其他头部穴位。针尖应刺入颅骨附近的皮下层，而非更浅表。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利耳部
* 宣利牙齿、牙龈和口唇
* 清热

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

耳鸣，耳聋，耳部流脓，耳后红肿，耳廓红肿。
牙痛，龋齿，牙龈肿痛不能咀嚼，口唇僵硬，口唇干燥，外障眼病（翳膜），颈项僵硬不能转头。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

尽管**[SJ20 角孙](/point/SJ20.md)** 临床上较少使用，但值得注意的是，它在传统上主治并出现在组合中，用于**牙龈肿痛**、**口唇干燥**、**不能咀嚼**、**牙痛**和**龋齿**，因此可被视为治疗此类疾病的有用邻近穴位。就口唇僵硬而言，值得注意的是，传统上少数治疗这些疾病的穴位中，大多数（例如**[SJ20 角孙](/point/SJ20.md)**、**[SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)**、**[GB3 上关](/point/GB3.md)**、**[GB17 正营](/point/GB17.md)**）都位于耳部区域。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 传统上主治**牙龈肿痛**、**口唇干燥**、**不能咀嚼**、**牙痛**和**龋齿**。
* 也主治**耳部疾病**：耳鸣，耳聋，耳部流脓，耳后红肿，耳廓红肿。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 牙龈疼痛：**[SJ20 角孙](/point/SJ20.md)** 和 **[SI8 小海](/point/SI8.md)**（《针灸大成》）。
* 不能咀嚼：**[SJ20 角孙](/point/SJ20.md)** 和 **[ST6 颊车](/point/ST6.md)**（《千金方》）。
