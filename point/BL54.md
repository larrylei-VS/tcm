### Order’s Limit ZHÌBIĀN
![[BL-54 秩边.png|250]]
#### Location

On the buttock, in the depression 3 cun lateral to the sacro-coccygeal hiatus.

#### Location Notes

i. The sacro-coccygeal hiatus is the depression between the bony prominences of the sacral and coccygeal cornua (just below the spinous process of the fourth sacral vertebra if this is palpable); ii. When the shoulder is relaxed, the three cun line corresponds to the medial border of the scapula; iii. Alternatively this point may be located midway between the midline and the lateral edge of the buttock, when the lateral edge is firmly pressed in with the palm of the hand.

#### Needling

i. Perpendicular insertion 1.5 to 2.5 cun; ii. Slanted towards the anus or the genitalia 2 to 3 cun.

#### Actions

- Benefits the lumbar region, activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Regulates urination and treats haemorrhoids

#### Indications

- Pain and coldness of the lumbar region and sacrum, Kidney deficiency lumbar pain, pain of the buttock, sciatica, painful obstruction and atrophy disorder of the lower limb.
- Difficult urination, retention of urine, dark urine, difficult defecation, seminal emission, haemorrhoids, turbid leucorrhoea, pain of the genitals.

#### Commentary

Zhìbiān BL-54 is an indispensable point in the treatment of pain of the lumbar region, sacrum and buttocks and of sciatica. Along with [Bāohuāng BL-53](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-53) and [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30), Zhìbiān BL-54 is one of the points that should be palpated, and needled if tender, in all cases where pain radiates to the buttock and down the leg.

According to Investigation Into Points Along the Channels by Yan Zhen-shi of the Ming dynasty, Zhìbiān BL-54 is indicated for "Kidney deficiency lumbar pain, seminal emission and turbid leucorrhoea". This quotation emphasises that the use of Zhìbiān BL-54 in the treatment of lumbar pain is not confined to cases of stagnation (such as painful obstruction), but extends also to aching of the lumbar region due to weakness of the Kidneys.

In common with many points of the sacral region which lies over the Bladder and intestines, Zhìbiān BL-54 is able to regulate obstructed and difficult urination and defecation. Zhìbiān BL-54 is also mentioned in a number of classics for the treatment of "the five types of haemorrhoids with swelling".

#### Summary of clinical application

- Commonly used in the treatment of pain of the lumbar region, sacrum and buttocks and of sciatica. Along with [Bāohuāng BL-53](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-53) and [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30) , this is one of the points that should be palpated, and needled if tender, in all cases where pain radiates to the buttock and down the leg.
- Treats obstructed urination and defecation as well as haemorrhoids.

#### Combinations

- Aversion to cold in the back and stiffness of the spine with difficulty in bending: Zhìbiān BL-54, [Géguān BL-46](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-46) and [Jīnggǔ BL-64](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-64) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Red and hesitant urinary flow: Zhìbiān BL-54, [Guānyuán REN-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-4), [Qìhǎi REN-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-6) and [Yánggāng BL-48](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-48) (Supplementing Life).
- Retention of urine: Zhìbiān BL-54 and [Bāohuāng BL-53](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-53) (Supplementing Life).

### **[BL54 秩边](/point/BL54.md)** (ZHÌBIĀN)

* **英文名称：** Order’s Limit
* **中文名称：** 秩边
* **国际代码：** BL-54

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于臀部，骶尾裂孔旁开3寸的凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 骶尾裂孔是骶骨角与尾骨角骨性隆起之间的凹陷（若能触及第四骶椎棘突，则在其下方）；
    * 肩部放松时，旁开3寸线对应肩胛骨内侧缘；
    * 也可定位在中线与臀部外侧缘之间中点（当手掌用力按压臀部外侧缘时）。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

i. 垂直刺1.5至2.5寸； ii. 斜刺，向肛门或生殖器方向2至3寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利腰部，通经活络，止痛
* 调理小便，治疗痔疮

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

腰骶部疼痛发冷，肾虚腰痛，臀部疼痛，坐骨神经痛，下肢痹证和痿证。
小便不利，尿潴留，尿黄，排便困难，遗精，痔疮，小便白浊，生殖器疼痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL54 秩边](/point/BL54.md)** 是治疗腰部、骶部和臀部疼痛以及坐骨神经痛不可或缺的穴位。与**[BL53 胞肓](/point/BL53.md)** 和**[GB30 环跳](/point/GB30.md)** 一起，当疼痛放射至臀部并向下肢延伸时，此穴是所有病例中都应触诊并（若有压痛）针刺的穴位之一。

根据明代严震时的《循经考穴编》记载，**[BL54 秩边](/point/BL54.md)** 主治“肾虚腰痛，遗精，白浊”。此引文强调了**[BL54 秩边](/point/BL54.md)** 在治疗腰痛方面的应用不限于气滞（如痹痛），也延伸至因肾虚引起的腰部酸痛。

与骶部许多穴位一样（这些穴位位于膀胱和肠道上方），**[BL54 秩边](/point/BL54.md)** 能够调理阻滞的小便和大便困难。**[BL54 秩边](/point/BL54.md)** 在一些经典中也被提及用于治疗“五种痔疮伴肿胀”。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 常用于治疗腰部、骶部和臀部疼痛以及坐骨神经痛。与**[BL53 胞肓](/point/BL53.md)** 和**[GB30 环跳](/point/GB30.md)** 一起，当疼痛放射至臀部并向下肢延伸时，此穴是所有病例中都应触诊并（若有压痛）针刺的穴位之一。
* 治疗大小便困难以及痔疮。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 背部怕冷，脊柱僵硬屈伸不利：**[BL54 秩边](/point/BL54.md)**，**[BL46 膈关](/point/BL46.md)** 和 **[BL64 京骨](/point/BL64.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 小便涩痛赤色：**[BL54 秩边](/point/BL54.md)**，**[REN4 关元](/point/REN4.md)**，**[REN6 气海](/point/REN6.md)** 和 **[BL48 阳纲](/point/BL48.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 尿潴留：**[BL54 秩边](/point/BL54.md)** 和 **[BL53 胞肓](/point/BL53.md)**（《资生经》）。
