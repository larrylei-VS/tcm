### Knee Yang Gate XĪYÁNGGUĀN
![[GB-33 膝阳关.png|250]]
#### Location

On the lateral side of the knee, in the depression above the lateral epicondyle of the femur, between the femur and tendon of biceps femoris.

#### Location Notes

Starting above the knee joint, run a finger down the lateral aspect of the thigh in the groove between the femur and the tendon of biceps femoris, until the finger falls into the depression just proximal to the lateral epicondyle of the femur.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 2 cun.  
**Note:** according to several classical texts, this point is contraindicated to moxibustion.

#### Actions

- Relaxes the sinews and benefits the joints
- Dispels wind-damp

#### Indications

- Redness, swelling and pain of the lateral aspect of the knee joint, inability to flex and extend the knee, wind painful obstruction with numbness, painful obstruction and numbness of the lower leg, leg qi.

#### Commentary

An alternative name for Xīyángguān GB-33 is Hanfu (Mansion of Cold). This name reflects the observation that pathogenic cold tends to concentrate at the lateral side of knee, and that it may be treated by using this point. In clinical practice, Xīyángguān GB-33 is a valuable local point for pain of the lateral side of the knee which extends upwards along the thigh.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Valuable local point for redness, swelling, stiffness and pain of the lateral side of the knee.

#### Combinations

- Numbness of the lower leg: Xīyángguān GB-33, [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30) and [Chéngjīn BL-56](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-56) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Contraction of the sinews and difficulty in flexing and extending the knee, with inability to walk: Xīyángguān GB-33, [Liángqiū ST-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-34) and [Qūquán LIV-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-8) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### 1. [GB33 膝阳关](/point/GB33.md)

#### 1.1. Knee Yang Gate 膝陽關

#### 1.2. 定位

位于膝外侧，股骨外上髁上方凹陷处，在股骨与股二头肌腱之间。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

从膝关节上方开始，沿大腿外侧在股骨与股二头肌腱之间的沟向下探查，直至手指落入股骨外上髁正上方的凹陷处。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直刺入，深1至2寸。  
**注意：** 根据多部经典文献，此穴禁灸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 舒筋益关节
- 祛风湿

#### 1.6. 主治

- 膝关节外侧红肿痛、膝不能屈伸、风痛痹伴麻木、小腿痛痹及麻木、腿气。

#### 1.7. 论述

[GB33 膝阳关](/point/GB33.md)的别名为“寒府”。此名称反映了寒邪易聚于膝外侧的观察，并可通过此穴治疗。在临床实践中，[GB33 膝阳关](/point/GB33.md)是治疗膝外侧疼痛（向上沿大腿延伸）的宝贵局部穴位。

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 治疗膝外侧红肿、僵硬及疼痛的宝贵局部穴位。

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 小腿麻木：[GB33 膝阳关](/point/GB33.md) + [GB30 环跳](/point/GB30.md) + [BL56 承筋](/point/BL56.md)（《千金方》）。
- 筋挛及膝屈伸困难、不能行走：[GB33 膝阳关](/point/GB33.md) + [ST34 梁丘](/point/ST34.md) + [LIV8 曲泉](/point/LIV8.md)（《千金方》）。
