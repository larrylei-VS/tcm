### Storehouse KÙFÁNG
![[ST-14 库房.png|250]]
#### Location

On the chest, in the first intercostal space, 4 cun lateral to the midline ([Huágài REN-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-20)), on the mamillary line.

#### Location Notes

i. First locate the costal cartilage of the second rib which is level with the sternal angle, then locate the first intercostal space above it; ii. Note that in males the nipple lies in the fourth intercostal space; iii. Note that the intercostal space curves upwards laterally, so that Kùfáng ST-14 will lie superior to the level of [Huágài REN-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-20).

#### Needling

Transverse-oblique insertion laterally or medially along the intercostal space 0.5 to 0.8 cun, or transverse insertion superiorly or inferiorly along the channel.  
**Caution:** deep or perpendicular insertion carries a substantial risk of puncturing the lung.

#### Actions

- Descends rebellious qi and unbinds the chest

#### Indications

- Distention and fullness of the chest and lateral costal region, cough, coughing of pus and blood, dyspnoea.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Both Kùfáng ST-14 and [Wūyì ST-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-15) are mostly indicated for local disorders of the chest, with [Wūyì ST-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-15) also indicated for breast pain and abscess, pain of the skin making wearing of clothes unbearable, generalised itching, heaviness of the body, swollen body, pain and weakness of the limbs.

#### Combinations

- Cough: Kùfáng ST-14, [Wūyì ST-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-15) and [Gāohuāngshū BL-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-43) (Supplementing Life).

### [ST14 库房](/point/ST14.md) (KÙFÁNG)

* **英文名称：** Storehouse
* **中文名称：** 库房
* **国际代码：** ST-14

#### 定位 (Location)

位于胸部，第一肋间隙中，距前正中线（[REN20 华盖](/point/REN20.md)）旁开4寸，在乳头线上。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 首先找到与胸骨角平齐的第二肋软骨，然后在其上方找到第一肋间隙；
    * 请注意，男性乳头位于第四肋间隙；
    * 请注意，肋间隙向外侧向上弯曲，因此[ST14 库房](/point/ST14.md) 将位于[REN20 华盖](/point/REN20.md) 的上方。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

横斜刺，沿肋间隙向外侧或内侧刺0.5至0.8寸，或沿经络向上或向下横刺。

* **注意 (Caution):** 深刺或垂直刺入有很大风险刺破肺脏。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 降逆气，宽胸散结

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

胸部和胁肋部胀满，咳嗽，咳脓血，呼吸困难。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST14 库房](/point/ST14.md) 临床上较少使用。[ST14 库房](/point/ST14.md) 和[ST15 屋翳](/point/ST15.md) 主要主治胸部局部疾病。[ST15 屋翳](/point/ST15.md) 还主治乳房疼痛和乳痈、皮肤疼痛以致不能穿衣、全身瘙痒、身体沉重、身体浮肿、肢体疼痛和无力。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治胸部和胁肋部胀满，咳嗽，咳脓血，呼吸困难。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 咳嗽：[ST14 库房](/point/ST14.md)，[ST15 屋翳](/point/ST15.md) 和[BL43 膏肓俞](/point/BL43.md)（《资生经》）。
