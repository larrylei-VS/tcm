### Eyebrows’ Pouring MÉICHŌNG
![[BL-3 眉冲.png|250]]
#### Location

Directly superior to [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2), 0.5 cun within the anterior hairline, level with [Shéntíng DU-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-22).

#### Location Notes

The distance between the anterior and posterior hairlines is measured as 12 cun. If the anterior hairline is indistinct, the distance is measured as 15 cun between the glabella [point [Yìntáng M-HN-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/M-HN-3)] and the posterior hairline; the location of the anterior hairline would thus be defined as one fifth of this distance. If the posterior hairline is indistinct, it can be measured as 1 cun inferior to [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16) which lies immediately below the external occipital protuberance.

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Note:** according to the Great Compendium of Acupuncture and Moxibustion this point is contraindicated to moxibustion.

#### Actions

- Expels wind, clears the head and alleviates pain
- Benefits the eyes and nose

#### Indications

- Headache, vertex headache, dizziness, dimness of vision, nasal congestion, nasal sores, dyspnoea, epilepsy, agitation and fullness of the Heart.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Headache, vertex headache, dimness of vision, nasal congestion, nasal sores, dyspnoea, dizziness, epilepsy, and agitation and fullness of the Heart.

#### Combinations

- Headache with nasal congestion: Méichōng BL-3, [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23), [Yíngxiāng LI-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-20) and [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4).

### **[BL3 眉冲](/point/BL3.md)** (MÉICHŌNG)

* **英文名称：** Eyebrows’ Pouring
* **中文名称：** 眉冲
* **国际代码：** BL-3

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于**[BL2 攒竹](/point/BL2.md)** 正上方，前发际内0.5寸，与**[DU24 神庭](/point/DU24.md)** 平齐。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 前后发际之间的距离计为12寸。如果前发际不明显，则从眉心（经外穴**[MHN3 印堂](/point/MHN3.md)**）到后发际的距离计为15寸；前发际的位置定义为此距离的五分之一处。如果后发际不明显，可从**[DU16 风府](/point/DU16.md)**（位于枕外隆突正下方）向下1寸处测量。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

横刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Note):** 根据《针灸大成》，此穴禁灸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 祛风，清头止痛
* 明目利鼻

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

头痛，头顶痛，眩晕，视物模糊，鼻塞，鼻疮，呼吸困难，癫痫，心烦胀满。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL3 眉冲](/point/BL3.md)** 临床上较少使用。它位于膀胱经在头部的前段，靠近前发际，其名称“眉冲”可能暗示其与眉部和气血冲和有关。

此穴主要作用于头部，能够祛风清头止痛，主治头痛，特别是头顶痛和眩晕。此外，它还能明目利鼻，对于视物模糊、鼻塞和鼻疮等症状有一定疗效。**[BL3 眉冲](/point/BL3.md)** 也能辅助治疗呼吸困难、癫痫和心烦胀满，这可能与膀胱经与肺、心等脏腑的联系以及其调气降逆的功效有关。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治头痛，头顶痛，视物模糊，鼻塞，鼻疮，呼吸困难，眩晕，癫痫，以及心烦胀满。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 头痛伴鼻塞：**[BL3 眉冲](/point/BL3.md)**，**[DU23 上星](/point/DU23.md)**，**[LI20 迎香](/point/LI20.md)** 和 **[LI4 合谷](/point/LI4.md)**。
