### Brain Hollow NǍOKŌNG
![[GB-19 脑空.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder channel with the Yang Linking vessel

#### Location

In the occipital region, directly above [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20), level with [Nǎohù DU-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-17).

#### Location Notes

i. Locate [Nǎohù DU-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-17) 1.5 cun directly above Fengfu DU‑16, in the depression directly superior to the external occipital protuberance; ii. Alternatively (if the external occipital protuberance is indistinct) locate Nǎokōng GB-19 one quarter of the distance between [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20) and [Chénglíng GB-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-18).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5-1.5 cun. See note [Hànyàn GB-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-4).

#### Actions

- Benefits the head and alleviates pain
- Pacifies wind and clears the sense organs

#### Indications

- Headache, head wind, brain wind, one-sided headache and heaviness of the head, stiffness and pain of the neck with inability to turn the head, wind dizziness.
- Redness, swelling and pain of the eyes, deafness and tinnitus, pain of the nose, nasal congestion, nosebleed.
- Fright palpitations, mania depression disorder, taxation disorders with emaciation, heat in the body.

#### Commentary

The Great Compendium of Acupuncture and Moxibustion records how Hua Tuo, the famous 2nd century physician, treated General Wei Tai-cu (the posthumously consecrated emperor of the Wei dynasty) for 'head wind, confused mind and visual dizziness'. After needling Nǎokōng GB-19, the General was cured. This story, however, also appears in an earlier source in which the point needled is [Yǒngquǎn KID-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-1).

#### Summary of clinical application

- Indicated for headache, head wind, brain wind, one-sided headache and heaviness of the head, stiffness and pain of the neck with inability to turn the head, wind dizziness, redness, swelling and pain of the eyes, deafness and tinnitus, pain of the nose, nasal congestion, nosebleed, fright palpitations, mania depression disorder, taxation disorders with emaciation, heat in the body.

#### Combinations

- Pain and heaviness of the head: Nǎokōng GB-19, [Nǎohù DU-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-17) and [Tōngtiān BL-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-7) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Head wind: Nǎokōng GB-19, [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) and [Tiānzhù BL-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-10) (Supplementing Life).

### 1. [GB19 脑空](point/GB19.md)

#### 1.1. Brain Hollow 腦空

- 胆经与阳维脉的交会穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于枕区，正对[GB20 风池](point/GB20.md)上方，与[DU17 脑户](point/DU17.md)平齐。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

i. 定位[DU17 脑户](point/DU17.md)在[DU16 风府](point/DU16.md)正上方1.5寸，位于枕外隆凸正上方的凹陷处；  
ii. 或者（若枕外隆凸不明显），[GB19 脑空](point/GB19.md)位于[GB20 风池](point/GB20.md)与[GB18 承灵](point/GB18.md)之间四分之一处。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

横刺，深0.5至1.5寸。参见[GB4 颔厌](point/GB4.md)的针刺说明。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 益头止痛
- 平风清感官

#### 1.6. 主治

- 头痛、头风、脑风、偏头痛及头重、颈项僵痛不能转头、风眩。
- 目赤肿痛、耳聾耳鳴、鼻痛、鼻塞、鼻衄。
- 惊悸、躁郁症、劳损伴消瘦、身热。

#### 1.7. 论述

《针灸大成》记载，2世纪著名医家华佗治疗魏太祖（魏朝追封皇帝）的“头风、心神迷乱及视觉眩晕”。针刺[GB19 脑空](point/GB19.md)后，太祖痊愈。然而，这一故事在更早的文献中也出现，当时针刺的穴位是[KID1 涌泉](point/KID1.md)。

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 适用于头痛、头风、脑风、偏头痛及头重、颈项僵痛不能转头、风眩、目赤肿痛、耳聾耳鳴、鼻痛、鼻塞、鼻衄、惊悸、躁郁症、劳损伴消瘦、身热。

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 头痛及头重：[GB19 脑空](point/GB19.md) + [DU17 脑户](point/DU17.md) + [BL7 通天](point/BL7.md)（《千金方》）。
- 头风：[GB19 脑空](point/GB19.md) + [DU20 百会](point/DU20.md) + [BL10 天柱](point/BL10.md)（《补命》）。
