### Suspended Pivot XUÁNSHŪ
![[DU-5 悬枢.png|250]]
#### Location

On the midline of the lower back, in the depression below the spinous process of the first lumbar vertebra.

#### Location Notes

This point is located two intervertebral spaces above the line connecting the highest points of the two iliac crests (this line is level with the lower border of L3).

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
Caution**:** the spinal canal lies between 1.25 and 1.75 cun deep to the skin surface, varying according to body build.

#### Actions

- Benefits the lumbar spine
- Benefits the lower jiao

#### Indications

- Stiffness of the lumbar spine with inability to flex and extend.
- Undigested food (in the stool), diarrhoea, running piglet qi, shan disorder, retraction of the testicles.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Stiffness of the lumbar spine with inability to flex and extend.
- Undigested food (in the stool), diarrhoea, running piglet qi, shan disorder, retraction of the testicles.

#### Combinations

- Undigested food (in the stool): Xuánshū DU-5, [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36), [Dàchángshū BL-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-25), [Sānyīnjiāo SP-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-6), [Xiàwǎn REN-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-10), [Sānjiāoshu BL-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-22) and [Liángmén ST-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-21) (Supplementing Life).

# 1. [DU5 悬枢](point/DU5.md)

## 1.1. Suspended Pivot 懸樞

### 1.2. 定位

位于下背部中线，第一腰椎棘突下方的凹陷中。

### 1.3. 定位说明

此穴位于连接两髂嵴最高点的线（此线与第三腰椎下缘平齐）上方两个椎间隙。

### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直刺入，深0.5至1寸。  
**注意：** 脊管位于皮肤表面下1.25至1.75寸，深度因体型而异。

### 1.5. 作用

- 益腰脊  
- 益下焦  

### 1.6. 主治

- 腰脊僵硬不能屈伸。  
- 泻下未消化食物、腹泻、奔豚气、疝病、睾丸上缩。  

### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 腰脊僵硬不能屈伸。  
- 泻下未消化食物、腹泻、奔豚气、疝病、睾丸上缩。  

### 1.8. 配伍

- 泻下未消化食物：[DU5 悬枢](point/DU5.md) + [ST36 足三里](point/ST36.md) + [BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md) + [SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md) + [REN10 下脘](point/REN10.md) + [BL22 三焦俞](point/BL22.md) + [ST21 梁门](point/ST21.md)（《补命》）。
