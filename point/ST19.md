### Not Contained BÙRÓNG
![[ST-19 不容.png|250]]
#### Location

On the abdomen, 2 cun lateral to the midline and 6 cun superior to the umbilicus, level with [Jùguè REN-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-14).

#### Location Notes

_i_. The 2 cun line is located halfway between the midline and the palpable lateral border of the rectus abdominis muscle; _ii_. In some patients with a narrow subcostal angle, this point may fall on the costal margin. The options then are to a. locate more medially, b. needle transversely on the costal margin, c. select a different point.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 0.8 cun.  
**Caution:** deep insertion may injure the heart on the left or the liver on the right if either of these organs is enlarged.

#### Actions

- Harmonises the middle jiao and lowers rebellion
- Descends qi and alleviates cough and wheezing

#### Indications

- Abdominal distention, epigastric pain, vomiting, vomiting blood, poor appetite, abdominal deficiency borborygmus.
- Cough, dyspnoea, difficult breathing with raised shoulders, pain of the chest, back, shoulder and intercostal region, pain of the Heart, dry mouth.

#### Commentary

Because of its location, Bùróng ST-19 is sometimes selected for the treatment of hypochondriac pain, nausea and vomiting due to cholecystitis or cholelithiasis. Its name 'Not Contained' or 'Uncontainable' refers to the inability of the Stomach to retain its contents.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Used for hypochondriac pain, nausea and vomiting, especially when due to cholecystitis or cholelithiasis.
- Also indicated for cough, dyspnoea, difficult breathing with raised shoulders, pain of the chest, back, shoulder and intercostal region and pain of the Heart.

#### Combinations

- Vomiting: Bùróng ST-19, [Shàngwǎn REN-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-13) and [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-7) (Supplementing Life).
- Stabbing pain of the Heart: Bùróng ST-19 and [Qímén LIV-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-14) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Fullness of the chest and lateral costal region: Bùróng ST-19 and [Zhāngmén LIV-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-13) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Acute pain of cholelithiasis: Bùróng ST-19 (right), Dannangxue (M-LE-23), [Yánglíngquán GB-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-34) (left), [Qímén LIV-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-14) (right), [Zhōngwǎn REN-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-12), [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) and [Tàichōng LIV-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-3).

### [ST19 不容](point/ST19.md) (BÙRÓNG)

* **英文名称：** Not Contained
* **中文名称：** 不容
* **国际代码：** ST-19

#### 定位 (Location)

位于腹部，距前正中线旁开2寸，脐上6寸，与[REN14 巨阙](point/REN14.md) 平齐。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 旁开2寸线位于前正中线与可触及的腹直肌外缘之间中点；
    * 对于一些剑突下角较窄的患者，此穴可能落在肋缘上。此时可选择：a. 向内侧定位； b. 在肋缘处横刺； c. 选择其他穴位。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直刺0.5至0.8寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 深刺可能刺伤心脏（左侧）或肝脏（右侧），特别是当这些脏器肿大时。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 调和中焦，降逆
* 降气，止咳平喘

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

腹胀，胃脘痛，呕吐，呕血，食欲不振，腹部虚性肠鸣。
咳嗽，呼吸困难，耸肩呼吸困难，胸、背、肩、肋间疼痛，心痛，口干。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

由于其位置，[ST19 不容](point/ST19.md) 有时被选用于治疗胆囊炎或胆石症引起的胁肋痛、恶心和呕吐。其名称“不容”指的是胃不能容纳其内容物。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 用于胁肋痛、恶心和呕吐，特别是胆囊炎或胆石症引起的。
* 也主治咳嗽，呼吸困难，耸肩呼吸困难，胸、背、肩、肋间疼痛以及心痛。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 呕吐：[ST19 不容](point/ST19.md)，[REN13 上脘](point/REN13.md) 和[PC7 大陵](point/PC7.md)（《资生经》）。
* 心痛如刺：[ST19 不容](point/ST19.md) 和[LIV14 期门](point/LIV14.md)（《千金方》）。
* 胸胁胀满：[ST19 不容](point/ST19.md) 和[LIV13 章门](point/LIV13.md)（《百症赋》）。
* 急性胆石症疼痛：[ST19 不容](point/ST19.md)（右侧），胆囊穴 (经外穴 M-LE-23)，[GB34 阳陵泉](point/GB34.md)（左侧），[LIV14 期门](point/LIV14.md)（右侧），[REN12 中脘](point/REN12.md)，[LI4 合谷](point/LI4.md) 和[LIV3 太冲](point/LIV3.md)。
