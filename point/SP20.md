### Encircling Glory ZHŌURÓNG
![[SP-20 周荣.png|250]]
#### Location

On the lateral side of the chest, in the second intercostal space, 6 cun lateral to the midline.

#### Location Notes

i. First locate the costal cartilage of the second rib which is level with the sternal angle, then locate the second intercostal space below it; ii. The six cun line is located two cun lateral to the mamillary line; iii. This point lies one intercostal space directly below [Zhōngfǔ LU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-1).

#### Needling

Transverse-oblique insertion along the intercostal space, 0.5 to 1 cun.

Caution:: perpendicular insertion, especially in thin patients, carries a substantial risk of inducing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Regulates and descends qi and unbinds the chest

#### Indications

- Distention and fullness of the chest and lateral costal region, cough, coughing with copious phlegm or pus, shortness of breath, difficult ingestion, desires to drink fluids.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for distention and fullness of the chest and lateral costal region, cough, coughing with copious phlegm or pus, shortness of breath, difficult ingestion, desires to drink fluids.

#### Combinations

- Difficult ingestion with desire to drink fluids: Zhōuróng SP-20 and [Dàchángshū BL-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-25) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Difficult ingestion: Zhōuróng SP-20, [Zhōngfǔ LU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-1), [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60), [Chéngmǎn ST-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-20) and [Yújì LU-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-10) (Supplementing Life).

### **[SP20 周荣](point/SP20.md)** (ZHŌURÓNG)

* **英文名称：** Encircling Glory
* **中文名称：** 周荣
* **国际代码：** SP-20

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于胸部外侧，第二肋间隙中，距前正中线旁开6寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 首先找到与胸骨角平齐的第二肋软骨，然后在其下方的第二肋间隙定位；
    * 旁开6寸线位于乳头线旁开2寸处；
    * 此穴位于 **[LU1 中府](point/LU1.md)** 正下方一个肋间隙。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

沿肋间隙横斜刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直刺入，特别是在瘦弱患者中，有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 调理降气，宽胸散结

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

胸部和胁肋部胀满，咳嗽，咳痰多或咳脓，气短，吞咽困难，口渴欲饮。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SP20 周荣](point/SP20.md)** 临床上较少使用。其名称“周荣”可能暗示其在胸部气血循环和肺腑宣发肃降方面的作用。

此穴主要用于缓解胸部和胁肋部因气机不畅导致的胀满，以及改善咳嗽、气短等肺系症状。它对咳痰多或咳脓的治疗也显示了其化痰排痰的功效。此外，**[SP20 周荣](point/SP20.md)** 也主治吞咽困难和口渴欲饮，这可能与脾胃运化失调或肺胃气机不降有关。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治胸部和胁肋部胀满，咳嗽，咳痰多或咳脓，气短，吞咽困难，口渴欲饮。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 吞咽困难伴口渴欲饮：**[SP20 周荣](point/SP20.md)** 和 **[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 吞咽困难：**[SP20 周荣](point/SP20.md)**，**[LU1 中府](point/LU1.md)**，**[BL60 昆仑](point/BL60.md)**，**[ST20 承满](point/ST20.md)** 和 **[LU10 鱼际](point/LU10.md)**（《资生经》）。
