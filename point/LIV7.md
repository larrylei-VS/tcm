### Knee Joint XĪGUĀN
![[LIV-7 膝关.png|250]]
#### Location

Posterior and inferior to the medial condyle of the tibia, 1 cun posterior to [Yīnlíngquán SP-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-9).

#### Location Notes

Locate [Yīnlíngquán SP-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-9) in the depression in the angle formed by the medial condyle of the tibia and the posterior border of the tibia, and then locate Xīguān LIV-7 one cun posterior to it.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 2 cun.

#### Actions

- Dispels wind-damp
- Benefits the knee and relaxes the sinews

#### Indications

- Swelling and pain of the knee, pain of the inner aspect of the knee radiating to the patella, crane's knee wind, wind painful obstruction, white tiger joint wind pain, difficulty in flexing and extending the knee, cold damp pouring downwards.
- Abdominal pain, pain of the throat.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Mostly indicated for local disorders such as swelling and pain of the knee, pain of the inner aspect of the knee radiating to the patella, crane’s knee wind, wind painful obstruction, white tiger joint wind pain, difficulty in flexing and extending the knee, cold damp pouring downwards.

#### Combinations

- Disorders of the knee and below the knee: moxa Xīguān LIV-7, [Dúbí ST-35](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-35), [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) and [Yánglíngquán GB-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-34) (Supplementing Life).
- Redness, swelling and pain of the knees: Xīguān LIV-7, [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40), [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) and [Yīnshì ST‑33](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-33) (Great Compendium).
- Redness, swelling and pain of the knees with inability to walk: Xīguān LIV-7 and [Xīyǎn (MN-LE-16)](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/MN-LE-16) (Song of the Jade Dragon).

### 1. [LIV7 膝关](/point/LIV7.md)

#### 1.1. Knee Joint 膝關

#### 1.2. 定位

位于胫骨内侧髁后下方，[SP9 阴陵泉](/point/SP9.md)后方1寸。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

先定位[SP9 阴陵泉](/point/SP9.md)，在胫骨内侧髁与胫骨后缘形成的角内凹陷处，然后[LIV7 膝关](/point/LIV7.md)位于其后方1寸。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直刺入，深1至2寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 祛风湿  
- 益膝舒筋  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 膝肿痛、膝内侧痛放射至髌骨、鹤膝风、风痹、白虎历节风痛、膝难屈伸、寒湿下注。  
- 腹痛、咽痛。  

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 主要用于局部疾患，如膝肿痛、膝内侧痛放射至髌骨、鹤膝风、风痹、白虎历节风痛、膝难屈伸、寒湿下注。  

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 膝及膝下疾患：灸[LIV7 膝关](/point/LIV7.md) + [ST35 犊鼻](/point/ST35.md) + [ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md) + [GB34 阳陵泉](/point/GB34.md)（《补命》）。  
- 膝红肿痛：[LIV7 膝关](/point/LIV7.md) + [BL40 委中](/point/BL40.md) + [ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md) + [ST33 阴市](/point/ST33.md)（《大成》）。  
- 膝红肿痛不能行走：[LIV7 膝关](/point/LIV7.md) + [MNLE16 膝眼](/point/MNLE16.md)（《玉龙歌》）。
