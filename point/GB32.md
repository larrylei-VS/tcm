### Middle Ditch ZHŌNGDÚ
![[GB-32 中渎.png|250]]
#### Location

On the lateral aspect of the thigh, 2 cun inferior to [Fēngshì GB-31](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-31).

#### Location Notes

i. First locate [Fēngshì GB-31](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-31) (see above) and then locate Zhōngdú GB-32, 2 cun inferior to it; ii. The distance between the prominence of the greater trochanter and the popliteal crease is 19 cun, thus Zhōngdú GB-32 is located at the tender point just proximal to the junction of the upper three quarters and the lower quarter of this line.

#### Needling

i. Perpendicular or slightly posteriorly oblique insertion 1 to 2 cun; ii. Oblique insertion, directed proximally or distally, 1.5 to 2.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Expels wind, damp and cold
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limb, sinew painful obstruction with numbness, numbness, hemiplegia, sciatica, pain of the back and hip, cold qi residing in the muscles and sinews.
- Urticaria.

#### Commentary

Both the Systematic Classic of Acupuncture and Moxibustion and the Great Compendium of Acupuncture and Moxibustion recommend Zhōngdú GB-32 for cold pathogenic qi which lodges in the muscles and sinews. This indication reflects the two principal ways of understanding and classifying painful obstruction that were discussed in the Yellow Emperor's Inner Classic. The first is according to the type of pathogen that predominates, i.e. cold, wind or damp. The second is according to the depth of penetration of the pathogen. In this method of differentiation, the most superficial level of invasion is the skin, followed by the muscles, vessels, sinews and bones. Each of these levels corresponds to one of the zang (the Lung, Spleen, Heart, Liver and Kidneys respectively) and an unresolved pathogen at any level might injure its related zang.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Indicated for atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limb, sinew painful obstruction with numbness, numbness, hemiplegia, sciatica, pain of the back and hip, cold qi residing in the muscles and sinews, urticaria.

#### Combinations

- Atrophy disorder: needleZhōngdú GB-32 and [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30), moxa [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) and Feishu BL‑13 (Glorious Anthology).


### 1. [GB32 中渎](/point/GB32.md)

#### 1.1. Middle Ditch 中瀆

#### 1.2. 定位

位于大腿外侧面，[GB31 风市](/point/GB31.md)下方2寸。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

i. 先定位[GB31 风市](/point/GB31.md)（见上文），然后定位[GB32 中渎](/point/GB32.md)在其下方2寸；  
ii. 股骨大转子突出点至腘横纹的距离为19寸，因此[GB32 中渎](/point/GB32.md)位于此连线约上四分之三与下四分之一交界处稍上方的敏感点。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

i. 垂直或稍向后斜刺，深1至2寸；  
ii. 向近端或远端斜刺，深1.5至2.5寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 祛风湿寒
- 活络止痛

#### 1.6. 主治

- 下肢萎症及痛痹、筋痛痹伴麻木、麻木、偏瘫、坐骨神经痛、背髋痛、寒气留于肌肉筋脉。
- 荨麻疹。

#### 1.7. 论述

《针灸系统经典》和《针灸大成》均推荐[GB32 中渎](/point/GB32.md)用于寒邪滞留于肌肉筋脉。这一适应症反映了《黄帝内经》中讨论痛痹的两种主要分类方法。第一种是根据主导病邪类型，即寒、风或湿。第二种是根据病邪侵入的深度。在此区分法中，最浅层侵袭为皮肤，其次为肌肉、血脉、筋脉和骨骼。每一层对应一脏（分别为肺、脾、心、肝、肾），任何未消散的病邪可能损伤其相关脏。

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 适用于下肢萎症及痛痹、筋痛痹伴麻木、麻木、偏瘫、坐骨神经痛、背髋痛、寒气留于肌肉筋脉、荨麻疹。

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 萎症：针[GB32 中渎](/point/GB32.md) + [GB30 环跳](/point/GB30.md)，灸[ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md) + [BL13 肺俞](/point/BL13.md)（《荣集》）。
