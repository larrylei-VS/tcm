### Lesser Sea SHÀOHǍI
![[HE-3 少海.png|250]]
- He-Sea and Water point of the Heart channel

#### Location

Midway between [Qūzé PC-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-3) and the medial epicondyle of the humerus, at the medial end of the transverse cubital crease when the elbow is fully flexed.

#### Needling

Oblique insertion distally or proximally, or directed towards [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11), 0.5 to 1.5 cun. Note: according to a number of classical sources this point is contraindicated to moxibustion.

#### Actions

- Calms the spirit, transforms phlegm and clears heat
- Activates the channel and benefits the arm

#### Indications

- Heart pain, fullness of the chest, pain of the axilla and lateral costal region, mania and laughter, epilepsy, tongue thrusting, poor memory, vomiting of foamy (watery) saliva, scrofula.
- Red eyes, headache and wind dizziness, visual dizziness, toothache accompanied by chills and fever, erosion and swelling of the gums.
- Trembling of the hand and arm, numbness of the upper limb, inability to raise the four limbs, swelling and pain of the elbow joint, flaccidity of the elbow, inability to turn the neck, wind painful obstruction.

#### Commentary

Shàohǎi HE-3 is the he-sea and water point of the Heart shaoyin (lesser yin) channel, hence the name Shaohai (Lesser Sea). It has three main spheres of action. Firstly it is able to calm the spirit, transform phlegm and clear heat, being indicated in such disorders as mania and laughter, epilepsy, tongue thrusting (where the tongue is repeatedly thrust out of the mouth like a snake's tongue) and poor memory, as well as other manifestations of phlegm such as vomiting of foamy saliva and scrofula. Clinically, however, it is less used for this purpose than points such as [Jiānshǐ PC-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-5), [Fēnglóng ST-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-40) and [Tiānjǐng SJ-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-10).

Secondly, as the water point of the Heart fire channel, Shàohǎi HE-3 is able to drain heat from the head manifesting as redness of the eyes, erosion and swelling of the gums, and toothache accompanied by chills and fever.

The third and main clinical application of Shàohǎi HE-3, however, is in the treatment of various disorders of the Heart channel in the upper limb. It is an important point for numbness and trembling of the arm and hand, and disorders of the elbow. According to the Classic of the Jade Dragon it is indicated for "fullness of the chest with agitation of the Heart accompanied by numbness and difficulty in raising the shoulder and arm". In the treatment of atrophy disorder and hemiplegia following windstroke, points of yangming channel, which is "abundant in qi and blood" have long been emphasised. Clinical practice has demonstrated, however, that better results are obtained when some points from yin channels are also selected. Due to the important action of Shàohǎi HE-3 on disorders of the whole arm, it is often stimulated by through needling from [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11) in the treatment of these disorders.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Mainly used to treat disorders of the arm and hand: trembling and numbness, wind painful obstruction, inability to raise the four limbs, swelling, pain or flaccidity of the elbow joint.
- Combined with or through-needled from Quchi LI-11 for chronic hemiplegia or atrophy disorder of the arm.
- Transforms phlegm which disturbs the spirit: mania, laughter, epilepsy, poor memory, vomiting of foamy (watery) saliva.

#### Combinations

- Vomiting of foam: Shàohǎi HE-3, [Duìduān DU-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-27) and [Běnshén GB-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-13) (Supplementing Life).
- Vomiting of foamy (watery) saliva: Shàohǎi HE-3 and [Zhúbīn KID-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-9) (Supplementing Life).
- Scrofula: Shàohǎi HE-3, [Tiānchí PC-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-1), [Zhāngmén LIV-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-13), [Zúlínqì GB-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-41), [Zhīgōu SJ-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-6), [Yángfǔ GB-38](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-38), [Jiānjǐng GB-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-21) and [Shǒusānlǐ LI-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-10) (Great Compendium).
- Scrofula: Shàohǎi HE-3 and [Tiānjǐng SJ-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-10) (Song More Precious Than Jade).
- Manic raving fear and fright: Shàohǎi HE-3, [Zhīzhèng SI-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-7), [Yújì LU-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-10), [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4), [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11) and [Wàngǔ SI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-4) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Stiff tongue, tongue thrusting: Shàohǎi HE-3, [Huáròumén ST-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-24) and [Wēnliū LI-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-7) (Supplementing Life).
- Stubborn numbness of both forearms: Shàohǎi HE-3 and [Shǒusānlǐ LI-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-10) (One Hundred Symptoms).

### **[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)** (SHÀOHǍI)

* **英文名称：** Lesser Sea
* **中文名称：** 少海
* **国际代码：** HE-3
* **穴位属性：** **[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)** 是心经的合穴和水穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于肘横纹内侧端，在 **[PC3 曲泽](/point/PC3.md)** 与肱骨内上髁之间中点，完全屈肘时取穴。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺，向远端或近端，或针尖朝向 **[LI11 曲池](/point/LI11.md)**，进针0.5至1.5寸。
注意：根据一些经典文献，此穴禁灸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宁心安神，化痰清热
* 通经活络，利臂

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

心痛，胸中胀满，腋窝和胁肋部疼痛，癫狂喜笑，癫痫，舌体强硬（吐舌），记忆力差，呕吐泡沫（清水）唾液，瘰疬。
眼睛红赤，头痛伴头风眩晕，视物眩晕，牙痛伴恶寒发热，牙龈糜烂肿胀。
手和手臂颤抖，上肢麻木，四肢不能抬举，肘关节肿痛，肘部痿软，颈项不能转动，风痹。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)** 是心少阴经的合穴和水穴，故名少海。它主要有三个作用领域。首先，它能够宁心安神，化痰清热，主治癫狂喜笑、癫痫、舌体强硬（舌头反复伸出嘴外，像蛇信一样）和记忆力差等疾病，以及呕吐泡沫唾液、瘰疬等痰饮表现。然而，临床上它在这方面的使用频率低于 **[PC5 间使](/point/PC5.md)**、**[ST40 丰隆](/point/ST40.md)** 和 **[SJ10 天井](/point/SJ10.md)** 等穴。

其次，作为心火经的水穴，**[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)** 能够清泻头部之热，表现为眼睛红赤，牙龈糜烂肿胀，以及牙痛伴恶寒发热。

然而，**[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)** 的第三个也是主要的临床应用是治疗上肢心经的各种疾病。它是治疗手臂和手部麻木颤抖以及肘部疾病的重要穴位。根据《玉龙歌》记载，它主治“胸满心烦，伴手臂麻木，不能抬肩抬臂”。在治疗中风后偏瘫和痿证时，长期以来一直强调“多气多血”的阳明经穴位。然而，临床实践表明，同时选取一些阴经穴位能获得更好的效果。由于 **[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)** 对整个手臂疾病的重要作用，在治疗这些疾病时，常与 **[LI11 曲池](/point/LI11.md)** 透刺联合使用。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 主要用于治疗手臂和手部疾病：颤抖和麻木，风痹，四肢不能抬举，肘关节肿胀、疼痛或痿软。
* 常与 **[LI11 曲池](/point/LI11.md)** 联合或透刺，用于治疗慢性偏瘫或手臂痿证。
* 化解扰乱心神的痰湿：癫狂，喜笑，癫痫，记忆力差，呕吐泡沫（清水）唾液。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 呕吐泡沫：**[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)**，**[DU27 兑端](/point/DU27.md)** 和 **[GB13 本神](/point/GB13.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 呕吐泡沫（清水）唾液：**[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)** 和 **[KID9 筑宾](/point/KID9.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 瘰疬：**[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)**，**[PC1 天池](/point/PC1.md)**，**[LIV13 章门](/point/LIV13.md)**，**[GB41 足临泣](/point/GB41.md)**，**[SJ6 支沟](/point/SJ6.md)**，**[GB38 阳辅](/point/GB38.md)**，**[GB21 肩井](/point/GB21.md)** 和 **[LI10 手三里](/point/LI10.md)**（《针灸大成》）。
* 瘰疬：**[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)** 和 **[SJ10 天井](/point/SJ10.md)**（《玉龙赋》）。
* 狂言谵语伴恐惧：**[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)**，**[SI7 支正](/point/SI7.md)**，**[LU10 鱼际](/point/LU10.md)**，**[LI4 合谷](/point/LI4.md)**，**[LI11 曲池](/point/LI11.md)** 和 **[SI4 腕骨](/point/SI4.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 舌强，舌体强硬：**[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)**，**[ST24 滑肉门](/point/ST24.md)** 和 **[LI7 温溜](/point/LI7.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 双前臂顽固性麻木：**[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)** 和 **[LI10 手三里](/point/LI10.md)**（《百症赋》）。
