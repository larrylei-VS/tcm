### XÌNHUÌ
![[DU-22 囟会.png|250]]
#### Location

At the top of the head on the midline, 2 cun posterior to the anterior hairline.

#### Location Notes

i.Divide the distance between [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) and the anterior hairline in half and locate Xìnhuì DU-22 at 0.5 cun anterior to this midpoint; ii. Locate 1 cun posterior to [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23); iii. If the anterior hairline is indistinct, locate it 3 cun superior to the glabella and 5 cun anterior to [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** this point should not be needled in infants whose fontanelle has not yet closed.

#### Actions

- Benefits the nose
- Eliminates wind and benefits the head

#### Indications

- Nosebleed, nasal congestion, inability to distinguish the fragrant from the foul, excessive nasal discharge in children, pain of the nose, nasal polyps.
- Head wind, chronic headache, deficiency and cold of the brain, bursting headache due to excessive consumption of alcohol, dizziness, visual dizziness, chronic and acute childhood fright wind.
- Blue-green (qing) complexion, red and swollen face, swelling of the skin of the head, dandruff.
- Somnolence, fright palpitations.

#### Commentary

As is often the case, a point such as Xìnhuì DU-22 which is considered of minimal importance in modern clinical practice, was clearly ascribed greater significance in classical texts, witnessed by the many traditional indications and combinations. It shares with many points of the head region the ability to pacify interior wind and treat various disorders of the head. Its application in nose disorders, however, is overshadowed by [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23), and in head wind, headache and dizziness, by [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20).

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for nosebleed, nasal congestion, inability to distinguish the fragrant from the foul, excessive nasal discharge in children, pain of the nose, nasal polyps, head wind, chronic headache, deficiency and cold of the brain, bursting headache due to excessive consumption of alcohol, dizziness, visual dizziness, chronic and acute childhood fright wind, blue-green (qing) complexion, red and swollen face, swelling of the skin of the head, dandruff, somnolence, fright palpitations.

#### Combinations

- Somnolence: Xìnhuì DU-22 and [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) (Supplementing Life).
- Childhood fright epilepsy: Xìnhuì DU-22, [Qiándǐng DU-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-21), [Běnshén GB-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-13) and [Tiānzhù BL-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-10) (Supplementing Life).
- Head wind: Xìnhuì DU-22 and [Yùzhěn BL-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-9) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Head wind and dizziness: Xìnhuì DU-22, [Shéntíng DU-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-24) and [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23) (Supplementing Life).
- Obstruction of the nose with inability to distinguish the fragrant from the foul: Xìnhuì DU-22, [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23), [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) and [Chéngguāng BL-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-6) (Supplementing Life)

### 1. [DU22 囟会](/point/DU22.md)

#### 1.1. Fontanelle Meeting 囟會

#### 1.2. 定位

位于头顶中线，前发际后2寸。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

1. 将[DU20 百会](/point/DU20.md)与前发际间距平分，[DU22 囟会](/point/DU22.md)位于此中点前0.5寸；  
2. 位于[DU23 上星](/point/DU23.md)后1寸；  
3. 若前发际不清，前发际位于眉心上3寸，[DU20 百会](/point/DU20.md)前5寸。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

平刺，深0.5至1寸。  
**注意：** 囟门未闭的婴儿禁针此穴。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 利鼻  
- 祛风益头  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 鼻衄、鼻塞、不能辨香臭、小儿鼻涕多、鼻痛、鼻息肉。  
- 头风、慢性头痛、脑虚寒、酒过爆头痛、眩晕、视眩、急慢性小儿惊风。  
- 青面色、面赤肿、头皮肿、头屑。  
- 嗜睡、惊悸。  

#### 1.7. 论述

现代临床中[DU22 囟会](/point/DU22.md)重要性较低，但在经典文献中意义较大，见多指征及配伍。它与头部多穴共能平内风及治头病，但在鼻病中被[DU23 上星](/point/DU23.md)盖过，在头风、头痛、眩晕中被[DU20 百会](/point/DU20.md)盖过。  

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 使用较少。  
- 用于鼻衄、鼻塞、不能辨香臭、小儿鼻涕多、鼻痛、鼻息肉、头风、慢性头痛、脑虚寒、酒过爆头痛、眩晕、视眩、急慢性小儿惊风、青面色、面赤肿、头皮肿、头屑、嗜睡、惊悸。  

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 嗜睡：[DU22 囟会](/point/DU22.md) + [DU20 百会](/point/DU20.md)（《补命》）。  
- 小儿惊痫：[DU22 囟会](/point/DU22.md) + [DU21 前顶](/point/DU21.md) + [GB13 本神](/point/GB13.md) + [BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)（《补命》）。  
- 头风：[DU22 囟会](/point/DU22.md) + [BL9 玉枕](/point/BL9.md)（《百症赋》）。  
- 头风及眩晕：[DU22 囟会](/point/DU22.md) + [DU24 神庭](/point/DU24.md) + [DU23 上星](/point/DU23.md)（《补命》）。  
- 鼻塞不能辨香臭：[DU22 囟会](/point/DU22.md) + [DU23 上星](/point/DU23.md) + [DU20 百会](/point/DU20.md) + [BL6 承光](/point/BL6.md)（《补命》）。
