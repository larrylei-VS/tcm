### Vital Region Shu GĀOHUĀNGSHŪ
![[BL-43 膏肓.png|250]]
#### Location

3 cun lateral to the midline, level with the lower border of the spinous process of the fourth thoracic vertebra (T4) and level with [Juéyīnshū BL-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-14).

#### Location Notes

When the shoulder is relaxed, the three cun line corresponds to the medial border of the scapula.

#### Needling

Oblique insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun. Caution: deep perpen-dicular or deep oblique needling in a medial direction carries a substantial risk of causing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Tonifies and nourishes the Lung, Heart, Kidneys, Spleen and Stomach
- Nourishes yin and clears heat
- Calms the spirit
- Fosters the original qi
- Resolves phlegm

#### Indications

- All kinds of deficiency, Lung deficiency-taxation, deficiency-taxation, the five taxations and the seven injuries, cough, asthma, coughing blood, night sweating, spontaneous sweating, steaming bone disorder, consumption, emaciation.
- Poor memory, pounding of the Heart, insomnia, phlegm-fire mania.
- Seminal emission, seminal emission with dreams, impotence.
- Feebleness and deficiency of the Spleen and Stomach, undigested food (in the stool), oesophageal constriction, weariness of the four limbs, vomiting blood.
- Visual dizziness, dizziness, phlegm disease, pain of the back and shoulder.

#### Commentary

The name 'Gaohuang' can be translated as 'Vital Region'. This concept, implying one of the deepest and most funda-mental regions of the body, is first mentioned in Master Zuo-jiu's Tradition of the Spring and Autumn Annals (580 BCE), one of the earliest textual references to acupuncture. The Prince of Jin who was gravely ill sent for the famous Doctor Yi Huan. After examining the patient, Doctor Huan declared that the disease had settled in the gaohuang region (between the Heart and the diaphragm) and hence "it cannot be purged, it cannot be reached [by needling], herbs will not penetrate it, there is nothing to be done"[61](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references).

Gāohuāngshū BL-43 was comprehensively discussed in a text called Method of Moxibustion at Gaohuangshu by Zhuang Zhuo in 1128. Zhuang was inspired to write this book after being cured of various diseases, including malaria and beri-beri, by three hundred moxa cones burnt on Gāohuāngshū BL-43. He measured the location of Gāohuāngshū BL-43 on people of various body builds and gave diagrams and specific instructions for locating the point.

In discussing this point, Sun Si-miao in the Thousand Ducat Formulas simply states "Gāohuāngshū BL-43, there is no [disorder] that it cannot treat" and "once moxibustion is completed, it causes a person's yang qi to be healthy and full". Many classical texts say that Gāohuāngshū BL-43 is contraindicated to needling and emphasise its treatment by moxibustion, for example the Illustrated Classic of Acupuncture Points on the Bronze Man recommends the application of a hundred or even as many as three hundred moxa cones to Gāohuāngshū BL-43. Early texts, however, stress that after moxibustion at Gāohuāngshū BL-43, moxibustion should also be applied to points below the umbilicus, e.g. [Qìhǎi REN-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-6) and [Guānyuán REN-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-4), in order to conduct downwards the heat thus generated.

Careful examination of the classical indications of this point demonstrate its actions on the Lung, Heart, Kidneys, Spleen and Stomach. Like [Fèishū BL-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-13) and [Pòhù BL-42](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-42), it is able to treat severe deficiency (especially of the yin) of the Lung, and is indicated for consumption, cough, coughing of blood, steaming bone disorder, night sweating and emaciation. By both nourishing and calming the Heart it is applicable in the treatment of poor memory, pounding of the Heart, insomnia and phlegm-fire mania. Acting on the Kidneys, it is able to treat seminal emission, with or without dreams, and impotence. By strengthening the middle jiao it is able to treat feebleness and deficiency of the Spleen and Stomach, undigested food in the stool and weariness of the four limbs. So great was the tonifying action of Gāohuāngshū BL-43 considered to be, that it was said to strengthen the original qi and treat every kind of deficiency, the five types of taxation and the seven injuries. Finally, it is interesting to note that Gāohuāngshū BL-43 was also indicated for 'phlegm diseases' and Sun Si-miao in his Supplement to the Thousand Ducat Formulas says it "stops phlegm in chronic disease". The importance of phlegm as a pathological factor in difficult and chronic diseases is long documented and is reflected in such sayings as "The hundred diseases all pertain to phlegm", "Strange diseases often involve phlegm" and "There is no place that phlegm cannot reach".

#### Summary of clinical application

- Tonifies extreme deficiency of the Lung, Heart, Kidneys, Spleen and Stomach: Lung deficiency taxation, cough, asthma, coughing blood, night sweating, spontaneous sweating, steaming bone disorder, consumption, emaciation, poor memory, pounding of the Heart, insomnia, seminal emission, impotence, feebleness and deficiency of the Spleen and Stomach, undigested food (in the stool), weariness of the four limbs, vomiting blood.
- “Gaohuangshu BL-43, there is no [disorder] that it cannot treat” (_Thousand Ducat Formulas)._

#### Combinations

- The hundred syndromes of deficiency-taxation: moxa Gāohuāngshū BL-43, Huanmen (M-BW-6) and the Four Flowers [[Géshū BL-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-17) and [Dǎnshū BL-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-19)] (Compilation).
- Consumption: Gāohuāngshū BL-43 and [Pòhù BL-42](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-42) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Spontaneous sweating: moxa Gāohuāngshū BL-43, [Dàzhuī DU-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-14) and [Fùliū KID-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-7) (Divine Moxibustion).
- Poor memory: Gāohuāngshū BL-43, [Shéndào DU-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-11), [Yōumén KID-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-21) and [Lièquē LU-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-7) (Supplementing Life).


### **[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)** (Gāohuāngshū)

* **英文名称：** Vital Region Shu
* **中文名称：** 膏肓俞
* **国际代码：** BL-43
* **穴位属性：** **[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)** 是膀胱经第二侧线上的穴位，与**[BL14 厥阴俞](point/BL14.md)** 平齐。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第四胸椎（T4）棘突下缘水平，距中线旁开3寸，与**[BL14 厥阴俞](point/BL14.md)** 平齐。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 肩部放松时，旁开3寸线对应肩胛骨内侧缘。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺0.3至0.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直深刺或向内侧深斜刺有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 补益肺、心、肾、脾、胃
* 滋阴清热
* 宁心安神
* 培补元气
* 化痰

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

各种虚损，肺虚劳，虚劳，五劳七伤，咳嗽，哮喘，咳血，盗汗，自汗，骨蒸潮热，痨瘵，消瘦。
记忆力差，心动不安，失眠，痰火狂躁。
遗精，梦遗，阳痿。
脾胃虚弱，大便完谷不化，噎膈，四肢倦怠，呕血。
视物眩晕，眩晕，痰饮病，背部和肩部疼痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

“膏肓”之名可译为“生命要害区域”。这个概念暗示着身体最深层、最基础的区域之一，最早见于《左传》（公元前580年），这是最早提及针灸的文本之一。晋景公病重，请来名医医缓。医缓诊察后宣告病已入膏肓之区（心膈之间），因此“弗可为也，病在膏肓之上，针弗及焉，药弗及焉，无可为也”。

**[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)** 在庄绰1128年所著的《膏肓俞穴灸法》中被全面讨论。庄绰在被此穴艾灸三百壮治愈疟疾和脚气等多种疾病后，受启发撰写此书。他测量了不同体型人群**[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)** 的位置，并提供了定位图和具体说明。

在讨论此穴时，孙思邈在《千金方》中简单地指出“**[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)**，无所不治”，“一旦灸毕，则令人阳气康健饱满”。许多经典著作指出**[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)** 禁针，并强调艾灸治疗，例如《铜人腧穴针灸图经》推荐对**[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)** 施灸一百甚至三百壮。然而，早期文献强调，在对**[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)** 施灸后，也应在脐下穴位（如**[REN6 气海](point/REN6.md)** 和**[REN4 关元](point/REN4.md)**）施灸，以将所产生的热量下引。

仔细审查此穴的经典主治，表明其对肺、心、肾、脾、胃都有作用。像**[BL13 肺俞](point/BL13.md)** 和**[BL42 魄户](point/BL42.md)** 一样，它能够治疗肺部严重虚损（特别是阴虚），主治痨瘵，咳嗽，咳血，骨蒸潮热，盗汗和消瘦。通过滋养和安神，它可用于治疗记忆力差，心动不安，失眠和痰火狂躁。作用于肾脏，它能治疗遗精（无论是否伴梦）和阳痿。通过强健中焦，它能治疗脾胃虚弱，大便完谷不化和四肢倦怠。**[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)** 的补益作用被认为非常强大，据说它能培补元气，治疗各种虚损，五劳七伤。最后，有趣的是，**[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)** 也主治“痰饮病”，孙思邈在《千金方翼方》中说它“止久病之痰”。痰作为疑难慢性病中的病理因素，其重要性早已被证实，并体现在“百病皆由痰作祟”、“怪病多痰”和“痰无处不到”等俗语中。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 补益肺、心、肾、脾、胃的极度虚损：肺虚劳，咳嗽，哮喘，咳血，盗汗，自汗，骨蒸潮热，痨瘵，消瘦，记忆力差，心动不安，失眠，遗精，阳痿，脾胃虚弱，大便完谷不化，四肢倦怠，呕血。
* “**[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)**，无所不治”（《千金方》）。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 虚劳百病：灸**[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)**、肓门（经外穴 **[MBW6 肓门](point/MBW6.md)**）和四花穴（**[BL17 膈俞](point/BL17.md)** 和 **[BL19 胆俞](point/BL19.md)**）（《聚英》）。
* 虚劳：**[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)** 和 **[BL42 魄户](point/BL42.md)**（《百症赋》）。
* 自汗：灸**[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)**、**[DU14 大椎](point/DU14.md)** 和 **[KID7 复溜](point/KID7.md)**（《神灸经纶》）。
* 记忆力差：**[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)**、**[DU11 神道](point/DU11.md)**、**[KID21 幽门](point/KID21.md)** 和 **[LU7 列缺](point/LU7.md)**（《资生经》）。
