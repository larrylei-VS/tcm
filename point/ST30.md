### Rushing Chong QÌCHŌNG
![[ST-30 气冲.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Stomach channel with the Penetrating vessel
- Point of the Sea of Water and Grain

#### Location

On the lower abdomen, 2 cun lateral to the midline, level with the superior border of the pubic symphysis ([Qūgǔ REN-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-2)).

#### Location Notes

The 2 cun line is located halfway between the midline and the palpable lateral border of the rectus abdominis muscle at the level of the umbilicus.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.  
**Caution:** in thin patients, _i_. deep insertion in a superior direction may penetrate the peritoneal cavity or a full bladder (the patient should therefore be asked to empty the bladder before needling); _ii_. deep insertion in an inferior direction in the male may penetrate the spermatic cord.

#### Actions

- Regulates qi in the lower jiao
- Regulates the Penetrating vessel
- Subdues running piglet qi

#### Indications

- Hypogastric pain, sudden fullness and distention of the abdomen, fullness of the abdomen with inability to lie down, twisting pain of the abdomen, heat in the abdomen, heat in the body with abdominal pain, hardness below the umbilicus, retention of urine and faeces, heat in the Large Intestine, prolapse of the rectum, injury by cold leading to heat in the Stomach, hot painful urinary dysfunction, stone oedema.
- Irregular menstruation, sudden amenorrhoea, abnormal uterine bleeding, infertility, disorders related to childbirth, retention of the placenta, difficult lactation.
- Swelling and pain of the external vagina, swelling and pain of the penis, pain of the testicles, retraction and pain of both testicles, shan disorder, impotence, pain of the lumbar region with difficulty in turning, fullness of the lateral costal region.
- Running piglet qi, qi rushing upwards to attack the Heart, foetus (foetal qi) rushes up to attack the Heart.

#### Commentary

The name of this point Qichong may be translated as 'Rushing Qi' or 'Pouring Qi'. The character 'chong' is the same as in the Penetrating vessel (chong mai). This name reflects both the ability of Qìchōng ST-30 to regulate the circulation of qi in the lower abdomen and its status as the point where the Penetrating vessel emerges on the abdomen. Qìchōng ST-30 has a broad action in treating many disorders affecting this area. By regulating qi, Qìchōng ST-30 may be used to disperse stagnation, pain, cold and heat in the genital region, intestines, Bladder and the abdomen as a whole, and is indicated for such disorders as pain, fullness and distention of the lower abdomen, abdominal heat, twisting pain of the abdomen, heat in the Large Intestine, retention of urine and faeces, hot painful urinary dysfunction, stone oedema etc.

The Penetrating vessel emerges at the perineum and meets the Stomach yangming channel at Qìchōng ST-30. The Penetrating vessel influences the lower abdomen as a whole, including the genital organs, and especially helps regulate the function of the uterus and menstruation. Qìchōng ST-30 is therefore indicated for such genital disorders as swelling and pain of the penis and vagina, pain and retraction of the testicles, impotence etc., and such gynaecological and obstetrical disorders as irregular menstruation, sudden amenorrhoea, abnormal uterine bleeding, infertility, retention of the placenta and difficult lactation.

According to the Classic of Difficulties, when the Penetrating vessel is diseased, there will be upsurging qi and acute abdominal disturbance. Qìchōng ST-30, located on the lower abdomen and the point where the Penetrating vessel emerges, is the single most important point in the treatment of running piglet qi. According to the Essentials from the Golden Cabinet "Running piglet disorder arises from the lower abdomen; it rushes up to the throat with such ferocity that the patient feels he is close to death. It attacks and then remits. It is brought about by fear and fright". Running piglet qi primarily arises when stagnant Liver qi transforms to heat, or when Kidney yang deficiency leads to accumulation of cold in the lower jiao. In both cases, qi is violently discharged and rushes upwards along the Penetrating vessel causing great agitation and anxiety. Qìchōng ST-30 is also indicated for foetal qi rushing up to the Heart. This condition, described by Zhu Dan-xi, manifests as distention, fullness and pain of the abdomen and Heart in a pregnant woman.

In the chapter 'Discourse On the Seas' in the Spiritual Pivot[17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-stomach-channel/references) Qìchōng ST-30 is given as the upper point of the 'sea of water and grain' ([Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) is the lower point). According to this passage, when the sea of water and grain is in excess, there is abdominal fullness, and when it is deficient there is hunger with inability to eat. Finally, the Essential Questions18 includes Qìchōng ST-30 among the eight points to clear heat from the Stomach (bilateral Qìchōng ST-30, [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36), [Shàngjùxū ST-37](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-37) and [Xiàjùxū ST-39](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-39)).

#### Summary of clinical application

- Disperses stagnation and pain, cold and heat, in the lower abdomen, the male and female genital regions, the uterus, intestines and Bladder: hypogastric pain, fullness, distention twisting pain and heat in the abdomen, hardness below the umbilicus, retention of urine and faeces, heat in the Large Intestine, prolapse of the rectum, hot painful urinary dysfunction, irregular menstruation, sudden amenorrhoea, abnormal uterine bleeding, infertility, retention of the placenta, swelling and pain of the external vagina, swelling and pain of the penis, pain of the testicles, retraction and pain of both testicles, shan disorder, impotence.

#### Combinations

- Insomnia: Qìchōng ST-30 and [Zhāngmén LIV-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-13) (Supplementing Life).
- Prolapse of the rectum: Qìchōng ST-30, [Dàchángshū BL-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-25), [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20), [Chángqiáng DU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-1), [Jiānjǐng GB-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-21) and [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) (Compilation).
- Stone oedema of the upper abdomen: moxa Qìchōng ST-30, [Rángǔ KID-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-2), [Sìmǎn KID-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-14) and [Zhāngmén LIV-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-13) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### [ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md) (QÌCHŌNG)

* **英文名称：** Rushing Chong
* **中文名称：** 气冲
* **国际代码：** ST-30
* **穴位属性：** [ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md) 是胃经与冲脉的交会穴，水谷之海的穴位。

#### 定位 (Location)

位于下腹部，距前正中线旁开2寸，平耻骨联合上缘（[REN2 曲骨](point/REN2.md)）。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 旁开2寸线位于脐水平的前正中线与可触及的腹直肌外缘之间中点。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直刺1至1.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 瘦弱患者需注意：
    * 向上深刺可能刺穿腹膜腔或充满的膀胱（因此，针刺前应请患者排空膀胱）；
    * 男性向下深刺可能刺伤精索。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 调理下焦气机
* 调理冲脉
* 平息奔豚气

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

少腹疼痛，腹部突然胀满，腹部胀满不能平卧，腹部绞痛，腹部发热，身热腹痛，脐下硬结，大小便潴留，大肠有热，脱肛，伤寒胃热，热淋（小便涩痛），石水（水肿伴小便不利）。
月经不调，突然闭经，崩漏（异常子宫出血），不孕症，产后相关疾病，胞衣不下，乳汁量少。
阴部肿痛，阴茎肿痛，睾丸疼痛，双侧睾丸缩引疼痛，疝气，阳痿，腰部疼痛转侧困难，胁肋部胀满。
奔豚气，气上冲心，胎气上冲心。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

此穴名称“气冲”可译为“奔涌之气”或“冲涌之气”。“冲”字与冲脉的“冲”相同。此名称既反映了[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md) 能够调理下腹部气血运行的能力，也反映了其作为冲脉在腹部起始点的地位。[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md) 在治疗影响此区域的多种疾病方面具有广泛作用。通过理气，[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md) 可用于散结、止痛、温散寒邪和清解热邪，治疗生殖器区域、肠道、膀胱和整个腹部的各种疾病，如少腹疼痛、胀满、腹部绞痛、腹热、大肠有热、大小便潴留、热淋、石水等。

冲脉起于会阴，在[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md) 处与足阳明胃经交会。冲脉影响整个下腹部，包括生殖器官，尤其有助于调理子宫和月经功能。因此，[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md) 主治阴茎和阴部肿痛、睾丸疼痛和缩引、阳痿等生殖器疾病，以及月经不调、突然闭经、崩漏、不孕症、胞衣不下和乳汁量少等妇科和产科疾病。

根据《难经》记载，冲脉病变时，会有气上冲和急性腹部不适。[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md) 位于下腹部，是冲脉的起始点，是治疗奔豚气最重要的穴位。根据《金匮要略》记载：“奔豚气从少腹上冲咽喉，发作欲死，复还止。皆从惊恐得之。”奔豚气主要发生于肝气郁结化热，或肾阳不足导致寒邪积聚下焦时。两种情况下，气机都剧烈冲动，沿冲脉向上奔涌，引起剧烈烦躁和焦虑。[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md) 也主治胎气上冲心。这种朱丹溪所描述的病症表现为孕妇腹部和心窝部胀满疼痛。

在《灵枢·卫气失常》篇中，[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md) 被列为“水谷之海”的上点（[ST36 足三里](point/ST36.md) 是下点）。根据该篇记载，水谷之海有余时，腹部胀满；不足时，则饥饿而不能进食。最后，《素问·热论》将[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md) 列为清胃热的八个穴位之一（双侧[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md)、[ST36 足三里](point/ST36.md)、[ST37 上巨虚](point/ST37.md) 和[ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md)）。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 散结止痛，温散寒邪，清解热邪，治疗下腹部、男女生殖器区域、子宫、肠道和膀胱的各种疾病：少腹疼痛、胀满、腹部绞痛、腹热、脐下硬结、大小便潴留、大肠有热、脱肛、热淋、月经不调、突然闭经、崩漏、不孕症、胞衣不下、阴部肿痛、阴茎肿痛、睾丸疼痛、双侧睾丸缩引疼痛、疝气、阳痿。
* 治疗奔豚气和胎气上冲心。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 失眠：[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md) 和[LIV13 章门](point/LIV13.md)（《资生经》）。
* 脱肛：[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md)，[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)，[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)，[DU1 长强](point/DU1.md)，[GB21 肩井](point/GB21.md) 和[LI4 合谷](point/LI4.md)（《聚英》）。
* 上腹部石水（水肿）：灸[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md)，[KID2 然谷](point/KID2.md)，[KID14 四满](point/KID14.md) 和[LIV13 章门](point/LIV13.md) 三十壮（《千金方》）。
