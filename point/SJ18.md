### Spasm Vessel  Chīmài
![[SJ-18 瘈脉.png|250]]
#### Location 

Posterior to the ear, in a small depression on the mastoid bone, one third of the distance along a curved line drawn from [Yìfēng SJ-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-17) to [Jiǎosūn SJ-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-20) following the line of the rim of the ear.

#### Needling 

Subcutaneous insertion along the course of the channel 0.3 to 0.5 cun, or prick to bleed.

#### Actions 

- Benefits the ears
- Calms fright and pacifies wind

#### Indications 

- Tinnitus, deafness, pain behind the ear.
- Headache, head wind, vomiting, diarrhoea, seminal emission, discharge from the eye, dimness of vision.
- Infantile fright epilepsy, clonic spasm, fright and fear.

#### Summary of clinical application 

- Rarely used.
- Tinnitus, deafness, pain behind the ear.
- Headache, head wind, vomiting, diarrhoea, seminal emission, discharge from the eye, dimness of vision.
- Infantile fright epilepsy, clonic spasm, fright and fear.

#### Combinations 

- Childhood epileptic convulsions, vomiting and diarrhoea, fright and fear: Qīmài SJ-18 and [Chángqiáng DU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-1) (Systematic Classic).
- Head wind and pain behind the ear: Qīmài SJ-18 and [Wángǔ GB-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-12) (Supplementing Life).
- Wind headache: Qīmài SJ-18, [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2), [Chéngguāng BL-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-6), [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23), [Sīzhúkōng SJ-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-23) and [Ěrhéliáo SJ-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-22) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[SJ18 瘛脉](/point/SJ18.md)** (QĪMÀI)

* **英文名称：** Spasm Vessel
* **中文名称：** 瘛脉
* **国际代码：** SJ-18

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于耳后，乳突上小凹陷中，沿耳轮边缘，从**[SJ17 翳风](/point/SJ17.md)** 画一弧线至**[SJ20 角孙](/point/SJ20.md)**，此线的三分之一处。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

沿经络皮下浅刺0.3至0.5寸，或点刺出血。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利耳部
* 镇惊息风

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

耳鸣，耳聋，耳后疼痛。
头痛，头风，呕吐，腹泻，遗精，目翳（眼睛流脓），视力减退。
小儿惊痫，痉挛，惊恐。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SJ18 瘛脉](/point/SJ18.md)** 临床上较少使用。其名称“瘛脉”意指其与痉挛和脉动相关，暗示其在治疗**惊风**和**抽搐**方面的作用。此穴位于耳后，是三焦经的循行要道，与耳部有密切联系。

此穴主要用于缓解**耳部疾病**，如耳鸣、耳聋和耳后疼痛。它对**头痛**、**头风**、**呕吐**、**腹泻**、**遗精**、**目翳**和**视力减退**等症状也有一定的辅助疗效。

**[SJ18 瘛脉](/point/SJ18.md)** 的**镇惊息风**功效使其适用于治疗**小儿惊痫**、**痉挛**和**惊恐**。这些症状往往与肝风内动或心神不宁有关，**[SJ18 瘛脉](/point/SJ18.md)** 通过调理三焦气机和安抚心神来发挥作用。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治耳鸣，耳聋，耳后疼痛。
* 主治头痛，头风，呕吐，腹泻，遗精，目翳，视力减退。
* 治疗小儿惊痫，痉挛，惊恐。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 小儿惊厥，呕吐腹泻，惊恐：**[SJ18 瘛脉](/point/SJ18.md)** 和 **[DU1 长强](/point/DU1.md)**（《针灸甲乙经》）。
* 头风和耳后疼痛：**[SJ18 瘛脉](/point/SJ18.md)** 和 **[GB12 完骨](/point/GB12.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 头风：**[SJ18 瘛脉](/point/SJ18.md)**，**[BL2 攒竹](/point/BL2.md)**，**[BL6 承光](/point/BL6.md)**，**[BL23 肾俞](/point/BL23.md)**，**[SJ23 丝竹空](/point/SJ23.md)** 和 **[SJ22 耳和髎](/point/SJ22.md)**（《千金方》）。
