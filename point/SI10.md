### Upper Arm Shu NĀOSHŪ
![[SI-10 臑俞.png|250]]
- _Meeting point of the Small Intestine and Bladder channels with the Yang Linking and Yang Motility vessels_

#### Location

On the posterior aspect of the shoulder, in the depression inferior to the scapular spine, directly superior to the posterior axillary crease when the arm hangs in the adducted position.

#### Location Notes

Slide a finger directly upwards from [Jiānzhēn SI-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-9) until it falls into the depression just below the scapular spine.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Benefits the shoulder
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Pain and swelling of the shoulder that radiates to the scapula, weakness and pain of the arm and shoulder, inability to raise the shoulder.
- Chills and fever, scrofula.

#### Commentary

Nāoshū SI-10 is a commonly used point for pain and stiffness of the shoulder, especially its posterior aspect, and allows deep penetration into the joint. Its influence on the shoulder is augmented by the fact that Nāoshū SI-10 is also a meeting point of the Yang Motility and Yang Linking vessels, which traverse the posterior (Yang Linking) and the superior and anterior (Yang Motility) portions of the shoulder. In clinical practice Nāoshū SI-10 is often used in combination with points such as [Jiānyú LI-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-15) and [Jiānliáo SJ-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-14).

#### Summary of clinical application

- Frequently used local point for disorders of the shoulder, scapula and upper arm. Meeting point of three channels which traverse the shoulder joint, the Small Intestine channel (posterior shoulder), the Yang Linking vessel (posterior shoulder) and the Yang Motility vessel (anterior and superior shoulder).

#### Combinations

- Acute pain of the posterior of the shoulder: Nāoshū SI-10 and [Yǎnglǎo SI-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-6).
- Immobility and pain of the shoulder joint: First needle [Tiáokǒu ST-38](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-38), then Nāoshū SI-10, [Jiānliáo SJ-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-14), [Jiānyú LI-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-15), [Jùgǔ LI-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-16), [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11) and [Yǎnglǎo SI-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-6).

### **[SI10 臑俞](point/SI10.md)** (NĀOSHŪ)

* **英文名称：** Upper Arm Shu
* **中文名称：** 臑俞
* **国际代码：** SI-10
* **穴位属性：** **[SI10 臑俞](point/SI10.md)** 是小肠经与膀胱经、阳维脉、阳跷脉的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于肩部后侧，肩胛冈下方凹陷中，手臂内收时，在腋后纹头正上方。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 从 **[SI9 肩贞](point/SI9.md)** 向上方滑动手指，直到其落入肩胛冈下方凹陷处。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺1至1.5寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利肩部
* 通经活络，止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

肩部疼痛肿胀放射至肩胛，手臂和肩部无力疼痛，不能抬肩。
恶寒发热，瘰疬。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SI10 臑俞](point/SI10.md)** 是治疗肩部疼痛和僵硬的常用穴位，特别是肩部后侧，可以深刺入关节。其对肩部的影响因其是阳跷脉和阳维脉的交会穴而增强，这两条经脉分别循行于肩部后侧（阳维脉）以及肩部上部和前侧（阳跷脉）。临床上 **[SI10 臑俞](point/SI10.md)** 常与 **[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md)** 和 **[SJ14 肩髎](point/SJ14.md)** 等穴位联合使用。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗肩部、肩胛和上臂疾病的常用局部穴位。
* 此穴是循行于肩关节的小肠经（肩部后侧）、阳维脉（肩部后侧）和阳跷脉（肩部前侧和上部）三条经脉的交会穴。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 肩部后侧急性疼痛：**[SI10 臑俞](point/SI10.md)** 和 **[SI6 养老](point/SI6.md)**。
* 肩关节活动不利和疼痛：先针 **[ST38 条口](point/ST38.md)**，然后针 **[SI10 臑俞](point/SI10.md)**，**[SJ14 肩髎](point/SJ14.md)**，**[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md)**，**[LI16 巨骨](point/LI16.md)**，**[LI11 曲池](point/LI11.md)** 和 **[SI6 养老](point/SI6.md)**。
