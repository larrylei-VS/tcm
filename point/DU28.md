### YÍNJIĀO
![[DU-28 齿龈.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Governing vessel with the Conception vessel and Stomach channel

#### Location

Inside the mouth, in the superior frenulum, at the junction of the upper lip and the gum.

#### Location Notes

The superior frenulum is the midline band of fibrous tissue connecting the upper lip and gum.

#### Needling

i. Oblique insertion directed superiorly 0.2 to 0.3 cun; ii. Prick to bleed.

#### Actions

- Clear heat and benefits the gums
- Benefits the nose and eyes

#### Indications

- Redness, swelling and pain of the gums, erosion of the gums, bleeding from the gums.
- Nasal polyps, nasal sores, nasal congestion, pain of the forehead and glabella.
- Excessive lacrimation, redness, itching and pain of the inner canthus, pain of the eyes and lack of clarity of vision, superficial visual obstruction.
- Red face with agitation, jaundice, stiffness of the neck with inability to turn the head.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for redness, swelling and pain of the gums, erosion of the gums, bleeding from the gums, nasal polyps, nasal sores, nasal congestion, pain of the forehead and glabella, excessive lacrimation, redness, itching and pain of the inner canthus, pain of the eyes and lack of clarity of vision, superficial visual obstruction, red face with agitation, jaundice, stiffness of the neck with inability to turn the head.

#### Combinations

- Stiffness and pain of the neck with inability to turn the head: Yínjiāo DU-28 and [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16) (Supplementing Life).
- Madness: Yínjiāo DU-28 and [Rénzhōng DU-26](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-26) (Systematic Classic).

# 1. [DU28 龈交](/point/DU28.md)

## 1.1. Gum Intersection 龈交

### 1.2. 定位

位于口腔内，上唇系带处，上唇与牙龈交界。

### 1.3. 定位说明

上唇系带为连接上唇与牙龈的中线纤维带。

### 1.4. 针刺方法

1. 向上斜刺，深0.2至0.3寸；  
2. 刺血。

### 1.5. 作用

- 清热利龈  
- 利鼻目  

### 1.6. 主治

- 牙龈红肿痛、龈蚀、龈出血。  
- 鼻息肉、鼻疮、鼻塞、前额眉心痛。  
- 多泪、内眦红痒痛、眼痛视不明、翳障。  
- 面赤烦躁、黄疸、颈僵不能转头。  

### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 使用较少。  
- 用于牙龈红肿痛、龈蚀、龈出血、鼻息肉、鼻疮、鼻塞、前额眉心痛、多泪、内眦红痒痛、眼痛视不明、翳障、面赤烦躁、黄疸、颈僵不能转头。  

### 1.8. 配伍

- 颈痛不能转头：[DU28 龈交](/point/DU28.md) + [DU16 风府](/point/DU16.md)（《补命》）。  
- 癫狂：[DU28 龈交](/point/DU28.md) + [DU26 人中](/point/DU26.md)（《系统经典》）。
