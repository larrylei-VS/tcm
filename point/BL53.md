### Bladder’s Vitals BĀOHUĀNG
![[BL-53 胞肓.png|250]]
#### Location

3 cun lateral to the midline, at the level of the spinous process of the second sacral vertebra.

#### Location Notes

i. For guidance on locating the sacral spinious processes see [Surface Anatomy](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-location-and-needling/surface-anatomy); ii. When the shoulder is relaxed, the three cun line corresponds to the medial border of the scapula; iii. Alternatively this point may be located midway between the midline and the lateral edge of the buttock, when the lateral edge is firmly pressed in with the palm of the hand.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Benefits the lumbar region, activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Regulates the lower jiao and benefits urination

#### Indications

- Pain and stiffness of the lumbar region, sciatica.
- Hardness and fullness of the hypogastrium, retention of urine, dribbling urination, oedema, inability to urinate or defecate.
- Borborygmus, abdominal distention, undigested food (in the stool), aversion to wind.

#### Commentary

Bāohuāng BL-53 is a commonly used point in the treatment of stiffness and pain of the lumbar region and sciatica. Along with [Zhìbiān BL-54](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-54) and [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30), Bāohuāng BL-53 is one of the points that should be palpated, and needled if tender, in all cases where pain radiates to the buttock and down the leg.

Bāohuāng BL-53 (Bladder's Vitals) lies lateral to [Pángguāngshū BL-28](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-28), the back-shu point of the Bladder, and in common with many of the points of the sacral region it is able to regulate urination, and to a lesser extent defecation, being indicated for retention of urine, dribbling urination, oedema and inability to urinate or defecate.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Commonly used for lumbar pain and sciatica. Along with [Zhìbiān BL-54](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-54) and [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30), this is one of the points that should be palpated, and needled if tender, in all cases where pain radiates to the buttock and down the leg.
- Regulates urination and defecation: retention of urine, dribbling urination, oedema, inability to urinate or defecate, borborygmus, abdominal distention, undigested food (in the stool).

#### Combinations

- Pain of the lumbar region and aversion to cold: Bāohuāng BL-53, [Cìliáo BL-32](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-32) and [Chéngjīn BL-56](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-56) (Supplementing Life).
- Retention of urine: Bāohuāng BL-53 and [Zhìbiān BL-54](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-54) (Supplementing Life).
- Fullness of the hypogastrium: Bāohuāng BL-53 and [Gānshū BL-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-18) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[BL53 胞肓](/point/BL53.md)** (BĀOHUĀNG)

* **英文名称：** Bladder’s Vitals
* **中文名称：** 胞肓
* **国际代码：** BL-53

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第二骶椎棘突水平，距中线旁开3寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 骶椎棘突的定位请参考《Surface Anatomy》（此为原文引用，请根据您的实际参考资料定位）；
    * 肩部放松时，旁开3寸线对应肩胛骨内侧缘；
    * 也可定位在中线与臀部外侧缘之间中点（当手掌用力按压臀部外侧缘时）。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺1至1.5寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利腰部，通经活络，止痛
* 调理下焦，宣利小便

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

腰部疼痛僵硬，坐骨神经痛。
小腹硬满，尿潴留，小便淋漓不尽，水肿，大小便不利。
肠鸣，腹胀，大便完谷不化，恶风。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL53 胞肓](/point/BL53.md)** 在治疗腰部僵硬疼痛和坐骨神经痛方面是常用穴位。与**[BL54 秩边](/point/BL54.md)** 和**[GB30 环跳](/point/GB30.md)** 一起，当疼痛放射至臀部并向下肢延伸时，此穴是所有病例中都应触诊并（若有压痛）针刺的穴位之一。

**[BL53 胞肓](/point/BL53.md)**（膀胱之肓），位于膀胱背俞穴**[BL28 膀胱俞](/point/BL28.md)** 外侧，与骶部许多穴位一样，能够调理小便，并能在一定程度上调理大便，主治尿潴留、小便淋漓不尽、水肿以及大小便不利。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 常用于**腰痛**和**坐骨神经痛**。与**[BL54 秩边](/point/BL54.md)** 和**[GB30 环跳](/point/GB30.md)** 一起，当疼痛放射至臀部并向下肢延伸时，此穴是所有病例中都应触诊并（若有压痛）针刺的穴位之一。
* 调理小便和大便：尿潴留，小便淋漓不尽，水肿，大小便不利，肠鸣，腹胀，大便完谷不化。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 腰部疼痛伴怕冷：**[BL53 胞肓](/point/BL53.md)**，**[BL32 次髎](/point/BL32.md)** 和 **[BL56 承筋](/point/BL56.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 尿潴留：**[BL53 胞肓](/point/BL53.md)** 和 **[BL54 秩边](/point/BL54.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 少腹胀满：**[BL53 胞肓](/point/BL53.md)** 和 **[BL18 肝俞](/point/BL18.md)**（《千金方》）。
