### Empty Basin QUĒPÉN
![[ST-12 缺盆.png|250]]
#### Location

In the supraclavicular area, posterior to the superior border of the clavicle and at its midpoint, 4 cun lateral to the midline, on the mamillary line.

#### Location Notes

This point should be located and needled behind the clavicle, paying strict attention to the caution below.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun along the posterior border of the clavicle.  
**Note:** according to the Systematic Classic of Acupuncture and Moxibustion and the Illustrated Supplement to the Classic of Categories this point is contra-indicated in pregnancy.  
**Caution:** deep or posterior insertion may injure the subclavian vessels or puncture the lung.

#### Actions

- Descends Lung qi and clears heat from the chest
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Cough, coughing blood, dyspnoea, fullness of the chest, heat and fullness in the chest, chills and fever with sweating, absence of sweating, oedema, scrofula, throat painful obstruction.
- Pain of the supraclavicular fossa, pain of the shoulder that radiates to the neck, numbness and painful obstruction of the upper limb, inability to raise the arm, lumbar pain with inability to turn.

#### Commentary

Quepen ST-12, is a meeting point of the Stomach channel with all the primary yang channels except the Bladder, and its principal function is to descend rebellion of qi. This is reflected both by its ability to treat rebellion of Lung qi manifesting as cough, dyspnoea and fullness of the chest, as well as by the traditional observation that it is contraindicated in pregnancy. _The Essential Questions_[8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-stomach-channel/references) includes Quepen ST-12 among the eight points (bilateral Quepen ST-12, [Dazhu BL-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-11), [Zhongfu LU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-1) and [Fengmen BL-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-12)) to clear heat from the chest.

Quepen ST-12 is also indicated for pain of the supraclavicular fossa, pain of the shoulder that radiates to the neck, and numbness and pain of the upper limb.

#### Summary of clinical application

- For rebellion of Lung qi manifesting as cough, dyspnoea and fullness of the chest.
- For pain of the supraclavicular fossa, pain of the shoulder that radiates to the neck, and numbness and painful obstruction of the upper limb.

#### Combinations

- Cough: Quēpén ST-12, [Shānzhōng REN-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-17) and [Jùguè REN-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-14) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Coughing and spitting blood: Quēpén ST-12, [Xīnshū BL-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-15), [Gānshū BL-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-18), [Jùguè REN-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-14) and [Jiūwéi REN-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-15) (Supplementing Life).
- Heat in the chest: Quēpén ST-12 and [Qímén LIV-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-14) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Pain of the shoulder and back: Quēpén ST-12, [Tiānyǒu SJ-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-16), [Shéndào DU-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-11), [Dàzhù BL-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-11), [Tiāntú REN-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-22), [Shuǐdào ST-28](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-28) and [Jùgǔ LI-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-16) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### [ST12 缺盆](point/ST12.md) (QUĒPÉN)

* **英文名称：** Empty Basin
* **中文名称：** 缺盆
* **国际代码：** ST-12
* **穴位属性：** [ST12 缺盆](point/ST12.md) 是胃经与除膀胱经外的所有阳明经脉的交会穴。

#### 定位 (Location)

位于锁骨上窝中央，锁骨上缘中点，距前正中线旁开4寸，在乳头线上。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 此穴应在锁骨后方定位和针刺，并严格注意下述注意事项。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

沿锁骨后缘垂直刺0.3至0.5寸。

* **注意 (Note):** 根据《针灸甲乙经》和《类经图翼》，此穴孕妇禁针。
* **注意 (Caution):**
    * 深刺或向后方针刺可能损伤锁骨下血管或刺破肺脏。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 肃降肺气，清泄胸热
* 通经活络，止痛

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

咳嗽，咳血，呼吸困难，胸中胀满，胸部灼热胀满，恶寒发热伴汗出，无汗，水肿，瘰疬，咽喉肿痛。
锁骨上窝疼痛，肩部疼痛放射至颈部，上肢麻木痹痛，不能抬臂，腰痛不能转侧。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST12 缺盆](point/ST12.md) 是胃经与除膀胱经外所有阳经的交会穴，其主要功能是肃降气逆。这体现在它能够治疗肺气上逆导致的咳嗽、呼吸困难和胸部胀满，以及传统上认为孕妇禁针。
《素问·热论》将[ST12 缺盆](point/ST12.md) 列为清胸热的八个穴位之一（包括双侧[ST12 缺盆](point/ST12.md)、[BL11 大杼](point/BL11.md)、[LU1 中府](point/LU1.md) 和[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)）。

[ST12 缺盆](point/ST12.md) 也主治锁骨上窝疼痛，肩部疼痛放射至颈部，以及上肢麻木疼痛。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 治疗肺气上逆引起的咳嗽、呼吸困难和胸部胀满。
* 治疗锁骨上窝疼痛，肩部疼痛放射至颈部，以及上肢麻木痹痛。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 咳嗽：[ST12 缺盆](point/ST12.md)，[REN17 膻中](point/REN17.md) 和[REN14 巨阙](point/REN14.md)（《千金方》）。
* 咳嗽吐血：[ST12 缺盆](point/ST12.md)，[BL15 心俞](point/BL15.md)，[BL18 肝俞](point/BL18.md)，[REN14 巨阙](point/REN14.md) 和[REN15 鸠尾](point/REN15.md)（《资生经》）。
* 胸中热：[ST12 缺盆](point/ST12.md) 和[LIV14 期门](point/LIV14.md)（《千金方》）。
* 肩背疼痛：[ST12 缺盆](point/ST12.md)，[SJ16 天牖](point/SJ16.md)，[DU11 神道](point/DU11.md)，[BL11 大杼](point/BL11.md)，[REN22 天突](point/REN22.md)，[ST28 水道](point/ST28.md) 和[LI16 巨骨](point/LI16.md)（《千金方》）。
