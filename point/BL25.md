### Large Intestine Shu DÀCHÁNGSHŪ
![[BL-25 大肠俞.png|250]]
- Back-Shu point of the Large Intestine

#### Location

1.5 cun lateral to the lower border of the spinous process of the fourth lumbar vertebra (L4).

#### Location Notes

Locate at the visible highest point of the paraspinal muscles.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Regulates the intestines
- Transforms stagnation and alleviates pain
- Strengthens the lumbar region and legs

#### Indications

- Borborygmus, cold or damp (dong) diarrhoea, undigested food (in the stool), dysenteric disorder, blood in the stool, intestinal abscess, difficulty in urination and defecation, constipation, prolapse of the rectum.
- Distention and pain of the abdomen, hypogastric pain, distention and fullness of the hypogastrium, twisting pain of the lower abdomen, cutting pain of the umbilical region, inability to eat and drink, remains thin despite much eating.
- Lumbar pain, stiffness and rigidity of the lumbar spine, pain and painful obstruction of the lower limbs, atrophy disorder, dysmenorrhoea.

#### Commentary

Dàchángshū BL-25 is the back-shu point of the Large Intestine, where the qi of the Large Intestine emanates from the interior to the body surface. It is an important point to regulate the function of the Large Intestine fu, and its application is very broad.

When the Spleen is deficient or suffers from dampness, there may be loose stools, diarrhoea, undigested food in the stool, blood in the stools, borborygmus and inability to gain weight despite eating copiously. In combination with points that tonify the Spleen and Stomach, Dàchángshū BL-25 is an important point to firm and tonify the intestines. When there is stagnation (of qi, blood, damp-heat, toxic heat etc.) in the intestinal region, there will be distention, pain, cutting pain, fullness, constipation, intestinal abscess etc. In combination with other suitable points, Dàchángshū BL-25 is able to regulate qi and dissipate stagnation. When the central qi is deficient, or when damp-heat sinks downwards, there may be prolapse of the rectum. Dàchángshū BL-25 is a valuable adjacent point used in the treatment of this disorder, in combination with points such as [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) and [Chángqiáng DU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-1), as well as points which treat the underlying disharmony.

Finally Dàchángshū BL-25 is an important local point in the treatment of lumbar pain, and like many points of the lower lumbar region is also indicated for painful obstruction and atrophy disorder of the lower limbs.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Treats all disorders of the intestines: borborygmus, cold or damp diarrhoea, undigested food (in the stool), dysentric disorder, blood in the stool, intestinal abscess, difficulty in urination and defecation, constipation, prolapse of the rectum, distention and pain of the abdomen, distention, pain and fullness of the hypogastrium, twisting pain of the lower abdomen, cutting pain of the umbilical region, inability to eat and drink, remains thin despite much eating.
- Treats the lumbar region and legs: lumbar pain, stiffness and rigidity of the lumbar spine, pain and painful obstruction of the lower limbs, atrophy disorder.

#### Combinations

- Cold or damp (dong) diarrhoea with undigested food: Dàchángshū BL-25 and [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23) (Supplementing Life).
- Undigested food (in the stool): Dàchángshū BL-25, [Liángmén ST-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-21), [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36), [Sānyīnjiāo SP-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-6), [Xiàwǎn REN-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-10), [Sānjiāoshu BL-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-22) and [Xuánshū DU-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-5) (Supplementing Life).
- Incontinence of faeces: Dàchángshū BL-25 and [Guānyuán REN-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-4) (Great Compendium).
- Borborygmus: Dàchángshū BL-25, [Tàibái SP-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-3), [Gōngsūn SP-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-4) and [Sānjiāoshu BL-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-22) (Supplementing Life).
- Prolapse of the rectum: Dàchángshū BL-25, [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20), [Chángqiáng DU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-1), [Jiānjǐng GB-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-21), [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) and [Qìchōng ST-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-30) (Compilation).
- Prolapse of the rectum in children: Dàchángshū BL-25, [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) and [Chángqiáng DU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-1) (Great Compendium).
- Pain of intestinal abscess: Dàchángshū BL-25, [Xiàngǔ ST-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-43) and [Tàibái SP-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-3) (Great Compendium).
- Difficult ingestion with desire to drink fluids: Dàchángshū BL-25 and [Zhōuróng SP-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-20) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)** (Dàchángshū)

* **英文名称：** Large Intestine Shu
* **中文名称：** 大肠俞
* **国际代码：** BL-25
* **穴位属性：** **[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)** 是大肠的背俞穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第四腰椎（L4）棘突下缘旁开1.5寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 位于脊柱旁肌肉的最高点。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺1至1.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直深刺有损伤腹膜腔内器官（如肠道）的风险。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 调理肠道功能
* 化积止痛
* 强健腰腿

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

肠鸣，寒泻或湿泻（洞泻），大便完谷不化，痢疾，便血，肠痈，大小便困难，便秘，脱肛。
腹部胀痛，小腹疼痛，少腹胀满，下腹部绞痛，脐周绞痛，不思饮食，多食不胖。
腰痛，腰椎僵硬强直，下肢疼痛痹痛，痿证，痛经。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)** 是大肠的背俞穴，大肠之气从内向体表输布。与所有背俞穴一样（特别是阳经背俞穴），**[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)** 在最深层次上调理其相应的腑。其主要作用是**调理大肠功能**，包括传导糟粕、吸收水液。

此穴是治疗各种肠道疾病的重要穴位，无论是**腹泻**、**痢疾**（包括便血、脓血便和里急后重），还是**便秘**。它通过调理大肠气机，促进传导，化湿清热，从而改善肠道功能紊乱引起的腹痛、腹胀、肠鸣等症状。

当脾虚或感受湿邪时，可能出现便溏、腹泻、大便完谷不化、便血、肠鸣以及多食不胖等症状。**[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)** 与补益脾胃的穴位联合使用时，是固摄和补益肠道的重要穴位。当肠道区域有气滞（气、血、湿热、热毒等）时，会出现胀满、疼痛、绞痛、实满、便秘、肠痈等。**[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)** 与其他合适穴位联合使用时，能够理气消滞。当中央之气虚弱，或湿热下陷时，可能出现脱肛。**[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)** 是治疗此症的重要邻近穴位，常与 **[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)** 和 **[DU1 长强](point/DU1.md)** 等穴以及治疗基础病因的穴位联合使用。

最后，**[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)** 是治疗**腰痛**的重要局部穴位，像腰部下段的许多穴位一样，它也主治下肢的**痹痛**和**痿证**。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* **治疗所有肠道疾病**：肠鸣，寒泻或湿泻，大便完谷不化，痢疾，便血，肠痈，大小便困难，便秘，脱肛，腹部胀痛，小腹疼痛，少腹胀满，下腹部绞痛，脐周绞痛，不思饮食，多食不胖。
* **治疗腰部和腿部**：腰痛，腰椎僵硬强直，下肢疼痛痹痛，痿证。
