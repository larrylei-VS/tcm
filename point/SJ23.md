### Silken Bamboo Hollow SĪZHÚKŌNG
![[SJ-23 丝竹空.png|250]]
#### Location

In the depression on the supraorbital margin, at the lateral end of the eyebrow.

#### Needling

Transverse insertion, medially along the eyebrow or posteriorly, 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Note:** according to several classical texts, this point is contraindicated to moxibustion.

#### Actions

- Eliminates wind and alleviates pain
- Benefits the eyes

#### Indications

- Headache, one-sided headache, head wind, aversion to wind and cold, dizziness, visual dizziness, tetany, epilepsy with foaming at the mouth, periodic mania with foaming at the mouth, childhood umbilical wind, toothache.
- Upward staring eyes, deviation of the face and eye, blurred vision, pain and redness of the eyes, twitching of the eyelids and eyebrows, ingrown eyelash.

#### Commentary

Sīzhúkōng SJ-23 is an important local point for the treatment of headache and disorders of the eye, eyelid and eyebrow. According to the Investigation Into Points Along the Channels Sīzhúkōng SJ-23 is indicated for "all disorders of the head, face, eyebrows and eyes whether swelling, redness, itching or numbness".

Sīzhúkōng SJ-23 is also able to pacify interior wind and soothe convulsions, and is indicated for dizziness, epilepsy, mania, foaming at the mouth and childhood umbilical wind. Childhood umbilical wind refers to infection of the umbilicus in the new born which gives rise to convulsions, opisthotonos and lockjaw.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Local point for the treatment of headache and disorders of the eye, eyelid and eyebrow: one-sided headache, head wind, blurred vision, pain and redness of the eyes, twitching of the eyelids and eyebrows, ingrown eyelash.
- Pacifies interior wind: dizziness, epilepsy, upward staring eyes, mania, foaming at the mouth.

#### Combinations

- One-sided or generalised wind headache that is difficult to cure: join Sīzhúkōng SJ-23 subcutaneously with [Shuàigǔ GB-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-8) (Song of the Jade Dragon).
- One-sided or generalised headache: Sīzhúkōng SJ-23, [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20) and [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) (Great Compendium).
- One-sided or generalised head wind: Sīzhúkōng SJ-23, [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20), [Qiándǐng DU-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-21), [Shéntíng DU‑24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-24), [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23), [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20), [Hegu LI‑4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4), [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2) and [Tóuwéi ST-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-8) (Great Compendium).
- Wind headache: Sīzhúkōng SJ-23, [Ěrhéliáo SJ-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-22), [Qīmài SJ-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-18), [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2), [Chéngguāng BL-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-6) and [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Wind epilepsy with upward staring eyes: Sīzhúkōng SJ-23, [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60) and [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) (Great Compendium).
- Toothache: Sīzhúkōng SJ-23 and [Ěrmén SJ-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-21) (One Hundred Symptoms)

### **[SJ23 丝竹空](point/SJ23.md)** (SĪZHÚKŌNG)

* **英文名称：** Silken Bamboo Hollow
* **中文名称：** 丝竹空
* **国际代码：** SJ-23

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于眉梢外侧，眶上缘凹陷中。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

横刺，沿眉毛向内侧或向后方刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Note):** 根据几部经典文献，此穴禁灸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 祛风止痛
* 明目

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

头痛，偏头痛，头风，恶风畏寒，眩晕，视物眩晕，破伤风，癫痫伴口吐白沫，周期性发狂伴口吐白沫，小儿脐风，牙痛。
眼睛上视，口眼歪斜，视物模糊，眼睛红肿疼痛，眼睑和眉毛跳动，倒睫。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SJ23 丝竹空](point/SJ23.md)** 是治疗头痛和眼部、眼睑、眉毛疾病的重要局部穴位。根据《循经考穴编》记载，**[SJ23 丝竹空](point/SJ23.md)** 主治“头面眉目一切肿、赤、痒、麻之症”。

**[SJ23 丝竹空](point/SJ23.md)** 还能**平息内风**，**止痉**，主治眩晕、癫痫、发狂、口吐白沫和小儿脐风。小儿脐风指新生儿脐部感染引起抽搐、角弓反张和牙关紧闭。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗头痛以及眼部、眼睑和眉毛疾病的局部穴位：偏头痛，头风，视物模糊，眼睛红肿疼痛，眼睑和眉毛跳动，倒睫。
* 平息内风：眩晕，癫痫，眼睛上视，发狂，口吐白沫。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 难治的偏头痛或全头风：**[SJ23 丝竹空](point/SJ23.md)** 皮下透刺至 **[GB8 率谷](point/GB8.md)**（《玉龙歌》）。
* 偏头痛或全头痛：**[SJ23 丝竹空](point/SJ23.md)**，**[GB20 风池](point/GB20.md)** 和 **[LI4 合谷](point/LI4.md)**（《针灸大成》）。
* 偏头痛或全头风：**[SJ23 丝竹空](point/SJ23.md)**，**[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)**，**[DU21 前顶](point/DU21.md)**，**[DU24 神庭](point/DU24.md)**，**[DU23 上星](point/DU23.md)**，**[GB20 风池](point/GB20.md)**，**[LI4 合谷](point/LI4.md)**，**[BL2 攒竹](point/BL2.md)** 和 **[ST8 头维](point/ST8.md)**（《针灸大成》）。
* 头风：**[SJ23 丝竹空](point/SJ23.md)**，**[SJ22 耳和髎](point/SJ22.md)**，**[SJ18 瘈脉](point/SJ18.md)**，**[BL2 攒竹](point/BL2.md)**，**[BL6 承光](point/BL6.md)** 和 **[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 风痫伴眼睛上视：**[SJ23 丝竹空](point/SJ23.md)**，**[BL60 昆仑](point/BL60.md)** 和 **[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)**（《针灸大成》）。
* 牙痛：**[SJ23 丝竹空](point/SJ23.md)** 和 **[SJ21 耳门](point/SJ21.md)**（《百症赋》）。
