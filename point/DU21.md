### QIÁNDǏNG
![[DU-21 前顶.png|250]]
#### Location

At the top of the head on the midline, 1.5 cun directly anterior to [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) and 3.5 cun posterior to the anterior hairline.

#### Location Notes

i. Divide the distance between [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) and the anterior hairline into thirds. Qiándǐng DU-21 lies slightly posterior to the junction of the first and middle thirds; ii. If the anterior hairline is indistinct, locate it 3 cun superior to the glabella and 5 cun anterior to [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** this point should not be needled in infants whose fontanelle has not yet closed.

#### Actions

- Eliminates wind and treats convulsions
- Benefits the head

#### Indications

- Head wind, visual dizziness, wind epilepsy, childhood fright epilepsy, acute and chronic childhood fright wind, clonic spasm.
- Pain and swelling of the vertex, swelling and redness of the face, oedema, aversion to wind-cold, copious clear nasal discharge.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for head wind, visual dizziness, wind epilepsy, childhood fright epilepsy, acute and chronic childhood fright wind, clonic spasm, pain and swelling of the vertex, swelling and redness of the face, oedema, aversion to wind-cold, copious clear nasal discharge.

#### Combinations

- Childhood fright epilepsy: Qiándǐng DU-21, [Xìnhuì DU-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-22), [Běnshén GB-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-13) and [Tiānzhù BL-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-10) (Supplementing Life).
- Wind dizziness and one-sided headache: Qiándǐng DU-21, [Hòudǐng DU-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-19) and [Hànyàn GB-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-4) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- One-sided or generalised head wind: Qiándǐng DU-21, [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20), [Shéntíng DU-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-24), [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23), [Sīzhúkōng SJ-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-23), [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20), [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4), [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2) and [Tóuwéi ST-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-8) (Great Compendium).
- Inability to speak following windstroke: Qiándǐng DU-21 and [Shéntíng DU-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-24) (Song of the Jade Dragon).
- Sudden swelling of the face: Qiándǐng DU-21, [Xìnhuì DU-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-22), [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23), [Xiàngǔ ST-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-43) and [Gōngsūn SP-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-4) (Supplementing Life).
- Redness and swelling of the face: Qiándǐng DU-21, [Xìnhuì DU-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-22), [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23), [Nǎohù DU-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-17) and [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Deficiency swelling of the face: Qiándǐng DU-21 and [Rénzhōng DU-26](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-26) (One Hundred Symptoms).

### 1. [DU21 前顶](point/DU21.md)

#### 1.1. Anterior Summit 前頂

#### 1.2. 定位

位于头顶中线，[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)正前方1.5寸，前发际后3.5寸。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

1. 将[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)与前发际间距分为三等分，[DU21 前顶](point/DU21.md)位于第一与第二等分交界处稍后；  
2. 若前发际不清，前发际位于眉心上3寸，[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)前5寸。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

平刺，深0.5至1寸。  
**注意：** 囟门未闭的婴儿禁针此穴。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 祛风治痫  
- 益头  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 头风、视眩、风痫、小儿惊痫、急慢性小儿惊风、痉挛。  
- 巅顶痛肿、面肿赤、水肿、恶风寒、鼻涕清多。  

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 使用较少。  
- 用于头风、视眩、风痫、小儿惊痫、急慢性小儿惊风、痉挛、巅顶痛肿、面肿赤、水肿、恶风寒、鼻涕清多。  

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 小儿惊痫：[DU21 前顶](point/DU21.md) + [DU22 囟会](point/DU22.md) + [GB13 本神](point/GB13.md) + [BL10 天柱](point/BL10.md)（《补命》）。  
- 风眩及偏头痛：[DU21 前顶](point/DU21.md) + [DU19 后顶](point/DU19.md) + [GB4 颔厌](point/GB4.md)（《千金方》）。  
- 单侧或全身头风：[DU21 前顶](point/DU21.md) + [DU20 百会](point/DU20.md) + [DU24 神庭](point/DU24.md) + [DU23 上星](point/DU23.md) + [SJ23 丝竹空](point/SJ23.md) + [GB20 风池](point/GB20.md) + [LI4 合谷](point/LI4.md) + [BL2 攒竹](point/BL2.md) + [ST8 头维](point/ST8.md)（《大成》）。
