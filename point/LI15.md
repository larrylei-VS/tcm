### Shoulder Bone JIĀNYÚ
![[LI-15 肩髃.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Large Intestine channel with the Yang Motility vessel

#### Location

In the depression which lies anterior and inferior to the acromion, at the origin of the deltoid muscle.  
**Note:** [Jiānliáo SJ-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-14) is located in the depression which lies posterior and inferior to the acromion).

#### Location Notes

If the arm is abducted, the two hollows will be more easily palpable and are often visible.

#### Needling

i. With the arm abducted, perpendicular insertion directed towards the centre of the axilla, 1 to 1.5 cun; ii. Transverse-oblique insertion directed distally towards the elbow, 1.5 to 2 cun.

#### Actions

- Dispels wind-damp, alleviates pain and benefits the shoulder joint
- Eliminates wind and regulates qi and blood
- Regulates qi and dissipates phlegm nodules

#### Indications

- Shoulder pain, weakness of the shoulder, wind-damp of the shoulder, heat sensation in the shoulder, redness and swelling in the shoulder, inability to raise the arm to the head, contraction and numbness of the arm, hemiplegia, wind paralysis, windstroke, wind atrophy disorder, wind disease, heat in the four limbs, inability to turn the head, painful obstruction of the fingers.
- Wind-heat urticaria, injury by cold with heat that does not dissipate.
- Scrofula, goitre, seminal emission due to taxation, hypertension.

#### Commentary

Jiānyú LI-15, a meeting point of the Large Intestine channel with the Yang Motility vessel, is also reached by the Lung and Bladder sinew channels, the Large Intestine divergent channel and the Small Intestine luo-connecting channel. Jiānyú LI-15 is considered the pre-eminent point for treating the shoulder, and clinically the majority of shoulder disorders affect this region.

Stiffness, pain, immobility or weakness of the shoulder or frozen shoulder may derive from: i. injury by exterior pathogenic wind, cold, damp or heat, i.e. painful obstruction, ii. stagnation of qi and blood from traumatic injury, misuse or overuse, or iii. deficiency of qi and blood from overuse, old age or prolonged obstruction of the channel. In all these cases Jiānyú LI-15 may be used, and in clinical practice is commonly combined with [Jiānliáo SJ-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-14). As far as needling is concerned, there are two principal methods. For pain and immobility of the shoulder joint, the arm should be abducted as far as is comfortable and supported on a rolled pillow, whilst the needle is directed into the shoulder joint towards the centre of the axilla. For pain extending down the arm, however, Jiānyú LI-15 is usually needled with a transverse-oblique insertion towards the elbow joint.

The shoulder joint is the pivot of the arm. As Jiānyú LI-15 is the foremost point affecting the shoulder, lies on yangming channel which is "abundant in qi and blood", and is a meeting point with the Lung sinew and Small Intestine luo-connecting channels, it is a vital point to promote circulation of qi and blood in the upper limb as a whole, and is indicated for all kinds of atrophy disorder and painful obstruction and hemiplegia. For this purpose it is commonly combined in the 'chain and lock' method with points [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11) and [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4).

Jiānyú LI-15 is also indicated in the treatment of urticaria due to attack of exterior wind-heat. This highlights the action of Jiānyú LI-15 in eliminating wind, which is emphasised in a variety of classical references to wind paralysis, windstroke, wind atrophy disorder and more generally simply 'wind disease'. Like [Bìnào LI-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-14), Jiānyú LI-15 is also indicated for scrofula and goitre.

Finally, according to the Essential Questions[16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-large-intestine-channel/references) Jiānyú LI-15 is one of the 'eight points for draining heat from the extremities' (although in fact only seven are listed) namely bilateral [Yúnmén LU-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-2), bilateral Jiānyú LI-15, bilateral [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) and [Yāoshū DU-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-2).

#### Summary of clinical application

- Primary point for all shoulder disorders: pain, weakness, winddamp painful obstruction, heat sensation, redness and swelling, inability to raise the arm to the head.
- Important point for all disorders of the arm, especially hemiplegia, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction. Often combined with Quchi LI-11 and Hegu LI-4 in the ‘chain and lock’ point association method.
- Eliminates wind in the form of urticaria, wind paralysis, windstroke, wind atrophy disorder etc.

#### Combinations

- Wasting and weakness of the arm with inability to raise the arm to the head: Jiānyú LI-15 and [Bìnào LI-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-14) (Preserving Life).
- Redness and swelling of the upper arm with pain of the joint: Jiānyú LI-15, [Zhǒuliáo LI-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-12) and [Wàngǔ SI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-4) (Great Compendium).
- Heat sensation of the shoulder with inability to turn the head: Jiānyú LI-15, [Jiānzhēn SI-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-9) and [Guānchōng SJ-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-1) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Contraction of the elbow: Jiānyú LI-15, [Chǐzé LU-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-5), [Xiǎohǎi SI-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-8), [Jiānshǐ PC-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-5), [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-7), [Hòuxī SI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-3) and [Yújì LU-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-10) (Great Compendium).
- Windstroke with one-sided withering and incessant pain: Jiānyú LI-15, [Xuánzhōng GB-39](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-39), [Tàixī KID-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-3), [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11), [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) and [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60) (Great Compendium).
- Urticaria from extreme heat: Jiānyú LI-15 and [Yángxī LI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-5) (One Hundred Symptoms).

### [LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md) (JIĀNYÚ)

- **英文名称：** Shoulder Bone
- **中文名称：** 肩髃
- **国际代码：** LI-15
- **穴位属性：** [LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md) 是大肠经与阳跷脉的交会穴。肺经筋、膀胱经筋、大肠经别、小肠经络也至此。

#### 定位 (Location)

位于肩峰前下方凹陷中，在三角肌起点处。

- **注意 (Note):** [SJ14 肩髎](point/SJ14.md) 位于肩峰后下方凹陷中。
- **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    - 外展手臂时，两个凹陷更容易触及，常可见到。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

i. 外展手臂，向腋窝中心直刺1至1.5寸； ii. 横斜刺，向下（朝向肘部）刺1.5至2寸。

#### 作用 (Actions)

- 祛风湿，止痛，利肩关节
- 祛风，调气血
- 调气散结（消散痰核）

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

肩部疼痛、无力、风湿痹痛、肩部发热、红肿，不能抬臂过头，手臂挛缩麻木，半身不遂，风痹，中风，风痿，风病，四肢发热，头不能转动，手指痹痛。

风热荨麻疹，伤寒邪热不散。

瘰疬，瘿瘤，劳淋（劳累后遗精），高血压。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md) 是大肠经与阳跷脉的交会穴，肺经筋、膀胱经筋、大肠经别、小肠经络也至此。[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md) 被认为是治疗肩部疾病最重要的穴位，临床上大多数肩部疾病都影响此区域。

肩部僵硬、疼痛、活动不利或无力（冻结肩）可能源于：i. 外邪风、寒、湿、热侵袭，即痹证；ii. 外伤、误用或过度劳损导致气血瘀滞；iii. 过度劳损、年老或经络长期阻塞导致气血亏虚。所有这些情况下都可使用[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md)，临床上常与[SJ14 肩髎](point/SJ14.md) 联合使用。针刺方法主要有两种。治疗肩关节疼痛活动不利时，手臂应尽量舒适地外展，用卷好的枕头支撑，针尖指向肩关节朝向腋窝中心。然而，治疗疼痛向下放射至手臂时，[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md) 通常采取横斜刺，针尖朝向肘关节。

肩关节是手臂的枢纽。[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md) 是影响肩部的首要穴位，位于“多气多血”的阳明经上，并且与肺经筋和小肠经络交会，是促进整个上肢气血运行的关键穴位，主治各种痿证、痹证和半身不遂。为此目的，它常与[LI11 曲池](point/LI11.md) 和[LI4 合谷](point/LI4.md) 联合使用，采用“链锁法”。

[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md) 也主治外感风热所致的荨麻疹。这突出了[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md) 具有祛风的作用，各种经典文献中提及的风痹、中风、风痿以及更广泛的“风病”都强调了这一点。与[LI14 臂臑](point/LI14.md) 一样，[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md) 也主治瘰疬和瘿瘤。

最后，根据《素问·热论》记载，[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md) 是“泻诸肢节之热”的八个穴位之一（尽管实际上只列出了七个），即双侧[LU2 云门](point/LU2.md)、双侧[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md)、双侧[BL40 委中](point/BL40.md) 和[DU2 腰俞](point/DU2.md)。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

治疗所有肩部疾病的主要穴位：疼痛、无力、风湿痹痛、发热感、红肿、不能抬臂过头。

治疗所有手臂疾病的重要穴位，特别是半身不遂、痿证和痹证。常与[LI11 曲池](point/LI11.md) 和[LI4 合谷](point/LI4.md) 联合使用，采用“链锁法”。

祛风，治疗荨麻疹、风痹、中风、风痿等。

治疗瘰疬、瘿瘤。

上臂红肿，关节疼痛：[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md)，[LI12 肘髎](point/LI12.md) 和[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)（《针灸大成》）。

肩部发热感，头不能转动：[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md)，[SI9 肩贞](point/SI9.md) 和[SJ1 关冲](point/SJ1.md)（《千金方》）。

肘部挛缩：[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md)，[LU5 尺泽](point/LU5.md)，[SI8 小海](point/SI8.md)，[PC5 间使](point/PC5.md)，[PC7 大陵](point/PC7.md)，[SI3 后溪](point/SI3.md) 和[LU10 鱼际](point/LU10.md)（《针灸大成》）。

中风偏枯，疼痛不止：[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md)，[GB39 悬钟](point/GB39.md)，[KID3 太溪](point/KID3.md)，[LI11 曲池](point/LI11.md)，[ST36 足三里](point/ST36.md) 和[BL60 昆仑](point/BL60.md)（《针灸大成》）。

壮热引起的荨麻疹：[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md) 和[LI5 阳溪](point/LI5.md)（《百症赋》）。
