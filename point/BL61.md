### Servant’s Respect PÚCĀN
![[BL-61 仆参.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Bladder channel with the Yang Motility vessel

#### Location

On the lateral side of the foot, 1.5 cun inferior to [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60), in a tender depression on the calcaneum.

#### Location Notes

The distance between the prominence of the lateral malleolus and the sole of the foot is 3 cun; locate Púcān BL-61 directly below [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60) and midway between this point and the sole.

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Relaxes the sinews, activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Headache, heavy head, heaviness of the head like a stone.
- Mania, manic raving, seeing ghosts, loss of consciousness, childhood epilepsy, vomiting, turbid painful urinary dysfunction.
- Lumbar pain, atrophy disorder of the leg, leg qi, sudden turmoil disorder with cramps, swelling of the knee, heel pain.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for headache, heavy head, heaviness of the head like a stone, mania, manic raving, seeing ghosts, loss of consciousness, childhood epilepsy, vomiting, turbid painful urinary dysfunction, lumbar pain, atrophy disorder of the leg, leg qi, sudden turmoil disorder with cramps, swelling of the knee, heel pain.

#### Combinations

- Cramps: Púcān BL-61, [Zhìyīn BL-67](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-67), [Jiěxī ST-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-41), [Qiūxū GB-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-40) and [Zúqiàoyīn GB-44](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-44) (Supplementing Life).

### **[BL61 仆参](point/BL61.md)** (PÚCĀN)

* **英文名称：** Servant’s Respect
* **中文名称：** 仆参
* **国际代码：** BL-61
* **穴位属性：** **[BL61 仆参](point/BL61.md)** 是膀胱经与阳跷脉的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于足外侧，**[BL60 昆仑](point/BL60.md)** 下方1.5寸，在跟骨的压痛凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 外踝尖到足底的距离为3寸；**[BL61 仆参](point/BL61.md)** 位于**[BL60 昆仑](point/BL60.md)** 直下方，且在此穴与足底之间的中点。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

横刺0.3至0.5寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 舒筋活络，止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

头痛，头重，头重如石。
癫狂，狂言谵语，见鬼，昏厥，小儿癫痫，呕吐，淋证（小便混浊疼痛）。
腰痛，腿部痿证，腿气，霍乱吐泻伴痉挛，膝关节肿胀，足跟痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL61 仆参](point/BL61.md)** 临床上较少使用。其名称“仆参”可能暗示此穴在下部，与足部的膀胱经和阳跷脉相关。作为膀胱经与阳跷脉的交会穴，它能够**舒筋活络，止痛**，主要用于缓解头痛、头重等头部症状，以及腰痛、腿部痿证和足跟痛等下肢疾病。

此穴也主治一系列情志和精神障碍，如癫狂、狂言谵语和见鬼，并可用于治疗小儿癫痫和昏厥。此外，它对呕吐和淋证等症状也有一定的辅助疗效，这与其调理气机、清热利湿的作用相关。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治头痛，头重，头重如石，癫狂，狂言谵语，见鬼，昏厥，小儿癫痫，呕吐，淋证（小便混浊疼痛），腰痛，腿部痿证，腿气，霍乱吐泻伴痉挛，膝关节肿胀，足跟痛。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 痉挛：**[BL61 仆参](point/BL61.md)**，**[BL67 至阴](point/BL67.md)**，**[ST41 解溪](point/ST41.md)**，**[GB40 丘墟](point/GB40.md)** 和 **[GB44 足窍阴](point/GB44.md)**（《资生经》）。
