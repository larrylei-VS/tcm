### Food Cavity SHÍDÒU
![[SP-17 食窦.png|250]]
#### Location

On the lateral side of the chest, in the fifth intercostal space, 6 cun lateral to the midline.

#### Location Notes

i. The intercostal spaces are most reliably counted downwards from the second intercostal space which lies immediately below the sternal angle. In males, the nipple almost invariably lies in the fourth intercostal space; ii. The six cun line is located two cun lateral to the mamillary line.

#### Needling

Transverse-oblique insertion along the intercostal space, 0.5 to 1 cun.  
Caution: perpendicular insertion, especially in thin patients, carries a substantial risk of inducing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Dissipates accumulation of food and fluid and promotes digestion

#### Indications

- Fullness of the chest and lateral costal region, ceaseless pain of the lateral costal region, abdominal distention, water swelling of the abdomen, oesophageal pain, pain of the diaphragm, belching, vomiting immediately after eating, undigested food (in the stool), borborygmus.
- Cough, swelling of the breasts, chronic Spleen malaria, jaundice.

#### Commentary

Shídòu SP-17 is also known by the alternative name of Mingguan (Gate of Life). According to the Book of Bian Que's Secrets Shídòu SP-17 "connects the real Spleen qi and cures thirty-six kinds of Spleen disease. In severe disease, when life is hanging by a thread, moxibustion at this point with 200-300 moxa cones will assure the patient's survival. Use this point in any major Spleen disease"[20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-spleen-channel/references). Despite this inspiring quotation, there are few references to this kind of application or combinations which include the point, in other classical texts, and Shídòu SP-17 is mainly indicated for pain of the lateral costal region, distention and pain of the abdomen and diaphragm with belching, vomiting and indigested food in the stool, all clear indications of food stagnation.

#### Summary of clinical application

- According to the Book of Bian Que’s Secrets Shidou SP-17 “connects the real Spleen qi and cures thirty-six kinds of Spleen disease. In severe disease, when life is hanging by a thread, moxibustion at this point with 200-300 moxa cones will assure the patient’s survival. Use this point in any major Spleen disease”.
- Rarely used in clinical practice.

### **[SP17 食窦](point/SP17.md)** (SHÍDÒU)

* **英文名称：** Food Cavity
* **中文名称：** 食窦
* **国际代码：** SP-17

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于胸部外侧，第五肋间隙中，距前正中线旁开6寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 肋间隙最可靠的计数方法是从胸骨角下方的第二肋间隙向下数。男性乳头几乎总位于第四肋间隙；
    * 旁开6寸线位于乳头线旁开2寸处。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

沿肋间隙横斜刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直刺入，特别是在瘦弱患者中，有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 消食化积，促进消化

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

胸胁胀满，胁肋部疼痛不止，腹胀，腹部水肿，食道疼痛，膈肌疼痛，嗳气，食后即吐，大便完谷不化，肠鸣。
咳嗽，乳房肿胀，慢性脾疟，黄疸。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SP17 食窦](point/SP17.md)** 又名“命关”。根据《扁鹊心书》记载，**[SP17 食窦](point/SP17.md)** “通真脾气，治三十六种脾病。重病危急时，在此穴艾灸200-300壮，可保病人不死。凡脾之大病者皆用之。”尽管有此振奋人心的引述，但其他经典文献中很少提及此类应用或包含此穴的组合。**[SP17 食窦](point/SP17.md)** 主要主治胁肋部疼痛，腹部和膈肌胀痛伴嗳气、呕吐和食不化，这些都是食积的明确指征。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 根据《扁鹊心书》记载，**[SP17 食窦](point/SP17.md)** “通真脾气，治三十六种脾病。重病危急时，在此穴艾灸200-300壮，可保病人不死。凡脾之大病者皆用之。”
* 临床上较少使用。
