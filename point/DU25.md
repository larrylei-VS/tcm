### SÙLIÁO
![[DU-25 素髎.png|250]]
#### Location

On the midline at the tip of the nose.

#### Needling

i. Perpendicular insertion 0.2 to 0.3 cun; ii. Transverse insertion directed superiorly 0.5 to 1 cun; iii. Prick to bleed.  
**Note:** according to several classical texts, this point is contraindicated to moxibustion.

#### Actions

- Benefits the nose

#### Indications

- Copious nasal discharge, rhinitis, inability to distinguish the fragrant from the foul, nasal obstruction, nasal sores, nosebleed, drinker's nose, nasal polyps, dyspnoea, hypotension.

#### Commentary

In recent times this point has been found to be effective in reducing alcohol intoxication by redirecting the route of elimination from the liver to the lung and thereby increasing the volume of alcohol present in the expired air. Other recent research has shown this point to be more effective than [Rénzhōng DU-26](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-26) in restoring loss of consciousness.

#### Summary of clinical application

- In recent times this point has been found to be effective in reducing alcohol intoxication.
- Indicated for copious nasal discharge, rhinitis, inability to distinguish the fragrant from the foul, nasal obstruction, nasal sores, nosebleed, drinker’s nose, nasal polyps, dyspnoea, hypotension.

#### Combinations

- Wind epilepsy: Sùliáo DU-25, [Shéntíng DU-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-24) and [Yǒngquǎn KID-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-1) (Great Compendium).

### 1. [DU25 素髎](/point/DU25.md)

#### 1.1. White Crevice 素髎

#### 1.2. 定位

位于鼻尖中线。

#### 1.3. 针刺方法

1. 垂直刺入，深0.2至0.3寸；  
2. 向上平刺，深0.5至1寸；  
3. 刺血。  
**注意：** 多部经典文献认为此穴禁灸。

#### 1.4. 作用

- 利鼻  

#### 1.5. 主治

- 鼻涕多、鼻炎、不能辨香臭、鼻塞、鼻疮、鼻衄、酒糟鼻、鼻息肉、呼吸困难、低血压。  

#### 1.6. 论述

近代研究发现[DU25 素髎](/point/DU25.md)能减轻酒醉，将酒精排泄路径从肝转向肺，增加呼气中酒精含量。另有研究显示其恢复昏迷效果优于[DU26 人中](/point/DU26.md)。  

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 近代发现有效减轻酒醉。  
- 用于鼻涕多、鼻炎、不能辨香臭、鼻塞、鼻疮、鼻衄、酒糟鼻、鼻息肉、呼吸困难、低血压。  

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 风痫：[DU25 素髎](/point/DU25.md) + [DU24 神庭](/point/DU24.md) + [KID1 涌泉](/point/KID1.md)（《大成》）。
