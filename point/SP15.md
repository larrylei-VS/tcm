### Great Horizontal DÀHÉNG
![[SP-15 大横.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Spleen channel with the Yin Linking vessel

#### Location

On the abdomen, in the depression at the lateral border of the rectus abdominis muscle level with the umbilicus.

#### Location Notes

This point is normally defined as 4 cun lateral to the midline, and on the abdomen the 4 cun line is located at the lateral border of the rectus abdominis muscle.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** i. in thin patients deep needling may penetrate the peritoneal cavity; ii. deep needling at this point may penetrate a substantially enlarged spleen or liver.

#### Actions

- Moves qi and regulates the intestines

#### Indications

- Lower abdominal pain, cold and pain of the lower abdomen, cold or damp (dong) diarrhoea, dysenteric disorder, constipation, sensation of heat in the hypo-gastrium with sighing.
- Propensity to sadness, sighing, inability to raise and move the four limbs, copious sweating.

#### Commentary

Dàhéng SP-15 is an important point for regulating the qi of the Large Intestine. It is indicated for the treatment of diarrhoea, especially of cold or damp type, and due to its ability to move the qi of the Large Intestine is also used in the treatment of constipation due to many different aetiologies. Both Dàhéng SP-15 and [Tiānshū ST-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-25), its neighbouring point, are effective in the treatment of a variety of disorders of the intestines. Clinically, however, [Tiānshū ST-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-25) is predominantly used for loose stools, diarrhoea and dysenteric disorder, whilst Dàhéng SP-15 is favoured for constipation.

Dàhéng SP-15 is also indicated and included in classical combinations for sadness, weeping and sighing. Whilst there is no special theoretical reason why Dàhéng SP-15 should have this action, it is interesting to compare it with its neighbouring points [Tàiyǐ ST-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-23) and [Huaroumen ST‑24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-24) which also have several psycho-emotional indications.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Commonly used for constipation, often in preference to Tianshu ST-25. Also indicated for cold or damp diarrhoea and dysenteric disorder
- Like Taiyi ST-23 and Huaroumen ST-24, treats disorders of the spirit: propensity to sadness, sighing.

#### Combinations

- Arched back with sorrowful weeping: Dàhéng SP-15 and [Tiānchōng GB-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-9) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Constipation: Dàhéng SP-15, [Dàchángshū BL-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-25) and [Zhīgōu SJ-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-6).

### **[SP15 大横](point/SP15.md)** (DÀHÉNG)

* **英文名称：** Great Horizontal
* **中文名称：** 大横
* **国际代码：** SP-15
* **穴位属性：** **[SP15 大横](point/SP15.md)** 是脾经与阴维脉的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于腹部，脐水平处，腹直肌外缘凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 此穴通常定义为距前正中线旁开4寸处，在腹部，旁开4寸线位于腹直肌可触及的外缘。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):**
    * 瘦弱患者深刺可能刺穿腹膜腔；
    * 深刺此穴可能刺穿明显肿大的脾脏或肝脏。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 行气，调理肠道

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

小腹疼痛，小腹冷痛，寒泻或湿泻（洞泻），痢疾，便秘，心下热感伴叹息。
易悲伤，叹息，四肢不能抬举活动，大量出汗。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SP15 大横](point/SP15.md)** 是调理大肠气机的重要穴位。它主治腹泻，特别是寒性或湿性腹泻，并且由于其行大肠气机的能力，也用于治疗多种病因引起的便秘。**[SP15 大横](point/SP15.md)** 和其邻近穴位 **[ST25 天枢](point/ST25.md)** 在治疗各种肠道疾病方面都有效。然而，临床上 **[ST25 天枢](point/ST25.md)** 主要用于便溏、腹泻和痢疾，而 **[SP15 大横](point/SP15.md)** 则更常用于治疗便秘。

**[SP15 大横](point/SP15.md)** 也主治悲伤、哭泣和叹息，并被纳入经典组合中。虽然没有特殊的理论原因解释 **[SP15 大横](point/SP15.md)** 具有此作用，但将其与邻近的 **[ST23 太乙](point/ST23.md)** 和 **[ST24 滑肉门](point/ST24.md)** 进行比较很有趣，这两个穴位也具有多种精神情志主治。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 常用于治疗便秘，通常优于 **[ST25 天枢](point/ST25.md)**。也主治寒泻或湿泻和痢疾。
* 像 **[ST23 太乙](point/ST23.md)** 和 **[ST24 滑肉门](point/ST24.md)** 一样，治疗情志障碍：易悲伤，叹息。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 弓背伴悲伤哭泣：**[SP15 大横](point/SP15.md)** 和 **[GB9 天冲](point/GB9.md)**（《百症赋》）。
* 便秘：**[SP15 大横](point/SP15.md)**，**[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)** 和 **[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)**。
