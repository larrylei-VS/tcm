### Middle Shoulder Shu JIĀNZHŌNGSHŪ
![[SI-15 肩中俞.png|250]]
#### Location

2 cun lateral to the lower border of the spinous process of C7 ([Dàzhuī DU-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-14)).

#### Location Notes

i. This point may be found two thirds of the distance between the midline and the medial border of the scapula, when the shoulder is in a relaxed position; ii. to locate C7 see page 68.

#### Needling

Oblique medial insertion towards the spine 0.5 to 1 cun.  
Caution: deep insertion inferiorly, especially in thin patients, carries a substantial risk of inducing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Descends Lung qi
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Cough, spitting blood, chills and fever, dimness of vision, pain of the shoulder and scapula.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for cough, spitting blood, chills and fever, dimness of vision, pain of the shoulder and scapula.

#### Combinations

- Rebellious qi cough: Jiānzhōngshū SI-15, [Tiānchí PC-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-1), [Tiāntú REN-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-22), [Shānzhōng REN-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-17) and [Jiěxī ST-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-41) (Supplementing Life).

### **[SI15 肩中俞](/point/SI15.md)** (JIĀNZHŌNGSHŪ)

* **英文名称：** Middle Shoulder Shu
* **中文名称：** 肩中俞
* **国际代码：** SI-15

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第七颈椎棘突下缘 (**[DU14 大椎](/point/DU14.md)**) 旁开2寸处。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 肩部放松时，此穴位于中线与肩胛骨内侧缘之间距离的三分之二处；
    * 定位第七颈椎（C7）的方法请参见“Page 68”（此处引用原文，请根据实际参考资料定位）。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺，向内侧（朝向脊柱）0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 向下深刺，特别是在瘦弱患者中，有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 肃降肺气
* 通经活络，止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

咳嗽，咳血，恶寒发热，视物模糊，肩胛疼痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SI15 肩中俞](/point/SI15.md)** 临床上较少使用。它主要通过肃降肺气来缓解咳嗽和咳血等症状。同时，其通经活络、止痛的功效使其适用于治疗肩胛疼痛。对于外感引起的恶寒发热，此穴也有一定的辅助作用。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治咳嗽，咳血，恶寒发热，视物模糊，肩胛疼痛。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 气逆咳嗽：**[SI15 肩中俞](/point/SI15.md)**，**[PC1 天池](/point/PC1.md)**，**[REN22 天突](/point/REN22.md)**，**[REN17 膻中](/point/REN17.md)** 和 **[ST41 解溪](/point/ST41.md)**（《资生经》）。
