### Second Crevice CÌLIÁO
![[BL-32 次髎.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Bladder and Gall Bladder channels

#### Location

Over the second posterior sacral foramen.

#### Location Notes

For guidance on locating the sacral foramen see [Surface Anatomy](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-location-and-needling/surface-anatomy "Surface Anatomy").

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun, or 1.5 to 2 cun through the foramen. Needling through the foramen is facilitated by a slightly oblique medial and inferior insertion.

#### Actions

- Regulates the lower jiao and facilitates urination and defecation
- Regulates menstruation and stops leucorrhoea
- Benefits the lumbar region and legs

#### Indications

- Painful urinary dysfunction, dark urine, retention of urine, enuresis, difficulty in urination and defecation, constipation, borborygmus, watery diarrhoea, shan disorder, hardness and distention below the Heart.
- Red and white leucorrhoea, dysmenorrhoea, irregular menstruation, infertility, pain of childbirth.
- Lumbar pain, sacral pain, numbness of the lumbar region extending down to the feet, cold sensation of the lumbar region, lumbar pain radiating to the genitals, sciatica, pain and painful obstruction of the lower limb, hemiplegia.

#### Commentary

The points [Shàngliáo BL-31](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-31), Cìliáo BL-32, [Zhōngliáo BL-33](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-33) and [Xiàliáo BL-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-34) are often considered as a group and are known as Baliao (Eight Crevices). In terms of their actions and indications they are very similar, sharing the common characteristics of regulating various disorders of the lower jiao, specifically: i. The urinary system. Cìliáo BL-32 and to a lesser extent [Zhōngliáo BL-33](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-33) are the strongest in their action on the urinary system, being indicated for such disorders as painful urinary dysfunction, retention of urine, difficult urination, enuresis etc. ii. The intestines. All four points treat difficult defecation, whilst [Xiàliáo BL-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-34) has the widest action on the intestines, being indicated in the Great Compendium of Acupuncture and Moxibustion for "borborygmus and diarrhoea, internal injury by damp and cold, blood in the stool", iii. Gynaecological disorders such as dysmenorrhoea, infertility, irregular menstruation and leucorrhoea as well as disorders of the genitals for which [Xiàliáo BL-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-34) is the strongest (swelling and pain, incessant green leucorrhoea with vaginal pain, pain of the testicles); iv. Invigorating the flow of qi and blood in the channels and collaterals of the local area and lower limbs, with Cìliáo BL-32 and [Zhōngliáo BL-33](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-33) having the strongest action in their ability to treat disorders such as lumbar pain, sacral pain, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limbs etc.

[Zhōngliáo BL-33](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-33) is indicated for "the five taxations, the seven injuries and the six extremes", all forms of extreme exhaustion. This underlines a further aspect of the actions of the eight liao as a whole, which is to benefit, warm, firm and supplement the Kidneys, having amongst their indications impotence, infertility, seminal emission and incessant white leucorrhoea. All eight liao are specifically indicated for difficulty in urination and/or defecation. Kidney deficiency is an important underlying pattern for this disorder, which is frequently seen in elderly and weak patients. Sun Si-miao, for example, in the Thousand Ducat Formulas prescribed moxibustion at all eight liao for difficult urination and defecation.

In clinical practice, Cìliáo BL-32 is the most commonly used of the four points, having the most wide-ranging indications. It is frequently used in the treatment of dysmenorrhoea which radiates to the sacral area, as well as for back pain during labour. To relieve labour pain, needling Cìliáo BL-32 is often combined with electro-acupuncture. For this purpose the handle of the needle may be bent to 90 degrees and taped to the skin, allowing the woman to lie on her back.

#### Summary of clinical application

- The most commonly used of the Baliao (Eight Crevices) points (i.e. Shangliao BL-31, Ciliao BL-32, Zhongliao BL-33 and Xialiao BL-34).
- Regulates the lower jiao: urination (retention of urine, painful urinary dysfunction, dark urine, enuresis, difficulty in urination and defecation), defecation (constipation, borborygmus, watery diarrhoea), menstruation (dysmenorrhoea, irregular menstruation, infertility), leucorrhoea, painful childbirth etc.
- Local point for pain of the sacrum and lower back: lumbar pain, sacral pain, numbness of the lumbar region extending down to the feet, cold sensation of the lumbar region, lumbar pain radiating to the genitals, sciatica, pain and painful obstruction of the lower limb, hemiplegia.

#### Combinations

- Difficult urination and defecation: moxa the eight liao ([Shàngliáo BL-31](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-31), Cìliáo BL-32, [Zhōngliáo BL-33](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-33) and [Xiàliáo BL-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-34) bilaterally) with one hundred cones (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Infertility: Cìliáo BL-32, [Yǒngquǎn KID-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-1) and [Shāngqiū SP-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-5) (Supplementing Life).
- Pain of the lumbar region and aversion to cold: Cìliáo BL-32, [Bāohuāng BL-53](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-53) and [Chéngjīn BL-56](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-56) (Supplementing Life).
- Rigidity of the lumbar spine with inability to turn: Cìliáo BL-32 and [Zhāngmén LIV-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-13) (Supplementing Life).
- Sciatica: Cìliáo BL-32, [Chéngfú BL-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-36), [Zhìbiān BL-54](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-54), [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) and [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60).

### **[BL32 次髎](point/BL32.md)** (CÌLIÁO)

* **英文名称：** Second Crevice
* **中文名称：** 次髎
* **国际代码：** BL-32
* **穴位属性：** **[BL32 次髎](point/BL32.md)** 是膀胱经与胆经的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第二骶后孔处。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 骶孔的定位请参考“Surface Anatomy”（此处引用原文，请根据实际参考资料定位）。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至1寸，或通过骶孔透刺1.5至2寸。透刺骶孔时，针尖稍向内侧和下方斜刺可使操作更容易。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 调理下焦，通利二便
* 调经止带
* 宣利腰腿

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

淋证（小便涩痛），尿黄，尿潴留，遗尿，大小便困难，便秘，肠鸣，水泻，疝气，心下硬满。
赤白带下，痛经，月经不调，不孕症，分娩疼痛。
腰痛，骶骨疼痛，腰部麻木放射至足部，腰部寒冷感，腰痛放射至生殖器，坐骨神经痛，下肢疼痛痹痛，半身不遂。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL31 上髎](point/BL31.md)**、**[BL32 次髎](point/BL32.md)**、**[BL33 中髎](point/BL33.md)** 和 **[BL34 下髎](point/BL34.md)** 常被视为一组穴位，统称为“八髎穴”。就其作用和主治而言，它们非常相似，共同具有调理下焦各种疾病的特点，具体包括：i. 泌尿系统。**[BL32 次髎](point/BL32.md)** 和作用稍弱的**[BL33 中髎](point/BL33.md)** 在泌尿系统方面作用最强，主治淋证、尿潴留、小便不利、遗尿等。ii. 肠道。所有四个穴位都治疗排便困难，其中**[BL34 下髎](point/BL34.md)** 对肠道作用最广，在《针灸大成》中主治“肠鸣腹泻，寒湿内伤，便血”。iii. 妇科疾病，如痛经、不孕症、月经不调和带下，以及生殖器疾病，其中**[BL34 下髎](point/BL34.md)** 作用最强（肿痛，阴道疼痛伴淋漓不尽的绿色带下，睾丸疼痛）。iv. 活络局部和下肢经络气血，其中**[BL32 次髎](point/BL32.md)** 和**[BL33 中髎](point/BL33.md)** 在治疗腰痛、骶骨疼痛、下肢痿证和痹痛等方面作用最强。

**[BL33 中髎](point/BL33.md)** 主治“五劳七伤六极”，都是极度虚损的表现。这突出了八髎穴整体作用的另一个方面，即益肾、温肾、固肾、补肾，其主治包括阳痿、不孕症、遗精和赤白带下不止。所有八髎穴都明确主治大小便困难。肾虚是导致此症的重要基础病机，常见于老年体弱患者。例如，孙思邈在《千金方》中就推荐艾灸所有八髎穴治疗大小便困难。

在临床实践中，**[BL32 次髎](point/BL32.md)** 是这四个穴位中最常用的，主治范围最广。它常用于治疗放射至骶骨区的痛经，以及分娩时的腰痛。为缓解分娩疼痛，**[BL32 次髎](point/BL32.md)** 针刺常与电针联合使用。为此，针柄可弯曲成90度并用胶带固定在皮肤上，以便产妇仰卧。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 八髎穴中最常用的穴位（包括**[BL31 上髎](point/BL31.md)**、**[BL32 次髎](point/BL32.md)**、**[BL33 中髎](point/BL33.md)** 和 **[BL34 下髎](point/BL34.md)**）。
* 调理下焦：小便（尿潴留，淋证，尿黄，遗尿，大小便困难），大便（便秘，肠鸣，水泻），月经（痛经，月经不调，不孕症），带下，分娩疼痛等。
* 治疗骶骨和腰部疼痛的局部穴位：腰痛，骶骨疼痛，腰部麻木放射至足部，腰部寒冷感，腰痛放射至生殖器，坐骨神经痛，下肢疼痛痹痛，半身不遂。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 大小便困难：灸八髎穴（双侧**[BL31 上髎](point/BL31.md)**、**[BL32 次髎](point/BL32.md)**、**[BL33 中髎](point/BL33.md)** 和 **[BL34 下髎](point/BL34.md)**）一百壮（《千金方》）。
* 不孕症：**[BL32 次髎](point/BL32.md)**，**[KID1 涌泉](point/KID1.md)** 和 **[SP5 商丘](point/SP5.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 腰部疼痛伴怕冷：**[BL32 次髎](point/BL32.md)**，**[BL53 胞肓](point/BL53.md)** 和 **[BL56 承筋](point/BL56.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 腰脊僵硬不能转动：**[BL32 次髎](point/BL32.md)** 和 **[LIV13 章门](point/LIV13.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 坐骨神经痛：**[BL32 次髎](point/BL32.md)**，**[BL36 承扶](point/BL36.md)**，**[BL54 秩边](point/BL54.md)**，**[BL40 委中](point/BL40.md)** 和 **[BL60 昆仑](point/BL60.md)**。
