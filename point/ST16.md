### Breast Window YĪNGCHUĀNG
![[ST-16 膺窗.png|250]]
#### Location

On the chest, in the third intercostal space, 4 cun lateral to the midline ([Yùtáng REN-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-18)), on the mamillary line.

#### Location Notes

i. First locate the second intercostal space (see [Wūyì ST-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-15) above), then find the third intercostal space below it; ii. Note that in males the nipple lies in the fourth intercostal space.

#### Needling

Transverse-oblique insertion laterally or medially along the intercostal space 0.5 to 0.8 cun, or transverse insertion superiorly or inferiorly along the channel.  
**Caution:** deep or perpendicular insertion carries a substantial risk of puncturing the lung.

#### Actions

- Alleviates cough and wheezing
- Benefits the breasts

#### Indications

- Cough, fullness of the chest with shortness of breath, restless sleep, fever and chills, swelling of the lips, borborygmus and watery diarrhoea.
- Breast abscess.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for cough, fullness of the chest with shortness of breath, restless sleep, fever and chills, swelling of the lips, borborygmus watery diarrhoea and breast abscess.

#### Combinations

- Swelling of the lips: Yīngchuāng ST-16 and [Tàichōng LIV-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-3) (Supplementing Life).
- Breast abscess, chills and fever with shortness of breath, restless sleep: Yīngchuān ST-16 and [Shénfēng KID-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-23) (Supplementing Life).

### [ST16 膺窗](point/ST16.md) (YĪNGCHUĀNG)

* **英文名称：** Breast Window
* **中文名称：** 膺窗
* **国际代码：** ST-16

#### 定位 (Location)

位于胸部，第三肋间隙中，距前正中线（[REN18 玉堂](point/REN18.md)）旁开4寸，在乳头线上。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 首先定位第二肋间隙（参见[ST15 屋翳](point/ST15.md)），然后在其下方的第三肋间隙定位；
    * 请注意，男性乳头通常位于第四肋间隙。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

横斜刺，沿肋间隙向外侧或内侧刺0.5至0.8寸，或沿经络向上或向下横刺。

* **注意 (Caution):** 深刺或垂直刺入有很大风险刺破肺脏。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 止咳平喘
* 宣利乳房

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

咳嗽，胸部胀满伴气短，烦躁不安入睡困难，发热恶寒，口唇肿胀，肠鸣水泻。
乳痈。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST16 膺窗](point/ST16.md) 临床上较少使用。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治咳嗽，胸部胀满伴气短，烦躁不安入睡困难，发热恶寒，口唇肿胀，肠鸣水泻以及乳痈。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 口唇肿胀：[ST16 膺窗](point/ST16.md) 和[LIV3 太冲](point/LIV3.md)（《资生经》）。
* 乳痈，恶寒发热伴气短，烦躁不安入睡困难：[ST16 膺窗](point/ST16.md) 和[KID23 神封](point/KID23.md)（《资生经》）。
