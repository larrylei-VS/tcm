### Yin Corner YĪNLIÁN
![[LIV-11 阴廉.png|250]]
#### Location

2 cun inferior to [Qìchōng ST-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-30) on the anterior border of m. adductor longus.

#### Location Notes

i. [Qìchōng ST-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-30) is located on the lower abdomen, 2 cun lateral to the midline, level with the superior border of the pubic symphysis ([Qūgǔ REN-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-2)); ii. The tendon of origin of adductor longus arises from the pubic bone and is identified as the most prominent tendon in the groin.

#### Needling

Perpendicular or oblique insertion 0.5 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Benefits the uterus

#### Indications

- Infertility, irregular menstruation, pain in the inner thigh and knee.

#### Commentary

Many classical texts recommend moxibustion at this point for the treatment of infertility.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Infertility, irregular menstruation, pain in the inner thigh and knee.



### 1. [LIV11 阴廉](point/LIV11.md)

#### 1.1. Yin Corner 陰廉

#### 1.2. 定位

位于[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md)下方2寸，沿长收肌前缘。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

i. [ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md)位于下腹部，距中线2寸，与耻骨联合上缘（[REN2 曲骨](point/REN2.md)）平齐；  
ii. 长收肌的起始腱起于耻骨，是腹股沟中最明显的腱。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直或斜刺，深0.5至1.5寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 益子宫  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 不孕症、月经不调、内侧大腿及膝痛。  

#### 1.7. 论述

许多经典文献推荐在此穴施灸治疗不孕症。  

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 适用于不孕症、月经不调、内侧大腿及膝痛。
