### Earth Five Meetings DÌWǓHÙI
![[GB-42 地五会.png|250]]
#### Location

Between the 4th and 5th metatarsal bones, in the depression proximal to the metatarsal heads, on the medial side of the tendon of m. extensor digitorum longus (branch to little toe).

#### Location Notes

Ask the patient to abduct their little toe in order to make the branch of m. extensor digitorum longus more prominent. Run a finger from [Xiáxī GB-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-43) towards the ankle, along the interspace between the fourth and fifth metatarsals, until it encounters the significant depression immediately before the tendon.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 0.8 cun. Incorrect angle of insertion will not enable the needle to pass between the shafts of the fourth and fifth metatarsal bones.  
**Note:** the Systematic Classic of Acupuncture and Moxibustion, Great Compendium of Acupuncture and Moxibustion, and Illustrated Classic of Acupuncture Points on the Bronze Man all say that this point should not be treated by moxibustion otherwise emaciation and death will follow within three years. Modern sources however agree that there are no contraindications to moxibustion.

#### Actions

- Spreads Liver qi
- Clears Gall Bladder heat

#### Indications

- Headache, redness, itching and pain of the eyes, tinnitus, deafness.
- Fullness of the chest, pain of the lateral costal region, swelling and pain of the axilla, distention and pain of the breasts, breast abscess, spitting blood due to internal injury.
- Swelling, redness and pain of the dorsum of the foot, lumbar pain.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Indicated for headache, redness, itching and pain of the eyes, tinnitus, deafness, fullness of the chest, pain of the lateral costal region, swelling and pain of the axilla, distention and pain of the breasts, breast abscess, spitting blood due to internal injury, swelling, redness and pain of the dorsum of the foot, lumbar pain.

#### Combinations

- Pain and itching of the eyes: reduce Dìwǔhùi GB-42 and [Guāngmíng GB-37](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-37) (Ode to Elucidate Mysteries).
- Swelling of the axilla: Dìwǔhùi GB-42, Yangfu GB‑38, [Shēnmài BL-62](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-62), [Wěiyáng BL-39](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-39), [Tiānchí PC-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-1) and [Zúlínqì GB-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-41) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Breast abscess: Dìwǔhùi GB-42 and [Liángqiū ST-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-34) (Supplementing Life).


### 1. [GB42 地五会](/point/GB42.md)

#### 1.1. Earth Five Meetings 地五會

#### 1.2. 定位

位于第4和第5跖骨之间，跖骨头近端的凹陷处，在小趾伸肌腱（分支至小趾）的内侧。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

嘱患者外展小趾，使小趾伸肌腱分支更明显。从[GB43 侠溪](/point/GB43.md)沿第4和第5跖骨间隙向踝部滑动手指，直至落入腱前方的明显凹陷处。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直刺入，深0.5至0.8寸。刺入角度不正确将无法使针通过第4和第5跖骨之间的间隙。  
**注意：** 《针灸系统经典》、《针灸大成》和《铜人腧穴针灸图经》均称此穴禁用灸，否则三年内会导致消瘦及死亡。然而，现代资料认为灸无禁忌。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 疏肝气  
- 清胆热  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 头痛、目赤痒痛、耳鳴、耳聾。  
- 胸满、胁痛、腋肿痛、乳房胀痛、乳痈、内伤吐血。  
- 足背肿赤痛、腰痛。  

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 适用于头痛、目赤痒痛、耳鳴、耳聾、胸满、胁痛、腋肿痛、乳房胀痛、乳痈、内伤吐血、足背肿赤痛、腰痛。  

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 目痛及痒：泻[GB42 地五会](/point/GB42.md) + [GB37 光明](/point/GB37.md)（《阐秘颂》）。  
- 腋肿：[GB42 地五会](/point/GB42.md) + [GB38 阳辅](/point/GB38.md) + [BL62 申脉](/point/BL62.md) + [BL39 委阳](/point/BL39.md) + [PC1 天池](/point/PC1.md) + [GB41 足临泣](/point/GB41.md)（《千金方》）。  
- 乳痈：[GB42 地五会](/point/GB42.md) + [ST34 梁丘](/point/ST34.md)（《补命》）。
