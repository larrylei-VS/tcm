### Armpit Abyss YUĀNYÈ
![[GB-22 渊腋.png|250]]
#### Location

On the mid-axillary line, in the fifth intercostal space, approximately 3 cun inferior to the apex of the axilla, at the level of the nipple.

#### Location Notes

i. The mid-axillary line is drawn vertically down from the apex of the axilla ([Jíquán HE-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-1)); ii. First locate the costal cartilage of the second rib which is level with the sternal angle, locate the second intercostal space below it and then locate the fifth intercostal space, three spaces below that; in males the nipple lies in the fourth intercostal space; iii. Some sources locate this point in the fourth intercostal space.

#### Needling

Transverse-oblique insertion along the intercostal space, 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** deep or perpendicular needling may induce a pneumothorax.  
**Note:** according to several classical texts, this point is contraindicated to moxibustion.

#### Actions

- Regulates qi and unbinds the chest
- Benefits the axilla

#### Indications

- Cough, fullness of the chest, chills and fever, pain of the lateral costal region, swelling of the axilla, scrofula of the axilla, sabre lumps.
- Pain of the shoulder and arm, inability to raise the arm.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for cough, fullness of the chest, chills and fever, pain of the lateral costal region, swelling of the axilla, scrofula of the axilla, sabre lumps, pain of the shoulder and arm, inability to raise the arm.

#### Combinations

- Sabre lumps of the axilla: Yuānyè GB-22, [Zhīgōu SJ-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-6), [Wàiguān SJ-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-5) and [Zúlínqì GB-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-41) (Illustrated Supplement).

### 1. [GB22 渊腋](/point/GB22.md)

#### 1.1. Armpit Abyss 淵腋

#### 1.2. 定位

位于腋中线上，第5肋间隙，约在腋窝顶点下方3寸，与乳头平齐。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

i. 腋中线从腋窝顶点（[HE1 极泉](/point/HE1.md)）垂直向下引出；  
ii. 首先定位第2肋软骨（与胸骨角平齐），下方为第2肋间隙，再向下数3个肋间隙为第5肋间隙；男性乳头位于第4肋间隙；  
iii. 一些资料将此穴定位于第4肋间隙。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

沿肋间隙横斜刺，深0.5至1寸。  
**注意：** 深刺或垂直刺入可能引起气胸。  
**备注：** 根据多部经典文献，此穴禁灸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 调气解胸
- 益腋

#### 1.6. 主治

- 咳嗽、胸满、寒热、胁痛、腋肿、腋下瘰疬、刀疮。
- 肩臂痛、不能举臂。

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 使用较少。
- 适用于咳嗽、胸满、寒热、胁痛、腋肿、腋下瘰疬、刀疮、肩臂痛、不能举臂。

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 腋下刀疮：[GB22 渊腋](/point/GB22.md) + [SJ6 支沟](/point/SJ6.md) + [SJ5 外关](/point/SJ5.md) + [GB41 足临泣](/point/GB41.md)（《图翼》）。
