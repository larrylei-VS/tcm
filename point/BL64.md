### Capital Bone JĪNGGǓ
![[BL-64 京骨.png|250]]
- Yuan-Source point of the Bladder channel

#### Location

On the lateral side of the foot, in the depression anterior and inferior to the tuberosity of the fifth metatarsal bone.

#### Location Notes

The tuberosity of the fifth metatarsal bone is the most palpable landmark on the lateral side of the foot.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Clears the head and eyes and eliminates wind
- Calms the spirit
- Relaxes the sinews, activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Painful and heavy head with cold feet, splitting headache, heat in the head, shaking of the head, redness of the inner canthus, superficial visual obstruction, visual dizziness, rhinitis with ceaseless nosebleed, chills and fever, malaria, no pleasure in eating.
- Palpitations, Heart pain, mania-depression, propensity to fright, epilepsy.
- Stiff neck, pain of the back and sides of the body, lumbar pain, aversion to cold and pain of the back, cracked and dry feet, cold damp leg qi.

#### Commentary

Jīnggǔ BL-64 is the yuan-source point of the Bladder channel, and like most of the distal Bladder channel points treats fullness of the head. This fullness may be due to attack of exterior pathogenic wind or wind-heat, to stirring of interior wind, to upsurging of yang due to Kidney deficiency, or simply to disharmony of the upper and lower parts of the body. Excess above manifests as heat, pain and heaviness of the head, splitting headache, shaking of the head, rhinitis with ceaseless nosebleed etc., whilst deficiency below gives rise to cold feet.

The Bladder channel ascends to the inner canthus and Jīnggǔ BL-64 is indicated for various eye disorders such as visual dizziness, redness of the inner canthus and superficial visual obstruction.

The Bladder primary channel enters the brain whilst the Bladder divergent channel enters the Heart, and like several distal Bladder channel points, Jīnggǔ BL-64 is indicated for disorders such as palpitations, Heart pain, epilepsy and mania-depression.

Finally, Jīnggǔ BL-64 is indicated for stiffness, pain and contraction along the course of the Bladder channel from the neck to the feet.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Clears fullness from the head and eyes due to exterior pathogenic wind, interior wind, upsurging of yang due to Kidney deficiency, or disharmony between the upper and lower parts of the body: heat, pain and heaviness of the head, splitting headache, shaking of the head, rhinitis with ceaseless nosebleed, visual dizziness, redness of the inner canthus, superficial visual obstruction, cold feet.
- Calms the Heart and spirit: palpitations, Heart pain, epilepsy, mania-depression.

#### Combinations

- Heat in the head and rhinitis with nosebleed: Jīnggǔ BL-64, [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60), [Fēiyáng BL-58](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-58), [Chéngshān BL-57](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-57) and [Yǐnbài SP-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-1) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Redness and erosion of the inner canthus: Jīnggǔ BL-64 and [Shùgǔ BL-65](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-65) (Supplementing Life).
- Superficial visual obstruction: Jīnggǔ BL-64 and [Qiángǔ SI-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-2) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Cold of the lower extremities: Jīnggǔ BL-64, [Rángǔ KID-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-2) and [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Contraction of the legs: Jīnggǔ BL-64, [Chéngjīn BL-56](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-56), [Chéngshān BL-57](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-57) and [Shāngqiū SP-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-5) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Pain of the genitals: Jīnggǔ BL-64, [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23), [Zhìshì BL-52](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-52) and [Tàichōng LIV-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-3) (Supplementing Life).

### **[BL64 京骨](point/BL64.md)** (Jīnggǔ)

* **英文名称：** Capital Bone
* **中文名称：** 京骨
* **国际代码：** BL-64
* **穴位属性：** **[BL64 京骨](point/BL64.md)** 是膀胱经的原穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于足外侧，第五跖骨粗隆前下方凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 第五跖骨粗隆是足外侧最易触及的骨性标志。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.3至0.5寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 清头明目，祛风
* 宁心安神
* 舒筋活络，止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

头重疼痛伴足冷，头痛如裂，头热，摇头，内眼角红赤，外障眼病，视物眩晕，鼻炎伴鼻出血不止，恶寒发热，疟疾，食欲不振。
心悸，心痛，癫狂，易受惊吓，癫痫。
颈项强直，背部和身体侧面疼痛，腰痛，背部怕冷疼痛，足部干裂，寒湿腿气。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL64 京骨](point/BL64.md)** 是膀胱经的原穴，像膀胱经大多数远端穴位一样，它主治**头部胀满**。这种胀满可能由于外感风邪或风热，内风扰动，肾虚阳亢，或仅仅是身体上下部不调所致。上部实证表现为头热、头痛、头重、头痛如裂、摇头、鼻炎伴鼻出血不止等，而下部虚证则表现为足冷。

膀胱经上行至内眼角，**[BL64 京骨](point/BL64.md)** 主治多种**眼部疾病**，如视物眩晕、内眼角红赤和外障眼病。

膀胱经正经入脑，而膀胱经别入心。像膀胱经的几个远端穴位一样，**[BL64 京骨](point/BL64.md)** 主治心悸、心痛、癫痫和癫狂等疾病，**宁心安神**。

最后，**[BL64 京骨](point/BL64.md)** 主治膀胱经从颈部到足部的僵硬、疼痛和挛缩，**舒筋活络，止痛**。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* **清泻头部和眼部胀满**，可由外感风邪、内风、肾虚阳亢或身体上下部不调引起：头热、头痛、头重、头痛如裂、摇头、鼻炎伴鼻出血不止、视物眩晕、内眼角红赤、外障眼病、足冷。
* **宁心安神**：心悸，心痛，癫痫，癫狂。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 头热和鼻炎伴鼻出血：**[BL64 京骨](point/BL64.md)**，**[BL60 昆仑](point/BL60.md)**，**[BL58 飞扬](point/BL58.md)**，**[BL57 承山](point/BL57.md)** 和 **[SP1 隐白](point/SP1.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 内眼角红赤糜烂：**[BL64 京骨](point/BL64.md)** 和 **[BL65 束骨](point/BL65.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 外障眼病：**[BL64 京骨](point/BL64.md)** 和 **[SI2 前谷](point/SI2.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 下肢寒冷：**[BL64 京骨](point/BL64.md)**，**[KID2 然谷](point/KID2.md)** 和 **[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 腿部挛缩：**[BL64 京骨](point/BL64.md)**，**[BL56 承筋](point/BL56.md)**，**[BL57 承山](point/BL57.md)** 和 **[SP5 商丘](point/SP5.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 生殖器疼痛：**[BL64 京骨](point/BL64.md)**，**[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)**，**[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)** 和 **[LIV3 太冲](point/LIV3.md)**（《资生经》）。
