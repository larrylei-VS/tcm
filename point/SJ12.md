### Dispersing Luo River  Xiāoluò
![[SJ-12 消泺.png|250]]
#### Location 

On the upper arm, on a line drawn between Tiānjǐng SJ-10 and Jiānliáo SJ-14, 4 cun proximal to Tiānjǐng SJ-10 and 6 cun distal to Jiānliáo SJ-14.

#### Location note 

i. Divide the distance between Tiānjǐng SJ-10 and Jiānliáo SJ-14 into half and locate this point 1 cun distal to this midpoint; ii. Locate in the depression within the triceps muscle, posterior to the shaft of the humerus (between the muscle bellies of the long and lateral heads of the triceps).

#### Needling 

Perpendicular or oblique insertion 1 to 2 cun.

#### Actions 

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications 

- Stiffness and pain of the nape and back with inability to turn the head, pain of the shoulder and arm, wind painful obstruction.
- Headache, chills and fever, dizziness, toothache, madness.

#### Summary of clinical application 

- Stiffness and pain of the nape and back with inability to turn the head, pain of the shoulder and arm, wind painful obstruction.
- Headache, chills and fever, dizziness, toothache, madness.

#### Combinations 

- Pain of the nape of the neck: Xiāoluò SJ-12 and Tóuquiàoyīn GB-11 (Supplementing Life).



### **[SJ12 消泺](point/SJ12.md)** (Xiāoluò)

* **英文名称：** Dispersing Luo River
* **中文名称：** 消泺
* **国际代码：** SJ-12

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于上臂部，在**[SJ10 天井](point/SJ10.md)** 与**[SJ14 肩髎](point/SJ14.md)** 的连线上，距**[SJ10 天井](point/SJ10.md)** 上方4寸，距**[SJ14 肩髎](point/SJ14.md)** 下方6寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 将**[SJ10 天井](point/SJ10.md)** 和**[SJ14 肩髎](point/SJ14.md)** 之间的距离平分，此穴位于中点下方1寸处；
    * 位于肱骨后方肱三头肌内（肱三头肌长头和外侧头肌腹之间）的凹陷中。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直或斜刺1至2寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 通经活络，止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

颈项和背部僵硬疼痛不能转头，肩臂疼痛，风痹。
头痛，恶寒发热，眩晕，牙痛，发狂。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SJ12 消泺](point/SJ12.md)** 临床上较少使用。其名称“消泺”意指消散、疏通，暗示其在经络不畅时活络止痛的作用。此穴位于上臂，是三焦经的循行要道之一。

此穴主要用于缓解**颈项和背部的僵硬疼痛**，特别是伴有转头困难的情况。它对**肩臂疼痛**和**风痹**也有疗效，这些都与经络阻滞、气血不畅或风邪侵袭有关。此外，**[SJ12 消泺](point/SJ12.md)** 对**头痛**、**恶寒发热**、**眩晕**、**牙痛**和**发狂**等症状也有一定的辅助作用，这与其**清热、祛风、宁神**的功效相关。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 主治颈项和背部僵硬疼痛不能转头，肩臂疼痛，风痹。
* 辅助治疗头痛，恶寒发热，眩晕，牙痛，发狂。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 颈项疼痛：**[SJ12 消泺](point/SJ12.md)** 和 **[GB11 头窍阴](point/GB11.md)**（《资生经》）。
