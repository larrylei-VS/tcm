### NǍOHÙ
![[DU-17 脑户.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Governing vessel with the Bladder channel

#### Location

At the back of the head on the midline, 1.5 cun directly above [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16), in the depression directly superior to the external occipital protuberance.

#### Location Notes

This point may be located at the junction of the lower quarter and the upper three quarters of the line connecting [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16) and [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion superiorly or inferiorly 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Note:** according to several classical texts, this point is contraindicated to both needling and moxibustion.

#### Actions

- Eliminates wind and alleviates pain
- Benefits the eyes
- Calms the spirit

#### Indications

- Heaviness of the head, head wind, aversion to wind in the head, wind dizziness, swelling and pain of the head, pain of the face, red face, stiffness and pain of the neck.
- Dimness of vision, short sightedness, eye pain, excessive lacrimation, yellow eyes, jaundice.
- Mania, epilepsy, clonic spasm, lockjaw, loss of voice, bleeding from the root of the tongue, goitre, chills and fever, sweating, pain of the bones.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Indicated for heaviness of the head, head wind, aversion to wind in the head, wind dizziness, swelling and pain of the head, pain of the face, red face, stiffness and pain of the neck, dimness of vision, short sightedness, eye pain, excessive lacrimation, yellow eyes, jaundice, mania, epilepsy, clonic spasm, lockjaw, loss of voice, bleeding from the root of the tongue, goitre, chills and fever, sweating, pain of the bones.

#### Combinations

- Pain and heaviness of the head: Nǎohù DU-17, [Tōngtiān BL-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-7) and [Nǎokōng GB-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-19) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Yellow eyes: Nǎohù DU-17, [Dǎnshū BL-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-19), [Yìshè BL-49](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-49) and [Yánggāng BL-48](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-48) (Supplementing Life).
- Pain of the eyes with inability to see: Nǎohù DU-17, [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20), [Yùzhěn BL-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-9), [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16) and [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Madness with vomiting: Nǎohù DU-17, [Luòquè BL-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-8), [Zhúbīn KID-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-9), [Yánggǔ SI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-5), [Hòudǐng DU-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-19), [Qiángjiān DU-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-18) and [Yùzhěn BL-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-9) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### 1. [DU17 脑户](/point/DU17.md)

#### 1.1. Brain Door 腦戶

#### 1.2. 定位

位于头部后中线，[DU16 风府](/point/DU16.md)正上方1.5寸，外枕隆突正上方的凹陷中。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

此穴可位于连接[DU16 风府](/point/DU16.md)与[DU20 百会](/point/DU20.md)的线之下四分之一与上四分之三交界处。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

向上或向下平刺，深0.5至1寸。  
**注意：** 多部经典文献认为此穴禁针禁灸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 祛风止痛  
- 益目  
- 定神  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 头重、头风、头恶风、风眩、头肿痛、面痛、面赤、颈僵痛。  
- 视蒙、近视、眼痛、多泪、黄目、黄疸。  
- 躁狂、癫痫、痉挛、牙关紧闭、失音、舌根出血、瘿病、寒热、出汗、骨痛。  

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 用于头重、头风、头恶风、风眩、头肿痛、面痛、面赤、颈僵痛、视蒙、近视、眼痛、多泪、黄目、黄疸、躁狂、癫痫、痉挛、牙关紧闭、失音、舌根出血、瘿病、寒热、出汗、骨痛。  

#### 1.8. 配伍
