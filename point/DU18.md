### QIÁNGJIĀN
![[DU-18 强间.png|250]]
#### Location

At the back of the head on the midline, 1.5 cun directly superior to [Nǎohù DU-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-17), midway between [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16) and [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20).

#### Location Notes

This point may be located at the midpoint of the line connecting [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16) and [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.

#### Actions

- Pacifies wind and alleviates pain
- Calms the spirit

#### Indications

- Headache, dizziness with agitation, nausea and vomiting of foamy (watery) saliva, stiffness of the neck with inability to turn the head.
- Epilepsy, shaking of the head, mad walking, insomnia, mania-depression, clonic spasm.

#### Commentary

As is often the case, a point such as Qiángjiān DU-18 which is considered of minimal importance in modern clinical practice, was clearly ascribed greater significance in classical texts, witnessed by numerous traditional combinations. It shares with many points of the head region, particularly its neighbouring points of the Governing vessel ([Nǎohù DU-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-17) and [Hòudǐng DU-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-19)) the ability to pacify interior wind and calm the spirit. It was primarily applied, however, for unendurable stabbing pain in the head.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Traditionally used for unendurable stabbing pain in the head.
- Also dizziness with agitation, nausea and vomiting of foamy (watery) saliva, stiffness of the neck with inability to turn the head, epilepsy, shaking of the head, mad walking, insomnia, mania-depression.

#### Combinations

- Headache that is difficult to endure: Qiángjiān DU-18 and [Fēnglóng ST-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-40) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Stabbing pain of the head with inability to move: Qiángjiān DU-18 and [Tóuqiàoyīn GB-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-11) (Supplementing Life).
- Stabbing pain of the head with inability to move: Qiángjiān DU-18 and [Zúqiàoyīn GB-44](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-44) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Deviation of the mouth with inability to speak: Qiángjiān DU-18, [Chéngqì ST-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-1), [Sìbái ST-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-2), [Jùliáo ST-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-3), [Kǒuhéliáo LI-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-10), [Shàngguān GB-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-3), [Dàyíng ST-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-5), [Quánliáo SI-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-18), [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20), [Yíngxiāng LI-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-20) and [Rénzhōng DU-26](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-26) (Supplementing Life).
- Agitation of the Heart: Qiángjiān DU-18, Baihui DU‑20 and [Chéngguāng BL-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-6) (Supplementing Life).
- Epileptic convulsions, mad walking, inability to sleep, agitation of the Heart: Qiángjiān DU-18, [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2), [Xiǎohǎi SI-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-8) and [Hòuxī SI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-3) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).


### 1. [DU18 强间](/point/DU18.md)

#### 1.1. Strong Space 強間

#### 1.2. 定位

位于头部后中线，[DU17 脑户](/point/DU17.md)正上方1.5寸，[DU16 风府](/point/DU16.md)与[DU20 百会](/point/DU20.md)之间中点。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

此穴可位于连接[DU16 风府](/point/DU16.md)与[DU20 百会](/point/DU20.md)的线之中点。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

平刺，深0.5至1寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 平风止痛  
- 定神  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 头痛、眩晕烦躁、呕吐泡沫（水样）涎、颈僵不能转头。  
- 癫痫、头摇、狂走、失眠、躁郁症、痉挛。  

#### 1.7. 论述

现代临床中[DU18 强间](/point/DU18.md)重要性较低，但在经典文献中意义较大，见诸多传统配伍。它与头部多穴，尤其是邻近督脉穴[DU17 脑户](/point/DU17.md)及[DU19 后顶](/point/DU19.md)，共能平内风及定神，主用于头痛难忍的刺痛。  

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 传统用于头痛难忍的刺痛。  
- 亦治眩晕烦躁、呕吐泡沫（水样）涎、颈僵不能转头、癫痫、头摇、狂走、失眠、躁郁症。  

#### 1.9. 配伍
