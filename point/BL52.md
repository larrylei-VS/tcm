### Residence of the Will ZHÌSHÌ
![[BL-52 志室.png|250]]
#### Location

3 cun lateral to the midline, level with the lower border of the spinous process of the second lumbar vertebra (L2) and level with [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23).

#### Location Notes

When the shoulder is relaxed, the three cun line corresponds to the medial border of the scapula.

#### Needling

Oblique insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.

  
**Caution:**: deep perpendicular needling carries a risk of injuring the kidney.

#### Actions

- Tonifies the Kidneys and benefits the essence
- Regulates urination
- Strengthens the lumbar region

#### Indications

- Lumbar pain and stiffness, back pain, dribbling urination, difficult urination, oedema, impotence, premature ejaculation, seminal emission with dreams, swelling and pain of the genitals.
- Fullness and pain of the lateral costal region, sudden turmoil disorder, vomiting, difficult defecation, hardness of the abdomen and hypogastrium.

#### Commentary

Zhìshì BL-52 is level with [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23), the back-shu point of the Kidneys. Despite its suggestive name 'Residence of the Will', there are no apparent references in any of the important classics of acupuncture to the use of this point for the treatment of emotional or psychological disorders.

Zhìshì BL-52 was also known as Jinggong (Palace of Essence), reflecting its ability to fortify the Kidney qi and yang, strengthen the sexual function and control discharge of semen, as evinced by its indications for impotence, premature ejaculation and seminal emission. It is similarly able to firm the Kidney qi and regulate urination, and is indicated for difficult or dribbling urination and oedema. Zhìshì BL-52 is also used clinically in the treatment of pain that spreads widely to the muscles of the lumbar region whether due to sprain, painful obstruction, or deficiency and disease of the Kidneys, for example renal colic. Unlike [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23), however, the application of Zhìshì BL-52 is confined to these patterns of Kidney deficiency and it lacks the wider ability of Shen-[Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23) to deeply nourish and tonify the Kidneys.

According to the Essential Questions[66](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references) the five outer Bladder points level with the five zang back-shu points (i.e. [Pòhù BL-42](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-42), [Shéntáng BL-44](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-44), [Húnmén BL-47](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-47), [Yìshè BL-49](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-49) and Zhìshì BL-52) drain heat from the five zang, an action shared by the back-shu points of the five zang.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Treats lumbar pain that spreads widely to the muscles of the lumbar region whether due to sprain, painful obstruction or deficiency and disease of the Kidneys, for example renal colic.
- Strengthens the Kidneys: dribbling urination, difficult urination, oedema, impotence, premature ejaculation, seminal emission with dreams.

#### Combinations

- Lumbar pain with tension of the spine: Zhìshì BL-52 and [Jīngmén GB-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-25) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Acute pain of both lateral costal regions: Zhìshì BL-52, [Gānshū BL-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-18), and [Píshū BL-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-20) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Pain and swelling of the genitals: Zhìshì BL-52 and [Bāohuāng BL-53](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-53) (Supplementing Life).
- Pain of the genitals: Zhìshì BL-52, [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23), [Jīnggǔ BL-64](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-64) and [Tàichōng LIV-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-3) (Supplementing Life).
- Dribbling urination: Zhìshì BL-52, [Wěiyáng BL-39](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-39) and [Zhōngliáo BL-33](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-33) (Supplementing Life).
- Renal colic, blood and stone painful urinary dysfunction with vomiting: Zhìshì BL-52, [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23), [Dàchángshū BL-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-25), [Jīngmén GB-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-25), [Tiānshū ST-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-25), [Dàhéng SP-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-15), [Sānyīnjiāo SP-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-6) and [Nèiguān PC-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-6).

### **[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)** (ZHÌSHÌ)

* **英文名称：** Residence of the Will
* **中文名称：** 志室
* **国际代码：** BL-52
* **穴位属性：** **[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)** 是膀胱经第二侧线上的穴位，与**[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)** 平齐。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第二腰椎（L2）棘突下缘水平，距中线旁开3寸，与**[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)** 平齐。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 肩部放松时，旁开3寸线对应肩胛骨内侧缘。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直深刺有损伤肾脏的风险。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 补益肾脏，益精
* 调理小便
* 强健腰部

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

腰痛僵硬，背痛，小便淋漓，小便困难，水肿，阳痿，梦遗，遗精，生殖器肿痛。
胁肋部胀满疼痛，霍乱吐泻，呕吐，排便困难，腹部和少腹硬满。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)** 与肾的背俞穴**[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)** 平齐。尽管其名称“志室”（意为“志的居所”）富有启发性，但在所有重要的针灸经典中，似乎都没有明确提及此穴用于治疗情志或心理障碍。

**[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)** 又名“精宫”，这反映了其能够固摄肾气和肾阳，增强性功能并控制精液排泄，这体现在其主治阳痿、早泄和梦遗。它同样能够固摄肾气和调理小便，主治小便困难、小便淋漓和水肿。**[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)** 在临床上也用于治疗广泛放射至腰部肌肉的疼痛，无论是扭伤、痹痛，还是肾虚和肾脏疾病，例如肾绞痛。然而，与**[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)** 不同，**[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)** 的应用仅限于这些肾虚证型，它不具备**[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)** 广泛而深入滋养和补益肾脏的能力。

根据《素问·灵兰秘典论》记载，与五脏背俞穴平齐的五个膀胱经外侧穴位（即**[BL42 魄户](point/BL42.md)**、**[BL44 神堂](point/BL44.md)**、**[BL47 魂门](point/BL47.md)**、**[BL49 意舍](point/BL49.md)** 和**[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)**）具有清泻五脏热邪的作用，这一作用与五脏背俞穴相同。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗广泛放射至腰部肌肉的疼痛，无论是扭伤、痹痛，还是肾虚和肾脏疾病，例如肾绞痛。
* 强健肾脏：小便淋漓，小便困难，水肿，阳痿，早泄，梦遗。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 腰痛伴脊柱紧张：**[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)** 和 **[GB25 京门](point/GB25.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 双胁肋部急性疼痛：**[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)**，**[BL18 肝俞](point/BL18.md)** 和 **[BL20 脾俞](point/BL20.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 生殖器疼痛肿胀：**[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)** 和 **[BL53 胞肓](point/BL53.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 生殖器疼痛：**[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)**，**[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)**，**[BL64 京骨](point/BL64.md)** 和 **[LIV3 太冲](point/LIV3.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 小便淋漓：**[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)**，**[BL39 委阳](point/BL39.md)** 和 **[BL33 中髎](point/BL33.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 肾绞痛，尿血，石淋伴呕吐：**[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)**，**[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)**，**[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)**，**[GB25 京门](point/GB25.md)**，**[ST25 天枢](point/ST25.md)**，**[SP15 大横](point/SP15.md)**，**[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md)** 和 **[PC6 内关](point/PC6.md)**。
