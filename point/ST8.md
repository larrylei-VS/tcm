### Head's Binding TÓUWÉI

![[ST-8 头维.png|250]]

- Meeting point of the Stomach and Gall Bladder channels with the Yang Linking vessel

#### Location

At the corner of the forehead, 4.5 cun lateral to [Shéntíng DU-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-24) and 0.5 cun within the anterior hairline.

#### Location Notes

There are three methods to locate this point: i. Find the meeting point of a horizontal line drawn 0.5 cun within the anterior hairline, and a vertical line drawn 0.5 cun posterior to the hairline of the temple; ii. Locate [Tóulínqì GB-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-15) which lies directly above the pupil when the patient is looking directly forwards, and [Shéntíng DU-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-24) which lies on the midline, 0.5 cun posterior to the anterior hairline. Tóuwéi ST-8 lies on the continuation of a line drawn between these two points and twice its distance; iii. 0.5 cun superior to the upper line of origin of the temporalis muscle, 0.5 cun posterior to a vertical line drawn directly above Taiyang (M-HN-9).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Note:** according to several classical texts, this point is contraindicated to moxibustion.

#### Actions

- Eliminates wind and alleviates pain
- Benefits the eyes

#### Indications

- Headache, splitting headache with chills and fever, dizziness, vomiting.
- Dimness of vision, bursting eye pain, lacrimation on exposure to wind, twitching of the eyelids.
- Dyspnoea with agitation and oppression, hemiplegia.

#### Commentary

Touwei ST-8 is an important point to treat headaches. Located at the corner of the forehead, it is a meeting point of the Stomach channel with the Gall Bladder channel and the Yang Linking vessel. This meeting of three channels which have such influence on the head is reflected in the name of this point ‘Head’s Binding’. The Stomach channel belongs to yangming which rules the forehead, the Gall Bladder channel belongs to shaoyang which rules the temporal region, whilst the Yang Linking vessel links all the yang channels of the body including the Governing vessel and the Bladder channel which rule the vertex. Whilst the forte of Touwei ST-8 is the treatment of frontal headaches, with different needle direction it may also be used for temporal and vertex headaches.

Touwei ST-8 is particularly indicated in headaches and eye disorders due to wind, whether exterior pathogenic wind invading the channels of the head, or internally generated wind. As far as exterior headaches are concerned, although it is suitable for both wind-cold and wind-heat, many classics stress its use in the treatment of splitting headaches with bursting eye pain, symptoms more usually associated with wind-heat. Heat is a yang pathogen and its nature is to flare upwards and expand, obstructing the collaterals and leading to a distending and splitting sensation of the head. In the treatment of headache accompanied by pain or twitching of the eyes, Touwei ST‑8 is frequently combined in classical prescriptions with [Zanzhu BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2). Touwei ST-8 is also indicated for severe headache accompanied by vomiting, and is especially suitable for migraine headache with nausea or vomiting and dimness of vision or bursting pain of the eyes. The ability of Touwei ST-8 to clear internally generated wind from the head is reflected not only in its use for this kind of headache, but also for dizziness and hemiplegia.

#### Summary of clinical application

- An important point for headache (meeting point of the Stomach and Gall Bladder channels and the Yang Linking vessel). Especially (needled anteriorly) for frontal and wind-heat headaches and migraines (with splitting sensation, bursting eye pain, dimness of vision, vomiting), but also (with different needling direction) for vertex and temporal headaches and headaches of any aetiology.

#### Combinations

- Headache with eye pain: Tóuwéi ST-8 and [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2) (Ode of the Jade Dragon).
- Head wind with splitting pain, bursting pain of the eyes and lacrimation: Tóuwéi ST-8 and [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2) (Golden Mirror).
- Pain between the eyebrows: Tóuwéi ST-8 and [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2) (Song of the Jade Dragon)
- Twitching of the eyelids: Tóuwéi ST-8 and [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2) (Great Compendium).
- Splitting headache with bursting eye pain: Tóuwéi ST-8 and [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-7) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- One-sided or generalised head wind: Tóuwéi ST-8, [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20), [Qiándǐng DU-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-21), [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23), [Shéntíng DU-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-24), [Sīzhúkōng SJ-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-23), [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20), [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) and [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2) (Great Compendium).
- Lacrimation on exposure to wind: Tóuwéi ST-8, [Jīngmíng BL-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-1), [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20) and [Tóulínqì GB-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-15) (Great Compendium).

### [ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md) (TÓUWÉI)

* **英文名称：** Head's Binding
* **中文名称：** 头维
* **国际代码：** ST-8
* **穴位属性：** [ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md) 是胃经、胆经与阳维脉的交会穴。

#### 定位 (Location)

位于前额发际角处，距[DU24 神庭](/point/DU24.md) 旁开4.5寸，前发际内0.5寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 定位此穴有三种方法：i. 找到前发际内0.5寸水平线与颞部发际后0.5寸垂直线的交点；ii. 定位瞳孔直上方，患者直视前方时，位于前发际后0.5寸中线上的[DU24 神庭](/point/DU24.md)，以及位于瞳孔直上方的[GB15 头临泣](/point/GB15.md)。[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md) 位于连接这两个穴位的延长线上，且距离是其两倍；iii. 位于颞肌起止线上方0.5寸，[MHN9 太阳](/point/MHN9.md) 直上方垂直线后0.5寸。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

横刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Note):** 根据几部经典文献，此穴禁灸。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 祛风止痛
* 明目

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

头痛，偏头痛伴恶寒发热，眩晕，呕吐。
视物模糊，眼痛如裂，迎风流泪，眼睑跳动。
呼吸困难伴心烦憋闷，半身不遂。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md) 是治疗头痛的重要穴位。它位于前额发际角，是胃经与胆经和阳维脉的交会穴。这三条对头部有重要影响的经脉在此交会，体现在此穴的名称“头维”（头部的纲维）中。胃经属阳明，主前额；胆经属少阳，主颞部；而阳维脉则联络全身所有阳经，包括主头顶的督脉和膀胱经。虽然[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md) 的特长是治疗前额头痛，但通过改变针刺方向，它也可用于治疗颞部和头顶头痛。

[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md) 特别适用于因风邪引起的头痛和眼部疾病，无论是侵袭头部经络的外感风邪，还是内生的风邪。就外感头痛而言，虽然它适用于风寒和风热两种情况，但许多经典著作强调其在治疗伴有眼痛如裂的偏头痛方面的应用，这些症状通常与风热相关。热为阳邪，其性上炎扩散，阻滞经络，导致头部胀痛如裂。在治疗伴有眼痛或眼睑跳动的头痛时，[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md) 在经典处方中常与[BL2 攒竹](/point/BL2.md) 联合使用。[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md) 还主治伴有呕吐的剧烈头痛，特别适用于伴有恶心或呕吐、视物模糊或眼痛如裂的偏头痛。[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md) 清泻头部内生风邪的能力不仅体现在它治疗这类头痛，也体现在它治疗眩晕和半身不遂方面。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 治疗头痛的重要穴位（胃经、胆经与阳维脉的交会穴）。特别适用于（向前针刺）前额痛、风热头痛和偏头痛（伴有胀裂感、眼痛如裂、视物模糊、呕吐），但也可（通过改变针刺方向）用于治疗头顶痛和颞部头痛，以及各种病因引起的头痛。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 头痛伴眼痛：[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md) 和[BL2 攒竹](/point/BL2.md)（《玉龙歌》）。
* 头风伴胀裂痛、眼痛如裂和流泪：[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md) 和[BL2 攒竹](/point/BL2.md)（《金鉴》）。
* 眉棱骨痛：[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md) 和[BL2 攒竹](/point/BL2.md)（《玉龙歌》）。
* 眼睑跳动：[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md) 和[BL2 攒竹](/point/BL2.md)（《针灸大成》）。
* 偏头痛伴眼痛如裂：[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md) 和[PC7 大陵](/point/PC7.md)（《千金方》）。
* 偏头痛或全头风：[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md)，[DU20 百会](/point/DU20.md)，[DU21 前顶](/point/DU21.md)，[DU23 上星](/point/DU23.md)，[DU24 神庭](/point/DU24.md)，[SJ23 丝竹空](/point/SJ23.md)，[GB20 风池](/point/GB20.md)，[LI4 合谷](/point/LI4.md) 和[BL2 攒竹](/point/BL2.md)（《针灸大成》）。
* 迎风流泪：[ST8 头维](/point/ST8.md)，[BL1 睛明](/point/BL1.md)，[GB20 风池](/point/GB20.md) 和[GB15 头临泣](/point/GB15.md)（《针灸大成》）。
