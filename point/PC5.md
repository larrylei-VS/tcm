### Intermediate Messenger JIĀNSHǏ
![[PC-5.png|250]]


- Jing-River and Metal point of the Pericardium channel

##### Location 

On the flexor aspect of the forearm, 3 cun proximal to [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/262), between the tendons of palmaris longus and flexor carpi radialis.

##### Location note 

i. In the absence of the tendon of palmaris longus, locate this point on the ulnar side of the tendon of flexor carpi radialis; ii. Divide the distance between the cubital crease and [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/262) into quarters and locate this point at the junction of the proximal three quarters and the distal quarter.

##### Needling 

Perpendicular insertion, 0.5 to 1 cun or oblique proximal insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

##### Actions 

- Transforms phlegm
- Settles and calms the spirit
- Descends rebellious qi and regulates the Stomach
- Regulates menstruation

##### Indications 

- Sudden Heart pain, palpitations, oppression of the chest, apprehension, propensity to fright, epilepsy, mania, agitation and restlessness, poor memory, loss of voice, halting speech, sudden mania, manic raving as if seeing ghosts, sudden fright disorder in children, ghost evil.
- Plumstone qi (globus hystericus), goitre, scrofula of the neck, drooling following windstroke, obstruction of qi following windstroke leading to impaired breathing.
- Epigastric pain, vomiting, retching, nausea, vomiting and spitting of blood, vomiting of foam, sudden turmoil disorder, abdominal pain, clonic spasm.
- Aversion to wind and cold, febrile disease, malaria, red face and yellow eyes.
- Irregular menstruation, dysmenorrhoea, diseases of menstruation, clotted menstrual flow, leucorrhoea, retention of lochia, painful urinary dysfunction, urethral pain.
- Swelling of the axilla, pain of the inner aspect of the elbow and arm, heat of the palms, weakness of the wrist.

##### Commentary 

The key action of Jiānshǐ PC-5 is to transform phlegm in the upper jiao, and predominantly in the Heart. Together with [Fēnglóng ST-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/ST/386) it is one of the two main acupuncture points to treat phlegm disorders. Phlegm obstructing the Heart may arise in one of the following ways: i. excess of any of the seven emotions results in stagnation of qi which hinders the smooth circulation of body fluids, and by transforming into fire, further condenses the stagnant body fluids to form phlegm; ii. stagnation of Liver qi impairs the Spleen's function of transforming and transporting fluids which form phlegm and rise with stagnant qi to disturb the Heart; iii. high fever condenses the body fluids into phlegm, in which case Chinese medicine convention ascribes the disorder to the Pericardium; iv. fright generates phlegm, a concept expounded by Gong Ju-zhong in A Spot of Snow on a Red Hot Stove[6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-pericardium-channel/references) "Phlegm is produced by fright. The spirit leaves its residence, and when the residence is empty, the fluids will form phlegm".

When phlegm or phlegm-fire obstruct and agitate the portals of the Heart, the spirit will be disturbed to varying degrees. There may be milder symptoms such as agitation, apprehension, propensity to fright, poor memory, restlessness and being easily startled, or more severe symptoms such as mania, manic raving, epilepsy and what was known as 'ghost evil', a disorder probably attributed to demonic possession. Jiānshǐ PC-5 also treats phlegm manifestations such as drooling following windstroke, vomiting of foam, swelling of the axilla, goitre, scrofula, and plumstone qi (globus hystericus), a form of stagnation and obstruction by qi and phlegm characterised by a sensation of throat blockage which worsens or ameliorates according to fluctuations in the emotional state. The importance of Jiānshǐ PC-5 in the treatment of mania disorder and epilepsy is reflected by the fact that Sun Si-miao's Supplement to the Thousand Ducat Formulas suggested that this point was Guilu (one of the thirteen ghost points), rather than [Shēnmài BL-62](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/BL/59) which was said to correspond to Guilu in the Thousand Ducat Formulas.

The Pericardium channel and its interiorly-exteriorly related Sanjiao channel both connect with the upper, middle and lower jiao. The action of Jiānshǐ PC-5, therefore, is not confined to the upper jiao. In the middle jiao, like [Nèiguān PC-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/261), it regulates the function of the Stomach and promotes its descending function, being indicated when Stomach qi stagnates giving rise to pain, and when Stomach qi rebels upwards giving rise to vomiting and nausea. Its ability to regulate the Stomach is considered less powerful than that of [Nèiguān PC-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/261), but it may be used when nausea and vomiting is due to retention of phlegm in the middle jiao. Jiānshǐ PC-5 is also indicated for sudden turmoil disorder characterised by acute vomiting and diarrhoea.

According to the Essential Questions[7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-pericardium-channel/references) "The bao mai (uterine channel) pertains to the Heart and is connected with the uterus". Jiānshǐ PC-5 is one of the few points of either the Heart or Pericardium channels that has an action on gynaecological disorders, being indicated for irregular menstruation, dysmenorrhoea, clotted menstrual flow, retention of the lochia and leucorrhoea. Its action on the lower jiao also extends to the treatment of painful urinary dysfunction and urethral pain.

Finally, according to the Spiritual Pivot[8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-pericardium-channel/references) the jing-river points are indicated for 'changes in the patient's voice' and Jiānshǐ PC-5 is indicated for halting speech and loss of voice.

##### Summary of clinical application 

- Important point to transform phlegm in the Heart and upper jiao: agitation, propensity to fright, poor memory, restlessness, easily startled, mania, manic raving, possession by 'ghost evil', epilepsy, drooling following windstroke, vomiting of foam, goitre, plumstone qi (globus hystericus).
- The Pericardium channel descends through the middle jiao: essential point for nausea, vomiting and epigastric pain.
- “The bao mai (uterine channel) pertains to the Heart and is connected with the uterus” (Essential Questions): [Jiānshǐ PC-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/260) treats irregular menstruation, dysmenorrhoea, clotted menstrual flow, retention of the lochia and leucorrhoea.

### **[PC5 间使](point/PC5.md)** (JIĀNSHǏ)

* **英文名称：** Intermediate Messenger
* **中文名称：** 间使
* **国际代码：** PC-5
* **穴位属性：** **[PC5 间使](point/PC5.md)** 是心包经的经穴和金穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于前臂屈侧，**[PC7 大陵](point/PC7.md)** 上方3寸，掌长肌腱与桡侧腕屈肌腱之间。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 若无掌长肌腱，此穴位于桡侧腕屈肌腱尺侧；
    * 将肘横纹与**[PC7 大陵](point/PC7.md)** 之间的距离分成四等份，此穴位于近端四分之三与远端四分之一的交界处。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至1寸，或向近端斜刺1至1.5寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 化痰
* 镇惊安神
* 降逆气，调理胃腑
* 调经

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

心痛暴作，心悸，胸部憋闷，心神不安，易受惊吓，癫痫，发狂，烦躁不安，记忆力差，失音，言语不利，突然癫狂，狂言谵语如见鬼魅，小儿急慢惊风，鬼邪。
梅核气（咽喉异物感），瘿瘤，颈部瘰疬，中风后流涎，中风后气机阻滞导致呼吸困难。
胃脘痛，呕吐，干呕，恶心，呕血，咯血，呕吐泡沫，霍乱吐泻，腹痛，痉挛。
恶风畏寒，热病，疟疾，面赤，眼睛发黄。
月经不调，痛经，月经病，月经血块，带下，恶露不尽，淋证（小便涩痛），尿道疼痛。
腋窝肿胀，肘臂内侧疼痛，掌心发热，腕部无力。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[PC5 间使](point/PC5.md)** 的关键作用是化解上焦，特别是心部的痰。它与**[ST40 丰隆](point/ST40.md)** 一起是治疗痰饮病的两大主要穴位。痰阻心窍可能以以下方式产生：i. 七情过极导致气滞，阻碍津液顺畅循环，并化火，进一步凝结滞留的津液形成痰；ii. 肝气郁滞影响脾的运化水液功能，形成痰，痰随气滞上扰心神；iii. 高热炼液成痰，在这种情况下，中医将此病归结于心包；iv. 惊吓生痰，龚居中在《红炉点雪》中阐发此概念：“痰因惊而生。神离其舍，舍空则津液结而为痰。”

当痰或痰火阻滞并扰乱心窍时，心神会受到不同程度的干扰。可能出现较轻的症状，如烦躁、心神不安、易受惊吓、记忆力差、烦躁不安和易受惊；或更严重的症状，如发狂、狂言谵语、癫痫以及被称为“鬼邪”的病症，后者可能被归因于魔邪附体。**[PC5 间使](point/PC5.md)** 也治疗痰饮表现，如中风后流涎、呕吐泡沫、腋窝肿胀、瘿瘤、瘰疬，以及梅核气（癔病性咽喉异物感），这是一种气痰阻滞的症状，特点是咽喉阻塞感，随情绪波动而加重或缓解。**[PC5 间使](point/PC5.md)** 在治疗癫狂和癫痫中的重要性体现在孙思邈的《千金方翼方》中，该书曾暗示此穴是鬼路（十三鬼穴之一），而非**[BL62 申脉](point/BL62.md)**（《千金方》中称其为鬼路）。

心包经及其相表里的三焦经都与上、中、下三焦相连。因此，**[PC5 间使](point/PC5.md)** 的作用不限于上焦。在中焦，它像**[PC6 内关](point/PC6.md)** 一样，调理胃腑功能，促进其下降功能，主治胃气郁滞引起疼痛，以及胃气上逆引起呕吐和恶心。其调理胃腑的能力被认为不如**[PC6 内关](point/PC6.md)** 强大，但当恶心呕吐是由于中焦痰饮内停时，也可使用。**[PC5 间使](point/PC5.md)** 也主治霍乱吐泻，其特点是急性呕吐和腹泻。

根据《素问·水热穴论》记载：“胞脉者，属心而络于胞中”。**[PC5 间使](point/PC5.md)** 是心经或心包经中少数几个对妇科疾病有作用的穴位之一，主治月经不调、痛经、月经血块、恶露不尽和带下。其对下焦的作用也延伸至治疗淋证（小便涩痛）和尿道疼痛。

最后，根据《灵枢·本输》记载，经穴主治“病在音声”的表现，而**[PC5 间使](point/PC5.md)** 主治言语不利和失音。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗心部和上焦痰饮的重要穴位：烦躁，易受惊吓，记忆力差，烦躁不安，易受惊吓，发狂，狂言谵语，鬼邪附体，癫痫，中风后流涎，呕吐泡沫，瘿瘤，梅核气（咽喉异物感）。
* 心包经下行通过中焦：治疗恶心、呕吐和胃脘痛的关键穴位。
* “胞脉者，属心而络于胞中”（《素问·水热穴论》）：**[PC5 间使](point/PC5.md)** 治疗月经不调、痛经、月经血块、恶露不尽和带下。
