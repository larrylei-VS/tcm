### Ancestral Meeting  HUÌZŌNG
- Xi-Cleft point of the Sanjiao channel
![[SJ-7 会宗.png|250]]



#### Location 

3 cun proximal to Yángchí SJ-4, level with and on the ulnar side of Zhīgōu SJ-6, in the depression between the ulna and the extensor digitorum communis muscle.

#### Location note 

i. Divide the distance between Yángchí SJ-4 and the lateral epicondyle into half and then halve the distance between this midpoint and Yángchí SJ-4; ii. The point lies approximately one finger breadth to the ulnar side of Zhīgōu SJ-6, close to the border of the ulna.

#### Needling 

Slightly oblique insertion towards the ulnar side or oblique proximal or distal insertion towards the elbow or wrist respectively, 0.5 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions 

- Clears the Sanjiao channel and benefits the ears

#### Indications 

- Deafness, tinnitus, epilepsy, pain of the skin and flesh.

#### Summary of clinical application 

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for deafness, tinnitus, epilepsy, pain of the skin and flesh.

#### Combinations 

- Impaired hearing and deafness: Huìzōng SJ-7 and Wàiguān SJ-5 (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Deafness: Huìzōng SJ-7, Yìfēng SJ-17 and Xiàguān ST-7 (Systematic Classic).

### **[SJ7 会宗](/point/SJ7.md)** (HUÌZŌNG)

* **英文名称：** Ancestral Meeting
* **中文名称：** 会宗
* **国际代码：** SJ-7
* **穴位属性：** **[SJ7 会宗](/point/SJ7.md)** 是三焦经的郄穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于**[SJ4 阳池](/point/SJ4.md)** 上方3寸，与**[SJ6 支沟](/point/SJ6.md)** 平齐并偏尺侧，在尺骨与指总伸肌之间凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 将**[SJ4 阳池](/point/SJ4.md)** 与肱骨外上髁之间的距离平分，然后将中点与**[SJ4 阳池](/point/SJ4.md)** 之间的距离再次平分；
    * 此穴大致位于**[SJ6 支沟](/point/SJ6.md)** 尺侧一横指处，靠近尺骨边缘。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

稍斜向尺侧进针，或斜向近端或远端（分别向肘部或腕部）进针，0.5至1.5寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 清三焦经邪气，宣利耳部
* 宁心安神

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

耳聋，耳鸣，癫痫，皮肤肌肉疼痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SJ7 会宗](/point/SJ7.md)** 临床上较少使用。作为三焦经的郄穴，其名称“会宗”可能指气血在此汇聚，或其与三焦总领一身气机之功能相关。

此穴主要用于**清泻三焦经的邪气**，特别是**热邪**，并**宣利耳部**。它主治**耳聋**、**耳鸣**，这些症状往往与三焦经循行耳部受阻或邪热郁结有关。此外，它对**癫痫**和**皮肤肌肉疼痛**也有一定的辅助疗效，这可能与其通经活络、清热的作用相关。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治**耳聋**、**耳鸣**、**癫痫**、**皮肤肌肉疼痛**。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 听力障碍和耳聋：**[SJ7 会宗](/point/SJ7.md)** 和 **[SJ5 外关](/point/SJ5.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 耳聋：**[SJ7 会宗](/point/SJ7.md)**，**[SJ17 翳风](/point/SJ17.md)** 和 **[ST7 下关](/point/ST7.md)**（《针灸甲乙经》）。
