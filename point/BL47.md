### Gate of the Ethereal Soul HÚNMÉN
![[BL-47 魂门.png|250]]
#### Location

3 cun lateral to the midline, level with the lower border of the spinous process of the ninth thoracic vertebra (T9) and level with [Gānshū BL-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-18).

#### Location Notes

When the shoulder is relaxed, the three cun line corresponds to the medial border of the scapula.

#### Needling

Oblique insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun. Caution: deep perpendicular or deep oblique needling in a medial direction carries a substantial risk of causing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Spreads Liver qi and relaxes the sinews
- Harmonises the middle jiao

#### Indications

- Fullness and distention of the chest and lateral costal region, back pain, contraction of the sinews, bone and joint pain of the whole body, corpse collapse walking disorder, aversion to wind and cold.
- Difficult ingestion, vomiting, diarrhoea, borborygmus, irregular defecation, dark urine.

#### Commentary

Húnmén BL-47 is level with [Gānshū BL-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-18), the back-shu point of the Liver. The Liver stores the ethereal soul (hun), but despite its striking name (Gate of the Ethereal Soul), psychological and emotional indications for this point are notable by their absence in major classical texts[63](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references). Húnmén BL-47 does, however, have some action on regulating the Liver qi and soothing the sinews, and is indicated for fullness and distention of the chest and lateral costal region, contraction of the sinews, and bone and joint pain of the whole body. It is also able to regulate the middle jiao, treating disharmony of both the Spleen (diarrhoea, borborygmus) and Stomach (difficult ingestion, vomiting).

According to the Essential Questions[64](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references), the five outer Bladder points level with the five zang back-shu points (i.e. [Pòhù BL-42](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-42), [Shéntáng BL-44](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-44), Húnmén BL-47, [Yìshè BL-49](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-49) and [Zhìshì BL-52](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-52)) drain heat from the five zang, an action shared by the back-shu points of the five zang.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Treats pain and spasm of the chest, back, sinews and joints: fullness and distention of the chest and lateral costal region, back pain, contraction of the sinews, bone and joint pain of the whole body.
- Treats the Spleen and Stomach: difficult ingestion, vomiting, diarrhoea, borborygmus, irregular defecation.

#### Combinations

- Chest pain: Húnmén BL-47, [Fèishū BL-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-13), [Yúnmén LU-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-2), [Zhōngfǔ LU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-1), [Yǐnbài SP-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-1), [Qímén LIV-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-14) and [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-7) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Cold Stomach with difficulty in digesting food: Húnmén BL-47 and [Wèishū BL-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-21) (One Hundred Symptoms).

### **[BL47 魂门](point/BL47.md)** (HÚNMÉN)

* **英文名称：** Gate of the Ethereal Soul
* **中文名称：** 魂门
* **国际代码：** BL-47
* **穴位属性：** **[BL47 魂门](point/BL47.md)** 是膀胱经第二侧线上的穴位，与**[BL18 肝俞](point/BL18.md)** 平齐。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第九胸椎（T9）棘突下缘水平，距中线旁开3寸，与**[BL18 肝俞](point/BL18.md)** 平齐。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 肩部放松时，旁开3寸线对应肩胛骨内侧缘。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺0.3至0.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直深刺或向内侧深斜刺有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 疏肝理气，舒筋活络
* 调和中焦

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

胸胁胀满，背痛，筋脉挛缩，全身骨节疼痛，尸厥（昏厥）伴行走障碍，恶风畏寒。
吞咽困难，呕吐，腹泻，肠鸣，排便不规律，尿黄。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL47 魂门](point/BL47.md)** 与肝的背俞穴**[BL18 肝俞](point/BL18.md)** 平齐。肝藏魂（魄），但尽管其名称引人注目（魂门，意为“魂魄之门户”），主要经典文献中却几乎没有提及此穴用于治疗心理或情志障碍。然而，**[BL47 魂门](point/BL47.md)** 对调理肝气和舒筋活络有一定作用，主治胸胁胀满、筋脉挛缩和全身骨节疼痛。它还能调和中焦，治疗脾（腹泻、肠鸣）和胃（吞咽困难、呕吐）的不和。

根据《素问·灵兰秘典论》记载，与五脏背俞穴平齐的五个膀胱经外侧穴位（即**[BL42 魄户](point/BL42.md)**、**[BL44 神堂](point/BL44.md)**、**[BL47 魂门](point/BL47.md)**、**[BL49 意舍](point/BL49.md)** 和**[BL52 志室](point/BL52.md)**）具有清泄五脏热邪的作用，这一作用与五脏背俞穴相同。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗胸部、背部、筋脉和关节的疼痛和痉挛：胸胁胀满，背痛，筋脉挛缩，全身骨节疼痛。
* 调理脾胃：吞咽困难，呕吐，腹泻，肠鸣，排便不规律。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 胸痛：**[BL47 魂门](point/BL47.md)**、**[BL13 肺俞](point/BL13.md)**、**[LU2 云门](point/LU2.md)**、**[LU1 中府](point/LU1.md)**、**[SP1 隐白](point/SP1.md)**、**[LIV14 期门](point/LIV14.md)** 和 **[PC7 大陵](point/PC7.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 胃寒伴消化困难：**[BL47 魂门](point/BL47.md)** 和 **[BL21 胃俞](point/BL21.md)**（《百症赋》）。
