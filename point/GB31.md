### Wind Market FĒNGSHÌ
![[GB-31 风市.png|250]]
#### Location

On the lateral aspect of the thigh, directly below the greater trochanter, 7 cun superior to the popliteal crease.

#### Location Notes

i. To palpate the prominence of the greater trochanter place a hand over the lateral aspect of the hip joint at the level of the perineum; ii. The distance between the prominence of the greater trochanter and the popliteal crease is 19 cun, thus Fēngshì GB-31 is located at the tender point approximately 1 cun proximal to the junction of the upper two thirds and lower third of this line; iii. This point is traditionally described as being located where the tip of the middle finger reaches when a person stands erect with their arms extended by their sides, although the significant differences found in body proportions render this unreliable.

#### Needling

i. Perpendicular or slightly oblique insertion posteriorly 1 to 2 cun; ii. Oblique insertion, directed proximally or distally, 1.5 to 2.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Eliminates wind
- Alleviates itching
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Hemiplegia, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limb, cold painful obstruction, sciatica, leg qi, numbness of the leg, weakness and feebleness of the legs, heavy sensation of the legs with difficulty in sitting, contraction and pain of the knees.
- Itching of the whole body, urticaria.

#### Commentary

As its name 'Wind Market' implies, Fēngshì GB-31 is an important point to treat disorders of wind, with three main spheres of application. Firstly it is indicated for pathogenic wind-damp which attacks the lower limb and gives rise to painful obstruction, especially where wind is the main pathogenic factor and the pain is wandering in nature. The Complete Works of Jing-yue stated "Fēngshì GB-31 is the essential point for treating wind painful obstruction". Secondly, Fēngshì GB-31 is recommended for when pathogenic wind gives rise to skin diseases characterised by rapid onset and itching, for example urticaria. Thirdly Fēngshì GB-31 is indicated for hemiplegia following windstroke.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Dispels wind-damp in the leg; especially for wandering painful obstruction.
- Dispels wind in skin disease (itching of the whole body, urticaria) marked by sudden onset and itching.
- For sciatica and hemiplegia.

#### Combinations

- Windstroke: first needle the healthy arm and leg and then the diseased arm and leg, using Fēngshì GB-31, [Qiūxū GB-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-40) and [Yánglíngquán GB-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-34) (Great Compendium).
- Weakness of the legs: Moxa Fēngshì GB-31, [Tàichōng LIV-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-3) and [Lìduì ST-45](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-45) (Outline of Medicine).
- Lack of strength in the legs: Fēngshì GB-31 and [Yīnshì ST-33](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-33) (Ode of the Jade Dragon).
- Lumbar pain that radiates down the leg: Fēngshì GB-31, [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30) and [Xíngjiān LIV-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-2) (Miscellaneous Diseases).
- Lumbar pain with difficulty in moving: Fēngshì GB-31, [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) and [Xíngjiān LIV-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-2) (Glorious Anthology).
- Pain of the legs and lumbar region: Fēngshì GB-31, [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30), [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40), [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60), [Yīnshì ST-33](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-33), [Chéngshān BL-57](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-57) and [Shēnmài BL-62](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-62) (Great Compendium).
- Painful obstruction of the calf: Fēngshì GB-31 and [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60) (Compilation).


### 1. [GB31 风市](point/GB31.md)

#### 1.1. Wind Market 風市

#### 1.2. 定位

位于大腿外侧面，股骨大转子正下方，腘横纹上方7寸。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

i. 为探查股骨大转子突出点，将手置于髋关节外侧，会阴水平处；  
ii. 股骨大转子突出点至腘横纹的距离为19寸，因此[GB31 风市](point/GB31.md)位于此连线约上三分之二与下三分之一交界处上方1寸的敏感点；  
iii. 传统描述此穴为站立时手臂自然下垂，中指尖所及之处，但由于人体比例差异，此方法不可靠。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

i. 垂直或稍向后斜刺，深1至2寸；  
ii. 向近端或远端斜刺，深1.5至2.5寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 祛风
- 止痒
- 活络止痛

#### 1.6. 主治

- 偏瘫、下肢萎症及痛痹、寒痛痹、坐骨神经痛、腿气、腿麻、腿软无力、腿重难坐、膝挛痛。
- 全身瘙痒、荨麻疹。

#### 1.7. 论述

正如其名“风市”所示，[GB31 风市](point/GB31.md)是治疗风病的重要穴位，主要有三大应用领域。首先，它适用于风湿侵袭下肢引起的痛痹，尤其是以风为主、疼痛游走的情况。《景岳全书》称“[GB31 风市](point/GB31.md)为治疗风痛痹的关键穴”。其次，[GB31 风市](point/GB31.md)推荐用于风邪引起的皮肤病，特征为起病急且瘙痒，如荨麻疹。第三，[GB31 风市](point/GB31.md)适用于中风后的偏瘫。

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 祛腿部风湿；特别是游走性痛痹。
- 祛皮肤病之风（全身瘙痒、荨麻疹），以突发及瘙痒为特征。
- 用于坐骨神经痛及偏瘫。
