### Earth Pivot DÌJĪ
![[SP-8 地机.png|250]]
- Xi-Cleft point of the Spleen channel

#### Location

On the medial side of the lower leg, 3 cun inferior to [Yīnlíngquán SP-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-9), in a depression just posterior to the medial crest of the tibia.

#### Location Notes

i. This point is most readily located one handbreadth inferior to [Yīnlíngquán SP-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-9); ii. Alternatively, it may be located at the junction of the upper third and the lower two thirds of a line drawn between the popliteal crease and the prominence of the medial malleolus.

#### Needling

Perpendicular or oblique insertion, 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Regulates menstruation and invigorates blood
- Harmonises the Spleen and resolves dampness
- Moderates acute conditions

#### Indications

- Irregular menstruation, dysmenorrhoea, abdominal (zheng jia) masses in women.
- Distention of the abdomen and lateral costal region, pain of the abdomen, poor appetite, diarrhoea with thin stools, dysenteric disorder, difficult urination, oedema, leucorrhoea, seminal emission, lumbar pain, insufficiency of essence.

#### Commentary

Dìjī SP-8 is the xi-cleft point of the Spleen channel. The xi-cleft points are where the qi and blood, which flow relatively superficially along the channels from the jing-well points, gather and plunge more deeply. The xi-cleft points in general are indicated in the treatment of acute conditions and pain, whilst the xi-cleft points of the yin channels have an additional action of treating disorders of blood.

The Spleen controls blood and its channel enters the lower abdomen, joining with the Conception vessel at [Zhōngjí REN-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-3) and [Guānyuán REN-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-4). Dìjī SP-8 has a specific and important action on resolving blood stasis in the uterus and lower abdomen, being indicated in the treatment of dysmenorrhoea, irregular menstruation, and abdominal masses in women due to either blood stasis or qi stagnation. A specific indication for this point mentioned in the Illustrated Classic of Acupuncture Points on the Bronze Man is a hot flowing sensation spreading down the inner thigh to the knee when pressure is applied to abdominal masses in women. As the xi-cleft point, Dìjī SP-8 is especially suited to treating acute conditions, and it is often combined with [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) in the treatment of acute dysmenorrhoea, both points being strongly reduced, often with the addition of electro-acupuncture. Dìjī SP-8 has a secondary action of harmonising the Spleen and resolving damp, being indicated in the treatment of abdominal distention, poor appetite, difficult urination, oedema and leucorrhoea, especially when these symptoms accompany disorders of menstruation.

The affinity of Dìjī SP-8 for disorders of the lower abdomen is emphasised in various classics. The Investigation Into Points Along the ChannelsInvestigation Into Points Along the Channels by Yan Zhen-shi of the Ming dynasty goes as far as to say "there is no disorder of the lower region that cannot be treated with Dìjī SP-8", whilst according to the Ode to Elucidate Mysteries Dìjī SP-8 dominates the lower region ([Dàbāo SP-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-21) dominates the upper region and [Tiānshū ST-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-25) the middle region). In the correspondence system of early Chinese thought, the upper region is heaven, the middle region is man and the lower region is earth, hence the name of this point 'Earth Pivot'.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Important point for dysmenorrhoea, uterine masses, abdominal masses and irregular menstruation.
- Often combined with Hegu LI-4 for acute dysmenorrhoea.
- For abdominal distention, poor appetite, difficult urination, oedema and leucorrhoea, especially when these symptoms accompany disorders of menstruation.

#### Combinations

- Irregular menstruation: Dìjī SP-8 and [Xuèhǎi SP-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-10) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Cold-damp shan disorder: Dìjī SP-8, [Dàjù ST-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-27) and [Zhōngdū LIV-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-6) (Systematic Classic).
- No pleasure in eating: Dìjī SP-8, [Yīnlíngquán SP-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-9), [Shuǐfēn REN-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-9), [Yōumén KID-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-21) and [Xiǎochángshū BL-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-27) (Supplementing Life).
- Acute dysmenorrhoea: Dìjī SP-8 and [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4).

### [SP8 地机](/point/SP8.md) (DÌJĪ)

* **英文名称：** Earth Pivot
* **中文名称：** 地机
* **国际代码：** SP-8
* **穴位属性：** [SP8 地机](/point/SP8.md) 是脾经的郄穴。

#### 定位 (Location)

位于小腿内侧，距[SP9 阴陵泉](/point/SP9.md) 下方3寸，胫骨内侧缘后方的凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 此穴最易定位在[SP9 阴陵泉](/point/SP9.md) 下方一横掌处；
    * 或者，它可定位在腘横纹与内踝尖连线上方三分之一与下方三分之二的交界处。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直或斜刺1至1.5寸。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 调经活血
* 调和脾胃，化湿
* 缓急止痛（缓解急性病症）

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

月经不调，痛经，女性腹部癥瘕积聚。
腹部和胁肋部胀满，腹痛，食欲不振，大便稀溏，痢疾，小便不利，水肿，带下，遗精，腰痛，精气不足。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[SP8 地机](/point/SP8.md) 是脾经的郄穴。郄穴是气血从井穴沿经脉相对浅层流注到此聚集并深入的地方。郄穴通常主治急性病症和疼痛，而阴经的郄穴还有治疗血症的额外作用。

脾脏主血，其经脉进入下腹部，在[REN3 中极](/point/REN3.md) 和[REN4 关元](/point/REN4.md) 处与任脉交会。[SP8 地机](/point/SP8.md) 在化解子宫和下腹部血瘀方面具有特定而重要的作用，主治痛经、月经不调以及女性腹部癥瘕积聚（由血瘀或气滞引起）。《铜人腧穴针灸图经》中提及此穴的一个特定主治是，按压女性腹部肿块时，会感觉热流沿大腿内侧向下扩散至膝盖。作为郄穴，[SP8 地机](/point/SP8.md) 特别适合治疗急性病症，常与[LI4 合谷](/point/LI4.md) 联合用于治疗急性痛经，这两个穴位均采用强泻法，常加用电针。[SP8 地机](/point/SP8.md) 具有调和脾胃、化湿的次要作用，主治腹胀、食欲不振、小便不利、水肿和带下，特别是当这些症状伴随月经失调时。

[SP8 地机](/point/SP8.md) 对下腹部疾病的亲和力在各种经典中都有强调。明代严震时著《循经考穴编》甚至指出“下部之疾，无不取地机而愈”，而根据《标幽赋》记载，[SP8 地机](/point/SP8.md) 主宰下部（[SP21 大包](/point/SP21.md) 主宰上部，[ST25 天枢](/point/ST25.md) 主宰中部）。在早期中医思想的对应系统中，上部为天，中部为人，下部为地，因此此穴得名“地机”。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 治疗痛经、子宫癥瘕、腹部癥瘕和月经不调的重要穴位。
* 常与[LI4 合谷](/point/LI4.md) 联合治疗急性痛经。
* 治疗腹胀、食欲不振、小便不利、水肿和带下，特别是当这些症状伴随月经失调时。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 月经不调：[SP8 地机](/point/SP8.md) 和[SP10 血海](/point/SP10.md)（《百症赋》）。
* 寒湿疝气：[SP8 地机](/point/SP8.md)，[ST27 大巨](/point/ST27.md) 和[LIV6 中都](/point/LIV6.md)（《针灸甲乙经》）。
* 食欲不振：[SP8 地机](/point/SP8.md)，[SP9 阴陵泉](/point/SP9.md)，[REN9 水分](/point/REN9.md)，[KID21 幽门](/point/KID21.md) 和[BL27 小肠俞](/point/BL27.md)（《资生经》）。
* 急性痛经：[SP8 地机](/point/SP8.md) 和[LI4 合谷](/point/LI4.md)。
