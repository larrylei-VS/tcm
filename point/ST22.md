### Pass Gate GUĀNMÉN
![[ST-22 关门.png|250]]
#### Location

On the abdomen, 2 cun lateral to the midline and 3 cun superior to the umbilicus, level with [Jiànlǐ REN-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-11).

#### Location Notes

The 2 cun line is located halfway between the midline and the palpable lateral border of the rectus abdominis muscle.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.  
**Caution:** in thin subjects, deep needling may penetrate the peritoneal cavity.

#### Actions

- Regulates qi and alleviates pain
- Regulates the intestines and benefits urination

#### Indications

- Abdominal qi accumulation, sensation of qi moving in the abdomen, abdominal pain, abdominal distention and fullness, acute periumbilical pain.
- Constipation, borborygmus, diarrhoea, dysenteric disorder, poor appetite, oedema, enuresis, phlegm malaria with cold shivering.

#### Commentary

Guānmén ST-22 is included in two elegant combinations for the treatment of enuresis. Both also include [Shénmén HE-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-7), reflecting the principle that the involuntary discharge of urine, especially during sleep, indicates a disorder of the spirit as much as a disorder of the urinary system.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for abdominal qi accumulation, sensation of qi moving in the abdomen, abdominal pain, abdominal distention and fullness, acute periumbilical pain, constipation, borborygmus, diarrhoea, dysenteric disorder, poor appetite, oedema, enuresis, phlegm malaria with cold shivering.

#### Combinations

- Enuresis: Guānmén ST-22, [Shénmén HE-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-7) and [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) (Systematic Classic).
- Enuresis: Guānmén ST-22, [Zhōngfǔ LU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-1) and [Shénmén HE-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-7) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### [ST22 关门](point/ST22.md) (GUĀNMÉN)

* **英文名称：** Pass Gate
* **中文名称：** 关门
* **国际代码：** ST-22

#### 定位 (Location)

位于腹部，距前正中线旁开2寸，脐上3寸，与[REN11 建里](point/REN11.md) 平齐。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 旁开2寸线位于前正中线与可触及的腹直肌外缘之间中点。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直刺1至1.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 瘦弱患者深刺可能刺穿腹膜腔。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 理气止痛
* 调理肠道，利尿

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

腹部气积，腹内气动感，腹痛，腹部胀满，急性脐周疼痛。
便秘，肠鸣，腹泻，痢疾，食欲不振，水肿，遗尿，痰疟伴恶寒发抖。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST22 关门](point/ST22.md) 被纳入治疗遗尿的两个精妙组合中。两者都包含[HE7 神门](point/HE7.md)，这反映了非自主排尿，尤其是在睡眠期间，既是泌尿系统疾病，也是神志疾病的原则。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治腹部气积，腹内气动感，腹痛，腹部胀满，急性脐周疼痛，便秘，肠鸣，腹泻，痢疾，食欲不振，水肿，遗尿，痰疟伴恶寒发抖。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 遗尿：[ST22 关门](point/ST22.md)，[HE7 神门](point/HE7.md) 和[BL40 委中](point/BL40.md)（《针灸甲乙经》）。
* 遗尿：[ST22 关门](point/ST22.md)，[LU1 中府](point/LU1.md) 和[HE7 神门](point/HE7.md)（《千金方》）。
