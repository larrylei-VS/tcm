### Linking Path WÉIDÀO
![[GB-28 维道.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder channel with the Girdling vessel

#### Location

0.5 cun anterior and inferior to [Wǔshū GB-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-27).

#### Location Notes

See [Wǔshū GB-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-27).

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Regulates the Girdling vessel
- Regulates the lower jiao and transforms stagnation

#### Indications

- Uterine prolapse, irregular menstruation, leucorrhoea.
- Pain of the back and ilium, lower abdominal pain, shan disorder.
- No pleasure in eating, vomiting.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for uterine prolapse, irregular menstruation, leucorrhoea, pain of the back and ilium, lower abdominal pain, shan disorder, no pleasure in eating, vomiting.

### 1. [GB28 维道](point/GB28.md)

#### 1.1. Linking Path 維道

- 胆经与带脉的交会穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于[GB27 五枢](point/GB27.md)前方下方0.5寸。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

参见[GB27 五枢](point/GB27.md)的定位说明。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直刺入，深1至1.5寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 调带脉
- 调下焦，化滞

#### 1.6. 主治

- 子宫脱垂、不规则月经、带下病。
- 背髂痛、下腹痛、疝病。
- 食不甘味、呕吐。

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 使用较少。
- 适用于子宫脱垂、不规则月经、带下病、背髂痛、下腹痛、疝病、食不甘味、呕吐。
