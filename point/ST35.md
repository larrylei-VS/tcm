### Calf's Nose DÚBÍ
![[ST-35 犊鼻.png|250]]
#### Location

On the knee, in the hollow formed when the knee is flexed, immediately below the patella and lateral to the patellar ligament.

#### Location Notes

This point is also known as lateral Xiyan, forming a pair with medial [Xīyǎn MN-LE-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/MN-LE-16) [see Extraordinary Points] which lies immediately below the patella and medial to the patellar ligament.

#### Needling

With the knee flexed and supported by a rolled pillow, i. Perpendicular insertion, directed towards [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40), 1 to 2 cun; ii. Oblique insertion in a medial and superior direction, behind the patella 1 to 2 cun, iii. Behind the patellar ligament to join with medial [Xīyǎn MN-LE-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/MN-LE-16).

#### Actions

- Dispels wind-damp and reduces swelling
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Swelling and pain of the knee joint, difficulty in flexing and extending the knee, weakness of the knee joint, numbness of the knee, numbness of the lower limb, atrophy disorder of the lower limb, leg qi.

#### Commentary

Dúbí ST-35, located in the lateral visible hollow below the patella, is an essential point in the treatment of all knee disorders, whether due to deficiency or excess, heat or cold. In clinical practice it is frequently combined with the extra point medial Xiyan (located in the medial visible hollow below the patella), and is therefore most often known as lateral Xiyan.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Important local point (often in combination with medial Xiyan) for the treatment of knee disorders: swelling and pain, difficulty in flexing and extending, weakness, numbness etc.

#### Combinations

- Disorders of the knee and below the knee: moxa Dúbí ST-35, [Xīguān LIV-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-7), [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) and [Yánglíngquán GB-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-34) (Supplementing Life).
- Numbness of the knee: Dúbí ST-35, [Bìguān ST-31](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-31) and [Yánglíngquán GB-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-34) (Supplementing Life).

### [ST35 犊鼻](/point/ST35.md) (DÚBÍ)

* **英文名称：** Calf's Nose
* **中文名称：** 犊鼻
* **国际代码：** ST-35

#### 定位 (Location)

屈膝时，此穴位于膝部髌骨下方，髌韧带外侧形成的凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 此穴又称外膝眼，与位于髌骨下方、髌韧带内侧的内膝眼（[MNLE16 膝眼](/point/MNLE16.md) 经外穴）成对。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

屈膝，用卷起的枕头支撑，i. 垂直刺，针尖朝向[BL40 委中](/point/BL40.md)，1至2寸； ii. 斜刺，向内上方，髌骨后方，1至2寸； iii. 透刺髌韧带后方至内膝眼（[MNLE16 膝眼](/point/MNLE16.md)）。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 祛风湿，消肿
* 通经活络，止痛

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

膝关节肿痛，膝关节屈伸不利，膝关节无力，膝部麻木，下肢麻木，下肢痿证，腿气。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST35 犊鼻](/point/ST35.md) 位于髌骨下方外侧可见的凹陷中，是治疗所有膝关节疾病的关键穴位，无论是虚证或实证，热证或寒证。在临床实践中，它常与经外穴内膝眼（位于髌骨下方内侧可见的凹陷中）联合使用，因此最常被称为外膝眼。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 治疗膝关节疾病的重要局部穴位（常与内膝眼联合使用）：肿痛，屈伸不利，无力，麻木等。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 膝关节及膝下疾病：灸[ST35 犊鼻](/point/ST35.md)，[LIV7 膝关](/point/LIV7.md)，[ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md) 和[GB34 阳陵泉](/point/GB34.md)（《资生经》）。
* 膝部麻木：[ST35 犊鼻](/point/ST35.md)，[ST31 髀关](/point/ST31.md) 和[GB34 阳陵泉](/point/GB34.md)（《资生经》）。
