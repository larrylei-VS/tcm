### Upper Arm BÌNÀO
![[LI-14 臂臑.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Large Intestine channel with the Small Intestine and Bladder channels

#### Location

On the lateral side of the upper arm, in the visible and tender depression formed between the distal insertion of the deltoid muscle and the brachialis muscle, approximately three fifths of the distance along the line drawn between [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11) and [Jiānyú LI-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-15).

#### Location Notes

This point is easier to locate if the muscles of the upper arm are tensed.

#### Needling

Oblique insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Regulates qi and dissipates phlegm nodules
- Benefits the eyes

#### Indications

- Pain, numbness and painful obstruction of the upper arm and shoulder, wasting and weakness of the upper arm, inability to raise the arm, contraction and stiffness of the neck.
- Scrofula, goitre, chest pain.
- Redness, swelling and pain of the eyes.

#### Commentary

Although not a major point, Bìnào LI-14 is frequently employed for channel disorders in the upper arm, especially when pain radiates either downwards from the shoulder towards the elbow, or upwards from the elbow towards the shoulder.

Like several points of the Large Intestine and Sanjiao channels located between the elbow and the shoulder, Bìnào LI-14 is also indicated for scrofula and goitre. Scrofula and goitre, although different in aetiology, always involve phlegm, combined either with stagnant qi or with heat. In the case of Bìnào LI-14, its ability to treat these disorders derives mainly from the course of the Large Intestine channel through the lateral side of the neck, rather than a special ability to transform phlegm, and the action of 'regulating qi and transforming phlegm' in this context therefore only applies to scrofula and goitre. The explanation of why several points of the upper arm have this special action whilst the more distal shu-points do not, is unclear (see also [Tiānfǔ LU-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-3), [Jiānyú LI-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-15), [Shǒuwǔlǐ LI-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-13), [Tiānjǐng SJ-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-10) and [Nāohuì SJ-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-13)).

Finally, Bìnào LI-14 also shares with several points of the upper arm the ability to treat disorders of the eyes, in this case redness, swelling and heat.

#### Summary of clinical application

- For local disorders, especially pain of the upper arm which radiates between the elbow and shoulder.
- For scrofula and goitre, and redness, swelling and pain of the eyes.

#### Combinations

- Pain and inability to raise the arm: Bìnào LI-14 and [Zhǒuliáo LI-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-12) (Preserving Life).
- Wasting and weakness of the arm with inability to raise the arm to the head: Bìnào LI-14 and [Jiānyú LI-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-15) (Preserving Life).
- Scrofula: Bìnào LI-14 and [Shǒuwǔlǐ LI-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-13) (One Hundred Symptoms).

### [LI14 臂臑](point/LI14.md) (BÌNÀO)

- **英文名称：** Upper Arm
- **中文名称：** 臂臑
- **国际代码：** LI-14
- **穴位属性：** [LI14 臂臑](point/LI14.md) 是大肠经与小肠经、膀胱经的交会穴。

#### 定位 (Location)

位于上臂外侧，在三角肌远端止点与肱肌之间形成的明显压痛凹陷中，大约在连接[LI11 曲池](point/LI11.md) 与[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md) 连线长度的五分之三处。

- **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    - 上臂肌肉紧张时此穴更容易定位。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

斜刺1至1.5寸。

#### 作用 (Actions)

- 通经活络，止痛
- 调气散结（消散痰核）
- 明目

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

上臂和肩部疼痛、麻木、痹痛，上臂萎缩无力，不能抬臂，颈项拘急僵硬。

瘰疬，瘿瘤，胸痛。

眼睛红肿疼痛。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

尽管[LI14 臂臑](point/LI14.md) 不是一个主要穴位，但它常用于治疗上臂的经络病症，特别是当疼痛从肩部向下放射至肘部，或从肘部向上放射至肩部时。

与肘部和肩部之间大肠经和三焦经的几个穴位一样，[LI14 臂臑](point/LI14.md) 也主治瘰疬和瘿瘤。瘰疬和瘿瘤虽然病因不同，但都与痰有关，常伴有气滞或热邪。[LI14 臂臑](point/LI14.md) 治疗这些疾病的能力主要源于大肠经循行于颈部外侧，而非其特殊的化痰能力，因此在此情境下“调气化痰”的作用仅指瘰疬和瘿瘤。上臂的几个穴位具有这种特殊作用而远端的输穴不具备的原因尚不明确（参阅[LU3 天府](point/LU3.md)、[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md)、[LI13 手五里](point/LI13.md)、[SJ10 天井](point/SJ10.md) 和[SJ13 脑会](point/SJ13.md)）。

最后，[LI14 臂臑](point/LI14.md) 也与上臂的几个穴位一样，具有治疗眼部疾病的能力，此处主治眼睛红肿热痛。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

治疗局部病症，特别是上臂疼痛并放射至肘肩之间。

治疗瘰疬、瘿瘤以及眼睛红肿疼痛。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

手臂疼痛无力抬起：[LI14 臂臑](point/LI14.md) 和[LI12 肘髎](point/LI12.md)（《保命集》）。

手臂萎缩无力，不能抬臂过头：[LI14 臂臑](point/LI14.md) 和[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md)（《保命集》）。

瘰疬：[LI14 臂臑](point/LI14.md) 和[LI13 手五里](point/LI13.md)（《百症赋》）。
