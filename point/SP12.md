### Rushing Gate CHŌNGMÉN
![[SP-12 冲门.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Spleen and Liver channels with the Yin Linking vessel

#### Location

3.5 cun lateral to [Qūgǔ REN-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-2), on the lateral side of the femoral artery.

#### Location Notes

Palpate to locate the pulsation of the femoral artery, just over one handbreadth lateral to the midline, at the level of the upper border of the pubic symphysis. Locate Chōngmén SP-12 in the depression immediately lateral to this pulsation.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion, 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** deep needling in a medial direction may puncture the femoral artery, and in a lateral direction, the femoral nerve.

#### Actions

- Invigorates blood, regulates qi and alleviates pain
- Drains damp, clears heat and regulates urination

#### Indications

- Abdominal pain, cold in the abdomen with fullness, abdominal (ji ju) masses, shan disorder, pain of haemorrhoids, foetal qi rushes upward to the Heart leading to difficulty in breathing, difficult lactation.
- Difficult urination, retention of urine, painful urinary dysfunction, leucorrhoea, sudden turmoil disorder, heat in the body, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limb.

#### Commentary

Chōngmén SP-12 is a coalescent point of the Yin Linking vessel. The Classic of Difficulties says "When the Yin Linking vessel is diseased, Heart pain will result"[19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-spleen-channel/references). Chōngmén SP-12 (Rushing Gate), like [Qìchōng ST-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-30) (Rushing Qi), is indicated for foetal qi rushing up to the Heart. This condition, described by Zhu Dan-xi, manifests as distention, fullness and pain of the abdomen and Heart in a pregnant woman, and is common in the later stages of pregnancy.

#### Summary of clinical application

- For foetal qi rushing up to the Heart: distention, fullness and pain of the abdomen and Heart in a pregnant woman. “When the Yin Linking vessel is diseased, Heart pain will result” (Classic of Difficulties).
- Rarely used clinically.

#### Combinations

- Abdominal fullness and abdominal (ji ju) masses: Chōngmén SP-12 and [Fǔshè SP-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-13) (Supplementing Life).
- The five types of painful urinary dysfunction with inability to urinate: Chōngmén SP-12 and [Dàdūn LIV-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-1) (Supplementing Life).
- Post-partum leucorrhoea and uterine bleeding: Chōngmén SP-12 and [Qìchōng ST-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-30) (One Hundred Symptoms).

### **[SP12 冲门](point/SP12.md)** (CHŌNGMÉN)

* **英文名称：** Rushing Gate
* **中文名称：** 冲门
* **国际代码：** SP-12
* **穴位属性：** **[SP12 冲门](point/SP12.md)** 是脾经与肝经、阴维脉的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于下腹部，距 **[REN2 曲骨](point/REN2.md)** 旁开3.5寸，在股动脉外侧。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 在耻骨联合上缘水平，距中线旁开约一横掌处，触诊股动脉搏动。**[SP12 冲门](point/SP12.md)** 位于此搏动点的紧外侧凹陷中。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺，0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 向内侧深刺可能刺穿股动脉，向外侧深刺可能损伤股神经。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 活血理气，止痛
* 利湿清热，调理小便

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

腹痛，腹部冷痛伴胀满，腹部癥瘕积聚，疝气，痔疮疼痛，胎气上冲心导致呼吸困难，乳汁量少。
小便不利，尿潴留，淋证（小便涩痛），带下，霍乱吐泻，身热，下肢痿证和痹证。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SP12 冲门](point/SP12.md)** 是阴维脉的交会穴。《难经》记载：“阴维脉病，苦心痛。” **[SP12 冲门](point/SP12.md)** (Rushing Gate) 像 **[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md)** (Rushing Qi) 一样，主治胎气上冲心。朱丹溪描述此症表现为孕妇腹部和心窝胀满疼痛，常见于妊娠晚期。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗胎气上冲心：孕妇腹部和心窝胀满疼痛。
* “阴维脉病，苦心痛”（《难经》）。
* 临床上较少使用。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 腹部胀满和腹部癥瘕积聚：**[SP12 冲门](point/SP12.md)** 和 **[SP13 府舍](point/SP13.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 五淋（小便涩痛）伴小便不利：**[SP12 冲门](point/SP12.md)** 和 **[LIV1 大敦](point/LIV1.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 产后带下和子宫出血：**[SP12 冲门](point/SP12.md)** 和 **[ST30 气冲](point/ST30.md)**（《百症赋》）。
