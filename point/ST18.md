### Root of the Breast RǓGĒN
![[ST-18 乳根.png|250]]
#### Location

Directly below the nipple, in the fifth intercostal space.

#### Location Notes

i. First locate the second intercostal space (see [Wūyì ST-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-15) above), then find the fifth intercostal space, three spaces below it; ii. In males the nipple lies in the fourth intercostal space; iii. On a woman, this point lies at the root of the breast, inferior to the breast tissue itself; in females the nipple is unlikely to lie 4 cun lateral to the midline and should not be used as a reference point.

#### Needling

Transverse-oblique insertion laterally or medially along the intercostal space 0.5 to 1 cun, or transverse insertion superiorly or inferiorly along the channel.  
**Caution:** deep or perpendicular insertion carries a substantial risk of puncturing the lung.

#### Actions

- Benefits the breasts and reduces swelling
- Unbinds the chest and alleviates cough and wheezing

#### Indications

- Breast abscess, breast pain, scanty lactation, difficult labour.
- Cough, dyspnoea, oppression and pain below the chest, oppression of the diaphragm, oesophageal constriction with difficult ingestion, sudden turmoil disorder with cramps, inversion counterflow of the four limbs, swelling and pain of the arm.

#### Commentary

Rǔgēn ST-18 is the principle local point for treating disorders of the breast. According to Zhu Dan-xi of the Jin-Yuan dynasty "The breasts are where the yangming passes, and the nipples are ascribed to the jueyin"[9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-stomach-channel/references). Disharmony of the Stomach yangming or Liver jueyin channels is therefore involved in all breast disorders. Since Rǔgēn ST-18 regulates the qi and blood of the breast area as a whole, it may be used for any excess type breast disorder characterised by pain, distention, swelling or abscess, whether due to Stomach fire or oppression and stagnation of Liver qi (the two main excess interior pathological factors in breast disease).

Nursing mothers are particularly prone to breast disorders. Zhu Dan-xi went on to say "If the breast-feeding mother has thick flavoured [food] or bears indignation or grudges, qi will therefore stop circulating and the portals will become blocked. [Because] milk is no longer able to come out, the blood of the yangming becomes hot and transforms into pus"[10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-stomach-channel/references). As well as breast abscess due to these internal factors, Rǔgēn ST-18 is also indicated when exposure of the breast to exterior pathogenic factors, improper suckling, or even, according to Zhu Dan-xi, the burning hot breath of the new-born baby blowing onto the breast, lead to heat, stagnation and swelling. As far as scanty lactation is concerned, this is most commonly due to stagnation of Liver qi resulting in failure of free flow, or to insufficiency of qi and blood, mainly due to deficiency of the Stomach and Spleen. Rǔgēn ST-18 is indicated whatever the pattern.

According to the Great Compendium of Acupuncture and Moxibustion Rǔgēn ST-18 is recommended for "pain of the chest, diaphragm qi". This refers to oppression of the chest with shortness of breath due to stagnation of Liver qi. Rǔgēn ST-18 may also be used more widely for cough or asthma.

Finally, according to the Ode of Xi-hong, Rǔgēn ST-18 may be used to promote and hasten labour, although it is not traditionally contraindicated in pregnancy.

#### Summary of clinical application

- The principle local point for treating disorders of the breast (abscess, pain, disorders of lactation).
- Local point for stagnation of qi in the chest and diaphragm (cough, dyspnoea, oppression and pain).

#### Combinations

- Coughing and wheezing with phlegm: Rǔgēn ST-18 and [Shūfǔ KID-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-27) (Ode of the Jade Dragon).
- Breast abscess: Rǔgēn ST-18, [Yīngchuāng ST-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-16), [Xiàjùxū ST-39](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-39), [Tàichōng LIV-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-3) and [Fùliū KID-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-7) (Compilation).

### [ST18 乳根](/point/ST18.md) (RǓGĒN)

* **英文名称：** Root of the Breast
* **中文名称：** 乳根
* **国际代码：** ST-18

#### 定位 (Location)

位于乳头直下方，第五肋间隙中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 首先找到第二肋间隙（参见[ST15 屋翳](/point/ST15.md)），然后向下找到其下方三个肋间隙，即第五肋间隙；
    * 男性乳头位于第四肋间隙；
    * 女性此穴位于乳房根部，乳腺组织下方；女性乳头不太可能位于中线旁开4寸处，因此不应作为参考点。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

横斜刺，沿肋间隙向外侧或内侧刺0.5至1寸，或沿经络向上或向下横刺。

* **注意 (Caution):** 深刺或垂直刺入有很大风险刺破肺脏。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 宣利乳房，消肿散结
* 宽胸，止咳平喘

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

乳痈，乳房疼痛，乳汁量少，难产。
咳嗽，呼吸困难，胸下憋闷疼痛，膈肌压迫感，食道狭窄吞咽困难，霍乱吐泻伴痉挛，四肢厥逆，手臂肿痛。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST18 乳根](/point/ST18.md) 是治疗乳房疾病的主要局部穴位。金元时期的朱丹溪认为：“乳者阳明之所经，乳头属厥阴之所系。” 因此，胃阳明经或肝厥阴经的失调都与所有乳房疾病有关。由于[ST18 乳根](/point/ST18.md) 整体调理乳房区域的气血，因此可用于任何实证乳房疾患，表现为疼痛、胀满、肿胀或乳痈，无论是由于胃火还是肝气郁结（乳房疾病的两种主要实证内部病邪）。

哺乳期母亲尤其易患乳房疾病。朱丹溪进一步指出：“若哺乳之母食厚味，或抱恚怒，则气滞不行，窍道闭塞。乳汁不出，阳明之血亦热，化为脓也。”除了这些内因导致的乳痈外，[ST18 乳根](/point/ST18.md) 也主治因乳房暴露于外部病邪、不当哺乳，甚至根据朱丹溪的说法，新生儿炙热的呼吸吹向乳房，导致发热、气滞和肿胀的情况。至于乳汁量少，这最常见是由于肝气郁结导致乳汁不畅，或气血不足，主要由于脾胃虚弱所致。[ST18 乳根](/point/ST18.md) 适用于各种证型。

根据《针灸大成》记载，[ST18 乳根](/point/ST18.md) 推荐用于“胸痛，膈气”。这指的是肝气郁结导致的胸部憋闷伴气短。[ST18 乳根](/point/ST18.md) 也可更广泛地用于咳嗽或哮喘。

最后，根据《席弘赋》记载，[ST18 乳根](/point/ST18.md) 可用于催产，尽管传统上并不禁忌妊娠期使用。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 治疗乳房疾病（乳痈，乳房疼痛，乳汁量少）的主要局部穴位。
* 治疗胸膈气滞（咳嗽，呼吸困难，憋闷疼痛）的局部穴位。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 咳嗽喘息伴痰多：[ST18 乳根](/point/ST18.md) 和[KID27 俞府](/point/KID27.md)（《玉龙歌》）。
* 乳痈：[ST18 乳根](/point/ST18.md)，[ST16 膺窗](/point/ST16.md)，[ST39 下巨虚](/point/ST39.md)，[LIV3 太冲](/point/LIV3.md) 和[KID7 复溜](/point/KID7.md)（《聚英》）。
