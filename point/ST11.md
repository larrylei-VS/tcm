### Abode of Qi QÌSHÈ
![[ST-11 气舍.png|250]]
#### Location

At the root of the neck, superior to the medial end of the clavicle, directly below [Rényíng ST-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-9) in the depression between the sternal and clavicular heads of the sternocleidomastoid muscle.

#### Location Notes

Palpation of the sternal and clavicular heads is made easier if the patient turns their head away from the side to be needled, whilst you apply resistance at the chin.

#### Needling

i. Perpendicular insertion 0.2 to 0.3 cun, ii. the needle may then be directed inferiorly along the posterior border of the clavicle, up to 0.5 cun.  
**Caution:** this second method should not be attempted by those who have not had appropriate clinical supervision; deep insertion may penetrate the subclavian vessels or lung.

#### Actions

- Benefits the throat and neck and descends qi

#### Indications

- Swelling and pain of the throat, scrofula, goitre, stiffness of the neck with inability to turn the head.
- Dyspnoea, difficult ingestion, hiccup.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Both [Shuǐtú ST-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-10) and Qìshè ST-11 are indicated for swelling and pain of the throat, goitre, scrofula and symptoms such as cough and dyspnoea.
- Qìshè ST-11 is also indicated for difficult ingestion and hiccup.

#### Combinations

- Goitre, tumours of the neck and swollen throat: Qìshè ST-11, [Tiānfǔ LU-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-3) and [Nāohuì SJ-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-13) (Supplementing Life).
- Rebellious qi coughing: Qìshè ST-11 and [Pòhù BL-42](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-42) (Systematic Classic).
- Cough with rebellious qi, dyspnoea, vomiting of foam and clenched teeth: Qìshè ST-11, [Pòhù BL-42](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-42), [Fútú LI-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-18), [Tiānróng SI-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-17), [Liánquán REN-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-23) and [Yīxǐ BL-45](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-45) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### [ST11 气舍](point/ST11.md) (QÌSHÈ)

* **英文名称：** Abode of Qi
* **中文名称：** 气舍
* **国际代码：** ST-11

#### 定位 (Location)

位于颈根部，锁骨内侧端上方，[ST9 人迎](point/ST9.md) 直下方，胸锁乳突肌的胸骨头和锁骨头之间的凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 为便于触诊胸锁乳突肌的胸骨头和锁骨头，可请患者头部转向针刺侧的对侧，同时施加阻力于下巴。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

i. 垂直刺0.2至0.3寸； ii. 针尖可沿锁骨后缘向下斜刺，最深可达0.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):**
    * 第二种针刺方法（沿锁骨后缘向下斜刺）不应由未受过适当临床指导者尝试；
    * 深刺可能刺穿锁骨下血管或肺脏。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 宣利咽喉颈部，降气

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

咽喉肿痛，瘰疬，瘿瘤，颈项强直不能转头。
呼吸困难，吞咽困难，呃逆。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST11 气舍](point/ST11.md) 临床上较少使用。[ST10 水突](point/ST10.md) 和[ST11 气舍](point/ST11.md) 都主治咽喉肿痛、瘿瘤、瘰疬以及咳嗽、呼吸困难等症状。[ST11 气舍](point/ST11.md) 还主治吞咽困难和呃逆。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治咽喉肿痛、瘿瘤、瘰疬以及咳嗽、呼吸困难等症状。
* 也主治吞咽困难和呃逆。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 瘿瘤，颈部肿瘤和咽喉肿胀：[ST11 气舍](point/ST11.md)，[LU3 天府](point/LU3.md) 和[SJ13 脑会](point/SJ13.md)（《资生经》）。
* 气逆咳嗽：[ST11 气舍](point/ST11.md) 和[BL42 魄户](point/BL42.md)（《针灸甲乙经》）。
* 咳嗽气逆，呼吸困难，呕吐泡沫，牙关紧闭：[ST11 气舍](point/ST11.md)，[BL42 魄户](point/BL42.md)，[LI18 扶突](point/LI18.md)，[SI17 天容](point/SI17.md)，[REN23 廉泉](point/REN23.md) 和[BL45 噫嘻](point/BL45.md)（《千金方》）。
