### Upper Crevice SHÀNGLIÁO
![[BL-31 上髎.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Bladder and Gall Bladder channels

#### Location

Over the first posterior sacral foramen.

#### Location Notes

For guidance on locating the sacral foramen see page 69.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun, or 1.5 to 2 cun through the foramen. Needling through the foramen is facilitated by a slightly oblique medial and inferior insertion.  
**Note:** some classical sources forbid moxibustion at this point.

#### Actions

- Regulates the lower jiao and facilitates urination and defecation
- Regulates menstruation and stops leucorrhoea
- Benefits the lumbar region and legs

#### Indications

- Difficult urination and defecation.
- Irregular menstruation, dysmenorrhoea, infertility, leucorrhoea, incessant white leucorrhoea, red and white leucorrhoea, seminal emission, impotence, itching and pain of the genitals, uterine prolapse.
- Vomiting, nosebleed, malarial chills and fever.
- Lumbar pain, cold and pain of the back and knees.

#### Commentary

See [Cìliáo BL-32](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-32).

#### Summary of clinical application

- Difficult urination and defecation.
- Irregular menstruation, dysmenorrhoea, infertility, leucorrhoea, incessant white leucorrhoea, red and white leucorrhoea, seminal emission, impotence, itching and pain of the genitals, uterine prolapse.
- Vomiting, nosebleed, malarial chills and fever.
- Lumbar pain, cold and pain of the back and knees.

#### Combinations

- Difficult urination and defecation: moxa the eight liao (Shàngliáo BL-31, [Cìliáo BL-32](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-32), [Zhōngliáo BL-33](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-33) and [Xiàliáo BL-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-34) bilaterally) with one hundred cones (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Febrile disease with absence of sweating: Shàngliáo BL-31 and [Kǒngzuì LU-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-6) (Systematic Classic).

### **[BL31 上髎](/point/BL31.md)** (SHÀNGLIÁO)

* **英文名称：** Upper Crevice
* **中文名称：** 上髎
* **国际代码：** BL-31
* **穴位属性：** **[BL31 上髎](/point/BL31.md)** 是膀胱经与胆经的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第一骶后孔处。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 骶孔的定位请参考“Page 69”（此处引用原文，请根据实际参考资料定位）。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至1寸，或通过骶孔透刺1.5至2寸。透刺骶孔时，针尖稍向内侧和下方斜刺可使操作更容易。

* **注意 (Note):** 某些经典文献禁止在此穴施灸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 调理下焦，通利二便
* 调经止带
* 宣利腰腿

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

大小便困难。
月经不调，痛经，不孕症，带下，赤白带下不止，遗精，阳痿，生殖器瘙痒疼痛，子宫脱垂。
呕吐，鼻出血，疟疾恶寒发热。
腰痛，腰背部和膝部寒痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

请参阅**[BL32 次髎](/point/BL32.md)**。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 大小便困难。
* 月经不调，痛经，不孕症，带下，赤白带下不止，遗精，阳痿，生殖器瘙痒疼痛，子宫脱垂。
* 呕吐，鼻出血，疟疾恶寒发热。
* 腰痛，腰背部和膝部寒痛。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 大小便困难：灸八髎穴（双侧**[BL31 上髎](/point/BL31.md)**、**[BL32 次髎](/point/BL32.md)**、**[BL33 中髎](/point/BL33.md)** 和 **[BL34 下髎](/point/BL34.md)**）一百壮（《千金方》）。
* 热病无汗：**[BL31 上髎](/point/BL31.md)** 和 **[LU6 孔最](/point/LU6.md)**（《针灸甲乙经》）。
