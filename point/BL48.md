### Yang’s’ Key Link YÁNGGĀNG
![[BL-48 阳纲.png|250]]
#### Location

3 cun lateral to the midline, level with the lower border of the spinous process of the tenth thoracic vertebra (T10) and level with [Danshu BL‑19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-19).

#### Location Notes

When the shoulder is relaxed, the three cun line corresponds to the medial border of the scapula.

#### Needling

Oblique insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.  
**Caution:** deep perpendicular or deep oblique needling in a medial direction carries a substantial risk of causing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Regulates the Gall Bladder and clears damp-heat
- Harmonises the middle jiao

#### Indications

- Heat in the body, yellow eyes and face, pain of the lateral costal region, dark and hesitant urination, indolence.
- Abdominal pain, abdominal distention, borborygmus, diarrhoea, dysenteric disorder with blood, irregular defecation, difficult ingestion, no pleasure in eating.

#### Commentary

Yánggāng BL-48 is level with [Dǎnshū BL-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-19), the back-shu point of the Gall Bladder. Like [Dǎnshū BL-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-19), it is indicated for damp-heat obstructing the Gall Bladder and giving rise to fever, jaundice, pain of the lateral costal region and dark and hesitant urination. At the same time it is able to regulate the middle jiao and clear dampness and heat, being indicated for distention and pain of the abdomen, diarrhoea, dysenteric disorder, irregular defecation and digestive disorders.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Clears Gall-Bladder damp-heat: yellow eyes and face, pain of the lateral costal region, dark and hesitant urination, indolence.
- Treats the Spleen and Stomach: abdominal pain and distention, borborygmus, diarrhoea, dysenteric disorder with blood, irregular defecation, difficult ingestion, no desire to eat.

#### Combinations

- Yellow eyes: Yánggāng BL-48 and [Dǎnshū BL-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-19) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Yellow eyes: Yánggāng BL-48, [Yìshè BL-49](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-49), [Nǎohù DU-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-17) and [Dǎnshū BL-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-19) (Supplementing Life).
- Difficult ingestion: Yánggāng BL-48, [Qímén LIV-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-14), [Shàoshāng LU-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-11) and [Láogōng PC-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-8) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Red and hesitant urinary flow: Yánggāng BL-48, [Guānyuán REN-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-4), [Zhìbiān BL-54](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-54) and [Qìhǎi REN-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-6) (Supplementing Life).

### **[BL48 阳纲](/point/BL48.md)** (YÁNGGĀNG)

* **英文名称：** Yang’s Key Link
* **中文名称：** 阳纲
* **国际代码：** BL-48
* **穴位属性：** **[BL48 阳纲](/point/BL48.md)** 是膀胱经第二侧线上的穴位，与**[BL19 胆俞](/point/BL19.md)** 平齐。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第十胸椎（T10）棘突下缘水平，距中线旁开3寸，与**[BL19 胆俞](/point/BL19.md)** 平齐。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 肩部放松时，旁开3寸线对应肩胛骨内侧缘。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺0.3至0.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直深刺或向内侧深斜刺有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 调理胆腑，清利湿热
* 调和中焦

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

身热，眼睛和面部发黄，胁肋部疼痛，小便色深不畅，倦怠。
腹痛，腹胀，肠鸣，腹泻，痢疾伴便血，排便不规律，吞咽困难，食欲不振。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL48 阳纲](/point/BL48.md)** 与胆的背俞穴**[BL19 胆俞](/point/BL19.md)** 平齐。像**[BL19 胆俞](/point/BL19.md)** 一样，它主治湿热阻滞胆腑，引起发热、黄疸、胁肋部疼痛以及小便色深不畅等症状。同时，它能够调和中焦，清利湿热，主治腹部胀痛、腹泻、痢疾、排便不规律和消化不良等。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 清利胆腑湿热：眼睛和面部发黄，胁肋部疼痛，小便色深不畅，倦怠。
* 调理脾胃：腹痛腹胀，肠鸣，腹泻，痢疾伴便血，排便不规律，吞咽困难，食欲不振。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 眼睛发黄：**[BL48 阳纲](/point/BL48.md)** 和 **[BL19 胆俞](/point/BL19.md)**（《百症赋》）。
* 眼睛发黄：**[BL48 阳纲](/point/BL48.md)**，**[BL49 意舍](/point/BL49.md)**，**[DU17 脑户](/point/DU17.md)** 和 **[BL19 胆俞](/point/BL19.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 吞咽困难：**[BL48 阳纲](/point/BL48.md)**，**[LIV14 期门](/point/LIV14.md)**，**[LU11 少商](/point/LU11.md)** 和 **[PC8 劳宫](/point/PC8.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 小便涩痛赤色：**[BL48 阳纲](/point/BL48.md)**，**[REN4 关元](/point/REN4.md)**，**[BL54 秩边](/point/BL54.md)** 和 **[REN6 气海](/point/REN6.md)**（《资生经》）。
