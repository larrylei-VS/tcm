### Welcome Fragrance YÍNGXIĀNG
![[LI-20 迎香.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Large Intestine and Stomach channels

#### Location

In the naso-labial groove, at the level of the midpoint of the lateral border of the ala nasi.

#### Needling

Transverse insertion medio-superiorly 0.3 to 0.5 cun or join to Bitong (M-HN-14) at the highest point of the naso-labial groove.  
**Note:** according to some classical and modern texts, this point is contraindicated to moxibustion.

#### Actions

- Opens the nasal passages
- Expels wind and clears heat

#### Indications

- Nasal congestion, nasal congestion and discharge, rhinitis, profuse nasal discharge, loss of sense of smell, nasal polyps, nasal sores, sneezing, nosebleed.
- Deviation of the mouth, swelling and itching of the face, pain and swelling of the lip, heat and redness of the eyes, dyspnoea, round worms in the bile duct.

#### Commentary

As its name 'Welcome Fragrance' implies, Yíngxiāng LI-20 is the foremost local point for treating all disorders of the nose. As such it is the primary local point for treating the Chinese disease categories of 'bi yuan', 'bi qiu', nasal polyps and sores. Bi yuan (literally 'nose pool' but translated in this text as nasal congestion and discharge) more or less corresponds to sinusitis and encompasses symptoms such as thin or thick nasal discharge and blocked and stuffy nose, commonly accompanied by pain and loss of sense of smell and taste. Bi qiu (translated in this text as rhinitis) also includes allergic rhinitis and encompasses such symptoms as sneezing, nasal discharge and itching.

Yangming channel governs the facial area, and Yíngxiāng LI-20, a meeting point of the Large Intestine and Stomach channels, is able to expel wind and heat from the face as a whole. It is particularly indicated for swelling and itching of the face, as well as for pain and swelling of the lip, heat and redness of the eyes and deviation of the mouth.

In recent years this point, needled to join with [Sìbái ST-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-2), has been much used to control the pain of biliary ascariasis. This disorder, which is endemic in China, is passed by eating contaminated raw vegetables fertilised by human 'night soil'. If the worms, which grow in the gall bladder, pass into the biliary duct, there is severe pain which is similar to that of cholelithiasis. The first step in treatment is to needle from Yíngxiāng LI-20 through to [Sìbái ST-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-2) with strong manipulation. This is usually effective to control the pain within one or two minutes. Additional points on the body are used to consolidate the effect, whilst herbal medicine is used subsequently to eliminate the worms.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Principal local point for all nose disorders: congestion and discharge, nosebleed, loss of sense of smell, nasal polyps, nasal sores, rhinitis, allergic rhinitis, sneezing.
- Local point for pain, swelling, itching and paralysis of the face.
- Joined to [Sìbái ST-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-2) for treating pain of biliary ascariasis (worms in the bile duct).

#### Combinations

- Nasal congestion with inability to distinguish the fragrant from the foul: Yíngxiāng LI-20, [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23), [Wǔchù BL-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-5) and [Kǒuhéliáo LI-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-19) (Great Compendium).
- Rhinitis with clear nasal discharge: Yíngxiāng LI-20, [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4), [Fēngmén BL-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-12), [Shéntíng DU-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-24), [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2), [Zhìyīn BL-67](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-67) and [Fùtōnggǔ KID-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-20) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Rhinitis with nose bleed: Yíngxiāng LI-20, [Èrjiān LI-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-2) and [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16) (Great Compendium).
- Itching and swelling of the face: Yíngxiāng LI-20 and [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) (Ode of Xi-hong).

### [LI20 迎香](/point/LI20.md) (YÍNGXIĀNG)

- **英文名称：** Welcome Fragrance
- **中文名称：** 迎香
- **国际代码：** LI-20
- **穴位属性：** [LI20 迎香](/point/LI20.md) 是大肠经与胃经的交会穴。

#### 定位 (Location)

位于鼻唇沟中，平鼻翼外缘中点处。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

向上内横刺0.3至0.5寸，或透刺至鼻唇沟最高点处的[MHN14 鼻通](/point/MHN14.md) (经外穴)。

- **注意 (Note):** 根据一些经典和现代文献，此穴禁灸。

#### 作用 (Actions)

- 通利鼻窍
- 祛风清热

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

鼻塞，鼻塞流涕，鼻炎，鼻流清涕，嗅觉丧失，鼻息肉，鼻疮，喷嚏，鼻出血。

口歪，面部肿胀瘙痒，口唇疼痛肿胀，眼睛灼热红肿，呼吸困难，胆道蛔虫症（胆管内蛔虫）。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

正如其名称“迎香”所示，[LI20 迎香](/point/LI20.md) 是治疗所有鼻部疾病的首要局部穴位。因此，它是治疗中医病症“鼻渊”、“鼻鼽”、鼻息肉和鼻疮的主要局部穴位。鼻渊（字面意思为“鼻部水池”，但在本文中译为鼻塞流涕）大致相当于鼻窦炎，包括鼻涕清稀或浓稠、鼻塞鼻闷等症状，常伴有疼痛和嗅觉味觉丧失。鼻鼽（本文中译为鼻炎）也包括过敏性鼻炎，涵盖喷嚏、流鼻涕和鼻痒等症状。

阳明经主面部，[LI20 迎香](/point/LI20.md) 作为大肠经与胃经的交会穴，能够驱散整个面部的风热。它尤其主治面部肿胀瘙痒，以及口唇疼痛肿胀、眼睛灼热红肿和口歪。

近年来，此穴透刺至[ST2 四白](/point/ST2.md)，被广泛用于控制胆道蛔虫症的疼痛。这种疾病在中国流行，通过食用受人类粪便污染的生菜传播。如果胆囊中的蛔虫进入胆管，会引起剧烈疼痛，类似于胆结石疼痛。治疗的第一步是[LI20 迎香](/point/LI20.md) 透刺至[ST2 四白](/point/ST2.md)，配合强刺激手法。通常在一两分钟内即可有效控制疼痛。全身其他穴位用于巩固疗效，随后使用中药驱虫。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

治疗所有鼻部疾病的主要局部穴位：鼻塞流涕，鼻出血，嗅觉丧失，鼻息肉，鼻疮，鼻炎，过敏性鼻炎，喷嚏。

治疗面部疼痛、肿胀、瘙痒和麻痹的局部穴位。

透刺[ST2 四白](/point/ST2.md) 治疗胆道蛔虫症疼痛。
