### Yin Market YĪNSHÌ
![[ST-33 阴市.png|250]]
#### Location

On the thigh, on a line drawn between the lateral border of the patella and the anterior superior iliac spine, in a depression 3 cun proximal to the superior border of the patella.

#### Location Notes

i. Locate Yīnshì ST-33 one handbreadth proximal to the upper border of the patella; ii. Locate Yīnshì ST-33 midway between [Fútù ST-32](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-32) and the upper border of the patella.

#### Needling

Perpendicular or oblique insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Dispels wind-damp

#### Indications

- Sensation like cold water in the lumbar region and legs, difficulty in stretching and bending the leg, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the leg, leg qi, weakness of the leg and knee, pain of the thigh and knee.
- Wasting and thirsting disorder, cold shan disorder, distention and pain of the abdomen, oedema with enlarged abdomen.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for a sensation like cold water in the lumbar region and legs, difficulty in stretching and bending the leg, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the leg, leg qi, weakness of the leg and knee, pain of the thigh and knee, wasting and thirsting disorder, cold shan disorder, distention and pain of the abdomen, oedema with enlarged abdomen.

#### Combinations

- Lack of strength in the legs: Yīnshì ST-33 and [Fēngshì GB-31](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-31) (Ode of the Jade Dragon).
- Pain of the legs and lumbar region: Yīnshì ST-33, [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30), [Fēngshì GB-31](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-31), [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40), [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60), [Chéngshān BL-57](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-57) and [Shēnmài BL-62](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-62) (Great Compendium).
- Redness, swelling and pain of the knees: Yīnshì ST-33, [Xīguān LIV-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-7), [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) and [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) (Great Compendium).
- Disorders of the knee and the region above the knee: moxa Yīnshì ST-33 and [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30) (Great Compendium).
- Cold abdominal shan disorder: Yīnshì ST-33, [Gānshū BL-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-18) and [Tàixī KID-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-3) (Great Compendium).

### [ST33 阴市](/point/ST33.md) (YĪNSHÌ)

* **英文名称：** Yin Market
* **中文名称：** 阴市
* **国际代码：** ST-33

#### 定位 (Location)

位于大腿部，髌骨外缘与髂前上棘连线上，髌骨上缘上方3寸的凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 定位[ST33 阴市](/point/ST33.md) 可在髌骨上缘上方一横掌处；
    * 定位[ST33 阴市](/point/ST33.md) 可在[ST32 伏兔](/point/ST32.md) 与髌骨上缘之间中点。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直或斜刺1至1.5寸。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 通经活络，止痛
* 祛风湿

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

腰腿部有冷水感，腿部屈伸不利，腿部痿证和痹证，腿气，腿膝无力，大腿和膝关节疼痛。
消渴病，寒疝，腹部胀痛，腹部肿大伴水肿。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST33 阴市](/point/ST33.md) 临床上较少使用。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治腰腿部有冷水感，腿部屈伸不利，腿部痿证和痹证，腿气，腿膝无力，大腿和膝关节疼痛，消渴病，寒疝，腹部胀痛，腹部肿大伴水肿。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 腿部无力：[ST33 阴市](/point/ST33.md) 和[GB31 风市](/point/GB31.md)（《玉龙歌》）。
* 腿部和腰部疼痛：[ST33 阴市](/point/ST33.md)，[GB30 环跳](/point/GB30.md)，[GB31 风市](/point/GB31.md)，[BL40 委中](/point/BL40.md)，[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)，[BL57 承山](/point/BL57.md) 和[BL62 申脉](/point/BL62.md)（《针灸大成》）。
* 膝关节红肿疼痛：[ST33 阴市](/point/ST33.md)，[LIV7 膝关](/point/LIV7.md)，[BL40 委中](/point/BL40.md) 和[ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md)（《针灸大成》）。
* 膝关节及膝上部疾病：灸[ST33 阴市](/point/ST33.md) 和[GB30 环跳](/point/GB30.md)（《针灸大成》）。
* 腹部寒疝：[ST33 阴市](/point/ST33.md)，[BL18 肝俞](/point/BL18.md) 和[KID3 太溪](/point/KID3.md)（《针灸大成》）。
