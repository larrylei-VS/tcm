### Lower Cavity XIÀWǍN
![[CV-10 下脘.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Conception vessel with the Spleen channel

#### Location

On the midline of the abdomen, 2 cun above the umbilicus and 6 cun below the sternocostal angle.

#### Location Notes

To locate the sternocostal angle see [Surface Anatomy](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-location-needling/surface-anatomy).

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.8 to 1.5 cun.  
**Caution:** in thin patients deep needling may penetrate the peritoneal cavity. According to the Illustrated Supplement to the Classic of Categories, moxibustion is contraindicated in pregnancy.

#### Actions

- Harmonises the Stomach and regulates qi
- Dispels food stagnation

#### Indications

- Abdominal fullness, abdominal hardness, epigastric pain, nausea and vomiting after eating, focal distention, undigested food (in the stool), no pleasure in eating, cold qi of the six fu.
- Thready pulse lacking in force, emaciation, dark urine.

#### Commentary

Xiàwǎn CV-10 (Lower Cavity) is also known as Xiaguan (Lower Controller). This point was traditionally considered to affect the lower portion of the Stomach, whilst [Shàngwǎn CV-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/CV-13) (Upper Cavity) was considered to affect its upper portion. Between these two points lies [Zhōngwǎn CV-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/CV-12) (Middle Cavity). The Spiritual Pivot [17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-conception-vessel-points/references) states "When there is difficult ingestion and obstruction in the diaphragm, this indicates the pathogen is in the Stomach. When the disorder is in the upper cavity, then needle [Shàngwǎn CV-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/CV-13) to restrain the rebellion and descend it. When the disorder is in the lower cavity, then needle Xiàwǎn CV-10 to scatter and move it". The Song More Precious Than Jade says "when the Stomach is cold, Xiàwǎn CV-10 is excellent" whilst the Systematic Classic of Acupuncture and Moxibustion states " Xiàwǎn CV-10 is the main point to use when food and drink are not digested and regurgitated after ingestion".

The principal use of this point is in cases of food stagnation with such symptoms as abdominal fullness, epigastric pain, nausea and vomiting after eating, lack of appetite, and undigested food in the stools.

#### Summary of clinical application

- For food stagnation: “When there is difficult ingestion and obstruction in the diaphragm, this indicates the pathogen is in the Stomach ... when the disorder is in the lower cavity, then needle Xiawan CV-10 to scatter and move it” (Spiritual Pivot). For abdominal fullness, epigastric pain, nausea and vomiting after eating, lack of appetite, undigested food in the stools.

#### Combinations

- Undigested food (in the stool), vomiting immediately after ingestion: first needle Xiàwǎn CV-10 and then reduce [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Undigested food (in the stool): Xiàwǎn CV-10, [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36), [Liángmén ST-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-21), [Sānyīnjiāo SP-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-6), [Dàchángshū BL-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-25), [Sānjiāoshu BL-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-22) and [Xuánshū DU-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-5) (Supplementing Life).
- Borborygmus: Xiàwǎn CV-10 and [Xiàngǔ ST-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-43) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Hardness of the abdomen: Xiàwǎn CV-10 and [Zhōngwǎn CV-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/CV-12) Ode of Spiritual Brightness).

### 1. [CV10 下脘](/point/CV10.md)

#### 1.1. Lower Cavity 下脘

- 任脉与脾经的交会穴  

#### 1.2. 定位

位于腹部中线，脐上2寸，胸肋角下6寸。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

定位胸肋角见表面解剖学。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直刺入，深0.8至1.5寸。  
**注意：** 瘦弱者深刺可能穿透腹膜腔。《类经图翼》称妊娠期禁灸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 和胃调气  
- 消食积  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 腹满、腹硬、胃脘痛、食后恶心呕吐、局灶性胀满、泻下未消化食物、食不甘味、六腑寒气。  
- 脉细无力、消瘦、尿色深。  

#### 1.7. 论述

[CV10 下脘](/point/CV10.md)别名“下管”，传统认为其影响胃的下部，而[CV13 上脘](/point/CV13.md)影响胃的上部，二者之间为[CV12 中脘](/point/CV12.md)。《灵枢》称：“吞咽难及膈阻，病在胃。若病在上脘，针[CV13 上脘](/point/CV13.md)抑逆降之；若病在下脘，针[CV10 下脘](/point/CV10.md)散之行之。”《玉机歌》谓：“胃寒，[CV10 下脘](/point/CV10.md)佳。”《针灸系统经典》称：“[CV10 下脘](/point/CV10.md)为主穴，用于饮食不化，吞后反吐。”  

此穴主要用于食积，症状如腹满、胃脘痛、食后恶心呕吐、食欲不振及泻下未消化食物。  

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 治疗食积：《灵枢》称：“吞咽难及膈阻，病在胃……若病在下脘，针[CV10 下脘](/point/CV10.md)散之行之。”用于腹满、胃脘痛、食后恶心呕吐、食欲不振、泻下未消化食物。  

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 泻下未消化食物、食后即吐：先针[CV10 下脘](/point/CV10.md)，再泻[ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md)（《千金方》）。  
- 泻下未消化食物：[CV10 下脘](/point/CV10.md) + [ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md) + [ST21 梁门](/point/ST21.md) + [SP6 三阴交](/point/SP6.md) + [BL25 大肠俞](/point/BL25.md) + [BL22 三焦俞](/point/BL22.md) + [DU5 悬枢](/point/DU5.md)（《补命》）。  
- 肠鸣：[CV10 下脘](/point/CV10.md) + [ST43 陷谷](/point/ST43.md)（《百症赋》）。  
- 腹硬：[CV10 下脘](/point/CV10.md) + [CV12 中脘](/point/CV12.md)（《灵明颂》）。
