### Shoulder Crevice Jiānliáo

![[SJ-14 肩髎.png|250]]
#### Location 

At the origin of the deltoid muscle, in the depression which lies posterior and inferior to the lateral extremity of the acromion.

#### Location note 

i. Locate with the arm abducted in order to enhance the depression; ii. Note that Jiānyú LI-15 is located in the depression which lies anterior and inferior to the lateral extremity of the acromion.

#### Needling 

i. With the arm abducted, perpendicular insertion directed towards the centre of the axilla, 1 to 1.5 cun; ii. Transverse-oblique insertion directed distally towards the elbow, 1.5 to 2 cun.

#### Actions 

- Dispels wind-damp
- Alleviates pain and benefits the shoulder joint

#### Indications 

- Shoulder pain, heaviness of the shoulder with inability to raise the arm, numbness, paralysis and pain of the arm.

#### Commentary 

Jiānliáo SJ-14 is an important point for treating all disorders of the shoulder joint, especially the posterior aspect. Stiffness, pain, immobility and weakness of the shoulder or frozen shoulder may derive from: i. injury by exterior pathogenic wind, cold, damp or heat, ii. stagnation of qi and blood from traumatic injury or overuse, or iii. deficiency of qi and blood from overuse, old age or prolonged obstruction of the channel. In all these cases Jiānliáo SJ-14 may be used, and in clinical practice is commonly combined with Jiānyú LI-15.

#### Summary of clinical application 

- Important point for treating all disorders of the shoulder joint, especially the posterior aspect, including stiffness, pain, weakness, immobility, and frozen shoulder.
- Commonly combined with Jiānyú LI-15 for shoulder and arm disorders.

#### Combinations 

- Pain of the arm: Jiānliáo SJ-14, Tiānzōng SI-11 and Yánggǔ SI-5 (Supplementing Life).
- Frozen shoulder: first needle Tiáokǒu ST-38 and ask the patient to rotate the shoulder whilst manipulating the needle. Then needle Jiānliáo SJ-14, Jiānyú LI-15, Bìnào LI-14, Hégǔ LI-4 and Wàiguān SJ-5.

### **[SJ14 肩髎](/point/SJ14.md)** (JIĀNLIÁO)

* **英文名称：** Shoulder Crevice
* **中文名称：** 肩髎
* **国际代码：** SJ-14

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于三角肌起点，肩峰外侧端后下方凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 臂外展时定位，以使凹陷更明显；
    * 请注意，**[LI15 肩髃](/point/LI15.md)** 位于肩峰外侧端前下方凹陷中。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

* i. 臂外展时，垂直刺，针尖朝向腋窝中央，1至1.5寸；
* ii. 横斜刺，针尖向远端（朝向肘部），1.5至2寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 祛风湿
* 止痛，宣利肩关节

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

肩部疼痛，肩部沉重不能抬臂，手臂麻木、麻痹、疼痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SJ14 肩髎](/point/SJ14.md)** 是治疗所有肩关节疾病的重要穴位，特别是肩部后侧。肩部僵硬、疼痛、活动不利或冻结肩可能源于：i. 外邪风、寒、湿、热侵袭；ii. 外伤或过度劳损导致气血瘀滞；或 iii. 过度劳损、年老或经络长期阻塞导致气血亏虚。在所有这些情况下，**[SJ14 肩髎](/point/SJ14.md)** 都可使用，临床上常与**[LI15 肩髃](/point/LI15.md)** 联合使用。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗所有肩关节疾病的重要穴位，特别是肩部后侧，包括僵硬、疼痛、无力、活动不利和冻结肩。
* 常与**[LI15 肩髃](/point/LI15.md)** 联合用于肩臂疾病。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 手臂疼痛：**[SJ14 肩髎](/point/SJ14.md)**，**[SI11 天宗](/point/SI11.md)** 和 **[SI5 阳谷](/point/SI5.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 冻结肩：先针**[ST38 条口](/point/ST38.md)**，并请患者在行针时转动肩部。然后针刺**[SJ14 肩髎](/point/SJ14.md)**，**[LI15 肩髃](/point/LI15.md)**，**[LI14 臂臑](/point/LI14.md)**，**[LI4 合谷](/point/LI4.md)** 和 **[SJ5 外关](/point/SJ5.md)**。
