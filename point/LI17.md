### Heaven's Tripod TIĀNDǏNG
![[LI-17 天鼎.png|250]]
#### Location

On the lateral side of the neck, 1 cun inferior to [Fútú LI-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-18), on the posterior border of the sternocleidomastoid muscle.

#### Needling

i. Perpendicular insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun; ii. Oblique insertion 0.5 to 0.8 cun.  
**Caution:** deeper needling may puncture the carotid artery or jugular vein.

#### Actions

- Benefits the throat and voice

#### Indications

- Sudden loss of voice, throat painful obstruction, rattling sound in the throat, goitre, scrofula, difficulty in breathing, difficult ingestion.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for sudden loss of voice, throat painful obstruction, rattling sound in the throat, goitre, scrofula, difficulty in breathing, difficult ingestion.

#### Combinations

- Halting speech and loss of voice: Tiāndǐng LI-17 and [Jiānshǐ PC-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-5) (One Hundred Symptoms).

### [LI17 天鼎](/point/LI17.md) (TIĀNDǏNG)

- **英文名称：** Heaven's Tripod
- **中文名称：** 天鼎
- **国际代码：** LI-17

#### 定位 (Location)

位于颈部外侧，在[LI18 扶突](/point/LI18.md) 下方1寸，胸锁乳突肌后缘。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

i. 垂直刺0.3至0.5寸； ii. 斜刺0.5至0.8寸。

- **注意 (Caution):** 深刺可能刺破颈总动脉或颈内静脉。

#### 作用 (Actions)

- 宣利咽喉，改善声音

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

暴喑（突然失音），咽喉肿痛，喉中痰鸣，瘿瘤，瘰疬，呼吸困难，吞咽困难。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[LI17 天鼎](/point/LI17.md) 临床上较少使用。主治暴喑，咽喉肿痛，喉中痰鸣，瘿瘤，瘰疬，呼吸困难，吞咽困难。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

临床上较少使用。

主治暴喑，咽喉肿痛，喉中痰鸣，瘿瘤，瘰疬，呼吸困难，吞咽困难。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

言语不利，失音：[LI17 天鼎](/point/LI17.md) 和[PC5 间使](/point/PC5.md)（《百症赋》）。
