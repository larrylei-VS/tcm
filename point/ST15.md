### Room Screen WŪYÌ
![[ST-15 屋翳.png|250]]
#### Location

On the chest, in the second intercostal space, 4 cun lateral to the midline ([Zǐgōng REN-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-19)), on the mamillary line.

#### Location Notes

i. First locate the costal cartilage of the second rib which is level with the sternal angle, then locate the second intercostal space below it; ii. Note that in males the nipple lies in the fourth intercostal space; iii Note that the intercostal space curves upwards laterally, so that Wūyì ST-15 will lie superior to the level of [Zǐgōng REN-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-19).

#### Needling

Transverse-oblique insertion laterally or medially along the intercostal space 0.5 to 0.8 cun, or transverse insertion superiorly or inferiorly along the channel.  
**Caution:** deep or perpendicular insertion carries a substantial risk of puncturing the lung.

#### Actions

- Descends rebellious qi and unbinds the chest
- Benefits the breasts
- Alleviates pain and itching of the skin

#### Indications

- Cough, wheezing, dyspnoea, shortness of breath, coughing of pus and blood, distention and pain of the chest and lateral costal region.
- Breast pain, breast abscess.
- Pain of the skin making wearing of clothes unbearable, generalised itching, heaviness of the body, swollen body, pain and weakness of the limbs.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Both ST-14 and ST-15 are mostly indicated for local disorders of the chest, with Wuyi ST-15 also indicated for breast pain and abscess, pain of the skin making wearing of clothes unbearable, generalised itching, heaviness of the body, swollen body, pain and weakness of the limbs.

#### Combinations

- Itching with much pain: Wūyì ST-15 and [Zhìyīn BL-67](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-67) (One Hundred Symptoms).

### [ST15 屋翳](/point/ST15.md) (WŪYÌ)

* **英文名称：** Room Screen
* **中文名称：** 屋翳
* **国际代码：** ST-15

#### 定位 (Location)

位于胸部，第二肋间隙中，距前正中线（[REN19 紫宫](/point/REN19.md)）旁开4寸，在乳头线上。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 首先找到与胸骨角平齐的第二肋软骨，然后在其下方的第二肋间隙定位；
    * 请注意，男性乳头通常位于第四肋间隙；
    * 请注意，肋间隙向外侧向上弯曲，因此[ST15 屋翳](/point/ST15.md) 将位于[REN19 紫宫](/point/REN19.md) 的上方。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

横斜刺，沿肋间隙向外侧或内侧刺0.5至0.8寸，或沿经络向上或向下横刺。

* **注意 (Caution):** 深刺或垂直刺入有很大风险刺破肺脏。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 降逆气，宽胸散结
* 宣利乳房
* 止痛止痒（皮肤）

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

咳嗽，喘息，呼吸困难，气短，咳脓血，胸部和胁肋部胀满疼痛。
乳房疼痛，乳痈。
皮肤疼痛以致不能穿衣，全身瘙痒，身体沉重，身体浮肿，肢体疼痛和无力。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST15 屋翳](/point/ST15.md) 临床上较少使用。[ST14 库房](/point/ST14.md) 和[ST15 屋翳](/point/ST15.md) 主要主治胸部局部疾病。[ST15 屋翳](/point/ST15.md) 还主治乳房疼痛和乳痈、皮肤疼痛以致不能穿衣、全身瘙痒、身体沉重、身体浮肿、肢体疼痛和无力。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治胸部和胁肋部胀满疼痛，咳嗽，喘息，呼吸困难，气短，咳脓血。
* 也主治乳房疼痛和乳痈。
* 治疗皮肤疼痛、全身瘙痒、身体沉重、身体浮肿、肢体疼痛和无力。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 瘙痒伴剧烈疼痛：[ST15 屋翳](/point/ST15.md) 和[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)（《百症赋》）。
