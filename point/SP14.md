### Abdomen Knot FÙJIÉ
![[SP-14 腹结.png|250]]
#### Location

On the lower abdomen, 1.3 cun inferior to [Dàhéng SP-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-21), 4 cun lateral to the midline.

#### Location Notes

The 4 cun line is determined by the palpable lateral border of the rectus abdominis muscle at the level of the umbilicus.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion, 1 to 1.5 cun.  
**Caution:** in thin patients deep needling may penetrate the peritoneal cavity.

#### Actions

- Warms and benefits the lower jiao
- Regulates qi and descends rebellion

#### Indications

- Periumbilical pain, diarrhoea and dysenteric disorder, diarrhoea due to cold in the abdomen, abdominal distention and constipation, painful shan disorder.
- Qi rushes up to the Heart, Heart pain, cough.

#### Commentary

The name of Fùjié SP-14 (Abdomen Knot) refers to its ability to resolve stagnation of qi in the abdominal region, especially in the area of the umbilicus. It is also able to regulate rebellious qi which either ascends to attack the Heart or which impairs the descending function of the Lung, leading to cough. Interestingly, Heart pain is an indication of disorder in the Yin Linking vessel, and whilst [Chōngmén SP-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-12), [Fǔshè SP-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-13), [Dàhéng SP-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-15) and [Fùāi SP-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-16) are coalescent points of this extraordinary vessel, Fùjié SP-14 is not.

Fùjié SP-14 also has the function of warming the lower jiao and scattering cold, particularly in the intestinal region, and is indicated for cold diarrhoea and dysenteric disorder.

#### Summary of clinical application

- For stagnation of qi in (and uprising of qi from) the abdominal region: periumbilical pain, abdominal distention and constipation, painful shan disorder, qi rushing up to the Heart, Heart pain, cough.

#### Combinations

- Rushing Heart: Fùjié SP-14 and [Xíngjiān LIV-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-2) (Supplementing Life).

### **[SP14 腹结](/point/SP14.md)** (FÙJIÉ)

* **英文名称：** Abdomen Knot
* **中文名称：** 腹结
* **国际代码：** SP-14

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于下腹部，**[SP15 大横](/point/SP15.md)** 下方1.3寸，距前正中线旁开4寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 旁开4寸线由脐水平的腹直肌可触及的外缘确定。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺，1至1.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 瘦弱患者深刺可能刺穿腹膜腔。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 温补下焦
* 理气降逆

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

脐周疼痛，腹泻和痢疾，腹部寒泻，腹胀便秘，疝气疼痛。
气冲上心，心痛，咳嗽。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SP14 腹结](/point/SP14.md)** (Abdomen Knot) 的名称指的是它能够化解腹部区域，特别是脐周的气滞。它还能调理逆气，这些逆气或上冲心部引起心痛，或影响肺的肃降功能导致咳嗽。有趣的是，心痛是阴维脉疾病的指征，而 **[SP12 冲门](/point/SP12.md)**、**[SP13 府舍](/point/SP13.md)**、**[SP15 大横](/point/SP15.md)** 和 **[SP16 腹哀](/point/SP16.md)** 都是这条奇经八脉的交会穴，但 **[SP14 腹结](/point/SP14.md)** 却不是。

**[SP14 腹结](/point/SP14.md)** 还具有温煦下焦、散寒的功能，特别是在肠道区域，主治寒泻和痢疾。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗腹部（及脐周）气滞和气机上逆：脐周疼痛，腹胀便秘，疝气疼痛，气冲上心，心痛，咳嗽。
* 温补下焦，散寒，治疗腹泻和痢疾，特别是寒性腹泻。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 奔豚气（Rushing Heart）：**[SP14 腹结](/point/SP14.md)** 和 **[LIV2 行间](/point/LIV2.md)**（《资生经》）。
