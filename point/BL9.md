### Jade Pillow YÙZHĚN
![[BL-9 玉枕.png|250]]
#### Location

1.3 cun lateral to [Nǎohù DU-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-17) (which is located in the depression superior to the external occipital protuberance, 1.5 cun superior to [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16)).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.

#### Actions

- Expels wind and cold and alleviates pain
- Benefits the nose and eyes

#### Indications

- Occipital headache, pain from head wind that is difficult to endure, dizziness, pain of the neck with inability to turn the head, pain of the head and neck with aversion to wind and absence of sweating, heaviness of the head and neck, cold sensation in half of the head, cold head with copious sweating, red face, pain of the cheek.
- Eye pain, bursting eye pain, short sightedness, nasal congestion, loss of sense of smell.
- Chills and fever, bone pain with chills and fever, vomiting, madness, mad walking, epilepsy, collapse on sudden standing.

#### Commentary

The name Yuzhen (Jade Pillow) refers both to the point Yùzhěn BL-9 and to the general region around the occipital protuberance. In this latter context, Yuzhen is one of what is known as the three gates (sanguan) through which it may be difficult to circulate the qi in the qigong 'small heavenly circuit' practice; that is the practice of circulating the qi through the Governing and Conception vessels in one continuous circuit using the mind and the breath. The three gates are Yuzhenguan, Jiajiguan (in the region of [Mìngmén DU-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-4)) and Weiluguan (in the region of [Chángqiáng DU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-1)).

Yùzhěn BL-9 itself is indicated for the treatment of i. occipital headache and severe head wind, stiffness and pain of the neck and coldness and sensitivity to wind in the head and neck region, ii. disorders of the nose and eyes (eye pain, short sightedness, nasal congestion and loss of sense of smell), and iii. madness, mad walking, epilepsy and sudden collapse on standing.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Indicated for the treatment of i. occipital headache and severe head wind, stiffness and pain of the neck and coldness and sensitivity to wind in the head and neck region, ii. disorders of the nose and eyes (eye pain, short sightedness, nasal congestion and loss of sense of smell), and iii. madness, mad walking, epilepsy and sudden collapse on standing.
- The name Yuzhen (Jade Pillow) refers both to the point Yùzhěn BL-9 and to the general region around the occipital protuberance. In this latter context, Yuzhen is one of what is known as the three gates (sanguan) through which it may be difficult to circulate the qi in the qigong ‘small heavenly circuit’ practice; that is the practice of circulating the qi through the Governing and Conception vessels in one continuous circuit using the mind and the breath. The three gates are Yuzhenguan, Jiajiguan (in the region of [Mìngmén DU-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-4)) and Weiluguan (in the region of [Chángqiáng DU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-1)).

#### Combinations

- Pain of the nape of the neck: Yùzhěn BL-9 and [Wángǔ GB-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-12) (Supplementing Life).
- Wind dizziness: Yùzhěn BL-9, [Hòudǐng DU-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-19) and [Hànyàn GB-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-4) (Supplementing Life).
- Head wind: Yùzhěn BL-9 and [Xìnhuì DU-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-22) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Nasal congestion: Yùzhěn BL-9, [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20), [Tóulínqì GB-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-15), [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23) and Danyang (Extra)[3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references) (Supplementing Life).

### **[BL9 玉枕](point/BL9.md)** (YÙZHĚN)

* **英文名称：** Jade Pillow
* **中文名称：** 玉枕
* **国际代码：** BL-9

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于**[DU17 脑户](point/DU17.md)**（枕外隆突上方凹陷中，距**[DU16 风府](point/DU16.md)** 上方1.5寸）旁开1.3寸。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

横刺0.5至1寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 祛风散寒，止痛
* 宣利鼻目

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

枕部头痛，头风剧痛难忍，眩晕，颈项疼痛不能转头，头颈疼痛怕风无汗，头颈沉重，半边头冷，头冷多汗，面赤，面颊疼痛。
眼痛，眼痛如裂，近视，鼻塞，嗅觉丧失。
恶寒发热，骨痛伴恶寒发热，呕吐，发狂，循衣摸床（或行走无度），癫痫，突然站立时昏厥。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

“玉枕”之名既指穴位**[BL9 玉枕](point/BL9.md)**，也指枕外隆突周围的整个区域。在后一种语境中，玉枕是气功“小周天”练习中气机难以通行的“三关”之一；即通过意念和呼吸使气血在督脉和任脉中连续循环的练习。三关分别为玉枕关、夹脊关（**[DU4 命门](point/DU4.md)** 区域）和尾闾关（**[DU1 长强](point/DU1.md)** 区域）。

**[BL9 玉枕](point/BL9.md)** 本身主治：i. 枕部头痛和剧烈头风，颈项僵硬疼痛以及头颈部受寒怕风；ii. 鼻部和眼部疾病（眼痛，近视，鼻塞和嗅觉丧失）；iii. 发狂，循衣摸床（或行走无度），癫痫和突然站立时昏厥。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 主治：i. 枕部头痛和剧烈头风，颈项僵硬疼痛以及头颈部受寒怕风；ii. 鼻部和眼部疾病（眼痛，近视，鼻塞和嗅觉丧失）；iii. 发狂，循衣摸床（或行走无度），癫痫和突然站立时昏厥。
* “玉枕”之名既指穴位**[BL9 玉枕](point/BL9.md)**，也指枕外隆突周围的整个区域。在后一种语境中，玉枕是气功“小周天”练习中气机难以通行的“三关”之一；即通过意念和呼吸使气血在督脉和任脉中连续循环的练习。三关分别为玉枕关、夹脊关（**[DU4 命门](point/DU4.md)** 区域）和尾闾关（**[DU1 长强](point/DU1.md)** 区域）。
