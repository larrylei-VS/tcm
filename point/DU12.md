### SHĒNZHÙ
![[DU-12 身柱.png|250]]
#### Location

On the midline of the upper back, in the depression below the spinous process of the third thoracic vertebra.

#### Location Notes

This point is located three intervertebral spaces below C7. To locate C7 see [Surface Anatomy](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-location-needling/surface-anatomy "Surface Anatomy").

#### Needling

Perpendicular-oblique superior insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** the spinal canal lies between 1.25 and 1.75 cun deep to the skin surface, varying according to body build.

#### Actions

- Clears heat from the Lung and Heart
- Calms the spirit
- Pacifies wind

#### Indications

- Heat in the chest, dyspnoea, sudden cough with lumbar pain, heat in the body, dry mouth, agitation with thirst, headache and sweating.
- Mad walking, delirious raving, seeing ghosts, rage with desire to kill people.
- Childhood fright epilepsy, clonic spasm, opisthotonos, clove sores.

#### Commentary

As early as the Essential Questions[33](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-governing-vessel/references) Shēnzhù DU-12 was said to clear heat from the chest, and this is reinforced by many indications found in subsequent classical texts. Shēnzhù DU-12, located below the third thoracic vertebra at the level of [Fèishū BL-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-13) the back-shu point of the Lung, is able to clear heat from the Lung, giving rise to cough, dyspnoea and fever. According to the Warp and Woof of Warm Febrile Diseases "The Lung and Heart are mutually connected; when there is Lung heat it most easily enters the Heart". This passage helps explain why Shēnzhù DU-12 is also indicated for heat in the Heart which gives rise to such excess pattern manifestations of spirit disturbance as mad walking, ranting and raving, 'seeing ghosts' and rage with desire to kill. This ability to clear heat from the Lung and calm the spirit in excess patterns can be contrasted to the action of the previous point, [Shéndào DU-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-11), which is primarily used for deficiency patterns of the Heart and Lung.

Like many other points of this channel, Shēnzhù DU-12 is also able to pacify wind in the Governing vessel and is indicated for epilepsy and clonic spasm.

#### Summary of clinical application

- For heat in the Lung: cough, dyspnoea, fever.
- For heat which disturbs the Heart and spirit: mad walking, ranting and raving, seeing ghosts, rage with desire to kill.

#### Combinations

- Madness: Shēnzhù DU-12 and [Běnshén GB-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-13) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Childhood fright epilepsy: Shēnzhù DU-12 and [Chángqiáng DU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-1) (Supplementing Life).
- Opisthotonos, clonic spasm, epilepsy and headache: Shēnzhù DU-12, [Wǔchù BL-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-5), [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40), [Wěiyáng BL-39](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-39) and [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### 1. [DU12 身柱](/point/DU12.md)

#### 1.1. Body Pillar 身柱

#### 1.2. 定位

位于上背部中线，第三胸椎棘突下方的凹陷中。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

此穴位于第七颈椎（C7）下方三个椎间隙。定位C7见表面解剖学。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

向上垂直-斜刺，深0.5至1寸。  
**注意：** 脊管位于皮肤表面下1.25至1.75寸，深度因体型而异。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 清肺心之热  
- 定神  
- 平风  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 胸热、呼吸困难、突发咳嗽伴腰痛、身热、口干、烦渴、头痛、出汗。  
- 狂_walk、谵语妄言、见鬼、怒欲杀人。  
- 小儿惊痫、痉挛、角弓反张、丁疮。  

#### 1.7. 论述

《素问》早称[DU12 身柱](/point/DU12.md)清胸热，后世经典多指征强化此说。[DU12 身柱](/point/DU12.md)位于第三胸椎下，与肺俞[BL13 肺俞](/point/BL13.md)同高，能清肺热，治咳嗽、呼吸困难及发热。《温病经纬》谓：“肺心相连，肺热易入心。”此解为何[DU12 身柱](/point/DU12.md)亦用于心热，致实证神扰如狂走、谵语、“见鬼”及怒欲杀人。此清肺热及平神实证作用，与前穴[DU11 神道](/point/DU11.md)主治心肺虚证形成对比。  

如督脉多穴，[DU12 身柱](/point/DU12.md)能平督脉风，用于癫痫及痉挛。  

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 肺热：咳嗽、呼吸困难、发热。  
- 心热扰神：狂走、谵语、见鬼、怒欲杀人。  

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 癫狂：[DU12 身柱](/point/DU12.md) + [GB13 本神](/point/GB13.md)（《百症赋》）。  
- 小儿惊痫：[DU12 身柱](/point/DU12.md) + [DU1 长强](/point/DU1.md)（《补命》）。  
- 角弓反张、痉挛、癫痫及头痛：[DU12 身柱](/point/DU12.md) + [BL5 五处](/point/BL5.md) + [BL40 委中](/point/BL40.md) + [BL39 委阳](/point/BL39.md) + [BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)（《千金方》）。
