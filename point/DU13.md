### TÁODÀO
![[DU-13 陶道.png]]
- Meeting point of the Governing vessel with the Bladder channel

#### Location

On the midline of the upper back, in the depression below the spinous process of the first thoracic vertebra.

#### Location Notes

This point is located one intervertebral space below C7. To locate C7 see [Surface Anatomy](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-location-needling/surface-anatomy "Surface Anatomy").

#### Needling

Perpendicular-oblique superior insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** the spinal canal lies between 1.25 and 1.75 cun deep to the skin surface, varying according to body build.

#### Actions

- Clears heat and treats malaria
- Regulates the Governing vessel

#### Indications

- Malaria, chronic malaria, chills and fever, absence of sweating, steaming bone disorder.
- Stiffness of the spine, heaviness of the head, visual dizziness, clonic spasm, agitation and fullness, unhappiness and disorientation.

#### Commentary

The Governing vessel is the 'sea of the yang channels' and many of its points are effective to reduce yang heat in the body, including febrile diseases and most especially malaria. In both traditional and modern clinical practice Táodào DU-13 has been an important point in the treatment of malaria, whether acute or chronic. This is primarily due to its ability to clear heat from the Governing vessel, and this action is reflected also in the indications for chills and fever and steaming bone disorder.

Like many points of the Governing vessel, Táodào DU-13 is also able to regulate the channel and to clear wind and is indicated for stiffness of the spine, heaviness of the head and clonic spasm.

Finally, as its name 'Way of Happiness' suggests, Táodào DU-13 is indicated for unhappiness and disorientation.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Important point in the treatment of malaria as well as other diseases involving chills and fever; also absence of sweating, steaming bone disorder.

#### Combinations

- Seasonal fever: Táodào DU-13 and [Fèishū BL-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-13) (Glorious Anthology).
- Headache: Táodào DU-13, [Hòuxī SI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-3), [Tiānzhù BL-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-10), [Dàzhù BL-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-11) and [Kǒngzuì LU-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-6) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Visual dizziness, dimness of vision with bursting eye pain: Táodào DU-13, [Tiānzhù BL-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-10) and [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60) (Supplementing Life)

### 1. [DU13 陶道](/point/DU13.md)

#### 1.1. Way of Happiness 陶道

#### 1.2. 定位

位于上背部中线，第一胸椎棘突下方的凹陷中。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

此穴位于第七颈椎（C7）下方一个椎间隙。定位C7见表面解剖学。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

向上垂直-斜刺，深0.5至1寸。  
**注意：** 脊管位于皮肤表面下1.25至1.75寸，深度因体型而异。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 清热治疟  
- 调督脉  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 疟疾、慢性疟疾、寒热、无汗、骨蒸。  
- 脊柱僵硬、头重、视物眩晕、痉挛、烦满、不乐神志迷乱。  

#### 1.7. 论述

督脉为“阳脉之海”，其多穴能减体内阳热，包括热病，尤疟疾。传统及现代临床中，[DU13 陶道](/point/DU13.md)为治疟疾（急慢性）的重要穴，主因其清督脉热之能，亦见于寒热及骨蒸指征。  

如督脉多穴，[DU13 陶道](/point/DU13.md)能调脉清风，用于脊柱僵硬、头重及痉挛。  

其名“陶道”（幸福之道）暗示，用于不乐及神志迷乱。  

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 治疗疟疾及其他寒热病；亦治无汗、骨蒸。  

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 时疫发热：[DU13 陶道](/point/DU13.md) + [BL13 肺俞](/point/BL13.md)（《荣集》）。  
- 头痛：[DU13 陶道](/point/DU13.md) + [SI3 后溪](/point/SI3.md) + [BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md) + [BL11 大杼](/point/BL11.md) + [LU6 孔最](/point/LU6.md)（《千金方》）。  
- 视物眩晕、视蒙伴眼胀痛：[DU13 陶道](/point/DU13.md) + [BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md) + [BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)（《补命》）。
