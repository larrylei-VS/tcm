### Grasping the Wind BǏNGFĒNG
![[SI-12 秉风.png|250]]
- _Meeting point of the Small Intestine, Large Intestine, Sanjiao and Gall Bladder channels_

#### Location

In the centre of the suprascapular fossa, directly above [Tiānzōng SI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-11), in a depression formed when the arm is raised.

#### Needling

Oblique medial insertion towards the spine 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution**: deep perpendicular insertion, especially in thin patients, carries a substantial risk of inducing a pneumo-thorax.

#### Actions

- Expels wind and benefits the shoulder and scapula

#### Indications

- Pain of the shoulder and scapula with inability to raise the arm, stiff neck with inability to turn the head, pain and numbness of the upper arm, cough with stubborn phlegm.

#### Commentary

Bǐngfēng SI-12 is a meeting point of the Small Intestine channel with the Large Intestine, Sanjiao and Gall Bladder channels, all of which traverse the scapular and shoulder regions and the neck. As its name 'Grasping Wind' (and by implication controlling wind) implies, Bǐngfēng SI-12 is a useful local point to invigorate these channels and expel pathogenic wind which has lodged in the muscles of the shoulder and scapula. Although most classical and contemporary sources recommend this point for local disorders only, the Ming dynasty classic Investigation Into Points Along the Channels suggests a broader action of expelling wind and recommends the use of Bǐngfēng SI-12 "when the interstices and pores do not close properly, wind pathogen easily enters [resulting in] cough with stubborn phlegm".

#### Summary of clinical application

- Local point for disorders of the shoulder, scapula and neck, especially when due to wind invasion.

#### Combinations

- Pain of the shoulder with inability to raise the arm: Bǐngfēng SI-12 and [Yúnmén LU-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-2) (Supplementing Life).
- Pain of the shoulder with inability to raise the arm: Bǐngfēng SI-12 and [Tiānróng SI-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-17) (Systematic Classic).

### **[SI12 秉风](/point/SI12.md)** (BǏNGFĒNG)

* **英文名称：** Grasping the Wind
* **中文名称：** 秉风
* **国际代码：** SI-12
* **穴位属性：** **[SI12 秉风](/point/SI12.md)** 是小肠经与大肠经、三焦经、胆经的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于肩胛骨冈上窝中央，**[SI11 天宗](/point/SI11.md)** 正上方，手臂抬起时形成的凹陷中。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺，向内侧（朝向脊柱）0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直深刺，特别是在瘦弱患者中，有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 祛风，宣利肩胛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

肩胛疼痛，不能抬臂，颈项僵硬不能转头，上臂疼痛麻木，咳嗽顽痰。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SI12 秉风](/point/SI12.md)** 是小肠经与大肠经、三焦经和胆经的交会穴，这些经脉都循行于肩胛、肩部和颈部区域。正如其名称“秉风”（意指执掌风邪，引申为控制风邪）所示，**[SI12 秉风](/point/SI12.md)** 是一个有用的局部穴位，能够活络这些经脉，驱除滞留于肩部和肩胛肌肉的风邪。
尽管大多数经典和现代文献推荐此穴仅用于局部病症，但明代经典《循经考穴编》认为其有更广泛的祛风作用，并推荐在“ Skin不密，风邪易入，故咳嗽顽痰”时使用**[SI12 秉风](/point/SI12.md)**。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗肩部、肩胛和颈部疾病的局部穴位，特别是风邪侵袭所致者。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 肩痛不能抬臂：**[SI12 秉风](/point/SI12.md)** 和 **[LU2 云门](/point/LU2.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 肩痛不能抬臂：**[SI12 秉风](/point/SI12.md)** 和 **[SI17 天容](/point/SI17.md)**（《针灸甲乙经》）。
