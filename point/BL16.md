### Governor Shu DŪSHŪ
![[BL-16 督俞.png|250]]
#### Location

1.5 cun lateral to the lower border of the spinous process of the sixth thoracic vertebra (T6).

#### Location Notes

Locate at the visible highest point of the paraspinal muscles.

#### Needling

Oblique insertion towards the spine, 0.5 to 1 cun, or transverse-oblique insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.  
**Caution:** perpendicular needling or oblique needling away from the spine carries a substantial risk of causing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Regulates qi in the chest and abdomen

#### Indications

- Heart pain, epigastric pain, abdominal distention, borborygmus, chills and fever, breast abscess, itching, psoriasis, alopecia.

#### Commentary

Although conventionally categorised as the back-shu point of the Governing vessel, it is interesting to note that Dūshū BL-16 was not discussed at all in such texts as the Systematic Classic of Acupuncture and Moxibustion, the Illustrated Classic of Acupuncture Points on the Bronze Man or Elucidation of the Fourteen Channels. This downplaying of Dūshū BL-16 is reflected in other classics by the absence of any clear indications of disorders of the Governing vessel, and the general paucity of indications and classical combination for this point as a whole. Its modern use is in the treatment of various skin disorders including psoriasis, alopecia and generalised pruritis.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Despite its status as the back-shu point of the Governing vessel, Dushu BL-16 has few indications of disorders of the Governing vessel.
- Modern application in the treatment of various skin disorders including psoriasis, alopecia and generalised pruritis.

#### Combinations

- Psoriasis: Dūshū BL-16, [Géshū BL-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-17), [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11) and [Xuèhǎi SP-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-10).

### **[BL17 膈俞](point/BL17.md)** (GÉSHŪ)

* **英文名称：** Diaphragm Shu
* **中文名称：** 膈俞
* **国际代码：** BL-17
* **穴位属性：** **[BL17 膈俞](point/BL17.md)** 是膈的背俞穴，八会穴之血会。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第七胸椎（T7）棘突下缘旁开1.5寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 位于脊柱旁肌肉的最高点。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺，向脊柱方向0.5至1寸，或横斜刺1至1.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直针刺或向远离脊柱方向斜刺有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 活血化瘀
* 调理气血
* 宽胸利膈
* 和胃降逆

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

胸中血瘀，胸痛（伴刺痛），胸中烦躁憋闷，膈肌胀痛，噎膈（吞咽困难）。
呕吐，呃逆，纳呆，食后呕吐，腹胀，黄疸。
痨瘵（肺痨），身体消瘦，疲乏，潮热，盗汗，咳血。
荨麻疹，皮肤瘙痒，瘰疬。
脊柱强直，背痛，腰痛，肩胛疼痛，骨痹。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL17 膈俞](point/BL17.md)** (Diaphragm Shu) 是膈的背俞穴，也是血之会穴。其名称“膈俞”反映了它与膈的关系，而“血会”则强调其在血液方面的强大作用。

其主要作用是**活血化瘀**。当血液循环受阻，产生瘀血时，可能导致各种疼痛和肿块。**[BL17 膈俞](point/BL17.md)** 对因瘀血引起的胸痛、胁肋痛以及各种积聚类病症尤其有效。此外，它还能**调理气血**，这对于治疗因气血不足或气滞血瘀导致的疲乏、消瘦等虚劳症状也十分重要。

此穴还具有**宽胸利膈**、**和胃降逆**的功效，因此常用于治疗胸膈胀痛、呃逆、呕吐、吞咽困难等消化道症状。在**黄疸**的治疗中，**[BL17 膈俞](point/BL17.md)** 也扮演着重要角色，这与它活血化瘀、疏通气机、促进胆汁排泄的作用相关。

在皮肤病方面，**[BL17 膈俞](point/BL17.md)** 因其活血凉血、祛风止痒的功效，常用于治疗**荨麻疹**、**皮肤瘙痒**等与血热或血瘀风邪相关的疾病。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* **血之会穴**，活血化瘀，治疗各种血瘀证：胸痛（刺痛），胸中血瘀，胸中胀满，胁肋痛，以及各种血瘀引起的积聚。
* **膈之背俞穴**，宽胸利膈，和胃降逆：呃逆，呕吐，吞咽困难，胸膈胀满。
* **调理气血**，**补益虚损**：痨瘵（肺痨），疲乏，消瘦，盗汗，咳血。
* **清热利湿退黄**：黄疸。
* **皮肤病**：荨麻疹，皮肤瘙痒。
* **局部疼痛**：脊柱强直，背痛，腰痛，肩胛疼痛，骨痹。
