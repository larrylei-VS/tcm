### Heavenly Gathering TIĀNZŌNG
![[SI-11 天宗.png|250]]
#### Location

On the scapula, in a tender depression one third of the distance from the midpoint of the inferior border of the scapular spine to the inferior angle of the scapula.

#### Location Notes

In practice it may be difficult to palpate the inferior angle of the scapula. An alternative method of location is to draw an equilateral triangle with [Jiānzhēn SI-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-9) and [Nāoshū SI-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-10), after first ensuring that the patient's shoulder is relaxed.

#### Needling

Perpendicular or oblique insertion 0.5 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Moves qi and unbinds the chest and lateral costal region
- Benefits the breasts

#### Indications

- Heaviness and pain of the shoulder, pain of the scapula, pain and inability to raise the elbow and arm, pain of the elbow, swelling of the cheek and submandibular region.
- Fullness of the chest and lateral costal region, cough, pain and swelling of the breast, breast abscess, insufficient lactation.

#### Commentary

The indications of Tiānzōng SI-11 reflect both its location and its ability to move qi and unbind the chest. It is an important point for pain of the scapular region whether due to sprain, painful obstruction, overuse, poor posture, Liver qi stagnation or referred pain from Gall Bladder disease, in which cases it is usually found to be acutely tender on palpation. Lying behind the breast, it is also used in combination with points such as [Shàozé SI-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-1) and [Shānzhōng REN-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-17) for a wide variety of disorders of the breast including pain, lumps and insufficient lactation.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Commonly used local point for any pain of the scapular region (whether due to sprain, painful obstruction, overuse, poor posture, Liver qi stagnation or referred pain from Gall Bladder disease).
- For breast disorders including pain, lumps and insufficient lactation.

#### Combinations

- Pain of the shoulder and arm: Tiānzōng SI-11 and [Wàngǔ SI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-4) (Supplementing Life).
- Pain of the arm: Tiānzōng SI-11, [Jiānliáo SJ-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-14) and [Yánggǔ SI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-5) (Supplementing Life).

### **[SI11 天宗](point/SI11.md)** (TIĀNZŌNG)

* **英文名称：** Heavenly Gathering
* **中文名称：** 天宗
* **国际代码：** SI-11

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于肩胛骨上，肩胛冈下缘中点与肩胛下角之间连线，下三分之一处的压痛凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 在实践中，肩胛下角可能难以触及。另一种定位方法是先确保患者肩部放松，然后以 **[SI9 肩贞](point/SI9.md)** 和 **[SI10 臑俞](point/SI10.md)** 为顶点绘制一个等边三角形。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直或斜刺0.5至1.5寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 通经活络，止痛
* 行气，宽胸利胁
* 宣利乳房

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

肩部沉重疼痛，肩胛疼痛，肘臂疼痛不能抬举，肘痛，面颊和颌下区肿胀。
胸胁胀满，咳嗽，乳房疼痛肿胀，乳痈，乳汁量少。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SI11 天宗](point/SI11.md)** 的主治反映了其位置及其行气宽胸的作用。它是治疗肩胛区疼痛的重要穴位，无论是由于扭伤、痹痛、过度使用、不良姿势、肝气郁结还是胆囊疾病引起的牵涉痛，触诊时通常可发现剧烈压痛。它位于乳房后方，也常与 **[SI1 少泽](point/SI1.md)** 和 **[REN17 膻中](point/REN17.md)** 等穴位联合使用，治疗各种乳房疾病，包括疼痛、结块和乳汁量少。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 治疗肩胛区任何疼痛（无论是由于扭伤、痹痛、过度使用、不良姿势、肝气郁结还是胆囊疾病引起的牵涉痛）的常用局部穴位。
* 治疗乳房疾病，包括疼痛、结块和乳汁量少。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 肩臂疼痛：**[SI11 天宗](point/SI11.md)** 和 **[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 手臂疼痛：**[SI11 天宗](point/SI11.md)**，**[SJ14 肩髎](point/SJ14.md)** 和 **[SI5 阳谷](point/SI5.md)**（《资生经》）。
