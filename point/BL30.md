### White Ring Shu BÁIHUÁNSHŪ
![[BL-30 白环俞.png|250]]
#### Location

1.5 cun lateral to the midline, at the level of the fourth posterior sacral foramen.

#### Location Notes

i. As the inner line of the Bladder channel on the back descends towards the sacrum, it runs closer to the midline; the 1.5 cun measurement in this area, therefore, will be relatively shorter; ii. For guidance on locating the sacral foramen see page 69.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Note:** some classical sources forbid moxibustion at this point.

#### Actions

- Benefits the lumbar region and legs
- Regulates menstruation
- Stops leucorrhoea and seminal emission

#### Indications

- Pain of the lumbar spine and sacrum, lumbar pain due to cold, pain of the lumbar region and hip, pain of the coccyx, difficulty in sitting and standing, debility of the leg and knee, contracted sinews associated with painful obstruction.
- Irregular menstruation, dysmenorrhoea, uterine bleeding, infertility.
- Seminal emission, seminal emission with dreams, red and white leucorrhoea, difficult defecation and urination, prolapse of the rectum, dark urine, white turbidity, shan disorder, warm malaria.

#### Commentary

Baihuanshu BL-30 is primarily used as a local point to activate the circulation of qi and treat pain of the lumbar region and sacrum, pain of the lumbar region and hip, and especially pain of the coccyx.

In common with many points located on the sacrum (see [Ciliao BL-32](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-32)) Baihuanshu BL-30 is also able to regulate the function of the uterus and genito-urinary system. It is specifically indicated for stopping leakage in the form of seminal emission and nocturnal emission, leucorrhoea and white turbid urine.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Mainly used for local pain of the lumbar spine, sacrum and coccyx.
- Also regulates the lower jiao: irregular menstruation, dysmenorrhoea, leucorrhoea, seminal emission, difficult defecation and urination, prolapse of the rectum, dark urine.

#### Combinations

- Back pain extending to the lumbar region: Báihuánshū BL-30 and [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Lumbar pain due to Kidney deficiency: Báihuánshū BL-30, [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23), [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) and [Tàixī KID-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-3) (Great Compendium).
- Red and white leucorrhoea: Báihuánshū BL-30, [Dàimài GB-26](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-26), [Guānyuán REN-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-4), [Qìhǎi REN-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-6), [Sānyīnjiāo SP-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-6) and [Jiānshǐ PC-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-5) (Great Compendium).

### **[BL30 白环俞](/point/BL30.md)** (BÁIHUÁNSHŪ)

* **英文名称：** White Ring Shu
* **中文名称：** 白环俞
* **国际代码：** BL-30

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第四骶后孔水平，距中线旁开1.5寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 膀胱经背部内侧线向骶部下降时，更靠近中线；因此，此区域的1.5寸测量相对较短；
    * 骶孔的定位请参考“Page 69”（此处引用原文，请根据实际参考资料定位）。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Note):** 某些经典文献禁止在此穴施灸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利腰腿
* 调经
* 止带，止遗精

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

腰骶部疼痛，腰部冷痛，腰髋疼痛，尾骨疼痛，坐卧困难，腿膝无力，筋脉挛缩伴痹痛。
月经不调，痛经，崩漏（子宫出血），不孕症。
遗精，梦遗，赤白带下，大小便困难，脱肛，尿黄，白浊，疝气，温疟。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL30 白环俞](/point/BL30.md)** 主要作为局部穴位，用于活络气血，治疗腰骶部疼痛、腰髋疼痛，特别是尾骨疼痛。

与骶骨上的许多穴位一样（参见**[BL32 次髎](/point/BL32.md)**），**[BL30 白环俞](/point/BL30.md)** 也能调理子宫和泌尿生殖系统功能。它明确主治遗精和梦遗、带下以及小便白浊等漏泄症状。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 主要用于腰椎、骶骨和尾骨的局部疼痛。
* 也调理下焦：月经不调，痛经，带下，遗精，大小便困难，脱肛，尿黄。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 背痛放射至腰部：**[BL30 白环俞](/point/BL30.md)** 和 **[BL40 委中](/point/BL40.md)**（《百症赋》）。
* 肾虚腰痛：**[BL30 白环俞](/point/BL30.md)**，**[BL23 肾俞](/point/BL23.md)**，**[BL40 委中](/point/BL40.md)** 和 **[KID3 太溪](/point/KID3.md)**（《针灸大成》）。
* 赤白带下：**[BL30 白环俞](/point/BL30.md)**，**[GB26 带脉](/point/GB26.md)**，**[REN4 关元](/point/REN4.md)**，**[REN6 气海](/point/REN6.md)**，**[SP6 三阴交](/point/SP6.md)** 和 **[PC5 间使](/point/PC5.md)**（《针灸大成》）。
