### Cheek Bone Crevice QUÁNLIÁO
![[SI-18 颧髎.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Small Intestine and Sanjiao channels

#### Location

Directly below the outer canthus, in the depression at the lower border of the zygomatic bone.

#### Needling

i. Transverse insertion joined to points such as [Dìcāng ST-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-4), [Xiàguān ST-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-7), [Yíngxiāng LI-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-20), [Jiáchē ST-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-6) etc.; ii. Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 0.7 cun.  
**Note:** according to the Illustrated Supplement to the Classic of Categories and a number of modern texts, this point is contraindicated to moxibustion.

#### Actions

- Eliminates wind and alleviates pain
- Clears heat and reduces swelling

#### Indications

- Deviation of the mouth and eye, ceaseless twitching of the eyelids, pain of the face, swelling of the cheek with toothache, inability to chew, abscess of the lip, red face, yellow eyes.

#### Commentary

The location of Quánliáo SI-18 renders it an important and commonly-used point in the treatment of facial disorders due to either interior or exterior wind, and it is indicated for facial paralysis, trigeminal neuralgia, twitching of the eyelids and toothache of the upper jaw. In the treatment of the first two conditions it is often joined to points such as [Yíngxiāng LI-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-20), [Dìcāng ST-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-4) and [Jiáchē ST-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-6) by transverse needling.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Local point for facial paralysis, trigeminal neuralgia, twitching of the eyelids and toothache of the upper jaw. For facial paralysis and trigeminal neuralgia it is often joined to points such as [Yíngxiāng LI-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-20), [Dìcāng ST-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-4) and [Jiáchē ST-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-6) by the transverse needling method.

#### Combinations

- Toothache: Quánliáo SI-18 and [Èrjiān LI-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-2) (Systematic Classic).
- Toothache with aversion to cold: Quánliáo SI-18, [Dàyíng ST-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-5), [Tīnghuì GB-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-2) and [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Pain and deviation of the mouth, aversion to wind and cold, inability to chew: Quánliáo SI-18 and [Jiáchē ST-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-6) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Red and yellow eyes: Quánliáo SI-18 and [Nèiguān PC-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-6) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[SI18 颧髎](/point/SI18.md)** (QUÁNLIÁO)

* **英文名称：** Cheek Bone Crevice
* **中文名称：** 颧髎
* **国际代码：** SI-18
* **穴位属性：** **[SI18 颧髎](/point/SI18.md)** 是小肠经与三焦经的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于外眼角直下方，颧骨下缘凹陷中。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

i. 横刺透向**[ST4 地仓](/point/ST4.md)**、**[ST7 下关](/point/ST7.md)**、**[LI20 迎香](/point/LI20.md)**、**[ST6 颊车](/point/ST6.md)** 等穴；ii. 垂直刺0.5至0.7寸。

* **注意 (Note):** 根据《类经图翼》和一些现代文献，此穴禁灸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 祛风止痛
* 清热消肿

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

口眼歪斜，眼睑跳动不止，面部疼痛，面颊肿胀伴牙痛，不能咀嚼，口唇痈肿，面赤，眼睛发黄。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SI18 颧髎](/point/SI18.md)** 的位置使其成为治疗内风或外风引起的面部疾病的重要常用穴位。它主治面瘫、三叉神经痛、眼睑跳动和上颌牙痛。在治疗前两种情况时，常采用横刺法透向**[LI20 迎香](/point/LI20.md)**、**[ST4 地仓](/point/ST4.md)** 和**[ST6 颊车](/point/ST6.md)** 等穴。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗面瘫、三叉神经痛、眼睑跳动和上颌牙痛的局部穴位。
* 治疗面瘫和三叉神经痛时，常采用横刺法透向**[LI20 迎香](/point/LI20.md)**、**[ST4 地仓](/point/ST4.md)** 和**[ST6 颊车](/point/ST6.md)** 等穴。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 牙痛：**[SI18 颧髎](/point/SI18.md)** 和**[LI2 二间](/point/LI2.md)**（《针灸甲乙经》）。
* 牙痛伴恶寒：**[SI18 颧髎](/point/SI18.md)**、**[ST5 大迎](/point/ST5.md)**、**[GB2 听会](/point/GB2.md)** 和**[LI11 曲池](/point/LI11.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 口痛口歪，怕风畏寒，不能咀嚼：**[SI18 颧髎](/point/SI18.md)** 和**[ST6 颊车](/point/ST6.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 目赤发黄：**[SI18 颧髎](/point/SI18.md)** 和**[PC6 内关](/point/PC6.md)**（《千金方》）。
