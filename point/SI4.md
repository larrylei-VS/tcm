### Wrist Bone WÀNGǓ
![[SI-4 腕骨.png|250]]
- Yuan-Source point of the Small Intestine channel

#### Location

On the ulnar border of the hand, in the depression between the base of the fifth metacarpal bone and the triquetral bone.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Clears heat and reduces swelling
- Clears damp-heat and treats jaundice

#### Indications

- Contraction of the five fingers with difficulty in flexing and extending, weakness and pain of the wrist, contraction of the arm and elbow with difficulty in flexing and extending, stiffness and swelling of the neck, headache, pain of the shoulder, coldness and pain of the shoulder and back, pain of the lateral costal region with inability to catch the breath, lumbar pain radiating to the leg, hemiplegia, clonic spasm.
- Pain and swelling of the cheek radiating to the ear, swelling of the neck, submandibular region and jaw, throat painful obstruction, tinnitus, superficial visual obstruction, lacrimation, loss of sense of taste.
- Jaundice, Spleen deficiency jaundice, febrile disease with absence of sweating, chills and fever, malaria, wasting and thirsting disorder, agitation and fullness.

#### Commentary

Wàngǔ SI-4 is the yuan-source point of the Small Intestine channel. Its main clinical application is in the treatment of contraction, stiffness and pain along the entire length of the channel including the hand, elbow, arm, shoulder, neck and back. It is one of the main points (along with [Yángxī LI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-5), [Zhōngzhǔ SJ-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-3) and [Zhīzhèng SI-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-7)) to treat disorders of all the five fingers, especially when characterised by contraction and pain.

Wàngǔ SI-4 has a specific effect in the treatment of swelling at the opposite end of the channel, especially the neck, cheek and submandibular region. Like all the previous points of this channel it is also indicated in the treatment of tinnitus, reflecting the pathways of the Small Intestine primary and sinew channels which enter the ear. This is an example of the use of acupuncture points to treat disorders of the channel rather than underlying zangfu disharmony, and Wàngǔ SI-4 may therefore be used in tinnitus due to any aetiology. Wàngǔ SI-4 is specifically indicated in combination (see below) to treat pain and swelling of the submandibular region radiating to the ear and giving rise to tinnitus.

Finally, Wàngǔ SI-4 has been found to be empirically effective in the treatment of different kinds of jaundice, whether due to damp-heat or cold damp, as well as pain of the lateral costal region with inability to catch the breath. This action cannot easily be explained, either by reference to the channel pathway or to the functions of the Small Intestine.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Treats disorders of the fingers, wrist, elbow, shoulder, neck and head.
- Clears heat and swelling from the upper portion of the channel: pain and swelling of the cheek radiating to the ear, swelling of the neck, submandibular region and jaw, throat painful obstruction, tinnitus, lacrimation, loss of sense of taste.
- Empirical point for jaundice.

#### Combinations

- Contraction of the five fingers with inability to flex and extend: Wàngǔ SI-4 and [Zhōngzhǔ SJ-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-3) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Pain of the shoulder and arm: Wàngǔ SI-4 and [Tiānzōng SI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-11) (Supplementing Life).
- Pain of the submandibular region giving rise to tinnitus and difficult hearing: Wàngǔ SI-4, [Yánggǔ SI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-5), [Jiānzhēn SI-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-9), [Xiáxī GB-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-43) and [Zúqiàoyīn GB-44](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-44) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Toothache and tooth decay: Wàngǔ SI-4, [Xiàguān ST-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-7), [Dàyíng ST-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-5) and [Yìfēng SJ-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-17) (Supplementing Life).
- Pain of the lateral costal region with inability to catch the breath: Wàngǔ SI-4 and [Yánggǔ SI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-5) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Spleen deficiency jaundice: Wàngǔ SI-4 and [Zhōngwǎn REN-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-12) (Ode of the Jade Dragon).

### **[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)** (WÀNGǓ)

* **英文名称：** Wrist Bone
* **中文名称：** 腕骨
* **国际代码：** SI-4
* **穴位属性：** **[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)** 是小肠经的原穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于手尺侧缘，第五掌骨基底与三角骨之间凹陷中。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.3至0.5寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 通经活络，止痛
* 清热消肿
* 清利湿热，治疗黄疸

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

五指挛缩，屈伸不利，腕部酸痛，手臂、肘部挛缩屈伸不利，颈部僵硬肿胀，头痛，肩痛，肩背部寒痛，胁肋部疼痛伴呼吸困难，腰痛放射至腿部，半身不遂，痉挛。
面颊疼痛肿胀放射至耳部，颈部、颌下区和下颌肿胀，咽喉肿痛，耳鸣，外障眼病（翳膜），流泪，味觉丧失。
黄疸，脾虚黄疸，热病无汗，恶寒发热，疟疾，消渴病，烦躁胀满。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)** 是小肠经的原穴。其主要临床应用是治疗经络循行全程的挛缩、僵硬和疼痛，包括手、肘、臂、肩、颈和背部。它是治疗所有五指疾病的主要穴位之一（与**[LI5 阳溪](point/LI5.md)**、**[SJ3 中渚](point/SJ3.md)** 和**[SI7 支正](point/SI7.md)** 一起），特别是以挛缩和疼痛为特征的。

**[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)** 在治疗经络另一端（头部）的肿胀方面有特定疗效，特别是颈部、面颊和颌下区。与该经脉所有先前的穴位一样，它也主治耳鸣，这反映了小肠经正经和经筋进入耳部的路径。这是针灸穴位用于治疗经络疾病而非潜在脏腑失调的一个例子，因此**[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)** 可用于治疗任何病因引起的耳鸣。**[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)** 在经典中被明确指出可与组合穴位（见下文）一起治疗颌下区疼痛肿胀放射至耳部并引起耳鸣。

最后，**[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)** 被发现对治疗各种黄疸，无论是湿热型还是寒湿型，以及胁肋部疼痛伴呼吸困难具有经验疗效。这一作用不易通过经络路径或小肠功能来解释。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗手指、手腕、肘部、肩部、颈部和头部疾病。
* 清泄经络上部的热邪和肿胀：面颊疼痛肿胀放射至耳部，颈部、颌下区和下颌肿胀，咽喉肿痛，耳鸣，流泪，味觉丧失。
* 治疗黄疸的经验穴。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 五指挛缩屈伸不利：**[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)** 和 **[SJ3 中渚](point/SJ3.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 肩臂疼痛：**[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)** 和 **[SI11 天宗](point/SI11.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 颌下区疼痛放射至耳部并引起耳鸣、听力困难：**[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)**，**[SI5 阳谷](point/SI5.md)**，**[SI9 肩贞](point/SI9.md)**，**[GB43 侠溪](point/GB43.md)** 和 **[GB44 足窍阴](point/GB44.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 牙痛和龋齿：**[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)**，**[ST7 下关](point/ST7.md)**，**[ST5 大迎](point/ST5.md)** 和 **[SJ17 翳风](point/SJ17.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 胁肋部疼痛伴呼吸困难：**[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)** 和 **[SI5 阳谷](point/SI5.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 脾虚黄疸：**[SI4 腕骨](point/SI4.md)** 和 **[REN12 中脘](point/REN12.md)**（《玉龙歌》）。
