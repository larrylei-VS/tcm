### Rushing Yang CHŌNGYÁNG
![[ST-42 冲阳.png|250]]
- Yuan-Source point of the Stomach channel

#### Location

On the dorsum of the foot, in the depression formed by the junction of the second and third metatarsal bones and the cuneiform bones (second and third), 1.5 cun distal to [Jiěxī ST-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-41), on the line drawn between [Jiěxī ST-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-41) and [Xiàngǔ ST-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-43), at the point where the pulsation of the dorsalis pedis artery may be palpated.

#### Location Notes

i. Run the finger proximally from [Xiàngǔ ST-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-43) towards [Jiěxī ST-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-41); the point is located in a depression approximately halfway between these two points; ii. This point may lie either side of the medial slip of the extensor digitorum longus tendon, which runs to the second toe.

#### Needling

Oblique or perpendicular insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.  
**Caution:** care should be taken not to puncture the dorsalis pedis artery which lies beneath this point.

#### Actions

- Clears heat from the Stomach channel
- Harmonises the Stomach fu
- Calms the spirit
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Deviation of the mouth and eye, swelling and pain of the face, swelling of the face, toothache, tooth decay, heat and pain of the inside of the mouth.
- Abdominal distention with no pleasure in eating, epigastric pain, vomiting.
- Fever with absence of sweating, chills and fever with malarial disorder.
- Mania-depression, desires to ascend to high places and sing, discards clothing and runs around.
- Swelling and pain of the dorsum of the foot, atrophy disorder of the foot.

#### Commentary

In terms of its actions and indications Chōngyáng ST-42, the yuan-source point of the Stomach channel, is similar to [Jiěxī ST-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-41), although less commonly used in current-day clinical practice.

Chōngyáng ST-42 is located over the pulsation of the dorsalis pedis artery, and the Essential Questions suggests that the knee be raised in order to allow the artery to pulsate more clearly and thus assist in locating this point. This advice is echoed by the traditional observation that if the point [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) is obstructed, the beating of the artery will be diminished, and in fact if the point [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) is pressed firmly, the pulsation will disappear entirely.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Similar to [Jiěxī ST-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-41), but less commonly used.
- The Essential Questions advises that the knee should be raised to allow the dorsalis pedis artery to pulsate more clearly and thus assist in locating this point. In fact if [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) (located near the knee) is pressed firmly, the pulsation will disappear entirely.

#### Combinations

- Facial swelling: Chōngyáng ST-42, [Tiānshū ST-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-25), [Fēnglóng ST-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-40), [Lìduì ST-45](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-45) and [Xiàngǔ ST-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-43) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Tooth decay: Chōngyáng ST-42 and [Qūbìn GB-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-7) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Mania, desires to ascend to high places and sing, discards clothing and runs around: Chōngyáng ST-42, [Shénmén HE-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-7) and [Hòuxī SI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-3) (Great Compendium).
- Mania disorder with rash behaviour, desires to ascend to high places and sing, discards clothing and runs around: Chōngyáng ST-42 and [Fēnglóng ST-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-40) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Flaccidity of the legs: Chōngyáng ST-42, [Yánglíngquán GB-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-34), [Tàichōng LIV-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-3) and [Qiūxū GB-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-40) (Great Compendium).
- One-sided wind (hemiplegia): Chōngyáng ST-42 and [Lièquē LU-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-7) (Great Compendium).

### [ST42 冲阳](/point/ST42.md) (CHŌNGYÁNG)

* **英文名称：** Rushing Yang
* **中文名称：** 冲阳
* **国际代码：** ST-42
* **穴位属性：** [ST42 冲阳](/point/ST42.md) 是胃经的原穴。

#### 定位 (Location)

位于足背部，第二、第三跖骨与第二、第三楔骨结合处形成的凹陷中，距[ST41 解溪](/point/ST41.md) 下方1.5寸，在连接[ST41 解溪](/point/ST41.md) 与[ST43 陷谷](/point/ST43.md) 的连线上，可触及足背动脉搏动处。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 从[ST43 陷谷](/point/ST43.md) 向上方（近端）滑动手指至[ST41 解溪](/point/ST41.md)；此穴位于这两个穴位之间大约中点的凹陷处；
    * 此穴可能位于趾长伸肌腱（通向第二足趾）内侧或外侧。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

斜刺或垂直刺0.3至0.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 需注意避免刺破位于此穴下方的足背动脉。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 清泄胃经之热
* 调和胃腑
* 宁心安神
* 通经活络，止痛

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

口眼歪斜，面部肿胀疼痛，面部肿胀，牙痛，龋齿，口内灼热疼痛。
腹胀伴食欲不振，胃脘痛，呕吐。
发热无汗，恶寒发热伴疟疾。
癫狂，欲登高而歌，弃衣奔走。
足背肿痛，足部痿证。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

就其作用和主治而言，[ST42 冲阳](/point/ST42.md) 作为胃经的原穴，与[ST41 解溪](/point/ST41.md) 相似，但目前临床上较少使用。

[ST42 冲阳](/point/ST42.md) 位于足背动脉搏动处，《素问·热论》建议抬高膝盖，以便足背动脉搏动更清晰，从而有助于定位此穴。这一建议与传统观察相呼应：如果[ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md) 受阻，动脉搏动会减弱；实际上，如果用力按压[ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md)，搏动会完全消失。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 作用与[ST41 解溪](/point/ST41.md) 相似，但临床上较少使用。
* 《素问·热论》建议抬高膝盖，以便足背动脉搏动更清晰，从而有助于定位此穴。实际上，如果用力按压[ST36 足三里](/point/ST36.md)（位于膝盖附近），搏动会完全消失。

* 癫狂病伴狂躁行为，欲登高而歌，弃衣奔走：[ST42 冲阳](/point/ST42.md) 和[ST40 丰隆](/point/ST40.md)（《千金方》）。
* 腿部痿弱无力：[ST42 冲阳](/point/ST42.md)，[GB34 阳陵泉](/point/GB34.md)，[LIV3 太冲](/point/LIV3.md) 和[GB40 丘墟](/point/GB40.md)（《针灸大成》）。
