### Leg Five Miles ZÚWǓLǏ
![[LIV-10 足五里.png|250]]
#### Location

3 cun inferior to [Qìchōng ST-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-30) on the anterior border of m. adductor longus.

#### Location Notes

i. [Qìchōng ST-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-30) is located on the lower abdomen, 2 cun lateral to the midline, level with the superior border of the pubic symphysis ( [Qūgǔ REN-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-2)); ii. Measure the 3 cun as one handbreadth inferior to [Qìchōng ST-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-30); iii. The tendon of origin of adductor longus arises from the pubic bone and is identified as the most prominent tendon in the groin.

#### Needling

Perpendicular or oblique insertion 0.5 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Clears damp-heat and benefits the lower jiao.

#### Indications

- Difficult urination, retention of urine, enuresis, itching of the genitals, swelling and pain of the testicles, fullness of the abdomen, fullness of the lower abdomen.
- Wind-taxation somnolence, difficult respiration, cough.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Difficult urination, retention of urine, enuresis, itching of the genitals, swelling and pain of the testicles, fullness of the abdomen, fullness of the lower abdomen, wind-taxation somnolence, difficult respiration, cough.

#### Combinations

- Somnolence with no desire to move the four limbs: Zúwǔlǐ LIV-10, [Sānyángluò SJ-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-8), [Tiānjǐng SJ-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-10), [Lìduì ST-45](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-45) and [Sānjiān LI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-3) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Jaundice with periodic low grade fever: Zúwǔlǐ LIV-10 and [Zhōngfēng LIV-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-4) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### 1. [LIV10 足五里](/point/LIV10.md)

#### 1.1. Leg Five Miles 足五里

#### 1.2. 定位

位于[ST30 气冲](/point/ST30.md)下方3寸，沿长收肌前缘。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

i. [ST30 气冲](/point/ST30.md)位于下腹部，距中线2寸，与耻骨联合上缘（[REN2 曲骨](/point/REN2.md)）平齐；  
ii. 从[ST30 气冲](/point/ST30.md)向下量一手掌宽（3寸）；  
iii. 长收肌的起始腱起于耻骨，是腹股沟中最明显的腱。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直或斜刺，深0.5至1.5寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 清湿热，益下焦  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 尿难、尿潴留、遗尿、生殖器瘙痒、睾丸肿痛、腹胀满、下腹胀满。  
- 风劳嗜睡、呼吸困难、咳嗽。  

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 适用于尿难、尿潴留、遗尿、生殖器瘙痒、睾丸肿痛、腹胀满、下腹胀满、风劳嗜睡、呼吸困难、咳嗽。  

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 嗜睡伴四肢不动欲：[LIV10 足五里](/point/LIV10.md) + [SJ8 三阳络](/point/SJ8.md) + [SJ10 天井](/point/SJ10.md) + [ST45 厉兑](/point/ST45.md) + [LI3 三间](/point/LI3.md)（《千金方》）。  
- 黄疸伴周期性低热：[LIV10 足五里](/point/LIV10.md) + [LIV4 中封](/point/LIV4.md)（《千金方》）。
