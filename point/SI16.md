### Heavenly Window TIĀNCHUĀNG
![[SI-16 天窗.png|250]]
- Point of the Window of Heaven

#### Location

On the posterior border of the sternocleidomastoid muscle, level with the laryngeal prominence.

#### Location Notes

i. Palpation of the posterior border of the sternocleidomastoid muscle is made easier if the patient turns their head away from the side to be needled, whilst you apply resistance at the chin; ii. In females the laryngeal prominence is not as pronounced as in males. If it is indistinct, palpate the depression formed by the lower border of the hyoid bone and the upper border of the thyroid cartilage at the midline. The laryngeal prominence lies just below this.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 0.8 cun.

#### Actions

- Benefits the ears, throat and voice
- Regulates qi and calms the spirit
- Activates the channel, alleviates pain and clears heat

#### Indications

- Deafness, tinnitus, ear pain.
- Sudden loss of voice, sudden loss of voice following windstroke, hoarseness due to cold in the throat, lockjaw, clenched teeth from windstroke, headache.
- Throat pain, goitre, swelling and pain of the cheek, heat sensation of the skin of the face, urticaria.
- Manic ghost talk, mania-depression.
- Shoulder pain radiating to the neck, shoulder pain leading to stiffness of the neck with inability to turn the head, neck pain.

#### Commentary

Tiānchuāng SI-16 is one of ten points listed in Chapter 2 of the Spiritual Pivot that have come to be known as Window of Heaven points. As a group, these ten points have the action of regulating rebellious and chaotic (inversion) qi. Tiānchuāng SI-16 illustrates many of the characteristic actions of these points in its ability to i. treat goitre and throat disorders, ii. treat headache and heat and swelling of the face and cheek, iii. treat disorders of the sense organs - in this case the ears, iv. treat sudden onset of disorders - in this case loss of voice. In addition, like two other points from this group ([Tiānzhù BL-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-10) and [Tiānfǔ LU-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-3)) Tiānchuāng SI-16 treats disorders characterised in the classical texts as seeing or communicating with ghosts and therefore attributed to some form of demonic possession. In terms of modern medicine these indications refer to various forms of severe mental disorder including schizophrenia. For a fuller discussion see the [Window of Heaven points](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-categories/points-of-the-window-of-heaven).

Sun Si-miao, the famous 7th century physician, particularly emphasised the use of moxibustion (fifty cones) at this point for loss of speech and hemiplegia.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Window of Heaven point: treats i. goitre and throat disorders (hoarseness, throat pain) ii. headache and heat and swelling of the face and cheek, iii. disorders of the sense organs - in this case the ears (deafness, tinnitus, ear pain), iv. sudden onset of disorders (sudden loss of voice).
- Treats mania-depression and ‘manic ghost talk’.

#### Combinations

- Ear pain, deafness and tinnitus: Tiānchuāng SI-16, [Yángxī LI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-5), [Guānchōng SJ-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-1), [Yèmén SJ-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-2) and [Zhōngzhǔ SJ-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-3) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Deafness and tinnitus: Tiānchuāng SI-16 and [Wàiguān SJ-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-5) (Supplementing Life).
- Throat pain: Tiānchuāng SI-16, [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16) and [Láogōng PC-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-8) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Goitre: Tiānchuāng SI-16 and [Nāohuì SJ-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-13) (Systematic Classic).
- Sudden loss of voice: Tiānchuāng SI-16, [Zhīgōu SJ-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-6), [Fútú LI-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-18), [Qūbìn GB-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-7) and [Língdào HE-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-4) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Sudden loss of voice with lockjaw: Tiānchuāng SI-16, [Língdào HE-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-4) and [Tiāntú REN-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-22) (Supplementing Life).
- Lockjaw: Tiānchuāng SI-16 and [Yìfēng SJ-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-17) (Supplementing Life).
- Heat sensation of the skin of the face: Tiānchuāng SI-16 and [Tiāntú REN-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-22) (Supplementing Life).

### **[SI16 天窗](point/SI16.md)** (TIĀNCHUĀNG)

* **英文名称：** Heavenly Window
* **中文名称：** 天窗
* **国际代码：** SI-16
* **穴位属性：** **[SI16 天窗](point/SI16.md)** 是天窗穴之一。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于胸锁乳突肌后缘，平喉结。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 要触诊胸锁乳突肌的后缘，请患者将头部转向欲针刺侧的对侧，同时您在下巴处施加阻力；
    * 女性喉结不像男性那样突出。如果喉结不明显，可在中线触诊舌骨下缘与甲状软骨上缘形成的凹陷，喉结就在此下方。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至0.8寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利耳、咽喉和声音
* 调理气机，宁心安神
* 通经活络，止痛，清热

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

耳聋，耳鸣，耳痛。
暴喑（突然失音），中风后突然失音，咽喉受寒声嘶，牙关紧闭，中风牙关紧闭，头痛。
咽喉疼痛，瘿瘤，面颊肿痛，面部皮肤热感，荨麻疹。
狂言鬼语，癫狂。
肩痛放射至颈部，肩痛导致颈项强直不能转头，颈痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SI16 天窗](point/SI16.md)** 是《灵枢·刺节真邪》第二篇中列出的十个穴位之一，这些穴位被称为天窗穴。作为一个组，这些十个穴位具有调理逆乱气机（气逆）的作用。**[SI16 天窗](point/SI16.md)** 展现了这些穴位的许多特征性作用：i. 治疗瘿瘤和咽喉疾病，ii. 治疗头痛以及面颊的热肿，iii. 治疗七窍疾病——此处指耳部，iv. 治疗突发性疾病——此处指失音。此外，像此组中的另外两个穴位（**[BL10 天柱](point/BL10.md)** 和 **[LU3 天府](point/LU3.md)**）一样，**[SI16 天窗](point/SI16.md)** 治疗经典文献中描述为“见鬼”或“与鬼交流”的疾病，因此归因于某种形式的魔邪附体。在现代医学中，这些主治指代各种形式的严重精神疾病，包括精神分裂症。更详细的讨论请参阅“天窗穴”。

七世纪名医孙思邈特别强调在此穴施灸（五十壮）治疗失语和半身不遂。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 天窗穴：主治 i. 瘿瘤和咽喉疾病（声嘶，咽痛）； ii. 头痛以及面颊的热肿； iii. 七窍疾病——此处指耳部（耳聋，耳鸣，耳痛）； iv. 突发性疾病（暴喑，突然失音）。
* 治疗癫狂和“狂言鬼语”。
