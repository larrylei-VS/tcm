### Upright Nutrition ZHÈNGYÍNG
![[GB-17 正营.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder channel with the Yang Linking vessel

#### Location

In the parietal region, on a curved line drawn between [Tóulínqì GB-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-15) and [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20), following the contour of the cranium, 1.5 cun posterior to [Mùchuāng GB-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-16).

#### Location Notes

i. See location note for [Tóulínqì GB-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-15); ii. First locate [Tóulínqì GB-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-15), 0.5 cun posterior to the hairline, and then locate [Chénglíng GB-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-18) directly lateral to [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20), on the line of the Gall Bladder channel. Zhèngyíng GB-17 is then located two thirds of the distance between [Tóulínqì GB-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-15) and [Chénglíng GB-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-18).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5-1.5 cun. See note [Hànyàn GB-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-4).

#### Actions

- Benefits the head and alleviates pain
- Pacifies the Stomach

#### Indications

- Headache, one-sided headache, toothache of the upper jaw.
- Visual dizziness, dizziness due to phlegm-fluid, ceaseless vomiting, nausea, stiff neck, stiffness of the lips, aversion to wind and cold, aversion to the sound of people talking.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Headache, toothache of the upper jaw, and aversion to cold.
- Indicated for dizziness due to phlegm-fluid, ceaseless vomiting, nausea, stiff neck, stiffness of the lips, aversion to the sound of people talking.

#### Combinations

- Toothache of the upper jaw: Zhèngyíng GB-17 and [Yánggǔ SI-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-5) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Pain from tooth decay: Zhèngyíng GB-17, Sanjian LI‑3 and [Dàyíng ST-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-5) (Supplementing Life).
- Stiffness of the lips and pain from tooth decay of the upper jaw: Zhèngyíng GB-17, [Duìduān DU-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-27), [Mùchuāng GB-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-16) and [Ěrmén SJ-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-21) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### 1. [GB17 正营](/point/GB17.md)

#### 1.1. Upright Nutrition 正營

- 胆经与阳维脉的交会穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于顶区，沿从[GB15 头临泣](/point/GB15.md)到[GB20 风池](/point/GB20.md)沿颅骨轮廓画出的弧线上，在[GB16 目窗](/point/GB16.md)后方1.5寸处。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

i. 参见[GB15 头临泣](/point/GB15.md)的定位说明；  
ii. 首先定位[GB15 头临泣](/point/GB15.md)（发际后0.5寸），然后定位[GB18 承灵](/point/GB18.md)（正对[DU20 百会](/point/DU20.md)的侧方，在胆经线上）。[GB17 正营](/point/GB17.md)位于[GB15 头临泣](/point/GB15.md)与[GB18 承灵](/point/GB18.md)之间三分之二处。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

横刺，深0.5至1.5寸。参见[GB4 颔厌](/point/GB4.md)的针刺说明。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 益头止痛
- 和胃

#### 1.6. 主治

- 头痛、偏头痛、上颌牙痛。
- 视觉眩晕、痰液引起的眩晕、持续呕吐、恶心、颈僵、唇僵、恶风寒、厌闻人语。

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 适用于头痛、上颌牙痛及恶寒。
- 适用于痰液引起的眩晕、持续呕吐、恶心、颈僵、唇僵、厌闻人语。

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 上颌牙痛：[GB17 正营](/point/GB17.md) + [SI5 阳谷](/point/SI5.md)（《千金方》）。
- 龋齿痛：[GB17 正营](/point/GB17.md) + [LI3 三间](/point/LI3.md) + [ST5 大迎](/point/ST5.md)（《补命》）。
- 唇僵及上颌龋齿痛：[GB17 正营](/point/GB17.md) + [DU27 兑端](/point/DU27.md) + [GB16 目窗](/point/GB16.md) + [SJ21 耳门](/point/SJ21.md)（《千金方》）。
