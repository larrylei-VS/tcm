### ZHŌNGSHŪ 
![[DU-7 中枢.png|250]]
#### Location

On the midline of the back, in the depression below the spinous process of the tenth thoracic vertebra.

#### Location Notes

This point is located five intervertebral spaces above the line connecting the highest points of the two iliac crests (this line is level with the lower border of L3).

#### Needling

Perpendicular-oblique superior insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** the spinal canal lies between 1.25 and 1.75 cun deep to the skin surface, varying according to body build.  
**Note:** according to the Illustrated Supplement to the Classic of Categories this point is contraindicated to moxibustion.

#### Actions

- Benefits the spine
- Benefits the middle jiao

#### Indications

- Pain of the lumbar region and back.
- Abdominal fullness, no desire to eat, jaundice, amenorrhoea.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for pain of the lumbar region and back, abdominal fullness, no desire to eat, jaundice, amenorrhoea.

### 1. [DU7 中枢](point/DU7.md)

#### 1.1. Central Pivot 中樞

#### 1.2. 定位

位于背部中线，第十胸椎棘突下方的凹陷中。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

此穴位于连接两髂嵴最高点的线（此线与第三腰椎下缘平齐）上方五个椎间隙。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

向上垂直-斜刺，深0.5至1寸。  
**注意：**  
1. 脊管位于皮肤表面下1.25至1.75寸，深度因体型而异；  
2. 《类经图翼》认为此穴禁灸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 益脊柱  
- 益中焦  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 腰背痛。  
- 腹满不欲食、黄疸、闭经。  

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 使用较少。  
- 适用于腰背痛、腹满不欲食、黄疸、闭经。
