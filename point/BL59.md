### Instep Yang FÙYÁNG
![[BL-59 跗骨.png|250]]
- Xi-Cleft point of the Yang Motility vessel

#### Location

On the lower leg, 3 cun directly superior to [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60).

#### Location Notes

i. Locate one handbreadth proximal to [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60); ii. This point is located in the depression between the Achilles tendon and the peroneal tendons.

#### Needling

Perpendicular or oblique insertion directed proximally or distally, 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Benefits the lumbar region and legs
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Lumbar pain with inability to stand for long, inability to stand after sitting, thigh pain, wind painful obstruction with numbness, atrophy disorder of the lower limb, inability to raise the four limbs, heaviness and soreness of the leg and knee, sciatica, cold damp leg qi, ulceration of the leg, redness and swelling of the lateral malleolus.
- Heavy head, chills and fever, pain of the brow, sudden turmoil disorder with cramps, clonic spasm.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for lumbar pain with inability to stand for long, inability to stand after sitting, thigh pain, wind painful obstruction with numbness, atrophy disorder of the lower limb, inability to raise the four limbs, heaviness and soreness of the leg and knee, sciatica, cold damp leg qi, ulceration of the leg, redness and swelling of the lateral malleolus, heavy head, chills and fever, pain of the brow, sudden turmoil disorder with cramps, clonic spasm.

#### Combinations

- Clonic spasm: Fùyáng BL-59 and [Tiānjǐng SJ-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-10) (Supplementing Life).
- Heaviness of the head: Fùyáng BL-59 [Tōngtiān BL-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-7) and [Yǎmén DU-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-15) (Supplementing Life).

### **[BL59 跗阳](/point/BL59.md)** (Fùyáng)

* **英文名称：** Instep Yang
* **中文名称：** 跗阳
* **国际代码：** BL-59
* **穴位属性：** **[BL59 跗阳](/point/BL59.md)** 是阳跷脉的郄穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于小腿部，**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 正上方3寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 在**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 近端约一横掌处定位；
    * 此穴位于跟腱与腓骨肌腱之间的凹陷中。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直或斜刺，针尖向近端或远端，1至1.5寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宣利腰腿
* 通经活络，止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

腰痛不能久站，坐后不能起立，大腿疼痛，风痹伴麻木，下肢痿证，四肢不能抬举，腿膝沉重酸痛，坐骨神经痛，寒湿腿气，腿部溃疡，外踝红肿。
头重，恶寒发热，眉棱骨痛，霍乱吐泻伴痉挛，痉挛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL59 跗阳](/point/BL59.md)** 临床上较少使用。作为阳跷脉的郄穴，其名称“跗阳”指其位于足部阳侧，暗示其在调理下肢阳经气机和治疗下肢疾病方面的作用。

此穴主要用于缓解**腰腿部疼痛**和**活动不利**，特别是腰痛伴不能久站或坐后不能起立，以及大腿疼痛、坐骨神经痛、下肢痿证和痹痛。其**祛风散寒**的功效也使其适用于风湿痹痛和寒湿腿气。

此外，**[BL59 跗阳](/point/BL59.md)** 对头部症状如头重、恶寒发热、眉棱骨痛也有一定的辅助疗效，这与其**调理阳跷脉**、**祛风清热**的作用相关。它也能辅助治疗霍乱吐泻伴痉挛等急性病症。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治腰痛不能久站，坐后不能起立，大腿疼痛，风痹伴麻木，下肢痿证，四肢不能抬举，腿膝沉重酸痛，坐骨神经痛，寒湿腿气，腿部溃疡，外踝红肿。
* 主治头重，恶寒发热，眉棱骨痛，霍乱吐泻伴痉挛，痉挛。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 痉挛：**[BL59 跗阳](/point/BL59.md)** 和 **[SJ10 天井](/point/SJ10.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 头重：**[BL59 跗阳](/point/BL59.md)**，**[BL7 通天](/point/BL7.md)** 和 **[DU15 哑门](/point/DU15.md)**（《资生经》）。
