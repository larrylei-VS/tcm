### Palace of Toil LÁOGŌNG
![[PC-8.png|250]]


- Ying-Spring and Fire point of the Pericardium channel
- Sun Si-miao Ghost point

##### Location 

Between the second and third metacarpal bones, proximal to the metacarpo-phalangeal joint, in a depression at the radial side of the third metacarpal bone.

##### Location note 

i. This point may be located at the place where the tip of the middle finger lands when a fist is made; ii. Some classical sources locate this point on the ulnar side of the third metacarpal bone, where the tip of the ring finger lands when a fist is made.

##### Needling 

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 cun.

##### Actions 

- Clears heat from the Pericardium and revives consciousness
- Clears heat from the Heart and calms the spirit
- Harmonises the Stomach and clears heat from the middle jiao
- Clears the nutritive level and cools blood

##### Indications 

- Fever, coma, loss of consciousness, febrile disease accompanied by sweating which continues for days, windstroke, hypertension.
- Epilepsy, mania-depression, fright, sadness, propensity to anger, apprehension, restless zang disorder, ceaseless laughter.
- Heart pain, pain of the chest and lateral costal region, cough.
- Erosion of the mouth and tongue (in children), mouth ulcers, foul breath, difficult ingestion, vomiting, hypogastric (ji ju) masses.
- Vomiting blood, ceaseless nosebleed, blood in the stool, haemorrhoids, dark urine, jaundice, yellow eyes, throat pain.
- Tremor of the hand, eczema and tinea of the hand, scaling of the skin of the hand, heat in the palm of the hand, sweating of the palms, hand painful obstruction.

##### Commentary 

Láogōng PC-8 is the ying-spring and fire point of the Pericardium channel. According to the Classic of Difficulties[13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-pericardium-channel/references) ying-spring points are indicated for "heat in the body". Láogōng PC-8 is a powerful point to revive consciousness and calm the spirit in cases where heat 'collapses' into the Pericardium during the course of febrile diseases, leading to disturbance of the emotions, and in severe cases coma.

There are two major discussions on the relationship of the Heart and the Pericardium channel in the Spiritual Pivot. At this early date, it is clear that the Pericardium channel was considered to be the main channel that linked with and treated the Heart itself, and indeed it was said that the Heart channel had no shu points of its own, Láogōng PC-8 being listed as the ying-spring and fire point of the Heart channel. It is therefore not only used to clear febrile heat from the Pericardium, the 'wrapping of the heart', but is one of the principal points to clear Heart fire generated by internal disharmony. Heart fire may result from a number of different factors (see [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/262)), but due to the close relationship of the Pericardium and Liver jueyin channels, Láogōng PC-8 is particularly indicated when stagnant Liver fire transmits to the Heart. If Heart fire agitates the spirit there will be various manifestations of psycho-emotional disharmony such as mania-depression, propensity to anger, ceaseless laughter and epilepsy. Like [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/262), Láogōng PC-8 is also able to bolster the spirit and is indicated for such manifestations of deficiency as fright, sadness and apprehension.

If Heart fire condenses body fluids it may generate phlegm, and when this is combined with wind stirred up by Liver yang, the resultant wind-phlegm may give rise to epilepsy or windstroke. The importance of Láogōng PC-8 in the treatment of epilepsy and also mania-depression is reflected in its inclusion (under its alternative name of Guiku 'Ghost Cave') by Sun Si-miao in his 'thirteen ghost points' for the treatment of these conditions.

The Heart flowers into the tongue and the Spleen opens into the mouth. Mouth and tongue ulcers are differentiated into six main patterns: i. Heart fire, ii. Spleen and Stomach smouldering heat, iii. Kidney and Heart yin deficiency, iv. Spleen qi deficiency complicated with heat, v. blood deficiency with dry heat, and vi. Kidney yang deficiency. Láogōng PC-8 is indicated for ulceration or erosion of the mouth and tongue from any of these patterns, especially when heat is involved.

The Pericardium primary and divergent channels pass through the diaphragm to the middle and lower jiao and the Pericardium channel is interiorly-exteriorly coupled with the Sanjiao channel. Like many points of the Pericardium channel, therefore, Láogōng PC-8 also has a strong action on the middle jiao and is indicated in cases where fire disrupts the descent of Stomach qi resulting in foul breath, vomiting and difficult ingestion.

In common with [Qūzé PC-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/258), [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/262) and [Zhōngchōng PC-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/264), Láogōng PC-8 clears heat from the nutritive and blood levels. It may therefore be used when febrile heat at the blood level leads to reckless bleeding disorders such as vomiting blood, nosebleed, blood in the stool etc. Its ability to clear heat from the blood as well as its location, make it suitable for skin disorders affecting the palm such as eczema, tinea and scaling, as well as for heat and abnormal sweating of the palms. It is also indicated for hand painful obstruction and hand tremors.

In qigong practice, Láogōng PC-8 can be thought of as mirroring [Yǒngquǎn KID-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/KID/149) on the soles of the feet. It is common practice to 'breathe through' either of these points, and focusing on and building the qi at Láogōng PC-8 is a pre-requisite for emitting qi in the treatment of disease by qigong therapy.

Finally Láogōng PC-8 is cited in the Song of the Nine Needles for Returning the Yang for the treatment of collapse of yang characterised by loss of consciousness, aversion to cold, cold counterflow of the limbs, purple lips etc.

##### Summary of clinical application 

- Important point to clear heat from the Pericardium and Heart: fever, coma, loss of consciousness, epilepsy, mania-depression, fright, sadness, propensity to anger, apprehension, restless zang disorder (see [Língdào HE-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/HE/143)), ceaseless laughter.
- Treats ulceration or erosion of the mouth and tongue.
- Clears Stomach heat: foul breath, vomiting and difficult ingestion.
- Clears heat from the blood: vomiting blood, nosebleed, blood in the stool, eczema, tinea and scaling of the palm.

### **[PC8 劳宫](point/PC8.md)** (Láogōng)

* **英文名称：** Palace of Toil
* **中文名称：** 劳宫
* **国际代码：** PC-8
* **穴位属性：** **[PC8 劳宫](point/PC8.md)** 是心包经的荥穴和火穴，孙思邈十三鬼穴之一。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第二、第三掌骨之间，掌指关节近端，第三掌骨桡侧凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 此穴可位于握拳时中指尖所指处；
    * 某些经典文献将此穴定位在第三掌骨尺侧，即握拳时无名指尖所指处。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 清心包热，开窍醒神
* 清心热，宁心安神
* 调和胃腑，清中焦热
* 清营凉血

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

发热，昏迷，昏厥，热病伴持续数日出汗，中风，高血压。
癫痫，癫狂，惊恐，悲伤，易怒，心神不安，脏躁，喜笑不休。
心痛，胸胁疼痛，咳嗽。
口舌糜烂（小儿），口疮，口臭，吞咽困难，呕吐，痞块（腹部癥瘕积聚）。
呕血，鼻出血不止，便血，痔疮，尿黄，眼睛发黄，咽喉疼痛。
手颤，手部湿疹和癣，手部皮肤脱屑，掌心发热，掌心多汗，手部痹痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[PC8 劳宫](point/PC8.md)** 是心包经的荥穴和火穴。根据《难经》记载，荥穴主治“身热”。当热邪在热病过程中“内陷”心包，导致情志扰乱，严重时甚至昏迷时，**[PC8 劳宫](point/PC8.md)** 是开窍醒神、宁心安神的强大穴位。

《灵枢》中有两段关于心与心包经关系的重大论述。在早期，心包经被认为是与心脏本身相连并治疗心脏的主要经脉，甚至有人说心经本身没有输穴，**[PC8 劳宫](point/PC8.md)** 被列为心经的荥穴和火穴。因此，它不仅用于清泻心包（心脏的“外衣”）的热邪，也是清泻内生心火的主要穴位之一。心火可能由多种不同因素引起（参见**[PC7 大陵](point/PC7.md)**），但由于心包经与肝厥阴经的密切关系，当肝郁化火传导至心时，**[PC8 劳宫](point/PC8.md)** 尤其主治。如果心火扰乱神志，则会出现癫狂、易怒、喜笑不止和癫痫等精神情志失调的表现。像**[PC7 大陵](point/PC7.md)** 一样，**[PC8 劳宫](point/PC8.md)** 也能扶助心神，主治惊恐、悲伤和心神不安等虚证表现。

如果心火凝结津液，可能生成痰湿，当痰湿与肝阳内风结合时，由此产生的风痰可能引起癫痫或中风。**[PC8 劳宫](point/PC8.md)** 在治疗癫痫和癫狂中的重要性体现在孙思邈将其列入其治疗这些疾病的“十三鬼穴”（以其别名“鬼窟”）中。

心开窍于舌，脾开窍于口。口舌溃疡分为六种主要证型：i. 心火，ii. 脾胃郁热，iii. 肾心阴虚，iv. 脾气虚兼热，v. 血虚兼燥热，以及 vi. 肾阳虚。**[PC8 劳宫](point/PC8.md)** 主治由任何这些证型引起的口舌溃疡或糜烂，特别是在有热邪参与时。

心包经正经和经别都通过膈膜下行至中焦和下焦，心包经与三焦经互为表里。因此，像心包经的许多穴位一样，**[PC8 劳宫](point/PC8.md)** 也对中焦有强大作用，主治火邪扰乱胃气下降，导致口臭、呕吐和吞咽困难。

与**[PC3 曲泽](point/PC3.md)**、**[PC7 大陵](point/PC7.md)** 和**[PC9 中冲](point/PC9.md)** 类似，**[PC8 劳宫](point/PC8.md)** 清泄营血分之热。因此，当血分热邪导致妄行出血性疾病时，如呕血、鼻出血、便血等，此穴皆可使用。其清血热的能力以及其位置，使其适用于治疗掌部皮肤病，如湿疹、癣和脱屑，以及掌心发热和多汗。它还主治手部痹痛和手颤。

在气功练习中，**[PC8 劳宫](point/PC8.md)** 可视为足底**[KID1 涌泉](point/KID1.md)** 的对应穴。通过这两个穴位“呼吸”是常见的做法，意守和培补**[PC8 劳宫](point/PC8.md)** 的气是气功疗法中发气治病的前提。

最后，**[PC8 劳宫](point/PC8.md)** 在《回阳九针歌》中被引用于治疗亡阳证，特点是昏厥、恶寒、四肢厥逆、口唇青紫等。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 清泻心包和心脏热邪的重要穴位：发热，昏迷，昏厥，癫痫，癫狂，惊恐，悲伤，易怒，心神不安，脏躁（见**[HE4 灵道](point/HE4.md)**），喜笑不休。
* 治疗口舌溃疡或糜烂。
* 清泻胃热：口臭，呕吐和吞咽困难。
* 清泻血分热邪：呕血，鼻出血，便血，手部湿疹，手癣和手部脱屑。
