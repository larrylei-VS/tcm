### Middle Crevice ZHŌNGLIÁO
![[BL-33 中髎.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Bladder and Gall Bladder channels

#### Location

Over the third posterior sacral foramen.

#### Location Notes

For guidance on locating the sacral foramen see [Surface Anatomy](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-location-and-needling/surface-anatomy "Surface Anatomy").

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun, or 1.5 to 2 cun through the foramen.

#### Actions

- Regulates the lower jiao and facilitates urination and defecation
- Regulates menstruation and stops leucorrhoea
- Benefits the lumbar region and legs

#### Indications

- Difficult urination and defecation, constipation, diarrhoea containing undigested food, retention of urine, painful urinary dysfunction, abdominal distention.
- Leucorrhoea, irregular menstruation, scanty menstruation, infertility.
- Lumbar pain, pain and cold of the sacrum and coccyx, cold sensation of the buttocks, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limb.
- The five taxations, the seven injuries and the six extremes.

#### Commentary

See [Cìliáo BL-32](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-32).

#### Summary of clinical application

- Difficult urination and defecation, constipation, diarrhoea containing undigested food, retention of urine, painful urinary dysfunction, abdominal distention.
- Leucorrhoea, irregular menstruation, scanty menstruation, infertility.
- Lumbar pain, pain and cold of the sacrum and coccyx, cold sensation of the buttocks, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limb.
- The five taxations, the seven injuries and the six extremes.

#### Combinations

- Difficulty in defecation: Zhōngliáo BL-33, [Shímén REN-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-5), [Chéngshān BL-57](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-57), [Tàichōng LIV-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-3), [Zhōngwǎn REN-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-12), [Tàixī KID-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-3), [Dàzhōng KID-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-4) and [Chéngjīn BL-56](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-56) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Dribbling urination: Zhōngliáo BL-33, [Wěiyáng BL-39](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-39) and [Zhìshì BL-52](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-52) (Supplementing Life).

### **[BL33 中髎](/point/BL33.md)** (ZHŌNGLIÁO)

* **英文名称：** Middle Crevice
* **中文名称：** 中髎
* **国际代码：** BL-33
* **穴位属性：** **[BL33 中髎](/point/BL33.md)** 是膀胱经与胆经的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第三骶后孔处。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 骶孔的定位请参考《Surface Anatomy》（此为原文引用，请根据您的实际参考资料定位）。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至1寸，或通过骶孔透刺1.5至2寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 调理下焦，通利二便
* 调经止带
* 宣利腰腿

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

大小便困难，便秘，大便完谷不化，尿潴留，淋证（小便涩痛），腹胀。
带下，月经不调，月经量少，不孕症。
腰痛，骶骨和尾骨疼痛发冷，臀部冷感，下肢痿证和痹痛。
五劳，七伤，六极（极度虚损）。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

请参阅**[BL32 次髎](/point/BL32.md)**。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 主治大小便困难，便秘，大便完谷不化，尿潴留，淋证（小便涩痛），腹胀。
* 主治带下，月经不调，月经量少，不孕症。
* 主治腰痛，骶骨和尾骨疼痛发冷，臀部冷感，下肢痿证和痹痛。
* 主治五劳，七伤，六极（极度虚损）。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 排便困难：**[BL33 中髎](/point/BL33.md)**，**[REN5 石门](/point/REN5.md)**，**[BL57 承山](/point/BL57.md)**，**[LIV3 太冲](/point/LIV3.md)**，**[REN12 中脘](/point/REN12.md)**，**[KID3 太溪](/point/KID3.md)**，**[KID4 大钟](/point/KID4.md)** 和 **[BL56 承筋](/point/BL56.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 小便淋漓：**[BL33 中髎](/point/BL33.md)**，**[BL39 委阳](/point/BL39.md)** 和 **[BL52 志室](/point/BL52.md)**（《资生经》）。
