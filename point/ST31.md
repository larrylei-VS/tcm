### Thigh Gate BÌGUĀN
![[ST-31 髀关.png|250]]
#### Location

On the upper thigh, in a depression just lateral to the sartorius muscle, at the junction of a vertical line drawn downward from the anterior superior iliac spine, and a horizontal line drawn level with the lower border of the pubic symphysis.

#### Location Notes

The horizontal level may also be taken as approximately the most prominent point of the greater trochanter.

#### Needling

Perpendicular or oblique insertion 1 to 2 cun.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Dispels wind-damp

#### Indications

- Atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limb, hemiplegia, numbness of the legs, cold damp leg qi, pain of the hip joint, pain of the thigh, sciatica, contraction of the thigh muscles, cold painful obstruction of the knees, inability to extend and bend the knee, lumbar pain, jaundice.

#### Commentary

Bìguān ST-31 (Thigh Gate) is located close to the pivotal hip region, the 'gate' of the lower limb. According to the Spiritual Pivot[19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-stomach-channel/references) "Yangming channel is abundant in qi and blood", and points of both arm and foot yangming channel are much used clinically to promote the circulation of qi and blood in the extremities, rendering them ideal for treating numbness, paralysis, stiffness and pain in the four limbs.

Bìguān ST-31 is an important point to regulate the circulation of qi and blood in the leg as a whole, and in the Stomach channel in particular. In this respect it may be viewed as the equivalent point on the lower limb of [Jiānyú LI-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-15) on the upper limb. Its main applications are in the treatment of atrophy disorder, hemiplegia and painful obstruction of the whole leg, or in the treatment of pain from the hip radiating down the Stomach channel. In such cases it is frequently combined in the 'chain and lock' method with [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) and [Jiěxī ST-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-41). A number of classics also specifically recommend Bìguān ST-31 for cold painful obstruction of the knees and for lumbar pain.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Regulates the circulation of qi and blood in the whole leg (yangming channel is rich in qi and blood): atrophy disorder and painful obstruction, hemiplegia, pain of the hip joint, numbness of the legs, thigh pain and contraction of the thigh muscles, cold painful obstruction of the knee, inability to extend and bend the knee.
- Commonly combined with [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) and [Jiěxī ST-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-41) for disorders of the whole leg, e.g. hemiplegia.

#### Combinations

- Numbness of the knee: Bìguān ST-31, [Dúbí ST-35](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-35), and [Yánglíngquán GB-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-34) (Supplementing Life).
- Weakness and paralysis of the legs: Bìguān ST-31, [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36), [Yánglíngquán GB-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-34), [Xuánzhōng GB-39](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-39) and [Jiěxī ST-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-41).

### [ST31 髀关](point/ST31.md) (BÌGUĀN)

* **英文名称：** Thigh Gate
* **中文名称：** 髀关
* **国际代码：** ST-31

#### 定位 (Location)

位于大腿上部，缝匠肌外侧的凹陷中，在髂前上棘向下垂直线与耻骨联合下缘水平线（约大转子最突出点）的交界处。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 水平线也可取大约大转子最突出点。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直或斜刺1至2寸。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 通经活络，止痛
* 祛风湿

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

下肢痿证和痹证，半身不遂，腿部麻木，寒湿腿气，髋关节疼痛，大腿疼痛，坐骨神经痛，大腿肌肉挛缩，膝关节寒痹，膝关节屈伸不利，腰痛，黄疸。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST31 髀关](point/ST31.md) (Thigh Gate) 位于靠近关键的髋部区域，是下肢的“关口”。根据《灵枢》记载：“阳明经多气多血”，手足阳明经的穴位在临床上广泛用于促进四肢气血循环，使其成为治疗四肢麻木、瘫痪、僵硬和疼痛的理想选择。

[ST31 髀关](point/ST31.md) 是调理整个腿部气血循环，特别是胃经气血的重要穴位。在这方面，它可视为上肢[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md) 在下肢的对应穴。其主要应用于治疗整个腿部的痿证、半身不遂和痹证，或治疗从髋部沿胃经向下放射的疼痛。在这种情况下，它常与[ST36 足三里](point/ST36.md) 和[ST41 解溪](point/ST41.md) 联合使用，采用“链锁法”。许多经典著作也特别推荐[ST31 髀关](point/ST31.md) 治疗膝关节寒痹和腰痛。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 调理整个腿部气血循环（阳明经气血充盛）：痿证和痹证，半身不遂，髋关节疼痛，腿部麻木，大腿疼痛和肌肉挛缩，膝关节寒痹，膝关节屈伸不利。
* 常与[ST36 足三里](point/ST36.md) 和[ST41 解溪](point/ST41.md) 联合使用，治疗整个腿部的疾病，如半身不遂。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 膝部麻木：[ST31 髀关](point/ST31.md)，[ST35 犊鼻](point/ST35.md) 和[GB34 阳陵泉](point/GB34.md)（《资生经》）。
* 腿部无力麻痹：[ST31 髀关](point/ST31.md)，[ST36 足三里](point/ST36.md)，[GB34 阳陵泉](point/GB34.md)，[GB39 悬钟](point/GB39.md) 和[ST41 解溪](point/ST41.md)。
