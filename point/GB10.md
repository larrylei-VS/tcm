### Floating White FÚBÁI
![[GB-10 浮白.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder and Bladder channels

#### Location

Posterior to the ear, along a curved line drawn from [Tiānchōng GB-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-9) to [Wángǔ GB-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-12) running within the hairline and more or less parallel to the line of the rim of the ear, in a depression about one third of the distance between [Tiānchōng GB-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-9) and [Wángǔ GB-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-12).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5-1.5 cun. See note [Hànyàn GB-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-4).

#### Actions

- Clears the head and benefits the neck region
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Headache, heaviness of the head, chills and fever, toothache, deafness, tinnitus.
- Stiffness and pain of the neck, goitre, swelling and pain of the neck, throat painful obstruction.
- Fullness of the chest with dyspnoea, chest pain, cough with expectoration of phlegm and foam.
- Pain of the shoulder and arm, inability to raise the arm, flaccidity of the legs with inability to walk.

#### Commentary

The points Fúbái GB-10, [Tóuqiàoyīn GB-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-11) and to a certain extent [Tiānchōng GB-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-9), have certain comparable indications and these are not dissimilar to the indications for the points of the [Window of Heaven](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/about-point-categories/points-of-the-window-of-heaven "Window of Heaven"). All are indicated for goitre, and Fúbái GB-10additionally for stiffness, swelling and pain of the neck. Both Fúbái GB-10 (headache, toothache, deafness and tinnitus) and [Tóuqiàoyīn GB-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-11) (headache and eye, ear, tongue and mouth disorders) are indicated for disorders of the head and sense organs and for rebellion of Lung qi characterised by cough. Both also treat disorders of the lower body, Fúbái GB-10 for flaccidity of the legs with inability to walk, and [Tóuqiàoyīn GB-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-11) for contraction of the sinews of the four limbs, bone consumption, and agitation and heat of the hands and feet. This latter application reflects the principle expounded in the Yellow Emperor's Inner Classic[1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-gall-bladder-channel/references) "When the disease is below select [points] from above".

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Headache, heaviness of the head, chills and fever, toothache, deafness, tinnitus.
- Stiffness and pain of the neck, goitre, swelling and pain of the neck, throat painful obstruction.
- Fullness of the chest with dyspnoea, chest pain, cough with expectoration of phlegm and foam.
- Pain of the shoulder and arm, inability to raise the arm, flaccidity of the legs with inability to walk.

#### Combinations

- Toothache and tooth decay: Fúbái GB-10 and [Wángǔ GB-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-12) (Systematic Classic).

### 1. [GB10 浮白](/point/GB10.md)

#### 1.1. Floating White 浮白

- 胆经与膀胱经的交会穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于耳后，沿从[GB9 天冲](/point/GB9.md)到[GB12 完骨](/point/GB12.md)的发际内弧线，弧线大致平行于耳廓边缘，在[GB9 天冲](/point/GB9.md)与[GB12 完骨](/point/GB12.md)之间约三分之一处的凹陷中。

#### 1.3. 针刺方法

横刺，深0.5至1.5寸。参见[GB4 颔厌](/point/GB4.md)的针刺说明。

#### 1.4. 作用

- 清头益颈
- 活络止痛

#### 1.5. 主治

- 头痛、头重、寒热、牙痛、耳聾、耳鳴。
- 颈項僵痛、瘿病、頸腫痛、咽痹。
- 胸滿氣喘、胸痛、咳嗽吐痰沫。
- 肩臂痛、不能舉臂、腿軟不能行。

#### 1.6. 论述

[GB10 浮白](/point/GB10.md)、[GB11 头窍阴](/point/GB11.md)以及一定程度上[GB9 天冲](/point/GB9.md)具有某些相似的适应症，这些适应症与天窗穴的适应症并无太大差异。它们均适用于瘿病，[GB10 浮白](/point/GB10.md)还额外适用于颈项僵硬、肿胀和疼痛。[GB10 浮白](/point/GB10.md)（头痛、牙痛、耳聾和耳鳴）和[GB11 头窍阴](/point/GB11.md)（头痛及眼、耳、舌、口疾患）均适用于头部及感官疾患，以及肺气上逆引起的咳嗽。两者还治疗下部疾患，[GB10 浮白](/point/GB10.md)适用于腿软不能行，[GB11 头窍阴](/point/GB11.md)适用于四肢筋挛、骨痨、手足烦热。这种下部疾患的治疗反映了《黄帝内经》中“病在下，取之上”的原则。

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 使用较少。
- 适用于头痛、头重、寒热、牙痛、耳聾、耳鳴。
- 适用于颈項僵痛、瘿病、頸腫痛、咽痹。
- 适用于胸滿氣喘、胸痛、咳嗽吐痰沫。
- 适用于肩臂痛、不能舉臂、腿軟不能行。

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 牙痛及龋齿：[GB10 浮白](/point/GB10.md) + [GB12 完骨](/point/GB12.md)（《系统经典》）。
