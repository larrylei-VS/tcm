### Lower Great Void XIÀJÙXŪ
![[ST-39 下巨虚.png|250]]
- Lower He-Sea point of the Small Intestine
- Point of the Sea of Blood

#### Location

On the lower leg, 3 cun inferior to [Shàngjùxū ST-37](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-37), one finger-breadth lateral to the anterior crest of the tibia.

#### Location Notes

Divide the distance between the tibiofemoral joint line (level with the popliteal crease) and the prominence of the lateral malleolus into two equal parts. The midpoint is [Tiáokǒu ST-38](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-38); Xiàjùxū ST-39 is located 1 cun below [Tiáokǒu ST-38](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-38).

#### Needling

Perpendicular or oblique insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Moves Small Intestine qi and transforms stagnation
- Regulates and harmonises the intestines and clears damp-heat
- Activates the channel and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Lower abdominal pain, lumbar pain that radiates to the testicles.
- Diarrhoea, diarrhoea containing undigested food, dysenteric disorder, pus and blood in the stool, insufficiency of Small Intestine qi, dark urine.
- Breast abscess, manic raving, sudden fright, drooling, dry lips, throat painful obstruction, absence of sweating, no pleasure in eating, pallor.
- Painful obstruction and atrophy disorder of the lower limb, hemiplegia of the leg, atrophy disorder of the foot, pain of the heel, extreme sensation of cold and heat in the shoulder, heat sensation in the area between the ring and little fingers, burning sensation in front of the ears.

#### Commentary

The Yellow Emperor's Inner Classic stated "The Large and Small intestines fall under the influence of the Stomach"[37](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-stomach-channel/references) and classified Xiàjùxū ST-39 as the lower he-sea point of the Small Intestine. The three yang channels of the arm (Large Intestine, Small Intestine and Sanjiao) are unique in that whilst the channels traverse the upper jiao, their respective fu belong to the lower jiao. Thus although the Spiritual Pivot states that the "He-sea points treat disorders of the internal fu", the he-sea points of these three channels ([Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11), [Xiǎohǎi SI-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-8), and [Tiānjǐng SJ-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-10) respectively) have relatively little action on their related fu. The Large and Small Intestines and the Sanjiao, however, each have a lower he-sea point ([Shàngjùxū ST-37](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-37), Xiàjùxū ST-39 and [Wěiyáng BL-39](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-39)) which predominantly treat disorders of their respective fu.

Xiàjùxū ST-39 may be used in two principal patterns of the Small Intestine, namely Small Intestine qi pain and Small Intestine deficient and cold. The former is characterised by severe pain of the lower abdomen which radiates to the waist and lumbar region and down to the testicles. The latter is characterised by (dull) lower abdominal pain (which is improved with warmth and pressure) and chronic diarrhoea or dysenteric disorder etc. Xiàjùxū ST-39 is further able to treat diarrhoea and dysenteric disorder due to damp heat which give rise to pus and blood in the stool.

The Essential Questions[38](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-stomach-channel/references) includes Xiàjùxū ST-39 among the eight points to clear heat from the Stomach, emphasising that as well as being the he-sea point of the Small Intestine, this point lies on the Stomach channel and is indicated for heat in the channel giving rise to disorders such as breast abscess, dry lips and throat painful obstruction. Unusually, Xiàjùxū ST-39 is also clearly ascribed some ability to regulate the Small Intestine channel, reflected in such indications as extreme sensation of cold and heat in the shoulder, heat sensation in the area between the ring and little fingers, and burning sensation in front of the ears.

For a discussion of Xiàjùxū ST-39 as a point of the 'sea of blood' refer to the commentary on [Shàngjùxū ST-37](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-37).

Finally, Xiàjùxū ST-39 belongs to yangming channel which is abundant in qi and blood, and is similar to points such as [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36) and [Shàngjùxū ST-37](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-37) in its ability to treat disorders of the lower limb. It is indicated for painful obstruction and atrophy disorder of the lower limb and foot, hemiplegia and heel pain.

#### Summary of clinical application

- For Small Intestine qi pain and Small Intestine deficiency cold: lower abdominal pain, lumbar pain that radiates to the testicles, diarrhoea, dysenteric disorder, pus and blood in the stool, insufficiency of Small Intestine qi.
- For painful obstruction, atrophy disorder, hemiplegia of the lower leg, atrophy disorder of the foot, heel pain.

#### Combinations

- Diarrhoea and dysenteric disorder with pus and blood: Xiàjùxū ST-39, [Yōumén KID-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-21) and [Tàibái SP-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-3) (Supplementing Life).
- Manic raving: Xiàjùxū ST-39 and [Qiūxū GB-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-40) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Fullness of the chest and lateral costal region radiating to the abdomen: Xiàjùxū ST-39, [Qiūxū GB-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-40), [Xiáxī GB-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-43) and [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23) (Great Compendium).
- Breast abscess: Xiàjùxū ST-39, [Zúsānlǐ ST-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-36), [Xiáxī GB-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-43), [Yújì LU-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-10), [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40), [Zúlínqì GB-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-41) and [Shàozé SI-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-1) (Great Compendium).

### [ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md) (XIÀJÙXŪ)

* **英文名称：** Lower Great Void
* **中文名称：** 下巨虚
* **国际代码：** ST-39
* **穴位属性：** [ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md) 是小肠经的下合穴，血海穴。

#### 定位 (Location)

位于小腿部，距[ST37 上巨虚](point/ST37.md) 下方3寸，胫骨前嵴旁开一横指。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 将胫股关节线（与腘横纹平齐）与外踝尖之间的距离分成两等份。中点为[ST38 条口](point/ST38.md)；[ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md) 位于[ST38 条口](point/ST38.md) 下方1寸。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直或斜刺1至1.5寸。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 调理小肠气机，化积消滞
* 调理肠道，清利湿热，止泻痢
* 通经活络，止痛

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

小腹疼痛，腰痛放射至睾丸。
腹泻，泻下完谷不化，痢疾，大便脓血，小肠气虚，小便色深。
乳痈，狂言谵语，突然惊恐，流涎，口唇干燥，咽喉肿痛，无汗，食欲不振，面色苍白。
下肢痹证和痿证，腿部偏瘫，足部痿证，足跟痛，肩部寒热感剧烈，无名指和小指之间区域灼热感，耳前灼热感。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

《黄帝内经》指出“大小肠皆属于胃”，并将[ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md) 归类为小肠的下合穴。手臂的三条阳经（大肠经、小肠经和三焦经）独特之处在于，虽然经脉循行上焦，但其各自的腑却属于下焦。因此，尽管《灵枢》记载“合穴治内腑”，但这些经脉的合穴（[LI11 曲池](point/LI11.md)、[SI8 小海](point/SI8.md) 和[SJ10 天井](point/SJ10.md)）对其各自的腑作用相对较小。然而，大肠、小肠和三焦各自都有一个下合穴（[ST37 上巨虚](point/ST37.md)、[ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md) 和[BL39 委阳](point/BL39.md)），主要治疗其各自腑的疾病。

[ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md) 可用于两种主要的小肠病证：小肠气痛和小肠虚寒。前者特点是小腹剧烈疼痛，放射至腰部和睾丸。后者特点是（隐痛性）小腹痛（得温按则缓解）和慢性腹泻或痢疾等。[ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md) 还能治疗因湿热引起的腹泻和痢疾，表现为大便脓血。

《素问·热论》将[ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md) 列为清胃热的八个穴位之一，强调其作为小肠下合穴，也位于胃经上，主治经络热证引起的乳痈、口唇干燥和咽喉肿痛等疾病。不同寻常的是，[ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md) 亦明确具有调理小肠经脉的能力，体现在其主治肩部寒热感剧烈、无名指和小指之间区域灼热感以及耳前灼热感。

关于[ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md) 作为“血海”穴的讨论，请参阅[ST37 上巨虚](point/ST37.md) 的阐释。

最后，[ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md) 属阳明经，多气多血，其治疗下肢疾病的能力与[ST36 足三里](point/ST36.md) 和[ST37 上巨虚](point/ST37.md) 相似。它主治下肢和足部痹证和痿证、偏瘫以及足跟痛。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 治疗小肠气痛和小肠虚寒：小腹疼痛，腰痛放射至睾丸，腹泻，痢疾，大便脓血，小肠气虚。
* 治疗下肢痹证、痿证、腿部偏瘫、足部痿证、足跟痛。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 腹泻痢疾伴脓血：[ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md)，[KID21 幽门](point/KID21.md) 和[SP3 太白](point/SP3.md)（《资生经》）。
* 狂言谵语：[ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md) 和[GB40 丘墟](point/GB40.md)（《千金方》）。
* 胸胁胀满放射至腹部：[ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md)，[GB40 丘墟](point/GB40.md)，[GB43 侠溪](point/GB43.md) 和[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)（《针灸大成》）。
* 乳痈：[ST39 下巨虚](point/ST39.md)，[ST36 足三里](point/ST36.md)，[GB43 侠溪](point/GB43.md)，[LU10 鱼际](point/LU10.md)，[BL40 委中](point/BL40.md)，[GB41 足临泣](point/GB41.md) 和[SI1 少泽](point/SI1.md)（《针灸大成》）。
