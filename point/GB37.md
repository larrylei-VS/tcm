### Bright Light GUĀNGMÍNG
![[GB-37 光明.png|250]]
- Luo-Connecting point of the Gall Bladder channel

#### Location

On the lateral aspect of the lower leg, 5 cun superior to the prominence of the lateral malleolus, at the anterior border of the fibula.

#### Location Notes

i. The distance between the tip of the lateral malleolus and the popliteal crease is 16 cun; locate Guāngmíng GB-37 just distal to the junction of the upper two thirds and lower third of this line; ii. Above the region of [Xuánzhōng GB-39](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-39), the fibula is not easily palpable because it is covered by the peroneus brevis muscle; therefore feel for the anterior border of the fibula above the ankle joint where it is easily palpable and extend a line towards the head of the fibula.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Benefits the eyes
- Dispels wind-damp, activates the channel and
- alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Eye pain, night blindness, itching of the eyes, long sightedness, short sightedness.
- One-sided headache, grinding of the teeth, distention and pain of the breast, febrile disease with absence of sweating, sudden mania.
- Knee pain, pain of the lower legs with inability to stand for long, atrophy disorder of the legs with difficulty in standing after sitting, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limb with numbness, numbness of the body, heat and pain of the lower leg.

#### Commentary

The Guide to the Classics of Acupuncture states "the luo-connecting points are located between two channels ... if they are punctured, symptoms of the interiorly-exteriorly related channels can be treated"[15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-gall-bladder-channel/references). The Liver 'opens' into the eyes, and the Liver, Gall Bladder, and Gall Bladder divergent channels connect with the eye. Many eye diseases derive from Liver pathology, and as its name 'Bright Light' suggests, Guāngmíng GB-37, the luo-connecting point of the Gall Bladder channel, is the principal distal point on the channel for the treatment of a wide range of eye disorders, including pain, redness, itching, long and short sightedness and night blindness.

Its second function of activating the channel and alleviating pain is important in two ways. Firstly, by dispelling wind-damp and regulating the circulation of qi in the lower limb, it is effective in treating a wide range of disorders of the lower leg including knee pain, pain of the lower legs with inability to stand for long, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of lower limb with numbness and heat, and pain of the lower leg. Secondly, the Gall Bladder primary channel passes through the chest whilst its sinew channel links with the breast, and Guāngmíng GB-37 is indicated for distention and pain of the breast due to stagnation of Liver qi.

Finally the Great Compendium of Acupuncture and Moxibustion gives specific indications for excess and deficiency of the luo-connecting points. In the case of Guāngmíng GB-37, these are heat sensation of the lower leg, knee pain, numbness of the body and propensity to grind the teeth (excess); atrophy disorder of the legs with difficulty in standing after sitting (deficiency).

#### Summary of clinical application

- For a wide range of eye disorders, including pain, redness, itching, long and short sightedness and night blindness.
- For disorders of the lower leg: knee pain, pain of the lower legs with inability to stand for long, atrophy disorder, painful obstruction of lower limb with numbness and heat.

#### Combinations

- Pain and itching of the eyes: reduce Guāngmíng GB-37 and [Dìwǔhùi GB-42](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-42) (Ode to Elucidate Mysteries).
- When [Jīngmíng BL-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-1) is ineffective in treating diseases of the eye, combine it with [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) and Guāngmíng GB-37 (Ode of Xi-hong).
- Grinding of the teeth: Guāngmíng GB-37 and [Zúlínqì GB-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-41) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Headache, swollen cheeks, difficult defecation, painful urinary dysfunction, susceptibility to anger with a blue-green (qing) colour, painful shan disorder and pain of the lateral costal region, hypogastrium and uterus: Guāngmíng GB-37 and [Tàichōng LIV-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-3) (Golden Mirror).

### 1. [GB37 光明](point/GB37.md)

#### 1.1. Bright Light 光明

- 胆经的络穴  

#### 1.2. 定位

位于小腿外侧面，外踝突出上方5寸，腓骨前缘凹陷处。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

i. 外踝尖至腘横纹的距离为16寸，[GB37 光明](point/GB37.md)位于此连线上三分之二与三分之一交界处稍下方；  
ii. 在[GB39 悬钟](point/GB39.md)上方区域，腓骨不易触及，因被腓骨短肌覆盖；因此，从踝关节上方易触及的腓骨前缘开始，向上延伸至腓骨头方向定位。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直刺入，深1至1.5寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 益目  
- 祛风湿，活络止痛  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 目痛、夜盲、目痒、远视、近视。  
- 偏头痛、咬牙、乳房胀痛、热病无汗、突发癫狂。  
- 膝痛、小腿痛不能久立、腿萎症难起、腿萎症及痛痹伴麻木、身体麻木、小腿热痛。  

#### 1.7. 论述

《针灸经义》称：“络穴位于两经之间……若刺之，可治表里相关经脉之症。”肝开窍于目，肝、胆及胆经别均与目相连。许多眼疾源于肝病理，如其名“光明”所示，[GB37 光明](point/GB37.md)作为胆经络穴，是治疗多种眼疾（如疼痛、发红、瘙痒、远近视及夜盲）的胆经主要远端穴位。  

其活络止痛的第二功能有两个重要方面。首先，通过祛风湿及调理下肢气血循环，[GB37 光明](point/GB37.md)有效治疗多种小腿疾患，包括膝痛、小腿痛不能久立、下肢萎症及痛痹伴麻木、小腿热痛。其次，胆经原经经过胸部，其筋脉与乳房相连，[GB37 光明](point/GB37.md)适用于肝气郁滞引起的乳房胀痛。  

《针灸大成》为络穴的虚实提供了具体适应症。对[GB37 光明](point/GB37.md)而言，实证包括小腿热感、膝痛、身体麻木及咬牙倾向；虚证为腿萎症坐后难起。  

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 治疗多种眼疾，包括疼痛、发红、瘙痒、远近视及夜盲。  
- 治疗小腿疾患：膝痛、小腿痛不能久立、萎症、下肢痛痹伴麻木及热感。  

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 目痛及痒：泻[GB37 光明](point/GB37.md) + [GB42 地五会](point/GB42.md)（《阐秘颂》）。  
- [BL1 睛明](point/BL1.md)治疗眼疾无效时，配[LI4 合谷](point/LI4.md) + [GB37 光明](point/GB37.md)（《西弘赋》）。  
- 咬牙：[GB37 光明](point/GB37.md) + [GB41 足临泣](point/GB41.md)（《千金方》）。  
- 头痛、颊肿、排便难、尿痛、易怒伴青色、疝痛、胁痛、脐下及子宫痛：[GB37 光明](point/GB37.md) + [LIV3 太冲](point/LIV3.md)（《金镜》）。
