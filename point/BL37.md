### Gate of Abundance YĪNMÉN
![[BL-37 殷门.png|250]]
#### Location

On the back of the thigh, in the depression between the hamstring muscles, 6 cun distal to [Chéngfú BL-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-36), and 8 cun proximal to [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40), on the line connecting [Chéngfú BL-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-36) and [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40).

#### Location Notes

i. Locate two handbreadths distal to [Chéngfú BL-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-36); ii. Find the midpoint between [Chéngfú BL-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-36) and [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) and locate Yīnmén BL-37 in the depression 1 cun proximal to this.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 2 cun.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Benefits the lumbar spine

#### Indications

- Pain and rigidity of the lumbar spine, sciatica, painful obstruction and atrophy disorder of the lower limb, pain of the thigh, swelling of the lateral thigh.
- Watery diarrhoea.

#### Commentary

The point Yīnmén BL-37 is frequently found to be tender on palpation in patients with sciatica, and is therefore commonly needled in the treatment of this condition. It is especially used when more proximal points such as [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30) or [Zhìbiān BL-54](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-54) fail to elicit propagated sensation extending down the leg.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Commonly needled, especially when tender, in sciatica with pain radiating down the Bladder channel from the buttock.
- Also indicated for pain and rigidity of the lumbar spine, painful obstruction and atrophy disorder of the lower limb.

#### Combinations

- Lumbar pain with inability to bend and extend: Yīnmén BL-37, [Wěiyáng BL-39](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-39), [Tàibái SP-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-3), [Yīnlíngquán SP-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-9) and [Xíngjiān LIV-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-2) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Sciatica: Yīnmén BL-37, [Dàchángshū BL-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-25), [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30), [Chéngshān BL-57](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-57), [Fēiyáng BL-58](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-58) and [Sānyīnjiāo SP-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-6).

### **[BL37 殷门](point/BL37.md)** (YĪNMÉN)

* **英文名称：** Gate of Abundance
* **中文名称：** 殷门
* **国际代码：** BL-37

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于大腿后部，在**[BL36 承扶](point/BL36.md)** 和**[BL40 委中](point/BL40.md)** 的连线上，距**[BL36 承扶](point/BL36.md)** 下方6寸，距**[BL40 委中](point/BL40.md)** 上方8寸，股后肌群之间的凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 在**[BL36 承扶](point/BL36.md)** 下方两横掌处定位；
    * 也可定位在**[BL36 承扶](point/BL36.md)** 和**[BL40 委中](point/BL40.md)** 之间的中点上方1寸的凹陷中。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺1至2寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 通经活络，止痛
* 宣利腰椎

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

腰椎疼痛僵硬，坐骨神经痛，下肢痹证和痿证，大腿疼痛，大腿外侧肿胀。
水泻。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL37 殷门](point/BL37.md)** 在坐骨神经痛患者中常发现有压痛，因此在治疗此症时常针刺此穴。当**[GB30 环跳](point/GB30.md)** 或**[BL54 秩边](point/BL54.md)** 等近端穴位未能引出向下肢放射的传导感时，此穴尤其常用。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 常用于坐骨神经痛，尤其当触诊有压痛，疼痛沿膀胱经从臀部向下肢放射时。
* 也主治腰椎疼痛僵硬，下肢痹证和痿证。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 腰痛不能屈伸：**[BL37 殷门](point/BL37.md)**，**[BL39 委阳](point/BL39.md)**，**[SP3 太白](point/SP3.md)**，**[SP9 阴陵泉](point/SP9.md)** 和**[LIV2 行间](point/LIV2.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 坐骨神经痛：**[BL37 殷门](point/BL37.md)**，**[BL25 大肠俞](point/BL25.md)**，**[GB30 环跳](point/GB30.md)**，**[BL57 承山](point/BL57.md)**，**[BL58 飞扬](point/BL58.md)** 和**[SP6 三阴交](point/SP6.md)**。
