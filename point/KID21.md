### Hidden Gate YŌUMÉN
![[KID-21 幽门.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Kidney channel with the Penetrating vessel

#### Location

On the upper abdomen, 6 cun above the umbilicus, 0.5 cun lateral to the midline ([Jùguè REN-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-14)).

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** deep needling, especially in thin subjects, will puncture the liver on the right side and the peritoneum on the left.

#### Actions

- Fortifies the Spleen, harmonises the Stomach, and lowers rebellion
- Spreads Liver qi, benefits the chest and breasts and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Abdominal pain, lower abdominal pain, abdominal urgency, vomiting, nausea and counterflow qi, retching, vomiting of foamy (watery) saliva, much spittle, nausea and vomiting of pregnancy, fullness with no pleasure in eating, difficult ingestion, focal distention below the Heart, fullness and agitation below the Heart, diarrhoea, blood in the stools, dysenteric disorder.
- Chest pain, distention of the lateral costal region, pain of the chest and lateral costal region radiating to the back, cough, coughing blood.
- Breast milk does not flow, breast abscess.
- Heart pain in women, poor memory, redness of the eyes originating at the inner canthus.

#### Commentary

According the Classic of Difficulties[28](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-kidney-channel/references) "When the Penetrating vessel is diseased there is counterflow qi and abdominal urgency". Like many of the Kidney channel points on the abdomen, Yōumén KID-21 (the final point of the Penetrating vessel on the Kidney channel) is indicated for counterflow qi manifesting as nausea, vomiting, retching and cough. The term abdominal urgency refers to a sensation of acute cramping pain, usually associated with dysenteric disorder.

Like the majority of the Penetrating vessel points on the Kidney channel, Yōumén KID-21 is indicated for redness of the eyes originating at the inner canthus. Whilst there is no simple explanation of this symptom, it is worth noting that although some descriptions of the Penetrating vessel suggest that it terminate at the lips, illustrations and other accounts of its pathway usually show it ending at the inner portion of the eyes.

#### Summary of clinical application

- The _Classic of Difficulties_ says "When the Penetrating Vessel is diseased there is counterflow qi and abdominal urgency". This is the terminal point of the Penetrating Vessel on the Kidney channel.
- Descends rebellious Lung and Stomach qi: abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting, vomiting of foamy (watery) saliva, nausea and vomiting of pregnancy, retching, fullness with no desire to eat, difficult ingestion, cough.
- Treats disorders of the chest, lateral costal region and breasts.

#### Combinations

- Agitation of the Heart with vomiting: Yōumén KID-21 and [Yùtáng REN-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-18) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Poor memory: Yōumén KID-21, [Shéndào DU-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-11), [Lièquē LU-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-7) and [Gāohuāngshū BL-43](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-43) (Supplementing Life).
- No pleasure in eating: Yōumén KID-21, [Dìjī SP-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-8), [Yīnlíngquán SP-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-9), [Shuǐfēn REN-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-9) and [Xiǎochángshū BL-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-27) (Supplementing Life).
- Vomiting: Yōumén KID-21, [Shāngqiū SP-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-5) and [Zútōnggǔ BL-66](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-66) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[KID21 幽门](point/KID21.md)** (Yōumén)

* **英文名称：** Hidden Gate
* **中文名称：** 幽门
* **国际代码：** KID-21
* **穴位属性：** **[KID21 幽门](point/KID21.md)** 是肾经与冲脉的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于上腹部，脐上6寸，距中线旁开0.5寸（**[REN14 巨阙](point/REN14.md)** 旁开0.5寸）。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 深刺，特别是瘦弱患者，右侧会刺穿肝脏，左侧会刺穿腹膜。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 健脾和胃，降逆
* 疏肝理气，宣利胸乳，止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

腹痛，小腹疼痛，腹部急迫感，呕吐，恶心伴气逆，干呕，呕吐泡沫（清水）唾液，多涎，妊娠恶心呕吐，食欲不振伴胀满，吞咽困难，心下痞块，心下胀满烦躁，腹泻，便血，痢疾。
胸痛，胁肋部胀满，胸胁疼痛放射至背部，咳嗽，咳血。
乳汁不通，乳痈。
女性心痛，记忆力差，内眼角红赤。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

根据《难经》记载：“冲脉病者，苦气逆而里急”。像腹部许多肾经穴位一样，**[KID21 幽门](point/KID21.md)**（肾经上冲脉的终点穴）主治气逆，表现为恶心、呕吐、干呕和咳嗽。“腹部急迫感”指急性绞痛，通常与痢疾相关。

像肾经上大多数冲脉穴位一样，**[KID21 幽门](point/KID21.md)** 主治内眼角红赤。虽然对此症状没有简单的解释，但值得注意的是，尽管有些对冲脉的描述表明它终止于口唇，但其经络图和其它记载通常显示它止于内眼角。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 《难经》记载：“冲脉病者，苦气逆而里急”。这是冲脉在肾经上的终点穴。
* 肃降肺胃之逆气：腹痛，恶心，呕吐，呕吐泡沫（清水）唾液，妊娠恶心呕吐，干呕，食欲不振伴胀满，吞咽困难，咳嗽。
* 治疗胸部、胁肋部和乳房的疾病。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 心烦伴呕吐：**[KID21 幽门](point/KID21.md)** 和 **[REN18 玉堂](point/REN18.md)**（《百症赋》）。
* 记忆力差：**[KID21 幽门](point/KID21.md)**，**[DU11 神道](point/DU11.md)**，**[LU7 列缺](point/LU7.md)** 和 **[BL43 膏肓俞](point/BL43.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 食欲不振：**[KID21 幽门](point/KID21.md)**，**[SP8 地机](point/SP8.md)**，**[SP9 阴陵泉](point/SP9.md)**，**[REN9 水分](point/REN9.md)** 和 **[BL27 小肠俞](point/BL27.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 呕吐：**[KID21 幽门](point/KID21.md)**，**[SP5 商丘](point/SP5.md)** 和 **[BL66 足通谷](point/BL66.md)**（《千金方》）。
