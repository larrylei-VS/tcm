### Purple Palace ZǏGŌNG
![[REN-19 紫宫.png|250]]
#### Location

On the midline of the sternum, level with the junction of the second intercostal space and the sternum.

#### Location Notes

Locate the costal cartilage of the second rib which is level with the sternal angle, then locate the second intercostal space below it.

#### Needling

Transverse insertion directed superiorly or inferiorly along the channel, 0.5 to 1 cun.

#### Actions

- Unbinds the chest
- Regulates and descends qi

#### Indications

- Pain of the chest, pain of the sternum, cough, coughing blood, saliva like white glue.
- Vomiting with uprising qi, difficult ingestion, agitation, painful obstruction, bone pain.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Pain of the chest, pain of the sternum, cough, coughing blood, saliva like white glue.
- Vomiting with uprising qi, difficult ingestion, agitation, painful obstruction, bone pain.

#### Combinations

- Cough with rebellious qi and agitation: Zǐgōng REN-19, [Yùtáng REN-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-18) and [Tàixī KID-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-3) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Difficult ingestion: Zǐgōng REN-19, [Zhōngtíng REN-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-16) and [Dǎnshū BL-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-19) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Bone pain: Zǐgōng REN-19, [Yùtáng REN-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-18) and [Géshū BL-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-17) (Supplementing Life).


### 1. [REN19 紫宫](point/REN19.md)

#### 1.1. Purple Palace 紫宮

#### 1.2. 定位

位于胸骨中线，第二肋间隙与胸骨交界处。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

定位第二肋软骨（与胸骨角平齐），向下找到第二肋间隙。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

沿经脉向上或向下平刺，深0.5至1寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 宽胸  
- 调气降逆  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 胸痛、胸骨痛、咳嗽、咳血、涎如白胶。  
- 呕吐伴气上逆、吞咽难、心烦、痹痛、骨痛。  

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 胸痛、胸骨痛、咳嗽、咳血、涎如白胶。  
- 呕吐伴气上逆、吞咽难、心烦、痹痛、骨痛。  

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 咳嗽伴气逆及烦躁：[REN19 紫宫](point/REN19.md) + [REN18 玉堂](point/REN18.md) + [KID3 太溪](point/KID3.md)（《千金方》）。  
- 吞咽难：[REN19 紫宫](point/REN19.md) + [REN16 中庭](point/REN16.md) + [BL19 胆俞](point/BL19.md)（《千金方》）。  
- 骨痛：[REN19 紫宫](point/REN19.md) + [REN18 玉堂](point/REN18.md) + [BL17 膈俞](point/BL17.md)（《补命》）。
