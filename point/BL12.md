### Wind Gate FĒNGMÉN
![[BL-12 风门.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Bladder channel with the Governing vessel

#### Location

1.5 cun lateral to the lower border of the spinous process of the second thoracic vertebra (T2).

#### Location Notes

Locate at the visible highest point of the paraspinal muscles.

#### Needling

Oblique insertion towards the spine, 0.5 to 1 cun, or transverse-oblique insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.  
**Caution:** perpendicular needling or oblique needling away from the spine carries a substantial risk of causing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Expels wind and releases the exterior
- Strengthens the defensive qi and firms the exterior
- Disseminates and descends Lung qi
- Benefits the nose

#### Indications

- Attack by wind with fever, cold shivering, dislike of wind and cold, headache, injury by cold with rigidity of the head and neck, flaccidity of the interstices with frequent coughing and clear watery nasal discharge, flaccidity of the interstices with susceptibility to catching wind-cold, desire to keep the eyes closed, wind dizziness, visual dizziness.
- Cough, cough with chest and back pain, coughing blood, dyspnoea, heat in the chest, wind-taxation coughing, wind-exhaustion vomiting, vomiting.
- Copious nasal discharge, nasal congestion, rhinitis, sneezing, nosebleed.
- Lumbar pain, stiff neck, urticaria, carbuncles of the back, shoulder pain, restless sleep.

#### Commentary

As its name 'Wind Gate' implies, Fēngmén BL-12 is an important point to expel wind and release pathogenic factors from the exterior portion of the body. This action may be explained by three principal factors: i. Fēngmén BL-12 belongs to taiyang channel, the most exterior of the six channels, and therefore most commonly the first to be attacked by exterior pathogenic factors; ii. Fēngmén BL-12 is located on the upper back, over the upper portion of the Lung which is known as the 'delicate zang' due to its predisposition to be easily injured by exterior pathogens; iii. Fēngmén BL-12 is a meeting point of the Bladder channel with the Governing vessel which dominates all the yang channels of the body and hence has a close relationship to the exterior (yang) portion of the body.

When exterior pathogenic wind attacks via the skin, nose or mouth, it may i. obstruct the defensive qi and impair its warming function, giving rise to chills, aversion to wind and cold and headache, whilst fever which accompanies chills is a sign of the struggle between the defensive qi and the pathogen; ii. impair the disseminating and descending function of the Lung, resulting in copious nasal discharge and cough; iii. obstruct taiyang channel giving rise to rigidity and pain of the head and neck. Fēngmén BL-12 may be treated by needling or cupping to release the exterior in all cases of attack by wind-cold, wind-heat or wind-dryness which bind the superficial portion of the body and give rise to such excess exterior pathology. In the case of wind-cold, moxibustion may be used. Fēngmén BL-12 is also indicated for patterns of exterior deficiency where attack by wind gives rise to disharmony of nutritive qi and defensive qi, characterised by fever and chills unrelieved by sweating, aversion to wind and a weak floating pulse.

The ability of Fēngmén BL-12 to firm the exterior and strengthen the defensive qi, thus helping the body to resist attack by exterior pathogenic factors, is discussed in various classics. The Song of the Jade Dragon recommends it for "flaccidity of the interstices with frequent coughing and clear watery nasal discharge", whilst Methods of Acupuncture and Moxibustion from the Golden Mirror of Medicine recommends it for "flaccidity of the interstices with susceptibility to catching wind-cold". In other words, as well as releasing the exterior and expelling wind, Fēngmén BL-12 may be treated by needling or moxibustion to tonify the defensive qi in patients with susceptibility to catching frequent colds.

The nose is the gateway of the Lung. According to the Spiritual Pivot[7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references) "The qi of the Lung opens into the nose; when the Lung is in harmony the nose is able to distinguish the fragrant from the foul". Methods of Acupuncture and Moxibustion from the Golden Mirror of Medicine recommends Fēngmén BL-12 for "nosebleed and all kinds of nose disorders". Nasal blockage and discharge commonly result from Lung and defensive qi deficiency with concomitant retention of pathogens in the nose and Lung, which render a person liable to frequent nasal stuffiness and discharge, sensitivity to dust and pollen, sneezing etc. The ability of Fēngmén BL-12 to firm the defensive qi, expel exterior pathogens and benefit the nose make it especially suitable in the treatment of such cases of perennial and seasonal allergic rhinitis.

It is useful to compare the points Fēngmén BL-12 and [Fèishū BL-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-13), each reflecting different aspects of the Lung function. Although Fēngmén BL-12 is indicated for painful cough and cough with bleeding, this is primarily in the context of exterior disease and its affinity for releasing the exterior and firming the defensive qi underlines its close connection with the more superficial and yang aspect of the Lung. It is significant however, that despite its ability to strengthen the defensive qi which is distributed by the Lung, it is not used to tonify other aspects of Lung deficiency for which the deeper (yin) action of [Fèishū BL-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-13) is preferred.

According to the Illustrated Classic of Acupuncture Points on the Bronze Man "If Fēngmén BL-12 is repeatedly needled it will clear the hot yang qi and the back will always be free of sores and carbuncles". Finally the Essential Questions[8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references) includes Fēngmén BL-12 among the eight points (bilateral [Quēpén ST-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-12), [Dàzhù BL-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-11), [Zhōngfǔ LU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-1) and Fēngmén BL-12) to clear heat from the chest.

#### Summary of clinical application

- For i. attack by exterior pathogens and ii. deficiency of defensive qi: attack by wind with fever, cold shivering, dislike of wind and cold, headache, injury by cold with rigidity of the head and neck, flaccidity of the interstices with frequent coughing and clear watery nasal discharge, flaccidity of the interstices with susceptibility to catching wind-cold.
- For nasal disorders: discharge, congestion, rhinitis, sneezing and nosebleed.
- For cough, cough with chest and back pain, coughing blood, dyspnoea and heat in the chest.

#### Combinations

- Rhinitis with clear nasal discharge: Fēngmén BL-12, [Shéntíng DU-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-24), [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2), [Yíngxiāng LI-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-20), [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4), [Zhìyīn BL-67](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-67) and [Fùtōnggǔ KID-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-20) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Constant sneezing: Fēngmén BL-12 and [Wǔchù BL-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-5) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Nosebleed with stifled breathing: Fēngmén BL-12, [Chénglíng GB-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-18), [Fēngchí GB-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-20), [Yīxǐ BL-45](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-45) and [Hòuxī SI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-3) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Pain of the shoulder and back: Fēngmén BL-12, [Jiānjǐng GB-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-21), [Zhōngzhǔ SJ-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-3), [Zhīgōu SJ-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-6), [Hòuxī SI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-3), [Wàngǔ SI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-4) and [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) (Great Compendium).
- Injury by cold with fever which clears and then reoccurs: Fēngmén BL-12, [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4), [Xíngjiān LIV-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-2) and [Xuánzhōng GB-39](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-39) (Outline of Medicine).

### **[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** (FĒNGMÉN)

* **英文名称：** Wind Gate
* **中文名称：** 风门
* **国际代码：** BL-12
* **穴位属性：** **[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 是膀胱经与督脉的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第二胸椎（T2）棘突下缘旁开1.5寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 位于脊柱旁肌肉的最高点。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺，向脊柱方向0.5至1寸，或横斜刺1至1.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直针刺或向远离脊柱方向斜刺有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 祛风解表
* 益卫固表
* 宣肺降气
* 宣利鼻窍

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

风邪侵袭伴发热，恶寒发抖，恶风恶寒，头痛，伤寒伴头颈强直，腠理不密伴频频咳嗽清稀鼻涕，腠理不密易感风寒，欲闭目，风眩，视物眩晕。
咳嗽，咳嗽伴胸背痛，咳血，呼吸困难，胸中灼热，风劳咳嗽，风劳呕吐，呕吐。
鼻涕量多，鼻塞，鼻炎，喷嚏，鼻出血。
腰痛，颈项强直，荨麻疹，背部痈疮，肩痛，烦躁不安入睡困难。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

正如其名称“风门”所示，**[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 是祛风解表的重要穴位。此作用可由三个主要因素解释：i. **[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 属太阳经，是六经中最表层的经脉，因此最常首先受外邪侵袭；ii. **[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 位于上背部，肺的上部，肺被称为“娇脏”，因其易受外邪侵袭而损伤；iii. **[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 是膀胱经与督脉的交会穴，督脉主宰全身所有阳经，因此与身体的表（阳）部有密切关系。

当外邪风邪通过皮肤、鼻子或口侵袭时，可能：i. 阻碍卫气，影响其温煦功能，引起恶寒、恶风恶寒和头痛，而伴随恶寒的发热是卫气与病邪搏斗的标志；ii. 影响肺的宣发肃降功能，导致鼻涕量多和咳嗽；iii. 阻滞太阳经，引起头颈僵硬疼痛。**[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 可通过针刺或拔罐解表，治疗所有风寒、风热或风燥之邪束表引起的实证表证。在风寒情况下，可施灸。**[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 也主治表虚证，此时风邪侵袭引起营卫不和，特点是发热恶寒不解，恶风，脉浮弱。

**[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 固表、益卫气的能力，从而帮助身体抵抗外邪侵袭，在各种经典中都有讨论。《玉龙歌》推荐其治疗“腠理不密，频频咳嗽，清稀鼻涕”，而《医宗金鉴·针灸法》推荐其治疗“腠理不密，易感风寒”。换句话说，除了解表祛风外，**[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 可通过针刺或艾灸补益卫气，治疗易感冒的患者。

鼻子是肺的门户。根据《灵枢·本神》记载：“肺气通于鼻；肺和则鼻能知香臭”。《医宗金鉴·针灸法》推荐**[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 治疗“鼻衄及各种鼻病”。鼻塞流涕通常是肺卫气虚，伴有邪气留滞鼻肺，导致患者易患鼻塞、流涕、对灰尘花粉敏感、打喷嚏等。**[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 固卫气、祛外邪、宣利鼻窍的能力，使其特别适合治疗此类常年性鼻炎和季节性过敏性鼻炎。

比较**[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 和**[BL13 肺俞](point/BL13.md)** 这两个穴位很有用，它们各自反映肺功能的不同方面。尽管**[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 主治疼痛性咳嗽和咳血，但这主要是在表证背景下，其解表和固卫气的能力突出了其与肺的更浅表和阳性方面的密切联系。然而，尽管它能够增强由肺输布的卫气，但它不用于补益肺虚的其他方面，对此更深层（阴）作用的**[BL13 肺俞](point/BL13.md)** 更受青睐。

根据《铜人腧穴针灸图经》记载：“**[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 反复针刺，可清热阳之气，使背部永无疮痈。”最后，《素问·热论》将**[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)** 列为清胸热的八个穴位之一（双侧**[ST12 缺盆](point/ST12.md)**、**[BL11 大杼](point/BL11.md)**、**[LU1 中府](point/LU1.md)** 和**[BL12 风门](point/BL12.md)**）。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗外邪侵袭和卫气虚：风邪侵袭伴发热，恶寒发抖，恶风恶寒，头痛，伤寒伴头颈强直，腠理不密伴频频咳嗽清稀鼻涕，腠理不密易感风寒。
* 治疗鼻部疾病：鼻涕，鼻塞，鼻炎，喷嚏和鼻出血。
* 治疗咳嗽，咳嗽伴胸背痛，咳血，呼吸困难和胸中灼热。
