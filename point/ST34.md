### Ridge Mound LIÁNGQIŪ
![[ST-34 梁丘.png|250]]
- Xi-Cleft point of the Stomach channel

#### Location

On the thigh, on a line drawn between the lateral border of the patella and the anterior superior iliac spine, in a depression 2 cun proximal to the superior border of the patella.

#### Location Notes

Since the height of the patella is measured as 2 cun, this point may be located one patella's length above its superior border.

#### Needling

Perpendicular or oblique insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Harmonises the Stomach and alleviates pain
- Moderates acute conditions

#### Indications

- Swelling and pain of the knee, difficulty in flexing and extending the knee, pain of the knee and leg, crane's knee wind, difficulty in walking, painful obstruction of the shin, cold painful obstruction with numbness, cold of the legs and feet, lumbar pain.
- Epigastric pain, acid regurgitation, breast abscess, pain and swelling of the breast, 'big fright'.

#### Commentary

Liángqiū ST-34 is the xi-cleft point of the Stomach channel and is the only one of the xi-cleft points of the twelve primary channels which is located proximal to the knee or elbow. It is an important adjacent point in the treatment of knee disorders (often combined with such points as Xiyan (MN-LE-16), [Xuèhǎi SP-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-10), [Yīnlíngquán SP-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-9) and [Yánglíngquán GB-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-34)) and for painful obstruction, cold and pain of the leg.

The xi-cleft points are where the qi and blood, which flow relatively superficially along the channels from the jing-well points, gather and plunge more deeply. The xi-cleft points in general are indicated in the treatment of acute conditions and pain. The yangming Stomach channel traverses the breast and passes through the nipple, and Liángqiū ST-34 is classically indicated for such acute disorders of the breast as swelling, pain and abscess. In clinical practice it is also used for acute epigastric pain, although this seems to be a modern rather than traditional usage.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Xi-cleft point of the Stomach channel: acute epigastric pain, breast pain and breast abscess.
- Adjacent point for knee disorders.

#### Combinations

- Breast abscess: Liángqiū ST-34 and [Dìwǔhùi GB-42](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-42) (Supplementing Life).
- Contracted sinews with difficulty in flexing and extending the knee, and inability to walk: Liángqiū ST-34, [Qūquán LIV-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-8) and [Xīyángguān GB-33](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-33) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Acute epigastric pain: Liángqiū ST-34 and [Liángmén ST-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-21).
- Stiffness, soreness and pain of the knee: Liángqiū ST-34, [Xuèhǎi SP-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-10), Xiyan (MN-LE-16), [Yánglíngquán GB-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-34) and [Yīnlíngquán SP-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-9).

### [ST34 梁丘](/point/ST34.md) (LIÁNGQIŪ)

* **英文名称：** Ridge Mound
* **中文名称：** 梁丘
* **国际代码：** ST-34
* **穴位属性：** [ST34 梁丘](/point/ST34.md) 是胃经的郄穴。

#### 定位 (Location)

位于大腿部，髌骨外缘与髂前上棘连线上，髌骨上缘上方2寸的凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 髌骨的高度被测定为2寸，因此此穴可位于髌骨上缘上方一髌骨长。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直或斜刺1至1.5寸。

#### 作用 (Actions)

* 通经活络，止痛
* 调和胃腑，止痛
* 缓急止痛（缓解急性病症）

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

膝关节肿痛，膝关节屈伸不利，膝腿疼痛，鹤膝风，行走困难，胫部痹痛，寒痹伴麻木，腿脚发冷，腰痛。
胃脘痛，吞酸，乳痈，乳房疼痛肿胀，“大惊”（极度惊恐）。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST34 梁丘](/point/ST34.md) 是胃经的郄穴，也是十二正经中唯一一个位于膝关节或肘关节近端的郄穴。它是治疗膝关节疾病（常与[MNLE16 膝眼](/point/MNLE16.md)、[SP10 血海](/point/SP10.md)、[SP9 阴陵泉](/point/SP9.md) 和[GB34 阳陵泉](/point/GB34.md) 等穴联合使用）以及腿部痹痛、寒痛的重要邻近穴位。

郄穴是气血从井穴沿经脉相对浅层流注到此聚集并深入的地方。郄穴通常主治急性病症和疼痛。阳明胃经循行乳房并经过乳头，[ST34 梁丘](/point/ST34.md) 在经典中主治乳房肿胀、疼痛和乳痈等急性乳房疾病。在临床实践中，它也用于治疗急性胃脘痛，尽管这似乎是现代而非传统的用法。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

* 胃经郄穴：治疗急性胃脘痛，乳房疼痛和乳痈。
* 治疗膝关节疾病的邻近穴位。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

* 乳痈：[ST34 梁丘](/point/ST34.md) 和[GB42 地五会](/point/GB42.md)（《资生经》）。
* 筋脉挛缩，膝关节屈伸不利，不能行走：[ST34 梁丘](/point/ST34.md)，[LIV8 曲泉](/point/LIV8.md) 和[GB33 膝阳关](/point/GB33.md)（《千金方》）。
* 急性胃脘痛：[ST34 梁丘](/point/ST34.md) 和[ST21 梁门](/point/ST21.md)。
* 膝关节僵硬、酸痛和疼痛：[ST34 梁丘](/point/ST34.md)，[SP10 血海](/point/SP10.md)，[MNLE16 膝眼](/point/MNLE16.md) (经外穴)，[GB34 阳陵泉](/point/GB34.md) 和[SP9 阴陵泉](/point/SP9.md)。
