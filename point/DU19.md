## DU-19 HÒUDǏNG

### Central Pivot
![[DU-19 后顶.png|250]]
#### Location

At the back of the head on the midline, 1.5 cun directly superior to [Qiángjiān DU-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-18) and 1.5 cun posterior to [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20).

#### Location Notes

This point may be located at the junction of the lower three quarters and the upper quarter of the line connecting [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16) and [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20).

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.

#### Actions

- Eliminates wind and alleviates pain
- Calms the spirit

#### Indications

- Stiffness and pain of the head and neck, one-sided headache, pain of the vertex, wind dizziness, aversion to wind and cold, painful obstruction with sweating.
- Mad walking, insomnia, epileptic convulsions.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Indicated for stiffness and pain of the head and neck, one-sided headache, pain of the vertex, wind dizziness, aversion to wind and cold, painful obstruction with sweating, mad walking, insomnia, epileptic convulsions.

#### Combinations

- Wind dizziness: Hòudǐng DU-19, [Yùzhěn BL-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-9) and [Hànyàn GB-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-4) (Supplementing Life).
- Wind dizziness and one-sided headache: Hòudǐng DU-19, [Hànyàn GB-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-4) and [Qiándǐng DU-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-21) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Pain of the neck with aversion to wind-cold: Hòudǐng DU-19 and [Wàiqiū GB-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-36) (Supplementing Life).
- Pain of the head and nape: Hòudǐng DU-19, [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) and [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4) (Great Compendium).
- Pain of the head and eyes: Hòudǐng DU-19, [Tōnglǐ HE-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-5) and [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) (Supplementing Life)

### 1. [DU19 后顶](point/DU19.md)

#### 1.1. Posterior Summit 後頂

#### 1.2. 定位

位于头部后中线，[DU18 强间](point/DU18.md)正上方1.5寸，[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)后1.5寸。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

此穴可位于连接[DU16 风府](point/DU16.md)与[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)的线之下四分之三与上四分之一交界处。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

平刺，深0.5至1寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 祛风止痛  
- 定神  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 头颈僵痛、偏头痛、巅顶痛、风眩、恶风寒、痹痛伴汗出。  
- 狂走、失眠、癫痫抽搐。  

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 用于头颈僵痛、偏头痛、巅顶痛、风眩、恶风寒、痹痛伴汗出、狂走、失眠、癫痫抽搐。  

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 风眩：[DU19 后顶](point/DU19.md) + [BL9 玉枕](point/BL9.md) + [GB4 颔厌](point/GB4.md)（《补命》）。  
- 风眩及偏头痛：[DU19 后顶](point/DU19.md) + [GB4 颔厌](point/GB4.md) + [DU21 前顶](point/DU21.md)（《千金方》）。  
- 颈痛伴恶风寒：[DU19 后顶](point/DU19.md) + [GB36 外丘](point/GB36.md)（《补命》）。  
- 头项痛：[DU19 后顶](point/DU19.md) + [DU20 百会](point/DU20.md) + [LI4 合谷](point/LI4.md)（《大成》）。  
- 头眼痛：[DU19 后顶](point/DU19.md) + [HE5 通里](point/HE5.md) + [DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)（《补命》）。
