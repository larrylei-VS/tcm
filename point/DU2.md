### Lumbar Shu YĀOSHŪ
![[DU-2 腰俞.png|250]]
#### Location

On the midline, in the sacro-coccygeal hiatus.

#### Location Notes

The sacro-coccygeal hiatus is located in-between the cornua of the sacrum and coccyx, in the depression inferior to the fourth sacral spinous process if this is palpable. Note, however, that the sacro-coccygeal hiatus may sometimes extend as high as the level of the third sacral foramina.

#### Needling

Oblique superior insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.

#### Actions

- Benefits the lumbar region and legs
- Dispels wind-damp

#### Indications

- Pain of the sacrum, pain of the lumbar region and hips with inability to flex and extend, lumbar pain radiating to the foot, cold painful obstruction with numbness of the leg.
- Irregular menstruation, red and white leucorrhoea, dark urine, haemorrhoids.
- Warm malaria with absence of sweating, epilepsy, injury by cold with ceaseless heat in the four limbs.

#### Commentary

The primary clinical use of Yāoshū DU-2 is to treat pain of the sacrum and lumbar region, especially when it radiates to the hips and down the legs. According to the Essential Questions[26](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-governing-vessel/references) Yāoshū DU-2 is one of the 'eight points for draining heat from the extremities' (although in fact only seven are listed) namely [Yúnmén LU-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-2), [Jiānyú LI-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-15), [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) and Yāoshū DU-2, and is indicated for 'injury by cold with ceaseless heat in the four limbs'.

#### Summary of clinical application

- For pain of the sacrum and lumbar region, especially when it radiates to the hips and down the legs.

#### Combinations

- Stiffness and pain of the lumbar region: Yāoshū DU-2, [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40), [Yǒngquǎn KID-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-1), [Xiǎochángshū BL-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-27) and [Pángguāngshū BL-28](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-28) (Great Compendium).
- Stiffness of the lumbar region and back with inability to bend to the side: Yāoshū DU-2 and [Fèishū BL-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-13) (Great Compendium).
- Cold wind painful obstruction that is difficult to cure: Yāoshū DU-2 and [Huántiào GB-30](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-30) (Ode of Xi-hong).
- Numbness of the legs: Yāoshū DU-2 and [Fēngfǔ DU-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-16) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Malaria: Yāoshū DU-2 and [Dàzhuī DU-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-14) (Supplementing Life).

### 1. [DU2 腰俞](point/DU2.md)

#### 1.1. Lumbar Shu 腰俞

#### 1.2. 定位

位于中线，骶尾裂孔内。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

骶尾裂孔位于骶骨角与尾骨之间凹陷处，若第四骶椎棘突可触及，则在其下方。需注意，骶尾裂孔有时可延伸至第三骶孔水平。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

向上斜刺，深0.5至1寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 益腰腿  
- 祛风湿  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 骶部痛、腰髋痛不能屈伸、腰痛放射至足、寒湿痹痛伴腿麻。  
- 月经不调、赤白带下、尿色深、痔疮。  
- 温疟无汗、癫痫、寒伤伴四肢持续发热。  

#### 1.7. 论述

[DU2 腰俞](point/DU2.md)的主要临床应用是治疗骶部及腰部疼痛，尤当疼痛放射至髋及腿部。《素问》将其列为“八穴泻四肢热”之一（实列七穴），包括[LU2 云门](point/LU2.md)、[LI15 肩髃](point/LI15.md)、[BL40 委中](point/BL40.md)及[DU2 腰俞](point/DU2.md)，用于“寒伤伴四肢持续发热”。  

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 治疗骶部及腰部疼痛，尤当放射至髋及腿部。  

#### 1.9. 配伍

- 腰部僵痛：[DU2 腰俞](point/DU2.md) + [BL40 委中](point/BL40.md) + [KID1 涌泉](point/KID1.md) + [BL27 小肠俞](point/BL27.md) + [BL28 膀胱俞](point/BL28.md)（《大成》）。  
- 腰背僵硬不能侧屈：[DU2 腰俞](point/DU2.md) + [BL13 肺俞](point/BL13.md)（《大成》）。  
- 寒风痹痛难愈：[DU2 腰俞](point/DU2.md) + [GB30 环跳](point/GB30.md)（《西弘颂》）。  
- 腿麻：[DU2 腰俞](point/DU2.md) + [DU16 风府](point/DU16.md)（《千金方》）。  
- 疟疾：[DU2 腰俞](point/DU2.md) + [DU14 大椎](point/DU14.md)（《补命》）。
