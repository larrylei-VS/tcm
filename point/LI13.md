### Arm Five Miles SHǑUWǓLǏ
![[LI-13 手五里.png|250]]
#### Location

On the lateral side of the upper arm, 3 cun proximal to [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11), on the line connecting [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11) with [Jiānyú LI-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-15).

#### Location Notes

i. Locate approximately one handbreadth superior to [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11), in the depression between the lateral border of biceps brachii and the humerus; ii. Locate at one third of the distance between [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11) and the axillary fold.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Alleviates coughing
- Regulates qi, drains damp and transforms phlegm

#### Indications

- Pain, numbness or contraction of the elbow and upper arm, inability to raise the arm, shoulder pain, wind-taxation with fear and fright.
- Cough, difficulty in breathing, vomiting blood.
- Scrofula, desire to sleep, diminished qi, inability to move the four limbs, yellow body with intermittent low-grade fever, malaria, blurred vision, fullness and distention below the Heart, fear and fright.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Local point for arm disorders.
- Traditionally indicated for scrofula (nodules on the sides of the neck, the axilla or inguinal region).

#### Combinations

- Scrofula: Shǒuwǔlǐ LI-13 and [Bìnào LI-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-14) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Scrofula: moxa Shǒuwǔlǐ LI-13 and [Rényíng ST-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-9) thirty times (Thousand Ducat Formulas).


### [LI13 手五里](/point/LI13.md) (SHǑUWǓLǏ)

- **英文名称：** Arm Five Miles
- **中文名称：** 手五里
- **国际代码：** LI-13

#### 定位 (Location)

位于上臂外侧，在连接[LI11 曲池](/point/LI11.md) 与[LI15 肩髃](/point/LI15.md) 的连线上，距[LI11 曲池](/point/LI11.md) 上方3寸。

- **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    - 位于[LI11 曲池](/point/LI11.md) 上方约一横掌处，在肱二头肌外缘与肱骨之间的凹陷中；
    - 位于[LI11 曲池](/point/LI11.md) 与腋窝横纹距离的三分之一处。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

垂直刺1至1.5寸。

#### 作用 (Actions)

- 通经活络，止痛
- 止咳平喘
- 调气，利湿化痰

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

肘臂疼痛、麻木或挛缩，不能抬臂，肩痛，风劳伴恐惧。

咳嗽，呼吸困难，咳血。

瘰疬，嗜睡，气虚，四肢活动不利，身黄伴间歇性低热，疟疾，视物模糊，心下胀满，恐惧。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[LI13 手五里](/point/LI13.md) 主要作为治疗手臂疾病的局部穴位使用，传统上用于治疗瘰疬（颈部、腋下或腹股沟区的结块）。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

治疗手臂疾病的局部穴位。

传统上主治瘰疬（颈侧、腋下或腹股沟区的结节）。

主治咳嗽，呼吸困难。

主治嗜睡，恐惧，心下胀满。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

瘰疬：[LI13 手五里](/point/LI13.md) 和[LI14 臂臑](/point/LI14.md)（《百症赋》）。

瘰疬：灸[LI13 手五里](/point/LI13.md) 和[ST9 人迎](/point/ST9.md) 三十壮（《千金方》）。
