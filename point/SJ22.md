### Ear Harmony Crevice ĚRHÉLIÁO
- Meeting point of the Sanjiao, Gall Bladder and Small Intestine channels
![[SJ-22 耳和髎.png|250]]
#### Location

Approximately 0.5 cun anterior to the upper border of the root of the ear, in a slight depression on the posterior border of the hairline of the temple.

#### Location Notes

This point lies just posterior to where the superficial temporal artery can be palpated.

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.

#### Actions

- Expels wind and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Tinnitus, pain and heaviness of the head, headache, head wind, swelling and pain of the tip of the nose, nasal discharge, deviation of the mouth, lockjaw, swelling of the submandibular region and neck, clonic spasm.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for tinnitus, pain and heaviness of the head, headache, head wind, swelling and pain of the tip of the nose, nasal discharge, deviation of the mouth, lockjaw, swelling of the submandibular region and neck, clonic spasm.

#### Combinations

- Deafness: Ěrhéliáo SJ-22, [Zhōngzhǔ SJ-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-3), [Wàiguān SJ-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-5), [Tīnghuì GB-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-2), [Tīnggōng SI-19](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-19), [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4), [Shāngyáng LI-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-1) and [Zhōngchōng PC-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-9) (Precious Mirror).
- Wind headache: Ěrhéliáo SJ-22, [Sīzhúkōng SJ-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-23), [Qīmài SJ-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-18), [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2), [Chéngguāng BL-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-6) and [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL23), (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[SJ22 耳和髎](point/SJ22.md)** (ĚRHÉLIÁO)

* **英文名称：** Ear Harmony Crevice
* **中文名称：** 耳和髎
* **国际代码：** SJ-22
* **穴位属性：** **[SJ22 耳和髎](point/SJ22.md)** 是三焦经、胆经和小肠经的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于耳根上方，前上方约0.5寸的轻微凹陷中，在颞部发际的后缘。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 此穴位于可触及颞浅动脉搏动处的后方。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

横刺0.3至0.5寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 祛风止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

耳鸣，头痛头重，头痛，头风，鼻尖肿痛，流鼻涕，口歪，牙关紧闭，颌下区和颈部肿胀，痉挛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SJ22 耳和髎](point/SJ22.md)** 临床上较少使用。其名称“耳和髎”暗示其与耳部和谐以及面部骨缝（髎）的关联，作为三焦经、胆经和小肠经的交会穴，它能够**祛风止痛**。

此穴主要用于缓解**头部**和**面部**因风邪引起的**疼痛、肿胀和活动受限**。它主治**耳鸣**，**头痛**（包括头重、头风），以及**鼻尖肿痛**、**流鼻涕**、**口歪**、**牙关紧闭**和**颌下区与颈部肿胀**。此外，它对**痉挛**也有一定的辅助疗效。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治耳鸣，头痛头重，头痛，头风，鼻尖肿痛，流鼻涕，口歪，牙关紧闭，颌下区和颈部肿胀，痉挛。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 耳聋：**[SJ22 耳和髎](point/SJ22.md)**，**[SJ3 中渚](point/SJ3.md)**，**[SJ5 外关](point/SJ5.md)**，**[GB2 听会](point/GB2.md)**，**[SI19 听宫](point/SI19.md)**，**[LI4 合谷](point/LI4.md)**，**[LI1 商阳](point/LI1.md)** 和 **[PC9 中冲](point/PC9.md)**（《备急千金要方》）。
* 头风：**[SJ22 耳和髎](point/SJ22.md)**，**[SJ23 丝竹空](point/SJ23.md)**，**[SJ18 瘈脉](point/SJ18.md)**，**[BL2 攒竹](point/BL2.md)**，**[BL6 承光](point/BL6.md)** 和 **[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)**（《千金方》）。
