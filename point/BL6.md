### Receiving Light CHÉNGGUĀNG
![[BL-6 承光.png|250]]
#### Location

1.5 cun posterior to [Wǔchù BL-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-5), 2.5 cun within the anterior hairline and 1.5 cun lateral to the midline.

#### Location Notes

i. To locate the anterior hairline if indistinct, see location note for [Méichōng BL-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-3); ii. Note that the distance from the anterior hairline at the midline to [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) is 5 cun.

#### Needling

Transverse insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Note:** according to several classical and modern texts, this point is contraindicated to moxibustion.

#### Actions

- Eliminates wind and clears heat
- Clears the head and benefits the eyes and nose

#### Indications

- Vertex headache, wind dizziness, superficial visual obstruction, blurred vision, short sightedness, copious clear nasal discharge, nasal congestion, inability to distinguish the fragrant from the foul, deviation of the mouth.
- Vomiting, agitation of the Heart with vomiting, febrile disease with absence of sweating.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Vertex headache, wind dizziness, superficial visual obstruction, blurred vision, short sightedness, copious clear nasal discharge, nasal congestion, inability to distinguish the fragrant from the foul, deviation of the mouth.
- Vomiting, agitation of the Heart with vomiting, febrile disease with absence of sweating.

#### Combinations

- Wind headache: Chéngguāng BL-6, [Zǎnzhú BL-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-2), [Shènshū BL-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-23), [Qīmài SJ-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-18), [Sīzhúkōng SJ-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-23) and [Ěrhéliáo SJ-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-22) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Obstruction of the nose with inability to distinguish the fragrant from the foul: Chéngguāng BL-6, [Shàngxīng DU-23](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-23), [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) and [Xìnhuì DU-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-22) (Supplementing Life).
- Vomiting: Chéngguāng BL-6 and [Dàdū SP-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-2) (Supplementing Life).
- Agitation of the Heart: Chéngguāng BL-6, [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) and [Qiángjiān DU-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-18) (Supplementing Life).

### **[BL6 承光](point/BL6.md)** (CHÉNGGUĀNG)

* **英文名称：** Receiving Light
* **中文名称：** 承光
* **国际代码：** BL-6

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于**[BL5 五处](point/BL5.md)** 后方1.5寸，前发际内2.5寸，距中线旁开1.5寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 如果前发际不明显，请参见**[BL3 眉冲](point/BL3.md)** 的定位要点；
    * 请注意，中线前发际到**[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)** 的距离为5寸。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

横刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Note):** 根据几部经典和现代文献，此穴禁灸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 祛风清热
* 清头，明目利鼻

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

头顶痛，头风眩晕，外障眼病（翳膜），视物模糊，近视，鼻流清涕量多，鼻塞，不辨香臭，口歪。
呕吐，心烦伴呕吐，热病无汗。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL6 承光](point/BL6.md)** 临床上较少使用。其名称“承光”意为承受光明，暗示其在明目和清利头面部的作用。

此穴主要作用于头部，能够祛风清热，清头利窍。它主治头顶痛、头风眩晕，以及外障眼病、视物模糊、近视等眼部疾病。同时，它对鼻部疾患如鼻流清涕量多、鼻塞、不辨香臭也有疗效。此外，**[BL6 承光](point/BL6.md)** 也辅助治疗心烦伴呕吐和热病无汗等全身症状，这可能与其清热、调气降逆的功效有关。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治头顶痛，头风眩晕，外障眼病，视物模糊，近视，鼻流清涕量多，鼻塞，不辨香臭，口歪。
* 主治呕吐，心烦伴呕吐，热病无汗。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 头风：**[BL6 承光](point/BL6.md)**，**[BL2 攒竹](point/BL2.md)**，**[BL23 肾俞](point/BL23.md)**，**[SJ18 瘛脉](point/SJ18.md)**，**[SJ23 丝竹空](point/SJ23.md)** 和 **[SJ22 耳和髎](point/SJ22.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 鼻塞不辨香臭：**[BL6 承光](point/BL6.md)**，**[DU23 上星](point/DU23.md)**，**[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)** 和 **[DU22 囟会](point/DU22.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 呕吐：**[BL6 承光](point/BL6.md)** 和 **[SP2 大都](point/SP2.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 心烦：**[BL6 承光](point/BL6.md)**，**[DU20 百会](point/DU20.md)** 和 **[DU18 强间](point/DU18.md)**（《资生经》）。
