### Jumping Circle HUÁNTIÀO
![[GB-30 环跳.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder and Bladder channels
- Ma Dan-yang Heavenly Star point

#### Location

On the postero-lateral aspect of the hip joint, one third of the distance between the prominence of the greater trochanter and the sacro-coccygeal hiatus ([Yāoshū DU-2](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-2)).

#### Location Notes

i. This point may most readily be located (and needled) with the patient lying on their side; ii. To accurately palpate the prominence of the greater trochanter place one hand over the lateral aspect of the hip joint at the level of the perineum, and with the other hand rotate the foot in order to feel the movement of the prominence of the greater trochanter; iii. The sacro-coccygeal hiatus is located in-between the cornua of the sacrum and coccyx, in the depression inferior to the fourth sacral spinous process if this is palpable. Note, however, that the sacro-coccygeal hiatus may sometimes extend as high as the level of the third sacral foramina.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion, directed towards the genitals, 2 to 3.5 cun.  
**Note:** a radiating or electric sensation may travel to the foot. Since this manifestation of deqi (arrival of qi) is particularly indicated in cases of sciatica it may be more readily induced by locating Huántiào GB-30 one cun inferior to its normal position.

#### Actions

- Activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Benefits the hip joint and leg
- Dispels wind-damp

#### Indications

- Pain of the buttock, pain or sprain of the hip and leg, sciatica, atrophy disorder and painful obstruction of the lower limb, hemiplegia, cold-wind-damp painful obstruction, numbness of the leg, inability to flex and extend the knee, contraction and pain of the thigh and knee, pain of the lumbar region and lateral costal region, pain of the lumbar region and leg, leg qi.
- Urticaria, eczema.

#### Commentary

Huántiào GB-30 was included by Ma Dan-yang, the great physician of the Jin dynasty, among the 'eleven heavenly star points'[9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-gall-bladder-channel/references), his grouping of the most vital acupuncture points, and was indicated by him for " ... cold wind and damp painful obstruction, pain radiating from the hip to the calf, sighing with pain when turning over". Huántiào GB-30 is unrivalled in importance for the treatment of disorders of the hip joint and buttock, whether due to traumatic injury, painful obstruction, stagnation of qi or deficiency.

Huántiào GB-30 is located at the pivotal hip region, the gate of the lower limb, and as its name 'Jumping Circle' implies, is able to influence the movement of the whole lower limb. It is a vital point to promote circulation of qi and blood in the treatment of all kinds of painful obstruction, atrophy disorder, numbness, stiffness, pain and contraction of the leg. In the treatment of sciatica, it is considered by many practitioners to be the single most important point which may be used whatever the nature and distribution of the pain. Its importance in the treatment of painful obstruction of the lower extremities was emphasised in the Secrets of the Heavenly Star which asked "Cold wind damp painful obstruction, where do you needle? First choose Huántiào GB-30, then [Yánglíngquán GB-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-34)". Its ability to promote circulation throughout the leg also renders it an essential point in the treatment of hemiplegia with an action similar to points of the yangming Stomach channel.

The Bladder channel unites with the Gall Bladder channel at this point, and Huántiào GB-30 can therefore also treat disorders of the lumbar region (through which the Bladder channel courses), whether extending to the hip and buttock, or radiating upwards along the Gall Bladder channel to the lateral costal region.

Finally Huántiào GB-30 is cited in the Song of the Nine Needles for Returning the Yang for the treatment of collapse of yang characterised by loss of consciousness, aversion to cold, cold counterflow of the limbs, purple lips etc.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Essential point for sciatica and for all disorders of the leg: painful obstruction, atrophy disorder, numbness, stiffness, pain and contraction.
- Essential local point for disorders of the hip joint; also for when pain radiates from the hip to the lumbar or lateral costal regions.

### 1. [GB30 环跳](point/GB30.md)

#### 1.1. Jumping Circle 環跳

- 胆经与膀胱经的交会穴，马丹阳天星穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于髋关节后外侧，股骨大转子突出点与骶尾间隙（[DU2 腰俞](point/DU2.md)）连线三分之一处。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

i. 患者侧卧时最易定位和针刺此穴；  
ii. 为准确探查股骨大转子突出点，将一只手置于髋关节外侧，会阴水平处，另一只手旋转足部以感受大转子突起的移动；  
iii. 骶尾间隙位于骶骨角与尾骨之间凹陷处，若可触及，为第4骶椎棘突下方。注意，骶尾间隙有时可能延伸至第3骶孔水平。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直刺入，朝生殖器方向，深2至3.5寸。  
**注意：** 针感可能放射至足部，这种得气（气至）表现特别适用于坐骨神经痛，可通过将[GB30 环跳](point/GB30.md)定位于正常位置下方1寸更容易诱发。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 活络止痛
- 益髋关节及腿
- 祛风湿

#### 1.6. 主治

- 臀痛、髋腿痛或扭伤、坐骨神经痛、下肢萎症及痛痹、偏瘫、寒风湿痛痹、腿麻、膝不能屈伸、大腿及膝挛痛、腰胁痛、腰腿痛、腿气。
- 荨麻疹、湿疹。

#### 1.7. 论述

[GB30 环跳](point/GB30.md)被金代名医马丹阳列入“十一天星穴”，为其认为最重要的穴位组合，他指其用于“寒风湿痛痹，髋痛放射至小腿，翻身时叹息疼痛”。[GB30 环跳](point/GB30.md)在治疗髋关节和臀部疾患方面无可匹敌，无论由外伤、痛痹、气滞还是虚证引起。

[GB30 环跳](point/GB30.md)位于髋部关键区域，下肢之门，如其名“环跳”所示，能影响整个下肢运动。它是促进气血循环的关键穴位，用于治疗各种痛痹、萎症、麻木、僵硬、腿部疼痛及挛缩。在治疗坐骨神经痛时，许多针灸师认为其为最重要的单一穴位，无论疼痛性质及分布如何。《天星秘诀》强调其治疗下肢痛痹的重要性，问道：“寒风湿痛痹，何处针？先取[GB30 环跳](point/GB30.md)，次[GB34 阳陵泉](point/GB34.md)。”其促进腿部气血循环的能力也使其成为治疗偏瘫的重要穴位，作用类似阳明胃经穴位。

膀胱经在此与胆经会合，因此[GB30 环跳](point/GB30.md)也能治疗膀胱经经过的腰部疾患，无论是延伸至髋部和臀部，还是沿胆经上行至胁部。

最后，《九针回阳歌》引用[GB30 环跳](point/GB30.md)用于治疗阳气衰竭，特征为意识丧失、恶寒、四肢厥冷、唇紫等。

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 治疗坐骨神经痛及所有腿部疾患的关键穴位：痛痹、萎症、麻木、僵硬、疼痛及挛缩。
- 治疗髋关节疾患的重要局部穴位；也适用于疼痛从髋部放射至腰部或胁部的情况。
