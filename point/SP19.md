### Chest Village XIŌNGXIĀNG
![[SP-19 胸乡.png|250]]
#### Location

On the lateral side of the chest, in the third intercostal space, 6 cun lateral to the midline.

#### Location Notes

i. The intercostal spaces are most reliably counted downwards from the second intercostal space which lies immediately below the sternal angle. In males, the nipple almost invariably lies in the fourth intercostal space; ii. The six cun line is located two cun lateral to the mamillary line.

#### Needling

Transverse-oblique insertion along the intercostal space, 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** perpendicular insertion, especially in thin patients, carries a substantial risk of inducing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Regulates and descends qi and unbinds the chest

#### Indications

- Fullness of the chest and lateral costal region extending to the back, inability to turn over when lying down, cough, shortness of breath.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Xiōngxiāng SP-19 is indicated for fullness of the chest and lateral costal region extending to the back, inability to turn over when lying down, cough, shortness of breath.

#### Combinations

- Pain of the chest radiating to the back: Xiōngxiāng SP-19, [Nèiguān PC-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-6) and [Xīnshū BL-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-15).

### **[SP19 胸乡](point/SP19.md)** (XIŌNGXIĀNG)

* **英文名称：** Chest Village
* **中文名称：** 胸乡
* **国际代码：** SP-19

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于胸部外侧，第三肋间隙中，距前正中线旁开6寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 肋间隙最可靠的计数方法是从胸骨角下方的第二肋间隙向下数。男性乳头几乎总位于第四肋间隙；
    * 旁开6寸线位于乳头线旁开2寸处。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

沿肋间隙横斜刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直刺入，特别是在瘦弱患者中，有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 调理降气，宽胸散结

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

胸部和胁肋部胀满放射至背部，卧时不能翻身，咳嗽，气短。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SP19 胸乡](point/SP19.md)** 临床上较少使用。它主要用于缓解胸部和胁肋部因气机不畅导致的胀满和疼痛，这些不适甚至可能放射到背部。其调理气机、宽胸散结的功效也有助于改善因胸闷而引起的卧时翻身困难、咳嗽和气短等症状。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治胸部和胁肋部胀满放射至背部，卧时不能翻身，咳嗽，气短。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 胸痛放射至背部：**[SP19 胸乡](point/SP19.md)**，**[PC6 内关](point/PC6.md)** 和 **[BL15 心俞](point/BL15.md)**。
