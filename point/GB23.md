### Flank Sinews ZHÉJĪN
![[GB-23 辄筋.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder and Bladder channels

#### Location

Below the axilla in the fifth intercostal space, 1 cun anterior to [Yuānyè GB-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-22), approximately at the level of the nipple.

#### Location Notes

See [Yuānyè GB-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-22).

#### Needling

Transverse-oblique insertion along the intercostal space, 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** deep or perpendicular needling may induce a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Unbinds the chest and lowers rebellion
- Regulates qi in the three jiao

#### Indications

- Sudden fullness of the chest, pain of the lateral costal region, dyspnoea, asthma, scrofula, pain of the shoulder and arm.
- Sighing and tendency to sadness, insomnia, heat of the lower abdomen.
- Vomiting, acid regurgitation, much spittle, loss of use of the four limbs.

#### Commentary

According to the Great Compendium of Acupuncture and Moxibustion this point rather than [Rìyuè GB-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-24) is the front-mu point of the Gall Bladder, and an alternative name for Zhéjīn GB-23 given in this classic was Danmu i.e. 'Gall Bladder Mu'. In fact the classical indications for Zhéjīn GB-23 and [Rìyuè GB-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-24) are very similar, with a greater emphasis on disorders of the chest in the case of Zhéjīn GB-23 reflecting its higher location.

Excessive anger, frustration and resentment, especially when not spontaneously expressed, will impair the free-flowing function of the Liver. Qi stagnates along the course of the Gall Bladder channel in the lateral costal region, and binds the chest, restricting its smooth expansion and contraction. Breathing is impaired leading to fullness and pain, sighing and sadness. Zhéjīn GB-23 is an important local point used in the treatment of these conditions. If stagnation of qi transforms to fire it may affect the upper jiao (Heart) giving rise to sleep disturbance, the middle jiao leading to vomiting and acid regurgitation, or the lower jiao giving rise to heat of the lower abdomen.

#### Summary of clinical application

- According to the _Great Compendium of Acupuncture and Moxibustion_ this point rather than [Rìyuè GB-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-24) was designated as the front mu point of the Gall Bladder.
- Moves stagnant qi: fullness of the chest, pain of the lateral costal region, dyspnoea, asthma, sighing and tendency to sadness, insomnia.

### 1. [GB23 辄筋](point/GB23.md)

#### 1.1. Flank Sinews 輒筋

- 胆经与膀胱经的交会穴

#### 1.2. 定位

位于腋下第5肋间隙，[GB22 渊腋](point/GB22.md)前方1寸，约与乳头平齐。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

参见[GB22 渊腋](point/GB22.md)的定位说明。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

沿肋间隙横斜刺，深0.5至1寸。  
**注意：** 深刺或垂直刺入可能引起气胸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 解胸降逆
- 调三焦之气

#### 1.6. 主治

- 突发胸满、胁痛、呼吸困难、哮喘、瘰疬、肩臂痛。
- 叹息及易悲、失眠、下腹热。
- 呕吐、反酸、多涎、四肢不用。

#### 1.7. 论述

根据《针灸大成》，[GB23 辄筋](point/GB23.md)而非[GB24 日月](point/GB24.md)是胆的募穴，该经典给出的[GB23 辄筋](point/GB23.md)别名为“胆募”。实际上，[GB23 辄筋](point/GB23.md)与[GB24 日月](point/GB24.md)的经典适应症非常相似，但[GB23 辄筋](point/GB23.md)更强调胸部疾患，反映其位置较高。

过度愤怒、挫折和怨恨，尤其是未自然宣泄时，会损伤肝的疏泄功能。气滞沿胆经在胁部积聚，束缚胸部，限制其顺畅扩张与收缩。呼吸受阻，导致胸满疼痛、叹息和悲伤。[GB23 辄筋](point/GB23.md)是治疗这些病症的重要局部穴位。若气滞化火，可能影响上焦（心）引起睡眠障碍，中焦导致呕吐反酸，或下焦引起下腹热。

#### 1.8. 临床应用总结

- 根据《针灸大成》，此穴而非[GB24 日月](point/GB24.md)被指定为胆的募穴。
- 疏通气滞：胸满、胁痛、呼吸困难、哮喘、叹息及易悲、失眠。
