### Stomach Granary WÈICĀNG
![[BL-50 胃仓.png|250]]
#### Location

3 cun lateral to the midline, level with the lower border of the spinous process of the twelfth thoracic vertebra (T12) and level with [Weishu BL‑21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-21).

#### Location Notes

When the shoulder is relaxed, the three cun line corresponds to the medial border of the scapula.

#### Needling

Oblique insertion 0.3 to 0.5 cun.  
**Caution:** deep perpendicular or deep oblique needling in a medial direction carries a substantial risk of causing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Harmonises the middle jiao

#### Indications

- Abdominal fullness, deficiency distention, difficult ingestion, childhood nutritional impairment, sudden turmoil disorder, oedema.
- Aversion to cold, much cold, back pain.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Abdominal fullness, deficiency distention, difficult ingestion, childhood nutritional impairment, sudden turmoil disorder, oedema.
- Aversion to cold, much cold, back pain.

#### Combinations

- Difficult ingestion: Wèicāng BL-50, [Yìshè BL-49](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-49) and [Géguān BL-46](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-46) (Supplementing Life).

### **[BL50 胃仓](/point/BL50.md)** (WÈICĀNG)

* **英文名称：** Stomach Granary
* **中文名称：** 胃仓
* **国际代码：** BL-50
* **穴位属性：** **[BL50 胃仓](/point/BL50.md)** 是膀胱经第二侧线上的穴位，与**[BL21 胃俞](/point/BL21.md)** 平齐。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第十二胸椎（T12）棘突下缘水平，距中线旁开3寸，与**[BL21 胃俞](/point/BL21.md)** 平齐。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 肩部放松时，旁开3寸线对应肩胛骨内侧缘。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺0.3至0.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直深刺或向内侧深斜刺有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 调和中焦

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

腹部胀满，虚胀，吞咽困难，小儿疳积，霍乱吐泻，水肿。
怕冷，畏寒甚，背痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL50 胃仓](/point/BL50.md)** 临床上较少使用。其名称“胃仓”直指其与胃腑功能的相关性，作为与**[BL21 胃俞](/point/BL21.md)** 平齐的穴位，它主要通过调和中焦来发挥作用。

此穴主治腹部胀满、虚胀、吞咽困难和小儿疳积等消化系统问题，这些症状往往与脾胃运化失调、气机不畅有关。它对霍乱吐泻和水肿也有辅助疗效，这与其调理中焦、运化水湿的功效相符。此外，**[BL50 胃仓](/point/BL50.md)** 也用于治疗怕冷、畏寒甚和背痛，这可能与其温中散寒、通经止痛的作用相关。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 主治腹部胀满，虚胀，吞咽困难，小儿疳积，霍乱吐泻，水肿。
* 主治怕冷，畏寒甚，背痛。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 吞咽困难：**[BL50 胃仓](/point/BL50.md)**，**[BL49 意舍](/point/BL49.md)** 和 **[BL46 膈关](/point/BL46.md)**（《资生经》）。
