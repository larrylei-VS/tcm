### Kunlun Mountains KŪNLÚN
![[BL-60 昆仑.png|250]]
- Jing-River and Fire point of the Bladder channel
- Ma Dan-yang Heavenly Star point

#### Location

Behind the ankle joint, in the depression between the prominence of the lateral malleolus and the Achilles tendon.

#### Location Notes

Locate in the centre of the depression, midway between the prominence of the lateral malleolus and the posterior border of the Achilles tendon.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun, or directed superiorly to join with [Tàixī KID-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-3), 1.5 to 2 cun.  
**Caution:** contraindicated in pregnancy.

#### Actions

- Clears heat and lowers yang
- Pacifies wind and leads down excess
- Activates the entire Bladder channel and alleviates pain
- Relaxes the sinews and strengthens the lumbar spine
- Promotes labour

#### Indications

- Childhood epilepsy, epilepsy, madness, lockjaw, headache, heat in the head, visual dizziness, redness pain and swelling of the eyes, bursting eye pain, pain of the upper teeth, rhinitis with nosebleed.
- Stiff neck, contraction of the shoulder and back, Heart pain that radiates to the back, lumbar pain, sacral pain, pain of the coccyx, sciatica, pain behind the knee, ankle pain, heel pain, hemiplegia.
- Fullness of the chest, dyspnoea, cough, malaria, malaria with copious sweating.
- Difficult labour, retention of placenta, difficulty in conceiving, swelling of the genitals, abdominal pain, difficult defecation.

#### Commentary

Kūnlún BL-60 is the fire point of the taiyang Bladder channel and an important point to clear and descend excess wind, fire and yang from the upper part of the body. To best understand the actions of this point, three factors should be taken into account: i. as a fire point, Kūnlún BL-60 is able to clear heat, fire and excess yang; ii. taiyang (supreme yang) channel is the most yang of the six channels; iii. Kūnlún BL-60 is located on the foot, towards the inferior end of the Bladder channel which traverses the entire posterior (yang) portion of the body.

The principle "for diseases of the head select [points from] the feet" applies well to this point. Kūnlún BL-60 is able to clear heat and lead down excess yang from the head in cases of headache, heat in the head, redness, pain and swelling of the eyes, bursting eye pain, nosebleed and toothache, and to extinguish wind from the head in cases of epilepsy and lockjaw. Indeed as long ago as the third century CE the Systematic Classic of Acupuncture and Moxibustion stated that Kūnlún BL-60 "drains wind from the head to the feet". Although Kūnlún BL-60 is principally used clinically in the treatment of occipital headache, it is worth noting that the primary Bladder channel meets the Governing vessel and the Gall Bladder channel at [Bǎihuì DU-20](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-20) on the vertex, [Shéntíng DU-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-24) and [Tóulínqì GB-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-15) towards the front of the head, and points [Qūbìn GB-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-7) through to [Wángǔ GB-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-12) on the side of the head. For this reason, Kūnlún BL-60 may be considered for pain in any region of the head.

It is interesting that many of the indications of this point, characterised by uprising of yang, flaring up of fire and stirring of wind, suggest a clinical picture of Liver disharmony. Whilst Kūnlún BL-60 has no direct action on the Liver, and is therefore unable to treat the root of such disorders, it has a profound action on subduing the manifestations. At the same time, there is a parallel between Kūnlún BL-60, the fire point of the 'supreme yang' channel, and the fierce, indomitable and strong quality of Liver yang.

The ability of Kūnlún BL-60 to activate the entire length of the Bladder channel as well as to relax the sinews and strengthen the lumbar spine, renders it an essential point in the treatment of pain and contraction anywhere along the channel. It is the foremost distal point in the treatment of occipital headache and is often combined with [Hòuxī SI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-3) for this purpose. It is equally vital in the treatment of disorders of the back and spine from the neck right down to the coccyx. Kūnlún BL-60 is one of the few distal points which are able to treat disorders of the middle and upper back, again often combined with [Hòuxī SI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-3). It is specifically indicated for Heart pain that radiates from the chest through to the upper back, reflecting the pathway of the Bladder divergent channel to the Heart. In disorders of the lumbar region, in which Kidney deficiency is often the root, Kūnlún BL-60 may be joined by through-needling to [Tàixī KID-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-3). Ma Dan-yang, the great physician of the Jin dynasty, listed Kūnlún BL-60 as one of the 'eleven heavenly star points'[70](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references) "for cramping of the lumbar region and sacrum ... inability to walk or even take a step, as soon as he moves he groans". Kūnlún BL-60 is equally valuable in treating obstruction of the Bladder channel in the lower limbs, especially in cases of sciatic pain radiating down the back of the leg, and is often combined with points such as [Chéngfú BL-36](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-36) and [Wěizhōng BL-40](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-40) in the 'chain and lock' point association method. In the ankle region, Kūnlún BL-60 is an important point for pain and stiffness, and like several distal points of the Bladder channel it is able to treat pain of the heel.

The ability of Kūnlún BL-60 to promote labour is another reflection both of the strong descending action of this point, and of the paired relationship of the Bladder and the Kidneys, which dominate the uterus. It may be used, in combination with points such as [Hégǔ LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4), [Zhìyīn BL-67](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-67) and [Sānyīnjiāo SP-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-6), to induce labour, hasten prolonged labour, control pain and promote the expulsion of the placenta. For this reason Kūnlún BL-60 is contraindicated in pregnancy. The Great Compendium of Acupuncture & Moxibustion somewhat surprisingly however, also suggests this point for difficult conception.

Finally, it is interesting to note that Kūnlún BL-60 is indicated for fullness of the chest, dyspnoea and cough. Whilst these indications have no apparent relationship to the Bladder channel, they do reflect the ability of jing-river points, according to the Classic of Difficulties[71](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references), to treat dyspnoea and cough.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Descends excess wind, fire and yang from the upper part of the body: headache, heat in the head, dizziness, epilepsy, lockjaw, redness, pain and swelling of the eyes, bursting eye pain, pain of the upper teeth, rhinitis with nosebleed.
- Treats pain along the whole course of the Bladder channel: stiff neck, contraction of the shoulder and back, Heart pain that radiates to the back, lumbar pain, sacral pain, pain of the coccyx, sciatica, pain behind the knee, ankle pain, heel pain, hemiplegia.
- Promotes labour and treats retention of the placenta.

### **[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** (KŪNLÚN)

* **英文名称：** Kunlun Mountains
* **中文名称：** 昆仑
* **国际代码：** BL-60
* **穴位属性：** **[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 是膀胱经的经穴和火穴，马丹阳天星穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于踝关节后方，外踝尖与跟腱之间的凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 位于凹陷中央，外踝尖与跟腱后缘之间的中点。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至1寸，或向上（近端）透刺**[KID3 太溪](/point/KID3.md)**，1.5至2寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 孕妇禁针。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 清热降阳
* 平息肝风，引邪下行
* 通调膀胱经，止痛
* 舒筋，强腰椎
* 催产

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

小儿癫痫，癫痫，发狂，牙关紧闭，头痛，头热，眼睛红肿疼痛，眼痛如裂，上牙痛，鼻炎伴鼻出血。
颈项强直，肩背挛缩，心痛放射至背部，腰痛，骶骨疼痛，尾骨疼痛，坐骨神经痛，膝后疼痛，踝关节疼痛，足跟痛，半身不遂。
胸中胀满，呼吸困难，咳嗽，疟疾，疟疾伴多汗。
难产，胞衣不下，受孕困难，生殖器肿胀，腹痛，排便困难。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 是足太阳膀胱经的火穴，是清泻和下引身体上部过盛之风、火、阳的重要穴位。要充分理解此穴的作用，应考虑三个因素：i. 作为火穴，**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 能够清热、泻火、降阳；ii. 太阳经（至阳）是六经中阳气最盛的经脉；iii. **[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 位于足部，膀胱经的下端，膀胱经循行全身后部（阳部）。

“头病取足”的原则很适用于此穴。**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 能够清热，下引头部过盛之阳，治疗头痛、头热、眼睛红肿疼痛、眼痛如裂、鼻出血和牙痛，并息灭头部之风，治疗癫痫和牙关紧闭。实际上，早在公元三世纪的《针灸甲乙经》就记载**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** “泻风从头至足”。
尽管**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 临床上主要用于治疗枕部头痛，但值得注意的是，膀胱经正经在头顶的**[DU20 百会](/point/DU20.md)**、头部前方的**[DU24 神庭](/point/DU24.md)** 和**[GB15 头临泣](/point/GB15.md)**，以及头部侧面的**[GB7 曲鬓](/point/GB7.md)** 至**[GB12 完骨](/point/GB12.md)** 等穴与督脉和胆经交会。因此，**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 可用于治疗头部任何区域的疼痛。

有趣的是，此穴的许多主治，以阳气上亢、火邪上炎和风邪内动为特征，提示了肝脏失调的临床表现。虽然**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 对肝脏没有直接作用，因此不能治疗这些疾病的根本，但它在平息症状方面有深远作用。同时，**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 作为“至阳”经的火穴，与肝阳的刚猛、不屈、强大品质之间存在相似之处。

**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 能够通调膀胱经全程，并舒筋健腰椎，使其成为治疗经络循行任何部位疼痛和挛缩的关键穴位。它是治疗枕部头痛的首要远端穴位，常与**[SI3 后溪](/point/SI3.md)** 联合使用。它在治疗从颈部到尾骨的背部和脊柱疾病方面同样重要。**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 是少数能够治疗中上背部疾病的远端穴位之一，也常与**[SI3 后溪](/point/SI3.md)** 联合使用。它明确主治心痛放射至背部，这反映了膀胱经别通向心脏的路径。在腰部疾病中，通常以肾虚为根本，**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 可透刺至**[KID3 太溪](/point/KID3.md)**。金代名医马丹阳将**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 列为“十一穴天星穴”之一，“治腰骶部拘急……不能行走，动则呻吟”。**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 在治疗下肢膀胱经阻滞方面同样有价值，特别是沿腿后侧放射的坐骨神经痛，常与**[BL36 承扶](/point/BL36.md)** 和**[BL40 委中](/point/BL40.md)** 等穴联合使用，采用“链锁法”。在踝关节区域，**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 是治疗疼痛和僵硬的重要穴位，像膀胱经的几个远端穴位一样，它能够治疗足跟痛。

**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 催产的能力是此穴强大下行作用以及膀胱与肾脏（主宰子宫）表里关系之间的又一体现。它可与**[LI4 合谷](/point/LI4.md)**、**[BL67 至阴](/point/BL67.md)** 和**[SP6 三阴交](/point/SP6.md)** 等穴联合使用，以催产、加速难产、控制疼痛并促进胞衣排出。因此，**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 在妊娠期禁针。然而，令人惊讶的是，《针灸大成》也建议此穴用于治疗受孕困难。

最后，有趣的是，**[BL60 昆仑](/point/BL60.md)** 主治胸中胀满、呼吸困难和咳嗽。虽然这些主治与膀胱经没有明显关系，但它们确实反映了经穴（经水穴），根据《难经》记载，能够治疗呼吸困难和咳嗽。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 清泻身体上部过盛之风、火、阳：头痛，头热，眩晕，癫痫，牙关紧闭，眼睛红肿疼痛，眼痛如裂，上牙痛，鼻炎伴鼻出血。
* 治疗膀胱经全程的疼痛：颈项强直，肩背挛缩，心痛放射至背部，腰痛，骶骨疼痛，尾骨疼痛，坐骨神经痛，膝后疼痛，踝关节疼痛，足跟痛，半身不遂。
* 催产，治疗胞衣不下。
