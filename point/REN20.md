### Magnificent Canopy HUÁGÀI
![[REN-20 华盖.png|250]]
#### Location

On the midline of the sternum, level with the junction of the first intercostal space and the sternum.

#### Location Notes

First locate the costal cartilage of the second rib which is level with the sternal angle, then locate the first intercostal space above it.

#### Needling

Transverse insertion directed superiorly or inferiorly along the channel, 0.5 to 1 cun.

#### Actions

- Unbinds the chest
- Regulates and descends qi

#### Indications

- Dyspnoea, wheezing, asthma, cough, rebellious qi, dyspnoea with inability to speak, pain and fullness of the chest and lateral costal region.
- Difficult ingestion.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Dyspnoea, wheezing, asthma, cough, rebellious qi, dyspnoea with inability to speak, pain and fullness of the chest and lateral costal margin.
- Difficult ingestion.

#### Combinations

- Shortness of breath and difficulty catching the breath with inability to speak: Huágài REN-20 and [Shānzhōng REN-17](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-17) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Chronic pain of the lateral costal region: Huágài REN-20 and [Qìhù ST-13](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-13) (One Hundred Symptoms).



### 1. [REN20 华盖](point/REN20.md)

#### 1.1. Magnificent Canopy 華蓋

#### 1.2. 定位

位于胸骨中线，第一肋间隙与胸骨交界处。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

先定位第二肋软骨（与胸骨角平齐），向上找到第一肋间隙。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

沿经脉向上或向下平刺，深0.5至1寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 宽胸  
- 调气降逆  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 呼吸困难、喘鸣、哮喘、咳嗽、气逆、气短不能言、胸胁痛满。  
- 吞咽难。  

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 呼吸困难、喘鸣、哮喘、咳嗽、气逆、气短不能言、胸胁痛满。  
- 吞咽难。  

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 气短难续不能言：[REN20 华盖](point/REN20.md) + [REN17 膻中](point/REN17.md)（《千金方》）。  
- 慢性胁痛：[REN20 华盖](point/REN20.md) + [ST13 气户](point/ST13.md)（《百症赋》）。
