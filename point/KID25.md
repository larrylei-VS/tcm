### Spirit Storehouse SHÉNCÁNG
![[KID-25 神藏.png|250]]
#### Location

In the second intercostal space, 2 cun lateral to the midline.

#### Location Notes

i. First locate the costal cartilage of the second rib which is level with the sternal angle, then locate the second intercostal space below it, ii. Note that in males the nipple lies in the fourth intercostal space; iii. The 2 cun line is located midway between the midline and the mamillary line.

#### Needling

Transverse-oblique insertion, directed laterally along the intercostal space 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** deep perpendicular or oblique needling may puncture the lung.

#### Actions

- Unbinds the chest
- Lowers rebellious Lung and Stomach qi

#### Indications

- Cough, asthma, wheezing, dyspnoea, pain and oppression of the chest, fullness and distention of the chest and lateral costal region.
- Vomiting, agitation and fullness, fullness of the chest with no desire to eat.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Cough, asthma, wheezing, dyspnoea, pain and oppression of the chest, fullness and distention of the chest and lateral costal region.
- Vomiting, agitation and fullness, fullness of the chest with no desire to eat.

#### Combinations

- Fullness of the chest with stiffness of the neck: Shéncáng KID-25 and [Xuánjī REN-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-21) (One Hundred Symptoms).
- Vomiting with fullness of the chest: Shéncáng KID-25, [Shūfǔ KID-27](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-27), [Língxū KID-24](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-24) and [Jùguè REN-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-14) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[KID25 神藏](point/KID25.md)** (SHÉNCÁNG)

* **英文名称：** Spirit Storehouse
* **中文名称：** 神藏
* **国际代码：** KID-25

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于第二肋间隙，距中线旁开2寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 首先找到与胸骨角平齐的第二肋软骨，然后在其下方的第二肋间隙定位；
    * 请注意，男性乳头位于第四肋间隙；
    * 旁开2寸线位于中线与乳头线之间中点。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

横斜刺，针尖向外侧沿肋间隙刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直深刺或斜刺可能刺破肺脏。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 宽胸散结
* 肃降肺胃之逆气

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

咳嗽，哮喘，喘息，呼吸困难，胸部憋闷疼痛，胸胁胀满。
呕吐，心烦胀满，胸中胀满不欲食。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[KID25 神藏](point/KID25.md)** 属于肾经的胸部穴位，其名称“神藏”可能暗示其在心神与胸部气机调畅方面的作用，如同心神之所藏。它与**[KID22 步廊](point/KID22.md)**、**[KID23 神封](point/KID23.md)**、**[KID24 灵墟](point/KID24.md)**、**[KID26 彧中](point/KID26.md)** 和**[KID27 俞府](point/KID27.md)** 一同被列为胸部的俞穴。

此穴主要用于缓解**胸部胀满、疼痛**以及**呼吸困难、咳嗽、哮喘**等肺系和胸膈疾患。这些症状往往与气机上逆、痰阻胸膈有关。此外，**[KID25 神藏](point/KID25.md)** 对**呕吐**、**心烦胀满**和**食欲不振**等胃腑不和的症状也有辅助疗效，这与其肃降肺胃之逆气的功效相关。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 主治咳嗽，哮喘，喘息，呼吸困难，胸部憋闷疼痛，胸胁胀满。
* 主治呕吐，心烦胀满，胸中胀满不欲食。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 胸中胀满伴颈项强直：**[KID25 神藏](point/KID25.md)** 和 **[REN21 璇玑](point/REN21.md)**（《百症赋》）。
* 呕吐伴胸中胀满：**[KID25 神藏](point/KID25.md)**，**[KID27 俞府](point/KID27.md)**，**[KID24 灵墟](point/KID24.md)** 和 **[REN14 巨阙](point/REN14.md)**（《千金方》）。
