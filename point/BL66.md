### Foot Connecting Valley ZÚTŌNGGǓ
![[BL-66 通谷.png|250]]
- Ying-Spring and Water point of the Bladder channel

#### Location

On the lateral side of the foot, in the depression anterior and inferior to the fifth metatarso-phalangeal joint.

#### Location Notes

First locate [Shùgǔ BL-65](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-65), then run the finger over the prominence of the metatarso-phalangeal joint until it falls into the depression at the base of the little toe.

#### Needling

Perpendicular-oblique insertion towards the sole 0.2 to 0.3 cun.

#### Actions

- Clears the head
- Descends Lung and Stomach qi

#### Indications

- Heaviness of the head, neck pain, visual dizziness, redness of the eyes, nosebleed, sweating without aversion to cold, malaria.
- Vomiting, undigested food (in the stool), cough and dyspnoea, fullness of the chest with congested fluids.
- Mania, propensity to fright.

#### Commentary

Zútōnggǔ BL-66 is the water point of the Bladder water channel. Like all the distal points of the Bladder channel, its principal action is to clear pathologically ascending qi and yang from the head. This was emphasised in the Spiritual Pivot[75](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-bladder-channel/references) which stated "When [chaotic] qi is in the head, select [Tiānzhù BL-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-10) and [Dàzhù BL-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-11). If needling these fails to work, select the spring and stream points of the foot taiyang" (i.e. [Shùgǔ BL-65](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-65) and Zútōnggǔ BL-66).

If chaotic qi affects not only the head, but also impairs the descending function of the Lungs and Stomach, the consequent rebellion of qi will give rise to coughing, dyspnoea, fullness of the chest and vomiting.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Clears the head: heaviness of the head, neck pain, visual dizziness, redness of the eyes, nosebleed.
- Descends Lung and Stomach qi: coughing, dyspnoea, fullness of the chest, vomiting.

#### Combinations

- Rhinitis with nose bleed: Zútōnggǔ BL-66, [Pianli LI-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-6), [Hegu LI-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-4), [Sanjian LI-3 and](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-3) [Kūnlún BL-60](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-60) (Supplementing Life).
- Sudden loss of voice: Zútōnggǔ BL-66, [Zhīgōu SJ-6](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-6) and [Sānyángluò SJ-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-8) (Supplementing Life).
- Vomiting: Zútōnggǔ BL-66, [Shāngqiū SP-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-5) and [Yōumén KID-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/KID-21) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).

### **[BL66 足通谷](point/BL66.md)** (ZÚTŌNGGǓ)

* **英文名称：** Foot Connecting Valley
* **中文名称：** 足通谷
* **国际代码：** BL-66
* **穴位属性：** **[BL66 足通谷](point/BL66.md)** 是膀胱经的荥穴和水穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于足外侧，第五跖趾关节前下方凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 首先定位**[BL65 束骨](point/BL65.md)**，然后将手指滑过跖趾关节的隆起处，直到落入小趾基底的凹陷中。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直斜刺，向足底方向0.2至0.3寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 清头
* 肃降肺胃之气

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

头重，颈痛，视物眩晕，眼睛红赤，鼻出血，出汗不恶寒，疟疾。
呕吐，大便完谷不化，咳嗽呼吸困难，胸中胀满伴水饮。
癫狂，易受惊吓。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[BL66 足通谷](point/BL66.md)** 是膀胱水经的水穴。像膀胱经的所有远端穴位一样，其主要作用是清泻病理性上逆之气和阳气。
《灵枢·本输》强调了这一点，其中记载：“其气在头者，取**[BL10 天柱](point/BL10.md)**、**[BL11 大杼](point/BL11.md)**。刺之不愈，取足太阳之荥输”（即**[BL65 束骨](point/BL65.md)** 和**[BL66 足通谷](point/BL66.md)**）。

如果逆乱之气不仅影响头部，还损害肺胃的肃降功能，由此产生的气逆会上冲，引起咳嗽、呼吸困难、胸中胀满和呕吐。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 清头：头重，颈痛，视物眩晕，眼睛红赤，鼻出血。
* 肃降肺胃之气：咳嗽，呼吸困难，胸中胀满，呕吐。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 鼻炎伴鼻出血：**[BL66 足通谷](point/BL66.md)**，**[LI6 偏历](point/LI6.md)**，**[LI4 合谷](point/LI4.md)**，**[LI3 三间](point/LI3.md)** 和 **[BL60 昆仑](point/BL60.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 暴喑（突然失音）：**[BL66 足通谷](point/BL66.md)**，**[SJ6 支沟](point/SJ6.md)** 和 **[SJ8 三阳络](point/SJ8.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 呕吐：**[BL66 足通谷](point/BL66.md)**，**[SP5 商丘](point/SP5.md)** 和 **[KID21 幽门](point/KID21.md)**（《千金方》）。
