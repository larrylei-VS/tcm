### Clasping the White XIÁBÁI
![[LU-4 侠白.png|250]]
#### Location

On the antero-lateral aspect of the upper arm, 4 cun inferior to the axillary fold and 5 cun superior to [Chǐzé LU-5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-5), in the depression between the lateral border of the biceps brachii muscle and the shaft of the humerus.

#### Location Notes

Divide the distance between the axillary fold and the cubital crease of the elbow into equal thirds and locate Xiábái LU-4 one cun inferior to the junction of the upper and middle third ([Tiānfǔ LU-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-3)).

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.

#### Actions

- Descends Lung qi
- Regulates qi and blood in the chest

#### Indications

- Cough, dyspnoea, asthma, shortness of breath.
- Heart pain, palpitations, agitation and fullness, retching.
- Pain in the medial aspect of the arm, purple-white wind blotches (pityriasis versicolor).

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used
- Indicated for cough, dyspnoea, asthma, shortness of breath, Heart pain, palpitations, agitation and fullness, retching, pain in the medial aspect of the arm, purple-white wind blotches (pityriasis versicolor).

#### Combinations

- Pain of the Heart with shortness of breath: Xiábái LU-4, [Qímén LIV-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LIV-14), [Chángqiáng DU-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/DU-1), [Tiāntú REN-22](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-22) and [Zhōngchōng PC-9](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-9) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Purple-white wind blotches (pityriasis versicolor): Xiábái LU-4 and [Tiānfǔ LU-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LU-3) (Investigation Into Points Along the Channels).

### 1. [LU4 侠白](/point/LU4.md)

#### 1.2. 定位

位于上臂前外侧，腋窝褶下4寸，[LU5 尺泽](/point/LU5.md)上5寸，肱二头肌外侧缘与肱骨干之间的凹陷处。

#### 1.3. 定位说明

将腋窝褶至肘窝分为三等分，[LU4 侠白](/point/LU4.md)位于上三分之一与中三分之一交界处（[LU3 天府](/point/LU3.md)）下方1寸。

#### 1.4. 针刺方法

垂直刺入，深0.5至1寸。

#### 1.5. 作用

- 降肺气  
- 调胸中气血  

#### 1.6. 主治

- 咳嗽、呼吸困难、哮喘、气短。  
- 心痛、心悸、烦满、干呕。  
- 臂内侧痛、紫白风斑（癣）。  

#### 1.7. 临床应用总结

- 使用较少。  
- 用于咳嗽、呼吸困难、哮喘、气短、心痛、心悸、烦满、干呕、臂内侧痛、紫白风斑（癣）。  

#### 1.8. 配伍

- 心痛伴气短：[LU4 侠白](/point/LU4.md) + [LIV14 期门](/point/LIV14.md) + [DU1 长强](/point/DU1.md) + [REN22 天突](/point/REN22.md) + [PC9 中冲](/point/PC9.md)（《千金方》）。  
- 紫白风斑（癣）：[LU4 侠白](/point/LU4.md) + [LU3 天府](/point/LU3.md)（《经穴探》）。
