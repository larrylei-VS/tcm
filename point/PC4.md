### Xi-Cleft Gate XĪMÉN
![[PC-4.png|250]]


- Xi-Cleft point of the Pericardium channel

##### Location 

On the flexor aspect of the forearm, 5 cun proximal to [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/262), on the line connecting [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/262) and [Qūzé PC-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/258), between the tendons of palmaris longus and flexor carpi radialis.

##### Location note 

i. In the absence of the tendon of palmaris longus, locate this point on the ulnar side of the tendon of flexor carpi radialis; ii. Divide the distance between the cubital crease and [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/262) into half and locate this point 1 cun distal to this midpoint.

##### Needling 

Perpendicular insertion, 0.5 to 1 cun or oblique proximal insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

##### Actions 

- Invigorates blood and dispels stasis
- Cools blood and stops bleeding
- Calms the spirit
- Moderates acute conditions

##### Indications 

- Chest pain, Heart pain, Heart pain with vomiting, vomiting blood, coughing blood, nosebleed, five palms agitated and hot.
- Agitation, insomnia, melancholy, fear and fright of people, insufficiency of spirit qi, epilepsy.
- Chronic malaria, chronic haemorrhoids.

##### Commentary 

Xīmén PC-4 is the xi-cleft point of the Pericardium channel. The xi-cleft points are where the qi and blood, which flow relatively superficially along the channels from the jing-well points, gather and plunge more deeply. The xi-cleft points in general are indicated in the treatment of acute conditions and pain, whilst the xi-cleft points of the yin channels have an additional action of treating disorders of blood. Xīmén PC-4 is an important point to treat both acute stasis of blood and heat in the blood.

Through its dual actions of invigorating blood and moderating acute conditions, Xīmén PC-4 is a primary point for treating stagnation of blood in the chest and Heart, giving rise to acute pain which may radiate to the neck, back or left arm, or be accompanied by vomiting. Its important role in the treatment of Heart pain was both emphasised in the classics and is borne out by modern clinical practice and research.

By virtue of its ability to clear heat from the blood and stop bleeding, Xīmén PC-4 is indicated for hot reckless bleeding in the upper jiao giving rise to nosebleed, and vomiting or coughing of blood.

The second principal group of indications for v includes a variety of mental and emotional disorders such as agitation of the Heart, insomnia, melancholy, and fear and fright of people. The relationship between Xīmén PC-4 and emotional disorders is expressed through its effect on the blood and the Heart qi. The Heart rules the blood and houses the spirit, and there is therefore a reciprocal relationship between blood and disturbance of the spirit. On the one hand when the blood is stagnant and does not flow freely, essential nourishment will not reach the Heart and the Heart's function of housing the spirit may be disrupted. On the other hand, emotional disturbance may lead to stagnation of blood. The Spiritual Pivot[5](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-pericardium-channel/references) states " ... Internally a person may be injured by worry and anger; when this occurs the qi will rebel upwards; when the qi rebels thus the six shu [points of the six channels] will not flow, the warm qi will not circulate and internally the congealed blood will coagulate and not scatter ...". By resolving stasis of blood Xīmén PC-4 is able both to treat emotional disharmony and to resolve the Heart blood stasis that results from it.

Blood stasis in the Heart most commonly occurs as a result of deficiency of Heart qi and yang. These patterns frequently give rise to feelings of fear, melancholy and a diminished spirit, typically seen in patients after a myocardial infarct or cardiac surgery (the shock of which may further injure the Heart qi). Xīmén PC-4 is able to regulate the Heart qi, as well as the blood, and is classically indicated for insufficiency of the spirit qi.

Finally there may be symptoms of mental and emotional agitation, as well as haemorrhage, when heat enters the nutritive or blood levels during febrile disease and rises to disturb the spirit. Thus the Treatise on Epidemic Warm Febrile Disease stated "When the nutritive system is invaded by heat, the blood is consumed, the spirit is disturbed and there is insomnia". Xīmén PC-4 is able to calm the spirit in such cases by clearing heat from the nutritive and blood levels.

##### Summary of clinical application 

- Xi-cleft point for blood stasis in the chest and Heart: much used for acute pain which may radiate to the neck, back or left arm, or be accompanied by vomiting.
- Xi-cleft point for haemorrhage due to heat (nosebleed, coughing or vomiting blood) and for heat entering the nutritive and blood levels.
- Treats emotional disorders such as agitation of the Heart, insomnia, melancholy, and fear and fright of people.

##### Combinations 

- Heart pain: Xīmén PC-4, [Qūzé PC-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/258) and [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/262) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Heart pain with retching, agitation and fullness: Xīmén PC-4 and [Jíquán HE-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/HE/140) (Supplementing Life).
- Coughing blood: Xīmén PC-4 and [Dàlíng PC-7](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/P/262) (Systematic Classic).
- Fright and fear of people, spirit qi insufficient: Xīmén PC-4 and [Dàzhōng KID-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/KID/170) (Thousand Ducat Formulas).
- Pain of the chest and lateral costal region: Xīmén PC-4, [Dàbāo SP-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/SP/344), [Sānyángluò SJ-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/SJ/329), [Yángfǔ GB-38](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/GB/127) and [Zúlínqì GB-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/manual/points/GB/131).



### **[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)** (Xīmén)

* **英文名称：** Xi-Cleft Gate
* **中文名称：** 郄门
* **国际代码：** PC-4
* **穴位属性：** **[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)** 是心包经的郄穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于前臂屈侧，**[PC7 大陵](/point/PC7.md)** 上方5寸，在连接**[PC7 大陵](/point/PC7.md)** 与**[PC3 曲泽](/point/PC3.md)** 的连线上，掌长肌腱与桡侧腕屈肌腱之间。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 若无掌长肌腱，此穴位于桡侧腕屈肌腱尺侧；
    * 将肘横纹与**[PC7 大陵](/point/PC7.md)** 之间的距离平分，此穴位于该中点下方1寸处。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至1寸，或向近端斜刺1至1.5寸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 活血化瘀
* 凉血止血
* 宁心安神
* 缓急止痛

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

胸痛，心痛，心痛伴呕吐，呕血，咳血，鼻出血，五心烦热。
烦躁，失眠，忧郁，恐惧怕人，神气不足，癫痫。
慢性疟疾，慢性痔疮。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)** 是心包经的郄穴。郄穴是气血从井穴沿经脉相对浅层流注到此聚集并深入的地方。郄穴通常主治急性病症和疼痛，而阴经的郄穴还有治疗血症的额外作用。**[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)** 是治疗急性血瘀和血热的重要穴位。

通过其活血化瘀和缓急止痛的双重作用，**[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)** 是治疗胸部和心脏血瘀的主要穴位，引起急性疼痛，可能放射至颈部、背部或左臂，或伴有呕吐。其在治疗心痛方面的重要作用在经典中得到强调，并为现代临床实践和研究所证实。

凭借其清热凉血、止血的能力，**[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)** 主治上焦热性妄行出血，引起鼻出血、呕血或咳血。

第二个主要主治包括多种精神情志障碍，如心烦、失眠、忧郁和恐惧怕人。**[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)** 与情志障碍的关系通过其对血液和心气的影响来表达。心主血，藏神，因此血液与神志扰乱之间存在相互关系。一方面，当血液瘀滞不畅时，精微物质无法到达心脏，心藏神的功能可能受到干扰。另一方面，情志扰乱也可能导致血瘀。
《灵枢·本神》记载：“……内伤于忧怒，此气逆也；气逆者，六输不通，温气不行，故内郁血凝而不散……”通过化解血瘀，**[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)** 既能治疗情志不和，又能化解由此产生的心脏血瘀。

心脏血瘀最常见是由于心气心阳不足所致。这些证型常引起恐惧、忧郁和神气不足感，典型见于心肌梗死或心脏手术后的患者（手术的冲击可能进一步损伤心气）。**[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)** 能够调理心气和血液，经典中主治神气不足。

最后，当热邪在热病期间进入营分或血分，并上扰心神时，可能出现精神情志烦躁不安以及出血症状。因此，《温病条辨》记载：“热邪入营，血耗神扰，则失眠”。**[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)** 能够通过清泄营血分热邪，在此类情况下宁心安神。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 郄穴，治疗胸部和心脏血瘀：广泛用于急性疼痛，可能放射至颈部、背部或左臂，或伴有呕吐。
* 郄穴，治疗热性出血（鼻出血，咳血或呕血）以及热邪入营血分。
* 治疗情志障碍，如心烦、失眠、忧郁和恐惧怕人。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 心痛：**[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)**，**[PC3 曲泽](/point/PC3.md)** 和 **[PC7 大陵](/point/PC7.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 心痛伴干呕，烦躁胀满：**[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)** 和 **[HE1 极泉](/point/HE1.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 咳血：**[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)** 和 **[PC7 大陵](/point/PC7.md)**（《针灸甲乙经》）。
* 恐惧怕人，神气不足：**[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)** 和 **[KID4 大钟](/point/KID4.md)**（《千金方》）。
* 胸胁疼痛：**[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)**，**[SP21 大包](/point/SP21.md)**，**[SJ8 三阳络](/point/SJ8.md)**，**[GB38 阳辅](/point/GB38.md)** 和 **[GB41 足临泣](/point/GB41.md)**。
