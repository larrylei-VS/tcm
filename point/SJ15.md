### Heavenly Crevice Tiānliáo
- Meeting point of the Sanjiao and Gall Bladder channels and the Yang Linking vessel
![[SJ-15 天髎.png|250]]

#### Location 

In the suprascapular fossa, in the depression midway between Jiānjǐng GB-21 and Qūyuán SI-13 (at the medial end of the suprascapular fossa).

#### Location note 

This point may also be located midway between the lateral extremity of the acromion and Dàzhuī DU-14, and 1 cun posterior to Jiānjǐng GB-21 (the highest point of the trapezius muscle).

#### Needling 

Oblique insertion directed according to the clinical manifestations, 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** perpendicular insertion, especially in thin patients, carries a substantial risk of inducing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions 

- Dispels wind-damp, activates the channel and alleviates pain
- Unbinds the chest and regulates qi

#### Indications 

- Pain of the shoulder and arm, stiffness and pain of the neck, tension of the nape of the neck, pain in the supraclavicular fossa.
- Agitation and oppression of the chest, heat and fullness of the chest, oppression of the Heart with absence of sweating, heat in the body with absence of sweating, chills and fever, febrile disease.

#### Commentary 

Tiānliáo SJ-15 and [Jiānjǐng GB-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-21) are neighbouring points with a similar effect in the treatment of stiffness, pain and spasm in the area between the shoulder joint and the neck. In such situations they should both be palpated, and the more tender should be needled. Like [Jiānjǐng GB-21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-21), the action of Tiānliáo SJ-15 also extends downwards into the chest, where it is indicated for agitation, oppression, heat and fullness.

#### Summary of clinical application 

- Treats stiffness, pain and spasm in the area between the shoulder joint and the neck as well as agitation, oppression, heat and fullness of the chest.

#### Combinations 

- Pain and heaviness of the shoulder with inability to raise the arm: Tiānliáo SJ-15 and [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11) (Supplementing Life).

### **[SJ15 天髎](point/SJ15.md)** (TIĀNLIÁO)

* **英文名称：** Heavenly Crevice
* **中文名称：** 天髎
* **国际代码：** SJ-15
* **穴位属性：** **[SJ15 天髎](point/SJ15.md)** 是三焦经与胆经、阳维脉的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于肩胛骨上窝，**[GB21 肩井](point/GB21.md)** 与 **[SI13 曲垣](point/SI13.md)**（肩胛骨上窝内侧端）之间中点的凹陷中。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 此穴也可定位在肩峰外侧端与 **[DU14 大椎](point/DU14.md)** 之间中点，以及 **[GB21 肩井](point/GB21.md)**（斜方肌最高点）后方1寸处。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

斜刺，根据临床表现调整针刺方向，0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直刺入，特别是在瘦弱患者中，有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 祛风湿，通经活络，止痛
* 宽胸理气

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

肩臂疼痛，颈部僵硬疼痛，颈项部紧张，锁骨上窝疼痛。
胸中烦躁憋闷，胸部灼热胀满，心胸憋闷伴无汗，身热无汗，恶寒发热，热病。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SJ15 天髎](point/SJ15.md)** 与 **[GB21 肩井](point/GB21.md)** 邻近，在治疗肩关节与颈部之间区域的僵硬、疼痛和痉挛方面有相似效果。在这种情况下，应同时触诊这两个穴位，并选择压痛更明显的进行针刺。像 **[GB21 肩井](point/GB21.md)** 一样，**[SJ15 天髎](point/SJ15.md)** 的作用也向下延伸至胸部，主治烦躁、憋闷、灼热和胀满。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 治疗肩关节与颈部之间区域的僵硬、疼痛和痉挛，以及胸部烦躁、憋闷、灼热和胀满。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 肩部沉重疼痛不能抬臂：**[SJ15 天髎](point/SJ15.md)** 和 **[LI11 曲池](point/LI11.md)**（《资生经》）。
