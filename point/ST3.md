## ST-3 

### Great Crevice JÙLIÁO
![[ST-3 巨髎.png|294]]
- Meeting point of the Stomach channel with the Yang Motility vessel

#### Location

With the eyes looking directly forwards, this point is located directly below the pupil, level with the lower border of the ala nasi, on the lateral side of the naso-labial groove.

#### Needling

i. Perpendicular insertion 0.3 to 0.4 cun; ii. Transverse insertion to join with such points as [Dìcāng ST-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-4), [Quánliáo SI-18](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-18) etc.

#### Actions

- Eliminates wind, dissipates swelling and alleviates pain

#### Indications

- Pain and swelling of the external nose and cheek, nosebleed, toothache, swelling and pain of the lips and cheek, deviation of the mouth, aversion to wind and cold in the face and eyes, superficial visual obstruction, excessive lacrimation, clonic spasm.
- Leg qi, swelling of the knee.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for pain and swelling of the external nose and cheek, nosebleed, toothache, swelling and pain of the lips and cheek, deviation of the mouth, aversion to wind and cold in the face and eyes, superficial visual obstruction, excessive lacrimation, clonic spasm, leg qi, swelling of the knee.

#### Combinations

- Pain and swelling of the cheek: Jùliáo ST-3 and [Tiānchuāng SI-16](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SI-16) (Supplementing Life).

### [ST3 巨髎](/point/ST3.md) (JÙLIÁO)

- **英文名称：** Great Crevice
- **中文名称：** 巨髎
- **国际代码：** ST-3
- **穴位属性：** [ST3 巨髎](/point/ST3.md) 是胃经与阳跷脉的交会穴。

#### 定位 (Location)

眼睛直视前方时，此穴位于瞳孔直下方，平鼻翼下缘，鼻唇沟外侧。

#### 针刺方法 (Needling)

i. 垂直刺0.3至0.4寸； ii. 横刺透向[ST4 地仓](/point/ST4.md)、[SI18 颧髎](/point/SI18.md) 等穴。

#### 作用 (Actions)

- 祛风消肿止痛

#### 主治病症 (Indications)

鼻外侧及面颊疼痛肿胀，鼻出血，牙痛，口唇面颊肿胀疼痛，口歪，面部和眼睛怕风畏寒，外障眼病，泪液过多，痉挛。

腿气，膝关节肿胀。

#### 阐释 (Commentary)

[ST3 巨髎](/point/ST3.md) 临床上较少使用。主治鼻外侧及面颊疼痛肿胀，鼻出血，牙痛，口唇面颊肿胀疼痛，口歪，面部和眼睛怕风畏寒，外障眼病，泪液过多，痉挛，腿气，膝关节肿胀。

#### 临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)

临床上较少使用。

主治鼻外侧及面颊疼痛肿胀，鼻出血，牙痛，口唇面颊肿胀疼痛，口歪，面部和眼睛怕风畏寒，外障眼病，泪液过多，痉挛，腿气，膝关节肿胀。

#### 穴位组合 (Combinations)

面颊疼痛肿胀：[ST3 巨髎](/point/ST3.md) 和[SI16 天窗](/point/SI16.md)（《资生经》）。
