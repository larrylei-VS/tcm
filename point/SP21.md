### Great Wrapping DÀBĀO
![[SP-21 大包.png|250]]
- Great Luo-Connecting point of the Spleen

#### Location

On the mid-axillary line, in the seventh intercostal space. Note: some sources locate this point in the sixth intercostal space.

#### Location Notes

i. The intercostal spaces are most reliably counted downwards from the second intercostal space which lies immediately below the sternal angle. In males, the nipple almost invariably lies in the fourth intercostal space; ii. the mid-axillary line is drawn vertically down from the apex of the axilla ([Jíquán HE-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-1)).

#### Needling

Transverse-oblique insertion along the intercostal space, 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** perpendicular insertion, especially in thin patients, carries a substantial risk of inducing a pneumothorax.

#### Actions

- Regulates qi and blood and firms the sinews and joints
- Unbinds the chest and benefits the lateral costal region

#### Indications

- Pain of the whole body, weariness of the four limbs, flaccidity of the limbs, flaccidity of the hundred joints.
- Cough, dyspnoea, chest pain, pain in the chest and lateral costal region on respiration, distention and fullness of the lateral costal region.

#### Commentary

The name of the point Dàbāo SP-21 is made up of two characters, 'Da' meaning great, and 'Bao' meaning 'to wrap', or 'to envelop'. According to the Spiritual Pivot[21](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/book/pages/channels/the-spleen-channel/references) "The great luo of the Spleen is known as Dabao; it emerges at three cun below the axilla spreading in the chest and lateral costal region. When it is excess there is pain of the whole body. When it is deficient the hundred joints are flaccid. This channel embraces the blood of all the luo [connecting channels]".

A possible explanation of this passage from the Spiritual Pivot is that one of the functions of the luo-connecting channels in general is to assist in the distribution of qi, and more especially blood, to all the tissues of the body via the network of the minute luo-connecting channels. Since the Spleen controls blood, its great luo-connecting channel dominates this function of blood distribution throughout the body. When blood stagnates "there is pain of the whole body"; when blood is deficient and unable to nourish the tissues "the hundred joints are flaccid".

Dàbāo SP-21 is also mentioned in the Ode to Elucidate Mysteries which says that it dominates the upper region ([Tiānshū ST-25](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/ST-25) dominates the middle region and [Dìjī SP-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-8) dominates the lower region). Despite these two classical references, Dàbāo SP-21 appears in no traditional combinations in any of the major classical texts.

#### Summary of clinical application

- The great-luo connecting point of the Spleen which is said to embrace the blood of all the luo-connecting channels: pain of the whole body, weariness of the four limbs, flaccidity of the limbs, flaccidity of the hundred joints.

#### Combinations

- Pain of the whole body and weariness of the four limbs: Dàbāo SP-21, [Yánglíngquán GB-34](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-34), [Qūchí LI-11](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-11) and [Tiānzhù BL-10](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/BL-10).
- Pain of the chest and lateral costal region: Dàbāo SP-21, [Sānyángluò SJ-8](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-8), [Xīmén PC-4](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/PC-4), [Yángfǔ GB-38](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-38) and [Zúlínqì GB-41](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/GB-41).

### **[SP21 大包](/point/SP21.md)** (DÀBĀO)

* **英文名称：** Great Wrapping
* **中文名称：** 大包
* **国际代码：** SP-21
* **穴位属性：** **[SP21 大包](/point/SP21.md)** 是脾之大络穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于腋中线上，第七肋间隙中。
注意：有些文献将此穴定位在第六肋间隙。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 肋间隙最可靠的计数方法是从胸骨角下方的第二肋间隙向下数。男性乳头几乎总位于第四肋间隙；
    * 腋中线是从腋窝顶点 (**[HE1 极泉](/point/HE1.md)**) 垂直向下画的线。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

沿肋间隙横斜刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 垂直刺入，特别是在瘦弱患者中，有很大风险引起气胸。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 调理气血，强筋健骨
* 宽胸利胁

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

全身疼痛，四肢倦怠，肢体痿软，百节痿软。
咳嗽，呼吸困难，胸痛，呼吸时胸胁疼痛，胁肋部胀满。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SP21 大包](/point/SP21.md)** 的名称由两个字组成，“大”意为大，而“包”意为“包裹”或“包容”。根据《灵枢·经脉》记载：“脾之大络，名曰大包，出腋下三寸，布胸胁。其有余也，全身皆痛；其不足也，百节皆纵。此络挟三阴之血而行。”（此引用为意译，原文略有不同）。

对《灵枢》这段经文的一种可能解释是，络脉的一般功能是协助将气，特别是血液，通过细微络脉的网络输布到身体的所有组织。由于脾脏主血，其大络主宰全身血液的输布功能。当血瘀时，“全身皆痛”；当血虚不能滋养组织时，“百节皆纵”。

**[SP21 大包](/point/SP21.md)** 在《标幽赋》中也有提及，其中说它主宰上部（**[ST25 天枢](/point/ST25.md)** 主宰中部，**[SP8 地机](/point/SP8.md)** 主宰下部）。尽管有这两部经典文献的提及，但 **[SP21 大包](/point/SP21.md)** 在任何主要经典著作中都没有出现在传统的穴位组合中。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 脾之大络穴，据称能包容所有络脉之血：全身疼痛，四肢倦怠，肢体痿软，百节痿软。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 全身疼痛和四肢倦怠：**[SP21 大包](/point/SP21.md)**，**[GB34 阳陵泉](/point/GB34.md)**，**[LI11 曲池](/point/LI11.md)** 和 **[BL10 天柱](/point/BL10.md)**。
* 胸胁疼痛：**[SP21 大包](/point/SP21.md)**，**[SJ8 三阳络](/point/SJ8.md)**，**[PC4 郄门](/point/PC4.md)**，**[GB38 阳辅](/point/GB38.md)** 和 **[GB41 足临泣](/point/GB41.md)**。
