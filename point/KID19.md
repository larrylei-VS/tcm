### Yin Metropolis YĪNDŪ
![[KID-19 阴都.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Kidney channel with the Penetrating vessel

#### Location

On the upper abdomen, 4 cun above the umbilicus, 0.5 cun lateral to the midline ([Zhōngwǎn REN-12](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-12)).

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.  
**Caution:** deep needling may penetrate the peritoneal cavity.

#### Actions

- Regulates qi and harmonises the Stomach
- Lowers rebellion and alleviates cough and wheezing

#### Indications

- Epigastric pain, counterflow qi, vomiting, nausea, fullness and agitation below the Heart, distention and pain of the abdomen, twisting pain in the abdomen, borborygmus, difficult defecation, infertility, malign blood in the uterus.
- Cough, distention of the chest, pain and heat of the lateral costal region, malaria, eye pain, redness and pain of the eye originating at the inner canthus.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for epigastric pain, counterflow qi, vomiting, nausea, fullness and agitation below the Heart, distention and pain of the abdomen, twisting pain in the abdomen, borborygmus, difficult defecation, infertility, malign blood in the uterus, cough, distention of the chest, pain and heat of the lateral costal region, malaria, eye pain, redness and pain of the eye originating at the inner canthus.

#### Combinations

- Malaria with generalised fever: Yīndū KID-19, Shao-[Shàohǎi HE-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/HE-3), [Shāngyáng LI-1](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-1), [Sānjiān LI-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/LI-3) and [Zhōngzhǔ SJ-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SJ-3) (Supplementing Life).
- Agitation and fullness of the Heart: Yīndū KID-19 and [Jùguè REN-14](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/REN-14) (Supplementing Life).

### **[KID19 阴都](/point/KID19.md)** (YĪNDŪ)

* **英文名称：** Yin Metropolis
* **中文名称：** 阴都
* **国际代码：** KID-19
* **穴位属性：** **[KID19 阴都](/point/KID19.md)** 是肾经与冲脉的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于上腹部，脐上4寸，距中线旁开0.5寸（**[REN12 中脘](/point/REN12.md)** 旁开0.5寸）。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺1至1.5寸。

* **注意 (Caution):** 深刺可能刺穿腹膜腔。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 理气，调和胃腑
* 降逆，止咳平喘

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

胃脘痛，气逆，呕吐，恶心，心下胀满烦躁，腹部胀痛，腹部绞痛，肠鸣，排便困难，不孕症，子宫恶血。
咳嗽，胸部胀满，胁肋部疼痛发热，疟疾，眼痛，内眼角红肿疼痛。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[KID19 阴都](/point/KID19.md)** 临床上较少使用。其名称“阴都”可能暗示其与肾阴和腹部的关系，作为肾经与冲脉的交会穴，它在调理腹部气机和胃腑功能方面具有作用。

此穴主要用于缓解**胃脘痛**、**呕吐**、**恶心**、**腹胀痛**和**肠鸣**等消化系统症状。它对**咳嗽**、**喘息**、**胸部胀满**等肺系症状也有一定的辅助疗效，这与其降逆、止咳平喘的功效相关。

此外，**[KID19 阴都](/point/KID19.md)** 也主治**不孕症**和**子宫恶血**（离经之血积聚），这与其调理冲脉、活血化瘀的作用有关。它对**胁肋部疼痛发热**和**眼部疾患**（如眼痛、内眼角红肿疼痛）也有治疗作用，这可能通过肾经与肝、眼部的联系实现。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治胃脘痛，气逆，呕吐，恶心，心下胀满烦躁，腹部胀痛，腹部绞痛，肠鸣，排便困难，不孕症，子宫恶血。
* 主治咳嗽，胸部胀满，胁肋部疼痛发热，疟疾，眼痛，内眼角红肿疼痛。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 全身发热的疟疾：**[KID19 阴都](/point/KID19.md)**、**[HE3 少海](/point/HE3.md)**、**[LI1 商阳](/point/LI1.md)**、**[LI3 三间](/point/LI3.md)** 和 **[SJ3 中渚](/point/SJ3.md)**（《资生经》）。
* 心烦胀满：**[KID19 阴都](/point/KID19.md)** 和 **[REN14 巨阙](/point/REN14.md)**（《资生经》）。
