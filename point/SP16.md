### Abdomen Sorrow FÙĀI
![[SP-16 腹哀.png|250]]
- Meeting point of the Spleen channel with the Yin Linking vessel

#### Location

On the abdomen, in the depression at the lateral border of the rectus abdominis muscle, 3 cun superior to [Dàhéng SP-15](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-15). Note: in some patients with a narrow subcostal angle, this point may fall on the costal margin; the options then are to i. locate more medially, ii. needle transversely on the costal margin, iii. select a different point.

#### Location Notes

This point is normally defined as 4 cun lateral to the midline, and on the abdomen the 4 cun line is located at the lateral border of the rectus abdominis muscle.

#### Needling

Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun.  
**Caution:** _i_. in thin patients deep needling may penetrate the peritoneal cavity; _ii_. deep needling at this point may penetrate an enlarged spleen.

#### Actions

- Regulates the intestines

#### Indications

- Periumbilical pain, cold of the abdomen, undigested food (in the stool), dysenteric disorder, stool containing pus and blood, constipation.

#### Summary of clinical application

- Rarely used.
- Indicated for periumbilical pain, cold of the abdomen, undigested food (in the stool), dysenteric disorder, stool containing pus and blood, constipation.

#### Combinations

- Undigested food (in the stool): Fùāi SP-16 and [Tàibái SP-3](https://app.amanualofacupuncture.com/point/SP-3) (Supplementing Life).

### **[SP16 腹哀](/point/SP16.md)** (FÙĀI)

* **英文名称：** Abdomen Sorrow
* **中文名称：** 腹哀
* **国际代码：** SP-16
* **穴位属性：** **[SP16 腹哀](/point/SP16.md)** 是脾经与阴维脉的交会穴。

#### **定位 (Location)**

位于腹部，腹直肌外缘凹陷中，**[SP15 大横](/point/SP15.md)** 上方3寸。

* **定位要点 (Location Notes):**
    * 此穴通常定义为距前正中线旁开4寸，腹部旁开4寸线位于腹直肌可触及的外缘。
    * 对于一些剑突下角较窄的患者，此穴可能落在肋缘上。此时可选择：i. 向内侧定位； ii. 在肋缘处横刺； iii. 选择其他穴位。

#### **针刺方法 (Needling)**

垂直刺0.5至1寸。

* **注意 (Caution):**
    * 瘦弱患者深刺可能刺穿腹膜腔；
    * 深刺此穴可能刺穿肿大的脾脏。

#### **作用 (Actions)**

* 调理肠道

#### **主治病症 (Indications)**

脐周疼痛，腹部受寒，大便完谷不化，痢疾，大便脓血，便秘。

#### **阐释 (Commentary)**

**[SP16 腹哀](/point/SP16.md)** 这个名称可能指的是腹部不适或悲伤的情绪，因为它能够缓解腹部的疼痛和功能失调。作为脾经的穴位，它主要作用于肠道，能够处理多种消化系统问题。

此穴主治脐周疼痛，这可能由气滞、寒凝或湿滞引起。它对腹部受寒引起的症状特别有效，例如腹泻和痢疾，特别是当大便中含有未消化食物或脓血时。此外，它也能调理肠道，帮助缓解便秘。

#### **临床应用总结 (Summary of clinical application)**

* 临床上较少使用。
* 主治脐周疼痛，腹部受寒，大便完谷不化，痢疾，大便脓血，便秘。

#### **穴位组合 (Combinations)**

* 大便完谷不化：**[SP16 腹哀](/point/SP16.md)** 和 **[SP3 太白](/point/SP3.md)**（《资生经》）。
